# Mirage Arcane

You make terrain in an area up to 1 square mile look, sound, smell, and even feel like some other sort of terrain.

---

> **Mirage Arcane**
> _7th-level Illusion_
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes
> **Range**: Sight
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 10 days
>
> Within the area, you can alter the appearance of structures, add them where none are present, or remove them. Similarly, you can alter the appearance of creatures so that they appear as other creatures, but they must be present in the area. The tactile characteristics of the terrain are also changed according to the visual illusion, so that creatures entering the area are fooled, as are those who are present at the time the spell is cast. If you change a dirt road into a river, for example, those who step onto the illusionary river would find themselves immersed in cold water and would be able to swim in it. If you turn a lake into a field, the creatures could walk on the grass as if it were real. Any piece of the illusion (such as a rock or stick) that is removed from the spell’s area disappears immediately.
>
> Creatures with truesight can see through the illusion to the terrain’s true form, however, all other senses are fooled.
