# Swift Quiver

The quiver's ammunition snaps to life, each bolt whirring with anticipation.

---

> **Swift Quiver**  
> _5th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a quiver containing at least one piece of ammunition)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You transmute your quiver so it produces an endless supply of mundane ammunition, which seems to leap into your hand when you reach for it. On each of your turns until the spell ends, you can use a Minor Action to make two attacks with a weapon that uses ammunition from the quiver. Each time you make such a ranged attack, your quiver magically replaces the piece of ammunition you used with a similar piece of mundane ammunition. Any ammunition created by this spell vanishes when the spell ends. If the quiver leaves your possession, the spell ends.

> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can make one additional attack with the weapon for every two slot levels above 5th, using a Minor Action on each of your turns.
