# Fate Tangle  

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
This incantation is often found in the repertoire of beings ancient enough to remember past stars.

> “Those who see fate as an immutable thing are bound to be undone by it.” — The Dreamcatcher, to a dying past wielder.

---  

> **Fate Tangle**  
> _12th-Level Legendary Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: S, V, M (a set of seven dice made from solidified fate threads)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour.  
>
> While concentrating on this spell, you sculpt seven threads of fate of your own design.
> Roll seven _fated dice_ (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100), and withhold their results.
> 
> Whenever you or a creature you can see within 60 feet makes a roll of any kind, you may choose to replace their roll with one of your _fated dice_.
> You can only replace dice of matching types (e.g., a d20 roll can only be replaced by your fated d20).
> If a roll involves multiple dice of the same type (such as 8d6 for damage), you replace all of them with the same _fated die_ result.
> 
> After the roll is replaced you must choose any one die result from the replaced roll to determine the outcome;
> - If the replaced roll was **lower** than your original _fated die_, the replaced roll becomes your new _fated die_ for later use.
> - If the replaced roll was **equal to or higher** than your original _fated die_, you lose that _fated die_ entirely.
> - If multiple dice were replaced, you choose the highest die result to determine whether it is higher or lower.
>
> While concentrating on this spell, you cannot critically strike on attacks, and you cannot critically miss either.
> If you lose all seven _fated dice_, the spell ends immediately.
