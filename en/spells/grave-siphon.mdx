# Grave Siphon

An incantation that finds itself in the repertoire of most who walk the thin line between life and death.
It seems that to take from the dead is to cling longer to the living.

---

> **Grave Siphon**  
> _1st-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 bonus action  
> **Range**: Self (15-feet cone)  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>
> You release a current of deathly energy. Each creature within a 15 ft. cone originating from you must make a **Constitution saving throw**, taking **1d8 necrotic damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
>
> You gain **temporary hit points equal to half the total necrotic damage dealt** by this spell (rounded down).
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **2nd level or higher**, the necrotic damage increases by **1d8** per slot level above 1st.
