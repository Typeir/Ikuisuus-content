# Dimension Door

Space folds into a narrow corridor beneath your feet, and you step through to reappear where the gap closes.

---

> **Dimension Door**  
> _4th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 500 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You teleport yourself from your current location to any other spot within range. You arrive at exactly the spot desired: a place you can see, one you can visualize, or one you can describe by distance and direction, such as "200 feet straight downward" or "upward to the northwest at a 45-degree angle, 300 feet."  
>  
> You can bring along objects up to what you can carry, and one willing creature of your size or smaller carrying gear up to its capacity. Any creature brought along must be within 5 feet of you when you cast the spell.  
>  
> If you would arrive in a place already occupied by an object or creature, you and any creature traveling with you each take [% 4d6 force %], and the spell fails to teleport you.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can bring **one additional willing creature** for each slot level above 4th, each teleporting to a space of your choice within 5 feet of your arrival point.
