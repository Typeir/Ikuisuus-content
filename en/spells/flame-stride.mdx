# Flame Stride

Flames sheath your legs, each step igniting the ground and flinging hot embers.

---

> **Flame Stride**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Your feet become wreathed in flames, granting you increased speed and the ability to scorch creatures as you move.  
>  
> The billowing flames around your feet give you a flying speed of 20 feet for the duration. When you move at least 5 feet on your turn, you can choose to deal 1d6 fire damage to one creature of your choice that you move within 5 feet of during this movement. A creature can take this damage only once per turn.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
