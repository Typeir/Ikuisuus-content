# Divine Favor

A thin aureole of white light coats the caster, each movement snapping with sudden, biting force.

---

> **Divine Favor**
> _1st-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> Your prayer empowers you with divine radiance.
>
> Your weapon attacks deal an extra 1d4 radiant damage on a hit. If you make multiple attacks, each one deals the extra damage. The spell ends if you lose concentration.
