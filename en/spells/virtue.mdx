# Virtue (UA)

You touch one creature and give it a tiny boost of vitality.

---

> **Virtue (UA)**
> _Abjuration cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
> You touch one creature and bestow upon it a magical enhancement. The creature gains 1 temporary hit point. If the creature already has temporary hit points, this spell has no effect.
