# Janiform Gift

You take on one of the Canvas's two faces — the assertion that makes, or the absence that unmakes.

---

> **Janiform Gift**  
> _6th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You invoke the Janiform duality of the Canvas and transform. Choose one aspect. In either, you gain: immunity to the charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, and poisoned conditions; a flying speed of 40 feet with hovering; the ability to attack twice when you take the Attack action; a weapon attack as a Minor Action, using your spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls; advantage on saving throws against spells; and a Major Action to end one spell or magical effect on yourself.  
>  
> **Will (the Celestial face).** Your creature type is treated as Celestial. You have **resistance to holy damage** and **resistance to Somatic damage** (poison, chemical, psychic). You emit bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for a further 30.  
>  
> **Null (the Null face).** Your creature type is treated as Null. You have **resistance to dark damage** and **resistance to Somatic damage**. Light bends and dims around you; you are lightly obscured to creatures more than 30 feet away.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the duration increases by 1 minute for each slot level above 6th.
