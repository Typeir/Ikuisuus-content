# Selkaran judgement

The image of lonely Selkara rises from the ground, six fossilized ribs bursting upwards from the earth.

---

> **Selkaran Judgement**  
> _6th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Six pillars of stone burst from the ground in locations you can see within range. Each pillar is a cylinder that has a radius of 5 feet and a height of up to 30 feet. The ground where a pillar appears must be wide enough for its diameter, and you can target the same area with only one pillar.
>
> When a pillar appears, each creature in its area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is lifted by the pillar or pushed to the nearest unoccupied space if it can't be lifted. A creature can choose to fail the save.
>
> If a pillar is prevented from reaching its full height because it comes into contact with a ceiling or other solid barrier, a creature on the pillar takes 10d6 bludgeoning damage and is restrained, pinched between the pillar and the barrier. The restrained creature can use a Major Action to make a Strength or Dexterity check (its choice) against your spell save DC. On a success, the creature is no longer restrained and must either move off the pillar or fall off it.
