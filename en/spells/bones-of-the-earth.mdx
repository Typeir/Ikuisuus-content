# Bones of the Earth

Ribbed stone spines explode from the ground, thrusting upward and shredding anything in their path.

---

> **Bones of the Earth**
> _6th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
>
> Six pillars of stone burst from the ground in locations you can see within range. Each pillar is a cylinder that has a diameter of 5 feet and a height of up to 30 feet. The ground where a pillar appears must be wide enough for its diameter, and you can target the same area with only one pillar.
>
> When a pillar appears, each creature in its area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is lifted by the pillar or pushed to the nearest unoccupied space if it can't be lifted. A creature can choose to fail the save.
>
> If a pillar is prevented from reaching its full height because it comes into contact with a ceiling or other solid barrier, a creature on the pillar takes 6d6 bludgeoning damage and is restrained, pinched between the pillar and the barrier. The restrained creature can use an action to make a Strength or Dexterity check (its choice) against your spell save DC. On a success, the creature is no longer restrained and must either move off the pillar or fall off it.
