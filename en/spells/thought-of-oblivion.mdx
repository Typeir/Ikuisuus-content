# Thought of Oblivion

When the Canvas first dreamt, some of these Nulls phased into the thought of disparity, the idea that if a thing is, it can be unmade. This is not so much a spell, but a fundamental truth of the universe given form.

> **Thought of Oblivion**  
> _9th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V (*That which exists can be unmade*)
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You utter a word of power that can compel one creature you can see within range to die instantly. If the creature you choose has 100 hit points or fewer, it dies. Otherwise, the spell has no effect.
