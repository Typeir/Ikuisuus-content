# Entropic Field

The area becomes blanketed by a shadow of tombsteel, the arcane ceases to be, eternally still.

> **Entropic Field**  
> _8th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (10-foot radius sphere)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a filing of tombsteel, kept in the dark)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 2 hours
>
> A 10-foot-radius sphere of severance forms around you and moves with you. Inside it, **the Fold cannot be reached**. Nothing on the far side answers, and nothing that was already answering continues to.
>
> **What Stops.** Fold-casting stops — spells cannot be cast within the sphere, and a slot spent trying is spent for nothing. Väki bargains go unheard; whatever agreed to the terms is no longer within earshot. Precursor crystals sit inert, the water refusing to react. Any spell or magical effect already running on a creature or object inside is suppressed while it remains there, and the time it spends suppressed still counts against its duration.
>
> **What Does Not.** **Will does.** Whatever a creature holds by will alone — not cast, not bargained for, not bought — keeps working inside the null. The field severs the machinery of the world; it has no purchase whatsoever on somebody simply refusing.
>
> **Areas, Items and Summonings.** No area of magic extends into the sphere; where one overlaps, that part of it is suppressed and a gap opens in it. Heirlooms and magic items are mundane within the sphere, and a magic weapon's properties return the instant it leaves. A creature or object summoned or created by magic winks out while inside, and returns the moment its space is clear of the sphere.
>
> **Travel.** Nothing enters or leaves the sphere by folding space. Passage between Folds of the world, and to the Clone Worlds, fails — whether the sphere is the origin or the destination — and any opening into a held space closes while it lies within.
>
> [_Objection_](/en/library/spells/objection) has no effect on the sphere, and two entropic fields do not cancel one another. Effects of genuinely divine make are unaffected; recognising one takes a DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check.
