# Incendiary Cloud

A roiling cloud of searing flame billows outward, scorching and setting alight anything caught inside.

---

> **Incendiary Cloud**
> _8th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 150 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> A swirling cloud of smoke shot through with white-hot embers appears in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within range. The cloud spreads around corners and is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it.
>
> When the cloud appears, each creature in it must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 10d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature must also make this saving throw when it enters the cloud for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there.
>
> The cloud moves 10 feet directly away from you in a direction that you choose at the start of each of your turns.
>
> The cloud is highly flammable. Any exposed flammable objects that aren’t being worn or carried ignite in the cloud.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 8th.
