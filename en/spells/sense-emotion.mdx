# Sense Emotion

Nearby emotions spill outward as pulsing colors and a dull pressure against the mind.

---

> **Sense Emotion**  
> _1st-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You can use your Major Action to sense the emotions of creatures around you.  
>  
> For the spell’s duration, you know whether any creatures within 30 feet of you are under the effect of the charmed or frightened condition. You also sense the presence of any creatures within 30 feet of you that have an emotional state, such as happiness, anger, fear, or sadness, but you do not learn their identities or specific thoughts.
