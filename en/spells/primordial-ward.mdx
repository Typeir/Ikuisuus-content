# Primordial Ward

A dome of primeval force snaps into being around you, its surface rippling as strikes and raw energies fray and unravel on contact. It is not built to hold. It is built to be spent.

---

> **Primordial Ward**  
> _3rd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A ward closes around you. For the duration you have **resistance** to every damage type in the strata the ward covers. Cast with a 3rd-level slot it covers **Physical** alone; each two slot levels above that add the next stratum.
>
> | Slot Level | Strata warded                              |
> | ---------- | ------------------------------------------ |
> | 3rd        | Physical (slashing, bludgeoning, piercing) |
> | 5th        | + Elemental (frost, fire, lightning)       |
> | 7th        | + Somatic (poison, chemical, psychic)      |
> | 9th        | + Akashic (holy, dark, force)              |
>
> **Dropping the Ward.** When you take damage of a kind the ward covers, you can use your **Reaction** to drop it. The spell ends. You take **no damage** from that instance, and gain **immunity** to all the damage types you warded against until the start of your next turn.
