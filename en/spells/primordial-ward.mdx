# Primordial Ward

A dome of primeval force snaps into being around you, its surface rippling as strikes and raw energies fray and unravel on contact.

---

> **Primordial Ward**  
> _6th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You are surrounded by an elemental energy barrier. Until the spell ends, you have resistance to chemical, frost, fire, lightning, and bludgeoning damage.  
>  
> Until the spell ends, you have resistance to chemical, frost, fire, lightning, and bludgeoning damage.  
>  
> As a Major Action, you can end the spell to grant yourself immunity to one damage type of your choice from among chemical, frost, fire, lightning, and bludgeoning until the end of your next turn.
