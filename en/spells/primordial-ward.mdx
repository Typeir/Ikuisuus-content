# Primordial Ward

You are surrounded by an elemental energy barrier. Until the spell ends, you have resistance to acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder damage.

---

> **Primordial Ward**
> _6th-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Until the spell ends, you have resistance to acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder damage.
>
> As an action, you can end the spell to grant yourself immunity to one damage type of your choice from among acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder until the end of your next turn.
