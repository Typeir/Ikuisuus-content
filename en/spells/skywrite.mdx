# Skywrite

Cloud-script, written across a dead sky. Whatever you have to say, Kuutar will be behind it, and will not comment.

---

> **Skywrite**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Sight  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 day  
>  
> You cause up to ten words to form in a part of the sky you can see. They appear to be made of cloud, remain in place for the duration, and dissipate when the spell ends. They can be seen — and so read — from up to 2 miles away by anything with eyes to do it.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can write ten additional words and they can be read from 2 miles further, for each slot level above 2nd.
