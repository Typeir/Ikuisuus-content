# Skywrite

You cause up to ten words to form in a part of the sky you can see. The words appear to be made of cloud and remain in place for the spell’s duration. The words dissipate when the spell ends. A creature must be able to see the words in order to read them. The words can be up to ten words long and can be seen from up to 2 miles away.
