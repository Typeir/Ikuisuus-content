# Warding Wind

A whipping gale roars around you, driving loose debris outward and turning the air into a biting, hissing rush.

---

> **Warding Wind**
> _2nd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
>
> A strong wind (20 miles per hour) blows around you in a 10-foot radius and moves with you, remaining centered on you.
>
> The wind has the following effects:
>
> - It deafens you and other creatures in its area.
> - It extinguishes unprotected flames in the area that are torch-sized or smaller.
> - It hedges out vapor, gas, and fog that can be dispersed by strong wind.
> - The area is difficult terrain for creatures other than you.
> - The attack rolls of ranged weapon attacks have disadvantage if they pass in or out of the wind.
