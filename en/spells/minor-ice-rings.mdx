# Minor Ice Rings

A spell of icy restraint; death does not need to chase—only to wait, unmoving.

---

> **Minor Ice Rings**  
> _1st-level Necromancy (Frost)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 minute  
>
> You conjure frozen shackles that bind a creature you can see within range.  
> The target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or have its speed reduced to **0** until the spell ends.
>
> At the start of each of its turns, a shackled creature takes **1d6 cold damage**.
>
> A shackled creature can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check against your spell save DC, ending the effect on a success.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **2nd level or higher**, the cold damage increases by **1d6** per slot level above 1st.
