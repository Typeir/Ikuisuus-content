# Drawmij's Instant Summons

The touched object tears free from local space and vanishes into a small extradimensional holding.

---

> **Drawmij's Instant Summons**
> _6th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute R
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Until dispelled
>
> You touch an object weighing 10 pounds or less whose longest dimension is 6 feet or less. The object is transported to a safe location you designate within 1 mile of you, where it remains until you use your action to speak the object's name and recall it. The object instantly appears in your hand regardless of distance, as long as you are on the same plane of existence. If another creature is holding or carrying the object, recalling it causes the spell to fail.
