> **Scatter**
> _6th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> The air quivers around up to five creatures of your choice that you can see within range. An unwilling creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw to resist this spell. Teleport each selected creature to an unoccupied space you can see within 120 feet of you. That space must be on the ground or on a floor.
