# Blur

The subject's outline fractures into shifting, translucent afterimages that blur and refract nearby light.

---

> **Blur**  
> _2nd-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Your body becomes blurred, shifting and wavering to all who can see you.  
>  
> Any creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against you. An attacker is immune to this effect if it doesn’t rely on sight, as with blindsight, or can see through illusions, as with truesight.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can blur one additional willing creature you can touch for each slot level above 2nd.
