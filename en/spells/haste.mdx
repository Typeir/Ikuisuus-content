# Haste

Muscles snap taut and motion accelerates into a hard blur, limbs striking with sudden, mechanical quickness.

---

> **Haste**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a shaving of liquorice root)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Choose a willing creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, the target's speed is doubled, it gains a **+2 bonus to AC**, and it has advantage on Dexterity saving throws.
>
> The target also gains a refreshing pool of **1 legendary deed**, regaining it at the start of each of its turns (see [Legendary Deeds](/en/library/rules/creatures-critters-and-characters/legendary-deeds)). It may spend this deed only on a **Legendary Deed: Act**, taken at the end of another creature's turn — one weapon attack, or the Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object action — and, like all deeds, it may use at most one per turn and counts against the round's shared deed cap.
>
> When the spell ends, the target is [Stunned](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#stunned) until the end of its next turn, as a wave of lethargy sweeps over it.
>
> The target then quickly pays the cost of the borrowed time; they instantly age as if a tenday had passed.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the pool gains **1 additional legendary deed** for each slot level above 3rd.
