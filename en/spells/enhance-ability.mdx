# Enhance Ability

A cool, luminous film settles over the subject, steadying the muscles and sharpening whatever the touch was meant to sharpen.

---

> **Enhance Ability**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (fur or a feather from a beast)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 8 hours
>
> You touch a creature and bestow one enhancement of your choice from the table below. For the duration, the target has advantage on **ability checks and saving throws using the chosen ability**, and gains that enhancement's additional effect.
>
> | Enhancement      | Ability      | Additional effect                                                                                          |
> | ---------------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
> | Bear's Endurance | Constitution | [% 2d6 %] temporary hit points                                                                             |
> | Bull's Strength  | Strength     | Doubled carrying capacity, and the target's melee dice advance (below)                                     |
> | Cat's Grace      | Dexterity    | Takes no damage from falling 30 feet or less while conscious                                               |
> | Eagle's Splendor | Charisma     | Can glide 5 feet horizontally for every feet fallen vertically, as long as the fall is larger than 10 feet |
> | Fox's Cunning    | Intelligence | Add your spellcasting modifier to Charisma checks.                                                         |
> | Owl's Wisdom     | Wisdom       | Roll initiative with a +5                                                                                  |
>
> **Advancing Dice.** While under Bull's Strength, the damage dice of the target's melee weapons and natural weapons advance along the [Dice Sequence](/en/library/rules/arms-armour-and-burden/weapons-and-dice-levels) by 1. A creature's claws, a wild-shaped bear's included, count as natural weapons.
>
> | Slot Level | Steps | Slot Level | Steps |
> | ---------- | ----- | ---------- | ----- |
> | 2nd        | 1     | 6th        | 5     |
> | 3rd        | 2     | 7th        | 6     |
> | 4th        | 3     | 8th        | 7     |
> | 5th        | 4     | 9th        | 8     |
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
> Every target receives the same enhancement.
> Additionally, the Dice Sequence advancement increases by 1 for each level above the 2nd
