# Enhance Ability

A cool, luminous film settles on the touched subject, steadying muscles and sharpening senses.

---

> **Enhance Ability**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (fur or a feather from a beast)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 8 hours
>  
>  
> You touch a creature and bestow upon it a magical enhancement. Choose one of the following effects; the target gains advantage on saving throws and skill checks of the chosen skill until the spell ends.  

| Enhancement      | skill        | Additional effect                                               |
| ---------------- | ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Bear’s Endurance | Constitution | [% 2d6 %] temporary hit points                                        |
| Bull’s Strength  | Strength     | doubled carrying capacity                                       |
| Cat’s Grace      | Dexterity    | doesn’t take damage from falling 20 feet or less while conscious |
| Eagle’s Splendor | Charisma     |                                                                 |
| Fox’s Cunning    | Intelligence |                                                                 |
| Owl’s Wisdom     | Wisdom       |                                                                 |

>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
