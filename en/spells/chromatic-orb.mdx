# Chromatic Orb

A sphere of energy coalesces in your palm, its color settling only as you choose what it is to be.

---

> **Chromatic Orb**  
> _1st-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a shard of refined alchemical precursor worth at least 50 gp)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You hurl a 4-inch-diameter sphere of energy at a creature that you can see within range. Choose a damage type from a stratum your slot level has unlocked (see the table below), then make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the creature takes [% 3d8 %] damage of the chosen type.  
>  
> If two of the damage dice show the same number, the orb splinters and leaps to a different creature of your choice within 30 feet of the target. Make a new attack roll against the new target with the same damage — which could cause the orb to leap again. A creature can be targeted only once by each casting of this spell.  
>  
> | Slot Level | Strata unlocked                          |
> | ---------- | ---------------------------------------- |
> | 1st        | Elemental (frost, fire, lightning)        |
> | 4th        | + Somatic (poison, chemical, psychic)    |
> | 8th        | + Akashic (holy, dark, force)            |
> | 12th       | + True                                   |
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 1st.
