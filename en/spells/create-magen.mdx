> **Create Magen**
> _7th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Hour
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M (a humanoid corpse, a diamond worth at least 500 gp, and a vial of quicksilver)
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You implant a portion of your own life essence into a humanoid corpse, creating a magical construct called a magen. The spell can only be cast at night during a full moon.
>
> You touch a humanoid corpse and infuse it with a portion of your own life essence, creating a magen—a magical construct under your control. The corpse must be that of a Medium or Small humanoid that has died within the last 10 days. The spell can only be cast at night during a full moon.
>
> The magen is friendly to you and your companions and obeys your commands. See its game statistics in the magen stat block. The magen exists until it is reduced to 0 hit points, at which point it dies and crumbles to dust.
>
> When you cast this spell, you lose hit points equal to half your hit point maximum, which can’t be reduced or prevented in any way. Only a wish spell can restore your hit point maximum to its normal value before you finish a long rest.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the magen’s hit point maximum increases by 20 for each slot level above 7th.
