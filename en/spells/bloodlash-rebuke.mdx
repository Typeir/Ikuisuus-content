# Bloodlash Rebuke

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
Those who heed the calls of the corpse-god **Küütar** and her master, the **Red Queen**, should find this trick in their repertoire, sooner or later.  
_This spell replaces **Silvery Barbs** if it is already known._

---

> **Bloodlash Rebuke**  
> _1st-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: Reaction, which you take when a creature within 60 feet of you succeeds on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You snap a blood-curse through the air, searing your foes.  
> The target must succeed on a **Dexterity saving throw** or take **1d10 acid + 1d10 fire damage**.  
> On a failed save, they must **reroll the triggering d20** and use the new result.  
> The next attack roll, ability check, or saving throw made by a creature in range of the spell of your choice before the end of its next turn is made with **advantage**.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by **1d10 acid + 1d10 fire** for each slot level above 1st.
