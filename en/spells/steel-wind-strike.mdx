# Steel Wind Strike

You flourish the weapon used in the spell’s casting and then vanish to strike like the wind.

---

> **Steel Wind Strike**
> _5th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: S, M (a melee weapon worth at least 1 sp)
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You flourish the weapon used in the spell’s casting and then vanish to strike like the wind. Choose up to five creatures you can see within range. Make a melee spell attack against each target. On a hit, a target takes 6d10 force damage. You can then teleport to an unoccupied space you can see within 5 feet of one of the targets you hit or missed.
