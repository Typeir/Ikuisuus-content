# Kivi

With a grasping motion you summon a small pebble. The source of all great inventions.

> **Kivi**  
> _Transmutation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 24h
>
> Conjure a pebble and imbue it with magic, as a Major Action, as part of the same casting, You can make a ranged spell attack with it. If thrown, a pebble has a long and short range of 30 feet.
>
> If you cast this spell again, all previous pebbles vanish.
>
> When you cast this spell, choose among the following additional effects to grant the pebble:
>
> **Brutal** - On a hit, the target takes bludgeoning damage equal to 1d6 + your spellcasting ability modifier. Whether the attack hits or misses, the pebble loses its magical properties.
>
> **Brilliant** - This stone has an aura that emits bright light in a 20 foot radius. If it hits a target or medium sized item, it will transfer its aura to it, and it will keep shining for a minute.
>
> **Excavating** - When thrown, the pebble will immediately excavate a 5x5 area of loose earth, sand, stone or similar, and turn it into an equal mass equivalent of rigid cobblestone. This mass counts as difficult terrain and medium cover for medium creatures.
>
> **Shrieking** - When the pebble hits a target, it emits a loud shriek audible up to 300 feet away. The sounds it emits can be anything as long as it lasts less than a second. The pebble shatters into a streak of dust and gravel on impact.
>
> This spell summons an additional stone and the stone damage increases by [% 1d6 %] when you reach 5th level, 11th level, and 17th level.
