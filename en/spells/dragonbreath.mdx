# Dragonbreath

You belch out flame son dense they coax reality back into Arkhé through sheer terror.

---

> **Dragonbreath**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You exhale destroying energy in a 30-foot cone. When you cast the spell, choose the breath's damage type: **fire, frost, or lightning** — the **Elemental** stratum (see [Damage](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/damage)).
>
> Each creature in the cone must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 6d6 %] of the chosen type on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d6 %] for each slot level above 3rd. At the **summit** of the spell's reach — a slot of 8th level or higher — you may instead have the breath deal **Force** damage — climbing from the Elemental stratum to the **Akashic**, the unmaking breath that returns what it touches toward raw Arkhé and is far harder to resist than any element. The breath never reaches the **True** stratum; that breath is not yours to exhale.
