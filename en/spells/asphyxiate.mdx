# Asphyxiate

A spell that manifests a pitch-black, opaque hand around a creature’s throat, crushing breath and will alike until it breaks free or collapses.

---

> **Asphyxiate**  
> _4th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You conjure a **featureless, pitch-black hand** that closes around the throat and torso of a creature you can see within range.
>
> The target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or become **restrained** and **silenced** for the duration, the shadowy hand visibly constricting its body.
>
> While restrained by this spell, the target **cannot breathe**.
>
> At the **start of the target’s next turn**, if it is still restrained by Asphyxiate, it must make a **Constitution saving throw**.  
> On a failure, the target becomes **stunned** until the end of its turn.
>
> At the **start of each of the target’s turns** while it is stunned by this spell, it takes **6d10 necrotic damage** as the hand tightens further.
>
> The target can repeat the **Strength saving throw** at the end of each of its turns, ending the spell on a success.
>
> If the target is ever more than **30 feet** away from you, the shadowy hand dissipates and the spell immediately ends.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **5th level or higher**, the necrotic damage increases by **1d10** per slot level above 4th.
