# Temple of the Gods

You cause a temple to shimmer into existence on ground you can see within range. The temple must fit within an unoccupied cube up to 120 feet on each side. The structure is made of opaque magical force that appears to be constructed of whatever material you choose. Each entrance is up to 10 feet wide and 20 feet tall, and you can dictate the temple’s appearance. The interior is filled with dim light, and the atmosphere is clean, fresh, and warm.

---

> **Temple of the Gods**
> _7th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 hour
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You cause a temple to shimmer into existence on ground you can see within range. The temple must fit within an unoccupied cube up to 120 feet on each side. The structure is made of opaque magical force that appears to be constructed of whatever material you choose. Each entrance is up to 10 feet wide and 20 feet tall, and you can dictate the temple’s appearance. The interior is filled with dim light, and the atmosphere is clean, fresh, and warm.
>
> The temple remains until the spell ends. It is impervious to most damage, though it can be dispelled by a successful casting of *dispel magic* (DC 17) or similar magic. The temple is immune to all nonmagical attacks and effects, and it is unaffected by spells of 6th level or lower unless those spells are cast using a 7th-level spell slot or higher.
>
> The temple’s interior is a cube up to 120 feet on each side. You can divide the space as you wish, designating rooms, passageways, doors, and so forth. The temple’s doors can be locked or unlocked at your command. The temple is furnished and decorated as you choose. It contains enough food to serve a nine-course banquet for up to 100 people, and the food magically replenishes itself each day at dawn.
>
> Any creature you designate when you cast the spell can pass through the temple’s doors freely. All other creatures are barred from entering. If you wish, you can allow a creature to enter or bar a creature from entering at any time while you are inside the temple. You can also set a password that, when spoken aloud, allows a creature to enter.
>
> The temple is a place of worship for you and your allies. While inside, you and your allies gain advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. In addition, any creature that spends at least 1 hour in the temple can recover from one level of exhaustion.
>
> Casting this spell on the same spot every day for a year makes the effect permanent.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the spell lasts for an additional day for each slot level above 7th.
