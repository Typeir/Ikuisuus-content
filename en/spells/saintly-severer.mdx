# Saintly Severer

A spell that forms a colossal bone blade, its splintered remnants orbiting the wielder in a grinding storm that strikes and shatters without command.

---

> **Saintly Severer**  
> _5th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a two-handed melee weapon)  
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> You wrench a massive bone blade from Selkara and bind it to your weapon.  
> For the duration, your weapon counts as **magical**, gains the **heavy** property if it did not already have it, and your **melee reach increases by 10 feet**.
>
> Fragments shear off the blade and begin to **orbit you**, forming a grinding halo of bone.
>
> While this spell lasts, you gain **Bone Charges**:
>
> - When you **hit a Small or larger creature** with a melee weapon attack, you gain **1d4 Bone Charges**.
> - When you **reduce a Small or larger creature to 0 hit points** with a melee weapon attack, you gain **4d4 Bone Charges** instead.
>
> You can have a maximum of **20 Bone Charges**.
>
> **Bone Storm Effects.**  
> The orbiting bone fragments act automatically:
>
> - **On Your Attacks.**  
>   When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, the orbiting bones lash out with the strike, dealing **1d4 piercing damage for each Bone Charge you currently have**.
> - **When You Are Hit.**  
>   When you take damage from an attack, the orbiting bones interpose and absorb part of the blow.  
>   Reduce the damage taken by **1d4 for each Bone Charge you currently have**. Lose as many Bone Charges as you rolled dice to reduce the damage.
>
> **Charge Decay.**  
> If you have not gained any Bone Charges in the last **1 minute**, you lose **1 Bone Charge at the end of each minute** until you gain charges again or have none remaining.
>
> When you have **no Bone Charges**, the orbiting fragments are gone until you gain more.
>
> If the spell ends or you drop the weapon used to cast it, all remaining Bone Charges are lost and the bone blade collapses into inert fragments.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **6th level or higher**, the duration increases by **1 additional hour** for each slot level above 5th.
