# Heal

The body is told, firmly, what it was — and it complies.

---

> **Heal**  
> _6th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> Choose a creature that you can see within range. A surge of vitality washes through it: the target regains **70 hit points**, and is cured of blindness, deafness, and any diseases. The dead are beyond it; constructs and the undead are unmoved.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The healing increases by 10 hit points for each slot level above 6th.  
> - **8th level or higher:** the surge also ends the charmed, frightened, paralyzed, and stunned conditions on the target.  
> - **9th level (Flood):** you may instead distribute **700 hit points** of healing among any number of creatures you can see within 60 feet; each creature healed this way also receives the full cleansing.
