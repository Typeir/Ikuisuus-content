# Tongues

The target's mouth and ears tingle as foreign words rearrange into intelligible speech.

---

> **Tongues**
> _3rd-level Divination_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, M
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
>
> This spell grants the creature you touch the ability to understand any spoken language it hears.
>
> The target can understand any spoken language it hears. Moreover, when the target speaks, any creature that knows at least one language and can hear the target understands what it says.
