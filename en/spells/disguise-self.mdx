# Disguise Self

Another face settles over your own, close enough to fool every eye that does not touch it.

---

> **Disguise Self**  
> _1st-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 4 hours  
>  
> You make yourself — including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person — look different until the spell ends or you dismiss it as a Major Action. You can seem 1 foot shorter or taller and appear thin, fat, or in between; you cannot change your basic body shape. The changes are otherwise up to you.  
>  
> The changes fail to hold up to physical inspection: a hand passes through an illusory hat, and a head meets an unseen brow. A creature that uses a Major Action to study your appearance can discern the illusion with an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **3rd level or higher**, you can also disguise a number of creatures within 30 feet equal to the slot level minus 2. An unwilling creature can make a Charisma saving throw to resist. The duration becomes 8 hours, and you decide each target's seeming; a disguised creature can't change its own.
