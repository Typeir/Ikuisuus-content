# True Resurrection

Decay peels outward as wounds close and warmth floods the body, breath returning in a ragged gasp — and this time, the world agrees to keep it.

---

> **True Resurrection**  
> _12th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Hour  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (holy water and diamonds worth at least 25,000 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> Resurrection is legend, and this is the single exception the **Golden One** left in the Canvas: his own Will, carved into reality as law. Invoking it, you call a creature back not as a borrowed breath but as a fixed truth of the world.  
>  
> You touch a creature that has been dead no longer than 200 years and did not die of old age. If its soul is free and willing, it is restored to life with all its hit points. If its body is gone, you speak its name and a new body forms in an unoccupied space within 10 feet of you. The spell closes every wound, clears every Grievous Wound, neutralizes poison, cures disease, lifts curses that clung to it in death, and restores missing parts and organs — but it cannot return lost memories.  
>  
> Thereafter, the creature is an **immovable part of reality**: it cannot be banished, teleported against its will, sunk into the Muck Dimension, or unmade by any effect short of the **True** stratum or another 12th-level working. Reality has written it back in, and does not easily strike the line.
