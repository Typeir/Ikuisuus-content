# Passing Familiarity

Threads of memory braid into your mind, images and facts snapping into place.

---

> **Passing Familiarity**  
> _2nd-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 4 hours
>
> You draw on knowledge from spirits of the past. Choose one skill in which you lack proficiency. For the spell’s duration, you have proficiency in the chosen skill.
