# Summon Rotworm

A spell that infests a creature with parasitic necromancy, forcing rot to crawl outward and take form.

---

> **Summon Rotworm**  
> _2nd-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a fragment of bone or flesh)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You curse a creature you can see within range, causing necrotic parasites to tear free from its body.
>
> One **rotworm** is summoned in an unoccupied space **within 5 feet of the target**.  
> The rotworm uses the statistics of a **[_Rotworm_](/en/library/monsters/rotworm)** and acts immediately after your turn.  
> It obeys your verbal commands (no action required).
>
> When a rotworm is summoned by this spell, the cursed creature must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** or take **2d6 necrotic damage**.
>
> Each rotworm uses **your spell attack modifier** for its attack rolls and adds **your Intelligence modifier** to its damage rolls.
>
> When a rotworm summoned by this spell is reduced to **0 hit points**, its **Necrotic Instability** trait triggers as normal, for a creature of your choice.
>
> If uninstructed for a turn, the rotworm attacks the cursed target if within range; otherwise, it moves toward the target by the shortest available route.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **3rd level or higher**, you summon **one additional rotworm** for each slot level above 2nd, and the target takes an additional **1d6 necrotic damage** for each slot level above 2nd.
> All rotworms are summoned adjacent to the same target, if possible.
