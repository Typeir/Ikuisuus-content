# Mantle of the Everdark

Light does not fall on you. It falls into you, and does not come back out.

---

> **Mantle of the Everdark**  
> _4th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a wick that burned out and was never relit)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A shroud of unlit air settles over you and begins to feed. For the duration, light within 20 feet of you is devoured: nonmagical light in that radius is snuffed outright, and magical light created by a spell of 4th level or lower is suppressed while it remains in the radius. The area cannot be lit again while the mantle holds, and creatures inside it are in darkness.
>
> You see through what the mantle eats. You gain darkvision out to 60 feet, and if you already have darkvision, its range increases by 30 feet.
>
> A creature that relies on sight has disadvantage on attack rolls against you. When a creature within 10 feet of you hits you with an attack, the shroud lashes back for [% 2d8 dark %].
>
> You have resistance to **holy** damage for the duration.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the radius increases by 10 feet for each slot level above 4th, and the mantle suppresses magical light created by a spell of that slot's level or lower.
