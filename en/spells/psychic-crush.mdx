# Psychic Crush

A crushing mental pressure rips a target's thoughts into jagged fractures that bloom across the skull and implode.

---

> **Psychic Crush**  
> _6th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 minute  
>  
> You wield your mind like a weapon, crushing the psyche of one creature you can see within range. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, it takes [% 12d6 psychic %] and is stunned for 1 minute; on a success, half as much damage and not stunned. The stunned target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The damage increases by [% 2d6 %] for each slot level above 6th.  
> - **7th level or higher:** you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 6th (at 9th level, four targets). A creature slain by the damage has its head burst, if it has one.
