# Mage Armor

An invisible shell of force snaps over the target, its surface tight and quietly crackling.

---

> **Mage Armor**  
> _1st-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a piece of cured leather)  
> **Duration**: 16 hours
>  
> You touch a willing creature who isn’t wearing armor, and a protective magical force surrounds it until the spell ends. The target’s base AC becomes 13 + its Dexterity modifier. The spell ends if the target dons armor or if you dismiss the spell as a Major Action.
