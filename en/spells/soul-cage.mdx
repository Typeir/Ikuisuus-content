> **Soul Cage**
> _6th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: Special  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a tiny silver cage worth 100 gp)
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> This spell snatches the soul of a humanoid as it dies and traps it inside the tiny cage you use for the material component. You can use the soul trapped in the cage in the following ways. You can use a trapped soul up to six times. After the sixth time, the soul is released, and the spell ends. If the spell ends and the soul is not used six times, the soul is released. If the cage is destroyed or the spell ends, the soul is released. A soul can only be trapped if it is within 60 feet of you when it dies.
>
> **Steal Life.** You can use a bonus action to drain vigor from the soul and regain 2d8 + 2 hit points.
>
> **Query Soul.** You can ask the soul a question (no action required) and receive a brief telepathic answer, which you can understand regardless of the language the soul knew in life. The soul knows only what it knew in life, but it must answer truthfully and to the best of its ability. The answer is no more than a sentence or two and might be cryptic.
>
> **Borrow Experience.** You can use a bonus action to bolster yourself with the soul’s life experience, making your next attack roll, ability check, or saving throw with advantage. If you don’t use this benefit before the start of your next turn, it is lost.
>
> **Eyes of the Dead.** You can use an action to name a place the humanoid saw in life, which you must have seen as well, and you receive a vision of that place as if you were there, using the soul’s senses. The vision lasts for up to 10 minutes and is as detailed as the soul’s memory allows. During this time, you are blind and deaf with regard to your own senses.
>
> **Eyes of the Living.** You can use an action to name a living creature you have seen before and that the humanoid also saw in life. You receive a vision of that creature’s current location as if you were there, using the soul’s senses. The vision lasts for up to 10 minutes and is as detailed as the soul’s memory allows. During this time, you are blind and deaf with regard to your own senses.
>
> **Release Soul.** You can use a bonus action to release the soul from the cage.
