# Imbue Weapon

Elemental energy coats the weapon, its edge crackling and shifting with furious force.

---

> **Imbue Weapon**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> Touch a weapon and choose one of the following damage types: chemical, cold, fire, lightning, or bludgeoning. The weapon now deals an extra 1d4 damage, and deals all its damage as the chosen type.  
> 
> While the spell lasts you know the following cantrip. additionally, the turn you cast imbue weapon, you may brandish it to cast the cantrip as a bonus action
> 

| damage type | cantrip        |
| ----------- | -------------- |
| chemical    | Acid Splash    |
| cold        | Chill          |
| fire        | Pyromancy      |
| lightning   | Shocking grasp |
| bludgeoning | Echoing Strike |

>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level, the extra damage increases by 1d4 for each level above third
