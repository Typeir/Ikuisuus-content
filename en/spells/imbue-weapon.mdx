# Imbue Weapon

Elemental energy coats the weapon, its edge crackling and shifting with furious force.

---

> **Imbue Weapon**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> Touch a weapon and choose one of the following damage types: chemical, frost, fire, lightning, or bludgeoning. The weapon now deals an extra [% 1d4 damage %], and deals all its damage as the chosen type.
>
> While the spell lasts you know the following cantrip. additionally, the turn you cast imbue weapon, you may brandish it to cast the cantrip as a Minor Action
>
> | damage type | cantrip                                              |
> | ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
> | frost       | [Chill ](/en/library/spells/chill)                   |
> | fire        | [Pyromancy ](/en/library/spells/pyromancy)           |
> | lightning   | [Shocking grasp ](/en/library/spells/shocking-grasp) |
>
> **At Higher Levels.**
>
> - The damage increases by [% 1d4 %] for each slot level above 3rd.
> - At 5th or higher level: you can now choose to imbue your weapon with the Somatic damage strata:
>
> | damage type | cantrip                                          |
> | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
> | chemical    | [Acid Splash ](/en/library/spells/acid-splash)   |
> | poison      | [Envenom ](/en/library/spells/envenom)           |
> | psychic     | [Psychic Link ](/en/library/spells/psychic-link) |
>
> - At 7th or higher level: you can now choose to imbue your weapon with the Akashic damage strata:
>
> | damage type | cantrip                                                 |
> | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
> | holy        | [Judgement ](/en/library/spells/judgement)              |
> | dark        | [Omen ](/en/library/spells/omen)                        |
> | force       | [Ashen Anointment](/en/library/spells/ashen-anointment) |
