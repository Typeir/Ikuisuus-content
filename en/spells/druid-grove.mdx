# Druid Grove

Roots surge from the soil and weave into a ring of gnarled wood, the bark knotting as the ring hardens into a compact living barricade.

---

> **Druid Grove**  
> _6th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a handful of seed from the ground you are warding)  
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You wake the land to protect an area outdoors or underground. The area can be as small as a 30-foot cube or as large as a 90-foot cube, and the spell fails if it includes a building or any other structure.
>
> You can create up to four protections within the area. Choose from the following when you cast the spell:
>
> - **Fog.** Solid fog fills a 20-foot-square area within the grove, heavily obscuring it. A creature that enters the fog for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must make a Constitution saving throw or be **poisoned** until it leaves. The fog lasts for the duration, or until a wind of at least 10 miles per hour disperses it.
> - **Undergrowth.** Grasping undergrowth covers a 20-foot-square area within the grove. The area is difficult terrain, and a creature that starts its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be **restrained** until the spell ends. A restrained creature can use a Major Action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, freeing itself on a success.
> - **Guardians.** Six awakened trees appear in unoccupied spaces you choose within the grove. They are **wildlife (plant)** of Challenge 2, they act as guardians, they cannot leave the grove, and they go inanimate when the spell ends.
> - **Spikes.** Up to four instances of [Spike Growth](/en/library/spells/spike-growth), each in a 10-foot-square area.
> - **Snares.** Up to four instances of [Entangle](/en/library/spells/entangle), each in a 10-foot-square area.
> - **Winds.** Up to four instances of [Breeze Barrier](/en/library/spells/breeze-barrier), each in a 10-foot-square area.
>
> When you cast this spell you can specify creatures that are unaffected by one or more of the protections. You can also set a password that, spoken aloud, makes the speaker and any creatures they designate immune to the protections for 1 minute.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can create **one additional protection** for each slot level above 6th, and the grove lasts an additional 24 hours.
