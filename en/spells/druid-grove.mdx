> **Druid Grove**
> _6th-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You invoke the spirits of nature to protect an area outdoors or underground. The area can be as small as a 30-foot cube or as large as a 90-foot cube. The spell fails if the area includes a building or other structure.
>
> When you cast this spell, you specify a point within range. The area within a 60-foot radius centered on that point becomes guarded for the duration. The spell fails if the area includes a building or other structure.
>
> You can create up to four protections within the area. When you cast the spell, choose from the following options:
>
> - Solid fog fills a 20-foot-square area within the grove. The area is heavily obscured. Any creature that enters the fog for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is poisoned until it leaves the fog. The fog lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it.
> - Grasping undergrowth covers a 20-foot-square area within the grove. The area is difficult terrain, and any creature that starts its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be restrained until the spell ends. A restrained creature can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, ending the effect on itself on a success.
> - Six awakened trees (see the Monster Manual for statistics) appear in unoccupied spaces you choose within the grove. They act as guardians. The awakened trees can't leave the grove. If the spell ends, the trees become inanimate.
> - You can place up to four spike growths (as the spell) in the grove. Each must be in a 10-foot-square area.
> - You can place up to four snare spells (as the spell) in the grove. Each must be in a 10-foot-square area.
> - You can place up to four wind walls (as the spell) in the grove. Each must be in a 10-foot-square area.
>
> When you cast this spell, you can specify creatures that are unaffected by one or more of the protections. You can also specify a password that, when spoken aloud, makes the speaker and any creatures they designate immune to the protections for 1 minute.
>
> The area is under the effect of a permanent hallow spell (see the spell) that you cast as part of casting druid grove.
