# Ray of Enfeeblement

A thin beam of shadow crawls over the target, slackening muscles and dulling strength.

---

> **Ray of Enfeeblement**  
> _2nd-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> A black beam of enervating energy springs from your finger toward one creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** or be enfeebled for the duration: it subtracts [% 1d8 %] from the damage of any attack it makes using Strength.  
>  
> The target repeats the saving throw at the end of any of its turns that it ends **more than 30 feet from you**, ending the effect on itself on a success. Standing close enough to feel the beam is standing too close to shrug it off.
