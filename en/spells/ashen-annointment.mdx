# Ashen Annointment

Perform an attack akin to a cremation rite on a creature within range, marking them as about to die or deceased.

---

> **Ashen Annointment**  
> _Necromancy Cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
>  
> A spout of ash and ghastly flame swirls around a creature, make a ranged spell attack against it. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 dark damage. If the attack hits, the target will be marked with ash, it can't regain hit points until the start of your next turn.
>  
> If you leave your target doomed or it is an undead, it also has disadvantage on attack rolls against you until the end of your next turn.  
>  
> The spell's damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
