> **Jim's Glowing Coin**
> _2nd-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: S, M
> **Duration**: 1 minute
>
> You cause a coin to shed bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet. As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the coin up to 60 feet to a space you can see. The coin can hover in the air. If the coin is ever more than 60 feet from you, the spell ends.
