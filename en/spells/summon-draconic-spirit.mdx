> **Summon Draconic Spirit**
> _5th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You call forth a draconic spirit. It manifests in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. This corporeal form uses the Draconic Spirit stat block. When you cast the spell, choose a damage type: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison. The creature resembles a dragon of that type, which determines certain traits in its stat block. The creature disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The summoned spirit is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the spirit shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> **Draconic Spirit**
>
> *Medium Dragon, Neutral*
>
> **Armor Class** 14 (natural armor)
>
> **Hit Points** 50 + 10 for each spell level above 5th
>
> **Speed** 30 ft., fly 60 ft.
>
> STR 19 (+4)  DEX 14 (+2)  CON 17 (+3)  INT 14 (+2)  WIS 11 (+0)  CHA 16 (+3)
>
> **Saving Throws** Dex +5, Wis +3, Cha +6
>
> **Damage Immunities** The damage type you chose when casting the spell
>
> **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
>
> **Languages** Draconic, understands the languages you speak
>
> **Challenge** —
>
> **Proficiency Bonus (PB)** equals your bonus
>
> **Multiattack.** The spirit makes a number of attacks equal to half this spell’s level (rounded down).
>
> **Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d10 + 4 + the spell’s level in force damage.
>
> **Elemental Breath (Recharge 5–6).** The spirit exhales energy in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC, taking 6d6 damage of the type you chose when casting the spell on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> **Flyby.** The spirit doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach.
>
> **Amphibious.** The spirit can breathe air and water.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, use the higher level wherever the spell’s level appears in the stat block.
