# Omen

Conjure a baleful sign, and drag a creature through the inescapable path towards the grave.

---

> **Omen**  
> _Necromancy cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  You conjure a small omen: the image and cry of a raven flying by, a small harmless tremor, a sudden breeze that slams or opens windows, the ringing of a bell, cause the flames in the area to flicker...
>  
>  Then choose one of the following effects
>  - Gain advantage on intimidation rolls against wounded creatures for a minute.
>  - You point at one creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d4 dark damage. If the target is wounded, deal an additional 1d4 dark damage. If the target is bloodied, again. If the target is doomed, one last time.
>  
> The spell’s damage increases by one die when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).
