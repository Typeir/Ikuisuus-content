> **Draconic Transformation**
> _7th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> With a roar, you draw on the magic of dragons to transform yourself, taking on draconic features. You gain the following benefits until the spell ends:
>
> Blindsight. You have blindsight with a range of 30 feet.
>
> Breath Weapon. When you cast the spell, and as a bonus action on subsequent turns, you can exhale a 60-foot cone of damaging energy. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d8 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> Wings. You gain spectral wings that give you a flying speed of 60 feet.
