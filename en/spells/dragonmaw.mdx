# Dragonmaw

Your jaw unhinges wider than a jaw should, fills with fangs that were never yours, and remembers a hunger from a shape drawn long before you.

---

> **Dragonmaw**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a fang taken from a dragon)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Your head and neck take on a draconic cast for the duration, and you gain a bite you know how to use.
>
> As a **Major Action**, you can bite one creature or object within 10 feet. Make a melee spell attack. On a hit, the target takes [% 2d10 piercing %]. If the target is a creature no more than one size larger than you, your jaws close on it: it is **grappled** (escape DC equals your spell save DC), and while it is held this way you can bite it again on later turns without an attack roll — it simply takes the damage as your jaws bear down.
>
> You can hold only one creature in your jaws at a time, and you cannot bite another while you do.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the bite's damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
