> **Summon Draconic Spirit (UA)**
> _5th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M (an object with the image of a dragon engraved on it, worth at least 500 gp)
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You call forth a draconic spirit. It manifests in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. This corporeal form uses the Draconic Spirit stat block. When you cast the spell, choose a family of dragon: Chromatic, Gem, or Metallic. The creature resembles a dragon of the chosen family, which determines certain traits in its stat block. The creature disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The summoned spirit is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the spirit shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> **Draconic Spirit**
>
> *Large Dragon*
>
> **Armor Class** 14 + the level of the spell (natural armor)
>
> **Hit Points** 50 + 10 for each spell level above 5th
>
> **Speed** 30 ft., fly 60 ft., swim 30 ft. (if gem or metallic)
>
> **STR** 19 (+4)  **DEX** 14 (+2)  **CON** 17 (+3)  **INT** 14 (+2)  **WIS** 15 (+2)  **CHA** 18 (+4)
>
> **Saving Throws** DEX +5, CON +6, WIS +5, CHA +7
>
> **Damage Resistances** See Draconic Resistance
>
> **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
>
> **Languages** Draconic, understands the languages you speak
>
> **Challenge** —
>
> **Proficiency Bonus (PB)** equals your bonus
>
> **Draconic Resistance.** The spirit has resistance to the damage type associated with its dragon family: acid (chromatic), force (gem), or lightning (metallic).
>
> **Multiattack.** The spirit makes a number of rend attacks equal to half this spell’s level (rounded down).
>
> **Rend.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1d6 + 4 + the spell’s level in force damage.
>
> **Breath Weapon (Recharge 5–6).** The spirit exhales destructive energy in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC, taking 6d6 damage of the type associated with its dragon family on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> **Elemental Affinity.** When you cast a spell that deals damage of the type associated with the spirit’s dragon family, it can channel its magic through you. When you do so, one creature of your choice that you can see takes an extra 1d6 damage of that type.
>
> **Flyby.** The spirit doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach.
>
> **Amphibious (Gem or Metallic Only).** The spirit can breathe air and water.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, use the higher level wherever the spell’s level appears in the stat block.
