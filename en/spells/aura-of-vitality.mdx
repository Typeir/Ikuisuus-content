# Aura of Vitality

A pulsing halo radiates from you, knitting torn flesh and steadying errant breath.

---

> **Aura of Vitality**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot radius)  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Healing energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius.  
>  
> Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. You can use a Minor Action to cause one creature in the aura (including you) to regain 2d6 hit points.
