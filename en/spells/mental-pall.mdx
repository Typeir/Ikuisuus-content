# Mental Pall

The blow is already coming. In the half-second you are given, you decide it is not going to land on you — and it does not.

---

> **Mental Pall**  
> _2nd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Reaction  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
> When you are subjected to an effect that allows an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw to take only half damage, you can draw the pall closed between yourself and the effect. If you succeed on the saving throw, you take **no damage** instead of half.
