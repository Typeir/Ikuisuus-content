# Envenom

A jet of acrid, sickly mist lashes from your palm, searing and withering exposed flesh.

---

> **Envenom**  
> _Conjuration cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and project a puff of noxious gas from your palm.
>
> The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take [% 1d12 poison %]. This spell’s damage increases by [% 1d12 %] when you reach 5th level ([% 2d12 %]), 11th level ([% 3d12 %]), and 17th level ([% 4d12 %]).
>
> **Decanting.** Instead of projecting the gas, you can condense it. Choose one:
>
> - **Coat a weapon.** You fill a vial with the stuff, enough to coat one weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition. The next hit made with it deals an extra [% 1d6 poison %].
> - **Poison food.** You taint a meal or a drinking vessel. A creature that consumes it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take [% 1d6 poison %] and be **poisoned** for 1 minute.
>
> **Both decay one minute after you make them**, vial and all. This is not a trade you can stockpile, you commit to it on the way through the door.
