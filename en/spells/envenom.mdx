# Envenom

A jet of acrid, sickly mist lashes from your palm, searing and withering exposed flesh.

---

> **Envenom**  
> _Conjuration cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  
> You extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and project a puff of noxious gas from your palm.  
>  
> The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take [% 1d12 poison %]. This spell’s damage increases by [% 1d12 %] when you reach 5th level ([% 2d12 %]), 11th level ([% 3d12 %]), and 17th level ([% 4d12 %]).
