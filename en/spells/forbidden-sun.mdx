# Forbidden Sun

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.
This is usually granted to those who heed the calls of the roaring black flames of the Everdark.

> “The true flames of this world were sealed away in the Everdark, beyond the Opaline belt, forbidden by the very arbiters of this world.”

---

> **Forbidden Sun**  
> _5th-Level Legendary Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action or reaction (trigger: a creature within 120 feet casts a spell or uses a magic item)  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You conjure a blistering orb of blackened flame, no larger than two feet across, and hurl it at a foe.
>
> **Make a ranged spell attack** against the target. On a hit:
>
> - The target takes **6d8 fire damage**.
> - A **20-foot cone behind the target** erupts in flame; creatures in the cone take **6d8 fire damage** (Dex save half).
> - The scorched ground in the cone remains for the duration as **difficult terrain**. Any creature that **starts its turn** in the area or **moves within it** takes **1d8 fire damage** for every 5 feet moved.
>
> If the target was casting a spell or using a magic item, it must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw at disadvantage** or the action is interrupted and wasted. On a failed save, the target is also **stunned until the end of its next turn.**
>
> **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the spell can interrupt spells of that slot level or lower, and its damage increases by **1d8** for each slot level above 5th (ranged attack, cone, and terrain damage).
