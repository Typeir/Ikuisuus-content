# Icingdeath's Frost (UA)

A blast of cold air erupts from your hand.

---

> **Icingdeath's Frost (UA)**
> _2nd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self (15-foot cone)
> **Components**: S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d8 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
