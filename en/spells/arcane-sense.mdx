# Arcane Sense

Gain a sixth sense for the duration. It allows you to perceive the arcane in the world.

> **Arcane Sense**  
> _1st-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action R  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 30 minutes
>  
> For the duration, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet of you. If you sense magic in this way, you can use your action to see a faint aura around any visible creature or object in the area that bears magic, and you learn its school of magic, if any.  
>  
> The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt.  
> 
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the range increases by 10 feet and the duration increases by 10 minutes for each level above first.
