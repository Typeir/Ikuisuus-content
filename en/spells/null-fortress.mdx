# Null fortress

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
This spell can be attained by the scions of ambition, if they ever survive the trial that comes with embracing true potential.

---

> **Null fortress**
> _8th-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You envelop a willing creature in a volatile field of counteractive energy that disrupts nearby magic.  
> When cast, all **temporary magical effects** affecting the target end immediately, including beneficial spells such as _Bless_, _Haste_, or _Invisibility_.
> For each effect consumed this way, the target gains temporary hit points equal to 10 + your spellcasting modifier.
>
> While protected by the **Null fortress**, the creature gains the following benefits:
>
> - Immunity to being **put to sleep** by magical means.
> - Immunity to being **charmed**, **polymorphed**, **frightened**, or **possessed** by spells or magical effects.
> - Advantage on saving throws against magical effects.
> - **Resistance to damage** dealt by spells and other magical effects.
>
> Whenever the protected creature is targeted by a spell or magical effect (including area effects that include it in their range), roll **1d6**:
>
> - **1–2**: The spell functions normally.
> - **3–5**: The spell **fails to affect** the creature.
> - **6**: The spell is **reflected** back at its caster, as if the creature were the point of origin.
>
> This spell does not supress **heirlooms**.
>
> ---
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level or higher, the shell’s duration increases by **10 minutes** for each slot level above 8th, and the base temporary HP by 5.
