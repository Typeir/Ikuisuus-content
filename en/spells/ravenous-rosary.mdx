# Ravenous Rosary

Three beads on a loop of wire, cool and heavy and wanting. They are not prayers. They are appetites, and they are patient.

---

> **Ravenous Rosary**  
> _6th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a wire loop strung through three drilled beads of alchemical precursor)  
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> Choose a damage type from a stratum your slot level has unlocked (see the table below). A rosary of **three beads** forms around your hand, each one cold, dense, and inert.
>
> **Plucking.** A bead does nothing while it hangs on the wire. As a **Minor Action** you can pluck one bead free, arming it. You can pluck no more than one bead per turn, and no one but you can pluck them.
>
> **Carrying.** An armed bead can be handed to any creature, and passed on from there. It is a stone the size of a sling bullet; anyone can hold one, and anyone can throw one.
>
> **Bursting.** An armed bead bursts on impact when thrown — 40 feet by hand, or a sling's normal range — and bursts on its own **one minute after it was plucked** if nobody has thrown it by then, wherever it is and whoever is holding it. Either way it opens into a 20-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in the area makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 4d6 %] damage of the chosen type on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
>
> When the hour ends, any bead still hanging on the wire goes cold and crumbles to grit.
>
> | Slot Level | Strata unlocked                       |
> | ---------- | ------------------------------------- |
> | 1st        | Elemental (frost, fire, lightning)    |
> | 4th        | + Somatic (poison, chemical, psychic) |
> | 8th        | + Akashic (holy, dark, force)         |
> | 12th       | + True                                |
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the rosary forms with one additional bead for each slot level above 6th.
