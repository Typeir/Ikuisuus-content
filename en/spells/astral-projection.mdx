# Astral Projection

Your consciousness detaches from your body, forming a pale, insubstantial double that drifts free to roam beyond the flesh.

---

> **Astral Projection**  
> _9th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Hour  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (for each creature you affect with this spell, you must provide one jacinth worth at least 1,000 gp and one silver bar worth at least 100 gp, all of which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Special  
>  
> You and up to eight willing creatures within range step out of your bodies as **astrals** — disembodied selves, the same shape you wore a moment ago. Each body left behind is unconscious and in suspended animation; it needs no food or air, and it does not age while you are out of it.  
>  
> An astral replicates your statistics and possessions. The one difference is the **cord**: a silvery thread that leaves you between the shoulder blades and trails away, fading from sight after a foot. It is your way back. While it holds, you can always find your body. If it is cut — and only an effect that says outright that it cuts the cord can do so — the spell ends, body and identity are parted, and you die where you stand.  
>  
> An astral moves through the **Folds of the world** freely, at the pace of thought rather than of legs, unbothered by walls and unable to affect them. It can cross into another **Clone World** where the Folds run close enough to allow it, and if it does, your body and possessions are drawn along the cord to meet you there.  
>  
> Your astral is a separate incarnation. Damage and effects that land on it do not touch your body and do not persist when you return.  
>  
> The spell ends when you use a Major Action to dismiss it, and everyone affected wakes in their own body. It also ends early for any individual whose body is damaged or moved, or against whom an [_Objection_](/en/library/spells/objection) is successfully cast.
