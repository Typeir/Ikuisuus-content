# Anaximander's Gift

When Anaximander opened the door to the hidden realm, he was rewarded with a boon from the lord of flies. A spell of ruin and rot; May his name remain forever in infamy.

> **Anaximander's Gift**  
> _9th-level Necromancy DC_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You target a creature you can see within range, putting its physical form through rapid and lethal aging. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 10d12 dark damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, it dies and instantly turns to dust. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and does not age.
