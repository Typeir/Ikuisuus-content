# Judgement

The image of an angel descends upon a creature, delivering justice.

---

> **Judgement**  
> _Evocation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  
> An angelic figure descends upon a creature within range. Choose its intent:
>  
> - **Spare.** The target becomes stable
>  
> - **Smite.** The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take [% 1d8 radiant %]. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw.  
>  
> The spell’s damage increases by [% 1d8 %] when you reach 5th level ([% 2d8 %]), 11th level ([% 3d8 %]), and 17th level ([% 4d8 %]).
