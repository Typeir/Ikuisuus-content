# Mirror Image

Three shapes peel from your silhouette and mirror your movements imprecisely, diverging just enough to survive the first moment of violence.

---

> **Mirror Image**  
> _2nd-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 minute  
>  
> Three illusory duplicates of yourself appear in your space. Until the spell ends they move with you and mimic your actions, shifting position so it is impossible to tell which shape is real. You can use a Major Action to dismiss them.  
>  
> While one or more duplicates remain, you gain a **+3 bonus to AC for each duplicate** — a phantom crowd the eye cannot cut through. Each time an attack roll **misses** you while at least one duplicate remains, one duplicate is destroyed as the blow passes through an image, and its share of the bonus is lost.  
>  
> A creature whose attack beats your raised AC has found the real you and strikes normally — the images are no dice-gate against a true hit. The spell ends when the last duplicate is destroyed or the duration expires.  
>  
> A creature is unaffected — and destroys no duplicate — if it can't see, if it relies on senses other than sight (such as blindsight), or if it perceives illusions as false (as with truesight).
