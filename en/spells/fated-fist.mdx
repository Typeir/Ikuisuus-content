# Fated Fist  

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
The storm of the Everdark coils within the fist, every blow fated since the dawn of time.  

> “One strike for every thread that dares bind you.” — The Dreamcatcher, to the white homunculus  

---  

> **Fated Fist**  
> _11th-Level Legendary Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self (melee attacks)  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>
> While concentrating on this spell, you channel the Everdark storm through your strikes.  
> You may replace any melee attack you make with a **Fated Fist strike**.  
>
> **Fated Fist Strike**  
> - Make a melee spell attack in place of your normal attack.  
> - On a hit, the target takes **5d12 lightning damage** and must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** or be **stunned until the start of your next turn**.  
> - A Fated Fist strike **critically strikes on a 15–20**, dealing **10d12 lightning damage** on a critical hit.  
>
> **Chain of Fate**  
> - When you hit a creature with Fated Fist, it becomes **marked** until the spell ends.  
> - You instantly move up to **30 feet in a straight line** toward a marked creature whenever you attempt to strike it with Fated Fist. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.  
> - You may mark multiple creatures, and may dash between them with successive strikes.  
>
> **At Higher Levels (12th+)**: Increase the strike damage by **+1d12 lightning** for each level above 10th.
