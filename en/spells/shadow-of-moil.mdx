# Shadow Of Moil

Flames of shadow wreathe your body, shedding dim light in a 10-foot radius and turning bright light in a 10-foot radius into dim light.

---

> **Shadow Of Moil**
> _4th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Until the spell ends, you have resistance to radiant damage, and whenever a creature within 10 feet of you hits you with an attack, the shadows lash out at that creature, dealing 2d8 necrotic damage to it.
>
> In addition, your form becomes blurred, shifting and wavering to all who can see you. Attack rolls against you have disadvantage, and you have advantage on any saving throw you make to avoid being blinded or deafened by magic.
