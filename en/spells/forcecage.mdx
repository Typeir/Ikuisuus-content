# Forcecage

A transparent cage of humming force snaps into being, sealing and holding everything within.

---

> **Forcecage**  
> _7th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 100 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> An immobile, invisible, cube-shaped prison composed of magical force springs into existence around an area you choose within range.
>
> You can choose either a cage or a box:
>
> **Cage.** The cage can be up to 20 feet on a side, made from 1/2-inch bars spaced 1/2 inch apart.
>
> **Box.** The box can be up to 10 feet on a side, creating a solid barrier.
>
> The prison can be oriented in any way you choose as long as it is anchored on a solid surface. The prison remains for the duration.
>
> When you cast the spell, any creature that is completely inside the cage's area is trapped. Creatures only partially within the area, or those too large to fit inside the area, are pushed away from the center until they are completely outside the area.
>
> A creature inside the cage can't leave it by mundane means. If the creature tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel to leave the cage, it must first make a Charisma saving throw. On a success, the creature can use that magic to exit the cage. On a failure, the creature can't exit the cage and wastes the use of the spell or effect. The cage also extends into the Ethereal Plane, blocking ethereal travel.
>
> The cage is **immune to all damage except force**. It has **100 hit points** and a **damage threshold of 30**; any single source of force damage that rolls 30 or more before threshold application pushes through. If the cage is reduced to 0 hit points or its threshold is beaten, it shatters and the spell ends.
>
> A _dispel magic_ spell cast at 7th level or higher ends the cage. A _dispel magic_ cast at a lower slot level forces the caster to make a **spellcasting ability check** against a DC of **13 + the spell level difference**. For example, a 6th-level _dispel magic_ must beat DC 14; a 3rd-level casting must beat DC 17. On a success, the cage shatters.
