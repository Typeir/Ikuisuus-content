# Thought Shield (UA)

A thin veil of static light envelops the target's mind, muffling external thoughts and blurring intrusive images.

---

> **Thought Shield (UA)**
> _2nd-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
>
> You weave a protective aura around a creature’s mind, shielding it from mental intrusion.
>
> For the duration, the target is immune to any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, as well as to all divination spells. The spell even foils wish spells and spells or effects of similar power used to affect the target’s mind or to gain information about the target.
