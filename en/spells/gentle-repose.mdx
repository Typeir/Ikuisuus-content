# Gentle Repose

A cold, waxy stillness settles over the corpse, cooling flesh and halting decay.

---

> **Gentle Repose**  
> _2nd-level Necromancy (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 10 days  
>  
> You touch a corpse or other remains. For the duration the target is protected from decay, and nothing can animate it — no rite, no spell, no accident of the Fold will make a construct of that body while the spell holds.  
>  
> Days spent under this spell do not count against the time limits of [Revivify](/en/library/spells/revivify) or any other effect that must reach a body before it has been dead too long. It buys the corpse time, which is often all anyone needed.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can touch one additional corpse for each slot level above 2nd.
