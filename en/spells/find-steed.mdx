# Find Steed

You call, and something that has always liked running answers, wearing the shape of a mount.

---

> **Find Steed**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A väki wearing the Idea of a mount takes shape in an unoccupied space within range: a warhorse, a pony, a camel, an elk, or a mastiff. It has the statistics of the chosen form, but its Intelligence is 6, it understands one language you speak, and the two of you can communicate telepathically while within 1 mile of each other.  
>  
> The steed serves you as a mount in combat and outside it, and it acts as a controlled mount while you ride it. While mounted, you can make any spell that targets only you also target the steed.  
>  
> When the steed drops to 0 hit points, it recedes back into its Idea, leaving no form behind. You can also dismiss it at any time as a Major Action, causing it to vanish. In either case, casting this spell again calls the same steed, restored to its hit point maximum. You can't have more than one steed at a time.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - **4th level or higher:** the steed may instead take a greater form — a griffon, a pegasus, a dire wolf, a rhinoceros, or a saber-toothed cat.  
> - **5th level or higher:** the steed's Intelligence becomes 10, and it may take any greater form, including flying forms.
