# Encode Thoughts

Faint, angular script crawls across your temples and sinks into your skin, leaving a cold, humming sigil.

---

> **Encode Thoughts**
> _Enchantment cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> You pull a memory, an idea, or a message from your mind and transform it into a tangible strand of glowing energy, called a thought strand, which persists for the duration or until you cast this spell again. The thought strand appears in your hand as a ribbonlike, intangible object about 1 foot long and weighs nothing. While holding the strand, you can use an action to send it to a creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The strand floats through the air and lands in the target’s space, where it remains until someone picks it up. A creature can use an action to pick up the strand and receive its contents, as described below.
>
> A thought strand contains whatever information you choose to put into it when you create it, up to a single memory, idea, or message that can be conveyed in 10 words or fewer. The strand is readable only by creatures that can touch it. A creature can use an action to touch the strand and receive the information contained within it, as long as it is within 5 feet of the strand. Once the information is received, the strand disappears.
