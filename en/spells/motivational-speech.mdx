# Motivational Speech

You deliver a stirring speech to inspire up to five creatures of your choice within range, bolstering their resolve.

---

> **Motivational Speech**
> _3rd-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> Choose up to five creatures within range that can hear you. For the duration, each target has advantage on Wisdom saving throws and temporary hit points equal to your spellcasting ability modifier at the start of each of its turns. If a target is hit by an attack, it has advantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn. Once a target loses these temporary hit points, the spell ends for that creature.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
