# Inflict Wounds

A jagged pulse of necrotic energy lashes from your hand, flesh blackens and splits.

---

> **Inflict Wounds**  
> _1st-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes [% 3d10 dark %].  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 1st.
