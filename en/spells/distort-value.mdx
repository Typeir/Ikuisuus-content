# Distort Value

You cast an illusion on an object no larger than 1 cubic foot, causing it to appear worth up to twice as much or half as much as its actual value. The spell does not affect the object's weight. The illusion lasts for the duration, unless you dismiss it as an action or cast this spell on another object.
