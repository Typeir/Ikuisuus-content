# Cure Wounds

Warmth pours through your hands, and torn flesh knits toward wholeness.

---

> **Cure Wounds**  
> _1st-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A creature you touch regains hit points equal to [% 2d8 %] + your spellcasting ability modifier. The dead are beyond it; constructs and the undead are unmoved.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The healing increases by [% 2d8 %] for each slot level above 1st.  
> - **5th level or higher (Mass):** you may instead choose up to six creatures within a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within 60 feet; each regains the rolled hit points.
