# Severing Orbit

A spell that forges a perfect loop of force into a spinning chakram, endlessly returning to its wielder in a deadly, disciplined arc.

---

> **Severing Orbit**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You conjure a **chakram of condensed force**, its edge humming as it spins in place before snapping forward on command. While the spell lasts, you can hurl this chakram repeatedly.
>
> When you cast the spell, and as an **action** on each of your turns for the duration, you can throw the chakram at a creature you can see within **120 feet**.
>
> When you do so, the chakram becomes a line of force **5 feet wide** that extends from you to the target.
>
> Each creature in the line, **excluding you and the target**, must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **2d6 force damage** on a failed save, or half as much damage on a success.
>
> Make a **ranged spell attack** against the target. On a hit, the target takes **5d6 force damage**.
>
> After the attack resolves, the chakram curves back through the air and **lands in your hand**, ready to be thrown again.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **5th level or higher**, the damage dealt to the target and the line increases by **1d6** for each slot level above 4th.
