> **Jim's Magic Missile**
> _1st-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You create three twisting, whistling, hypoallergenic, pink darts of magical force. Each dart can target a creature of your choice that you can see within range.
>
> Make a ranged spell attack for each missile. On a hit, a missile deals 2d4 + 1 force damage to its target. If the attack roll for any missile is a natural 1, all missiles miss their targets and the spell ends.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create one more missile for each slot level above 1st.
