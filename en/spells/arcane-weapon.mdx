# Arcane Weapon

Condensed arcane force coalesces into a weapon in your hand, its edge crackling with raw energy.

---

> **Arcane Weapon**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
>  
> You channel arcane energy into a weapon, temporarily transforming it into a magic weapon.  
>  
> You touch a weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. As a Minor Action on your turn, you can change the damage type dealt by the weapon to chemical, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or bludgeoning.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.
