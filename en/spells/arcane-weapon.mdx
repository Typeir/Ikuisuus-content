# Arcane Weapon (UA)

You channel arcane energy into a weapon, temporarily transforming it into a magic weapon.

---

> **Arcane Weapon (UA)**
> _1st-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. As a bonus action on your turn, you can change the damage type dealt by the weapon to acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.
