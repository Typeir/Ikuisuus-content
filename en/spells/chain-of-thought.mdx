# Chain of Thought

Faint, angular script crawls across your temples and sinks into your skin, leaving a cold, humming sigil.

---

> **Chain of Thought**  
> _Enchantment cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> You pull a memory, an idea, or a message out of your mind and set it in a **thought strand** — a ribbonlike, weightless thing about a foot long that appears in your hand and lasts for the duration or until you cast this spell again. A strand holds a single memory, idea, or message of **10 words or fewer**.
>
> While holding the strand, you can use a **Major Action** to send it to a creature you can see within 30 feet. It drifts through the air and settles in that creature's space, where it stays until somebody picks it up.
>
> A creature within 5 feet of a strand can pick it up and read it as a **Minor Action** — strands are readable only by touch. The strand disappears once its contents are received.
