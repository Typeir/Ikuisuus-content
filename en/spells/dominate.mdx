# Dominate

The target's limbs move on strings it cannot see, and its own will watches from the back row.

---

> **Dominate**  
> _4th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You seize the body of a creature you can see within range whose type the slot you cast this spell with can reach (see the table below). It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be [charmed](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#charmed) by you for the duration; it saves with advantage if you or your companions are fighting it.  
>  
> While the target is charmed, you have a telepathic link with it while you are both within the same Fold of the world. You can issue commands through the link (no action required), which it does its best to obey. You can use a Major Action to take total and precise control until the end of your next turn — using its actions, movement, and reactions as your own. Each time the target takes damage, it repeats the saving throw, ending the spell on a success.  
>  
> The [charmed](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#charmed) condition constrains its behavior utterly; its true [disposition](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/disposition-reputation-and-attitude) is unmoved, and it remembers everything.  
>  
> | Slot Level | Creature types you can target                | Duration            |
> | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
> | 4th        | Wildlife                                     | 1 minute            |
> | 5th        | + Humanoid                                   | 1 minute            |
> | 6th        | + Monstrosity                         | up to 10 minutes    |
> | 7th        | + Construct, Väki                       | up to 10 minutes    |
> | 8th        | + Aberration                                 | up to 1 hour        |
> | 9th        | + Celestial                                  | up to 8 hours       |
>  
> Each row includes the rows above it. All durations require concentration.
