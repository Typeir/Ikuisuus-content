# Infernal Calling

A jagged rift tears open and a hulking mass of flame and shadow drags through, scorching the air.

---

> **Infernal Calling**
> _5th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: 90 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M (a ruby worth at least 999 gp, which the spell consumes)
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You call forth a devil from the Nine Hells. The devil appears in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The devil disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> Choose a devil of challenge rating 6 or lower, such as a barbed devil or a bearded devil. The devil is friendly to you and your companions for the duration. Roll initiative for the devil, which has its own turns. It obeys any verbal commands that you issue to it (no action required by you), as long as they don’t violate its fiendish nature. If you don’t issue any commands to the devil, it defends itself from hostile creatures but otherwise takes no actions.
>
> If your concentration is broken before the spell ends, the devil doesn’t disappear. Instead, you lose control of the devil, it becomes hostile toward you and your companions, and it might attack. An uncontrolled devil can’t be dismissed by you, and it disappears 1 hour after you summoned it.
>
> The DM has the devil’s statistics.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the challenge rating increases by 1 for each slot level above 5th.
