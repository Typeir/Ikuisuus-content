# Earthbind

The ground surges up, entangling the target's legs in packed soil and stone and yanking them toward the earth.

---

> **Earthbind**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 300 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> Yellow strips of magical energy loop around a creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or its flying speed (if any) is reduced to 0 for the spell’s duration. An airborne creature affected by this spell safely descends at 60 feet per round until it reaches the ground or the spell ends.
