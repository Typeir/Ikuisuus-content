# Galder's Tower

A narrow stone tower erupts from the chosen spot, its walls pulsing with cold light and a low, grinding hum.

---

> **Galder's Tower**
> _3rd-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You conjure a two-story tower made of stone, wood, or similar suitably sturdy materials. The tower appears on a flat area of ground you can see within range. The tower is 20 feet on a side and 30 feet high, with a door and a few windows. The interior is divided into two floors, each with a 10-foot-high ceiling. The tower is furnished and decorated as you choose. The furnishings are nonmagical and made of materials appropriate for the tower’s size and location.
>
> The tower disappears after 24 hours, or when you cast this spell again. Any creatures or objects inside the tower when it disappears are harmlessly ejected into unoccupied spaces closest to the tower’s former location.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the tower is one additional story taller for each slot level above 3rd.
