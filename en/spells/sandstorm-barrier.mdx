# Sandstorm Barrier

A vertical surge of coarse sand erupts, forming a shifting wall whose whipping grains scour surfaces and choke sight.

---

> **Sandstorm Barrier**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action or 1 Reaction  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a handful of grit, thrown)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You conjure a wall of swirling sand on the ground at a point you can see within range. You can make the wall up to 30 feet long, 10 feet high, and 10 feet thick. The wall lasts for the duration.
>
> The wall blocks line of sight, but not movement. A creature is **blinded** while in the wall's space and must spend 3 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves there.
>
> **Letting It Settle.** As a **Minor Action** on any turn after the one you cast this spell, you can drop concentration deliberately. Any part of the wall that was conjured over sand stays where it is for the spell's full duration; the rest falls.
>
> **Solid barrier.** Area effects break against it. See [Barriers](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/barriers).
