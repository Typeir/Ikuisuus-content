# Aura of Life

Life-preserving energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius.

---

> **Aura of Life**
> _4th-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self (30-foot radius)
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> Each nonhostile living creature in the aura has resistance to necrotic damage, and its hit point maximum can't be reduced. In addition, a nonhostile living creature regains 1 hit point when it starts its turn in the aura with 0 hit points.
