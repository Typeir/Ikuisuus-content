# Aura of Life

A low, warm halo blooms outward, knitting torn flesh and steadying faltering breath.

---

> **Aura of Life**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot radius)  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> Life-preserving energy radiates from you in a 30-foot radius. The aura moves with you, centered on you.
>
> As a **Minor Action**, you can cause one creature in the aura — including yourself — to regain [% 2d6 %] hit points.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the aura also wards those inside it: each nonhostile living creature in it gains resistance to **dark** damage, and its hit point maximum cannot be reduced. The healing increases by [% 1d6 %] for each slot level above 4th.
