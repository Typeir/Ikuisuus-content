# Conjure Väki

You invite an idea to partake on your trials and tribulations, on specific terms; and it listens to the terms far more closely than you do.

---

> **Conjure Väki**  
> _4th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a precursor lodgment — a fired clay figure, a knot of sulfur, a stoppered measure of meltwater — which the parlay consumes)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You open a parlay, and a **Väki** — an Idea-fragment of the world, of flame or brine or hollow places, takes shape in an unoccupied space you can see within range. Their combined Challenge Rating may not exceed **twice this spell's level**. See [Summoning and Conjuration](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/summoning-and-conjuration) for the Challenge Budget, Swarms and Formations, and command.
>
> Never command a Väki; you may only ask, and it obeys because the terms hold. Caprice, not allegiance, governs what answers.
>
> **The bargain that slips.** If your concentration breaks, the Väki is not loosed as other summonings are — it is **freed**, and it cannot be dismissed by you or by any effect that ends a spell. It remains until it is destroyed or until it tires of the world, hostile to whatever stands nearest, and it remembers who broke faith.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** The Challenge Budget is always twice the level of the slot spent.
