# Death Grips

An invocation of forceful unmaking, The Revenant conjures a torrent of pure entropy to pull their enemies to their demise.

---

> **Death Grips**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You seize a creature or object with a violent surge of collapsing force.  
> Choose one creature or object you can see within range. You need to be able to effectively grapple or carry said target.
> The target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or be **pulled up to 30 feet** straight toward you and **knocked prone**.
>
> On a **successful** save, the creature is instead **pulled up to 10 feet** toward you and is **not** knocked prone.
>
> This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **2nd level or higher**, increase the distance the target is pulled on a failed save by **5 feet** per slot level above 1st.
