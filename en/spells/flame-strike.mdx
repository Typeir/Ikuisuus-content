# Flame Strike

A column of white-hot flame descends from above, searing flesh and shattering stone where it strikes.

---

> **Flame Strike**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (pinch of sulfur)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A column of Kultharja's light falls where you point, as though noon had been moved. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within range must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 4d6 fire %] **and** [% 4d6 holy %] on a failed save, or half as much of both on a successful one.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, **both** damage rolls increase by [% 1d6 %] for each slot level above 5th. From **7th level**, the radius also increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 6th.
