# Crusader's Mantle

A mantle of serrated light unfurls around you, its filaments snapping shut into a rigid, gleaming shell.

---

> **Crusader's Mantle**
> _3rd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Holy power radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius, awakening boldness in friendly creatures. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. While in the aura, each nonhostile creature (including you) in the aura deals an extra 1d4 radiant damage when it hits with a weapon attack.
