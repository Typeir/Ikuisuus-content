# Crusader's Mantle

A mantle of serrated light unfurls around you, its filaments snapping shut into a rigid, gleaming shell.

---

> **Crusader's Mantle**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You hold your god's Key open a crack — not enough to be answered, only enough to be overheard — and for one minute everyone standing near you fights like someone who has been told they are right.
>
> An aura extends 30 feet from you and moves with you for the duration. While in it, each nonhostile creature, you included, deals an extra [% 1d4 holy %] when it hits with a weapon attack.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d4 %] per additional level above the 3rd.
