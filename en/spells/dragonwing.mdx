# Dragonwing

A pair of half-real wings unfold from your back, throwing a shadow not quite yours.

---

> **Dragonwing**  
> _4th Level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (wing-bone of a dragon)  
> **Duration**: 1 minute
>
> You grow draconic wings for the duration.
>
> As a minor action, you unfurl your wings; you take the **disengage** action for free, and gain a flight equal to twice your movement speed.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the duration of this spell increases by 1 minute.
