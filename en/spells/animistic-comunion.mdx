# Animistic Communion

The splinters of the world dance to your tune, an investiture of the indifferent elements that shape the world around you.

---

> **Animistic Communion**  
> _6th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>
> When you learn this spell, choose one aspect: **Flame**, **Ice**, **Stone**, or **Wind**.  
> You gain that aspect's benefits for the duration.  
>
> **Flame.** Flames race across your body, shedding bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet.  
>
> - You are immune to fire damage and have resistance to cold damage.  
> - Any creature that moves within 5 feet of you for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there takes 1d10 fire damage.  
> - You can use your action to create a line of fire 15 feet long and 5 feet wide extending from you.  
> - Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
> - The line ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.  
>
> **Ice.** Ice rimes your body.  
>
> - You are immune to cold damage and have resistance to fire damage.  
> - You can move across difficult terrain created by ice or snow without spending extra movement.  
> - The ground in a 10-foot radius around you is icy and is difficult terrain for creatures other than you; the area moves with you.  
> - You can use your action to create a blast of cold air in a 15-foot cone.  
> - Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 4d6 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
> - A creature that fails has its speed reduced to 0 until the start of your next turn.  
>
> **Stone.** Bits of rock spread across your body.  
>
> - You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.  
> - You can move across difficult terrain made of earth or stone without expending extra movement.  
> - You can use your action to create a small earthquake on the ground within 15 feet of you.  
> - Each creature on that ground other than you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.  
> - You can also use an action to touch a stone object or surface and merge with it.  
> - While merged, you can see and hear through the stone as if in your normal form.  
> - You can use your movement to leave the stone at a point you can see within 5 feet of your entry point.  
> - If the spell ends while merged, you are ejected to the nearest unoccupied space and take 5d6 force damage.  
>
> **Wind.** A howling column of wind erupts from you.  
>
> - Ranged weapon attacks against you have disadvantage.  
> - You gain a flying speed of 60 feet.  
> - If you are still flying when the spell ends, you fall unless you can otherwise prevent it.  
> - You can use your action to create a 15-foot cube of swirling wind centered on a point you can see within 60 feet of you.  
> - Each creature in that cube must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 2d10 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
> - The wind disperses gas or vapor, and extinguishes candles, torches, and unprotected flames in the area.
