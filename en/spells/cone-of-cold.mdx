# Cone of Cold

A frigid blast erupts in a widening cone, freezing the air and coating flesh and stone in brittle rime.

---

> **Cone of Cold**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (60-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a small crystal or glass cone)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A blast of cold air erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes [% 8d8 frost %] on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.  
>  
> The cone remains where it fell: for 1 minute, the area is coated in rime and counts as difficult terrain.  
>  
> A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 5th. When you cast it using a slot of **6th level or higher**, you may instead drop the cold from above as **hailfall**: a 20-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within 300 feet, dealing [% 2d8 bludgeoning %] plus the spell's frost damage (Dexterity saving throw for half), leaving the same rime field.
