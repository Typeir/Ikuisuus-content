# Misty Step

Between one breath and the next, you are elsewhere. You went around a corner that was not there — corners conduct, and the world is full of them if you know how to look.

---

> **Misty Step**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You step through a fold in the world and out again up to 30 feet away, into an unoccupied space you can see.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the distance increases by 30 feet for each slot level above 2nd. From **4th level**, you can also bring one willing creature within 5 feet of you along, and one more for every two slot levels beyond that.
