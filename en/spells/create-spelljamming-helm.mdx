> **Create Spelljamming Helm**
> _5th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You touch a chair or similar seat and imbue it with magic, turning it into a spelljamming helm, a magical device that allows a ship to travel through wildspace and the Astral Sea. The helm is created in its standard form and functions as described in the "Spelljamming Helm" item description. The magic required to create the helm is so complex that only one can exist at a time for each caster. If you use this spell again, the previous helm created by you immediately loses its magic and becomes a nonmagical item.
