# Mind Sliver

A fragment of will is wedged into the gap between intent and action — too small to locate, precise enough to matter.

---

> **Mind Sliver**  
> _Enchantment cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You drive a disorienting spike of psychic energy into the mind of one creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take [% 1d6 psychic %] and subtract [% 1d4 %] from the next saving throw it makes before the end of your next turn.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** This spell’s damage increases by [% 1d6 %] when you reach 5th level ([% 2d6 %]), 11th level ([% 3d6 %]), and 17th level ([% 4d6 %]).
