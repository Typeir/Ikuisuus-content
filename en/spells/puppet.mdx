# Puppet

A red thread you did not tie runs from your hand to theirs. You pull, and somewhere, briefly, fate is not consulted.

---

> **Puppet**  
> _1st-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Choose one humanoid you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or move up to its speed in a direction you choose. You control the movement entirely, and it does not provoke opportunity attacks. The spell ends when the movement does.
>
> You can walk a target into open air, deep water, or a fire, but not into the obvious end of itself: the movement stops at the edge of anything that would plainly kill it. Everything short of that is fair.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional humanoid for each slot level above 1st.
