# Puppet (UA)

Invisible filaments erupt from your palm and seize a body's limbs, yanking them into abrupt, puppet-like motion.

---

> **Puppet (UA)**
> _1st-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You attempt to briefly control the actions of a humanoid. Choose one humanoid you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or move up to its speed in a direction you choose. During this movement, the creature is controlled by you, and it doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks. After this movement, the spell ends.
