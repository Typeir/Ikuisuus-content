> **Galder's Speedy Courier**
> _4th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 10 minutes
>
> You send a tiny object weighing 5 pounds or less to a creature you know on the same plane of existence as you. The object is transported through a small spectral portal that appears within 5 feet of you and disappears after the object passes through it. In the recipient’s vicinity, a corresponding portal appears within 10 feet of the target creature, and the object emerges from it. If the target is unwilling to receive the object, it doesn’t emerge and instead returns to you through a portal that appears within 5 feet of you.
>
> If the object is sent to an invalid destination (such as a creature you don’t know or a location not on your plane of existence), the object returns to you through a portal as described above.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the maximum weight of the object increases by 5 pounds for each slot level above 4th.
