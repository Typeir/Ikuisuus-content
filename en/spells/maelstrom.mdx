# Maelstrom

A mass of swirling water appears and spins in a 30-foot-radius, 10-foot-high cylinder centered on a point you can see within range. The water spreads around corners. The area is difficult terrain and any creature that starts its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take 6d6 bludgeoning damage and be pulled 10 feet toward the center.
