# Maelstrom

A column of black water stands where no water was, turning on itself and pulling everything loose toward the middle of it.

---

> **Maelstrom**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a whorled shell, held to the ear)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A mass of swirling water appears and spins in a 30-foot-radius, 10-foot-high cylinder centered on a point you can see within range. The water spreads around corners — corners conduct, and the spiral takes every one it is offered.
>
> The area is difficult terrain. A creature that starts its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take [% 6d6 bludgeoning %] and be pulled 10 feet toward the center.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d6 %] and the radius by 5 feet for each slot level above 5th.
