# Blade Barrier

A ragged wall of whirling blades tears through the air, shredding stone and flesh in its path.

---

> **Blade Barrier**  
> _6th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action or 1 Reaction  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You create a wall of whirling, razor-sharp blades at a point you choose within range. The wall can be up to 100 feet long, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick, or a ringed wall up to 60 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick. The wall provides three-quarters cover to creatures behind it, and its space is difficult terrain.
>
> When a creature enters the wall's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 6d10 slashing %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
>
> **The Blade in Your Hand.** When you cast this spell, you may keep one blade out of the wall. It costs nothing to take and lasts for the duration or until you let it go. As a **Major Action** you can strike one creature within 5 feet of you with it: make a melee spell attack, dealing [% 3d10 slashing %] on a hit.
>
> **Energy barrier.** A wall of moving blades is not a solid thing — area effects pass straight through it. See [Barriers](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/barriers).
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 6th, and the wall's height increases by 10 feet.
