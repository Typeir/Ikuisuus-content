# Prasinus's Path

The green knight's road runs through living wood: you step into a tree, and the world's surviving trees are all one door.

---

> **Prasinus's Path**  
> _5th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You gain the ability to enter a living tree at least your size and step from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 500 feet. Spending 5 feet of movement, you step into a tree and know the location of every tree of its kind within 500 feet large enough to receive you; you may emerge from one of them, appearing in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of it. With movement remaining, you may repeat this, tree to tree, for as long as your concentration lasts.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **6th level or higher (Greenway):** you may instead open a one-round conduit between a Large-or-larger plant within 10 feet and any plant you have seen or touched at least once, at any distance within the same Fold of the world. Any creature can step through, from the one plant to the other, using 5 feet of movement. In a dead universe, the great surviving trees are the last reliable roads — and Prasinus knows them all.
