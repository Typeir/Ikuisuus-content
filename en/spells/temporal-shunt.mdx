# Temporal Shunt

The target vanishes in a stuttering blur and reappears moments later as time collapses around them.

---

> **Temporal Shunt**
> _5th-level Transmutation DC_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Reaction
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
> You attempt to momentarily send another creature into the future. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or vanish, being thrown forward in time, appearing back in its space at the start of its next turn. The target is incapacitated while it is gone, and it can’t perceive anything around it. On its return, the target is disoriented and can’t take a reaction until the end of its next turn.
