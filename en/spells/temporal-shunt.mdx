# Temporal Shunt

The target vanishes in a stuttering blur and reappears moments later as time collapses around them.

---

> **Temporal Shunt**  
> _5th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Reaction, taken when a creature you can see makes an attack roll or casts a spell  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You throw the triggering creature a little way into the future — its attack or its spell goes with it, and neither arrives. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or vanish, reappearing in its space at the start of its next turn. While it is gone it is incapacitated and perceives nothing. On its return it is disoriented and **staggered** until the end of its next turn.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can target one additional creature involved in the same trigger for each slot level above 5th.
