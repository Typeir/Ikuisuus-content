> **Dream of the Blue Veil**
> _7th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 10 minutes
> **Range**: 20 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 6 hours
>
> You and up to eight willing creatures within range fall unconscious for the spell’s duration and experience visions of another world on the Material Plane, as described below. If the spell reaches its full duration, the visions conclude with each of you encountering a blue, shimmering veil. The spell then ends with you and the other creatures physically transported to the world that was in the visions (see “Traveling to Other Worlds” in the Dungeon Master’s Guide for more information about these worlds).
>
> To cast the spell, you must have a magic item that originated on the world you wish to reach, which must be used as a material component for the spell.
>
> If you are already on the world that is the spell’s destination, the spell fails but isn’t wasted.
>
> The spell ends early for a creature if that creature takes any damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor.
