# Nathair's Mischief

Shadowy motes spill from your fingertips, crawling over surfaces and warping nearby shapes into twitching, false outlines.

---

> **Nathair's Mischief**
> _2nd-level Illusion_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60ft
> **Components**: S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You fill a 20-foot cube you can see within range with fey and draconic magic. Roll on the Mischievous Surge table to determine the magical effect produced.
>
> Each creature in the cube must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become affected by the effect until the start of your next turn.
>
> You can move the cube up to 10 feet before you roll on the table at the start of each of your turns.
>
> **Mischievous Surge**
>
> | d4 | Effect |
> |----|--------|
> | 1  | The smell of apple pie fills the air, and each creature in the cube must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become charmed by you until the start of your next turn. |
> | 2  | Bouquets of flowers appear all around, and each creature in the cube must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be blinded until the start of your next turn as the flowers spray water in their faces. |
> | 3  | Each creature in the cube must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or begin giggling until the start of your next turn. A giggling creature is incapacitated and uses all its movement to move in a random direction each turn. |
> | 4  | Drops of molasses appear and hover in the cube, turning it into difficult terrain until the start of your next turn. |
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can move the cube an additional 5 feet for each slot level above 2nd.
