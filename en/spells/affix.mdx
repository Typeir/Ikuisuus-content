# Affix

The touched object roots into the ground, its mass locking rigid and refusing to shift.

---

> **Affix**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 hour  
>  
> You touch an object that weighs up to 10 pounds and cause it to become magically fixed in place. Until the spell ends, the object is immobile, and can’t be moved, even by force. If the object is fixed to a surface, such as a wall or a floor, it can support up to 2,000 pounds of weight. More weight causes the object to fall. A creature can use a Major Action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC. On a success, the creature can move the object up to 10 feet. On a failure it gains a level of exhaustion.
