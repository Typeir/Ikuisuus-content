# Conjure Knowbot (UA)

A small, insectile construct of humming metal and light assembles at your touch, lenses flickering as it scans the surroundings.

---

> **Conjure Knowbot (UA)**
> _4th-level Conjuration T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 10 minutes
>
>
> You touch a Tiny, nonmagical object and transform it into a knowbot—a magical construct that can answer questions and perform simple tasks.
>
> The object you touch becomes a knowbot, which appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you. The knowbot is friendly to you and your companions. Roll initiative for the knowbot, which has its own turns. It obeys any verbal commands that you issue to it (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any commands to the knowbot, it defends itself from hostile creatures but otherwise takes no actions.
>
> The knowbot is a Tiny construct with AC 13, 10 hit points, and a speed of 30 feet. It has immunity to poison and psychic damage, and to the charmed, exhaustion, and poisoned conditions. It can speak, read, and understand all languages you know.
>
> As an action, you can ask the knowbot a question. The knowbot magically searches its database and provides a concise answer to the best of its ability. The answer is limited to information that a typical creature of your level and experience would know, as determined by the DM. The knowbot can answer up to five questions before it disappears.
>
> The spell ends after 10 minutes, when the knowbot drops to 0 hit points, or when it has answered five questions, whichever comes first. When the spell ends, the knowbot reverts to its original object form.
