# Death Ward

You touch a creature and grant it a measure of protection from death.

---

> **Death Ward**
> _4th-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> The first time the target would drop to 0 hit points as a result of taking damage, the target instead drops to 1 hit point, and the spell ends.
>
> If the spell is still in effect when the target is subjected to an effect that would kill it instantaneously without dealing damage (such as a power word kill), that effect is instead negated against the target, and the spell ends.
