# Lightning Edge

A spell that condenses electrical energy into a singular cutting vector, striking like a blade drawn at impossible speed.

---

> **Lightning Edge**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>
> You carve a blade of compressed lightning and drive it forward in a single, decisive cut.
>
> Make a **melee spell attack** against a creature within range. On a hit, the target takes **4d10 lightning damage**.
>
> If the attack hits, the target must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** or be **stunned until the end of its next turn**.
>
> If the target is wearing metal armor or is made primarily of metal, it makes this saving throw **at disadvantage**.
