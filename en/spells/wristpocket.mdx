> **Wristpocket**
> _2nd-level Conjuration (ritual)_
> **Casting Time**: 1 action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You flick your wrist, causing a tiny extradimensional space to open at your fingertips, which you can use to store small objects. The space can hold up to 5 pounds or an object that fits in your hand. As part of the action used to cast the spell, or as a bonus action while the spell persists, you can place one object into the space or retrieve one object from it. You can only have one object in the space at a time. You can retrieve the object at any time, and if the spell ends, the object appears in your hand or at your feet (your choice).
>
> If you die while the object is in the space, it appears at your feet.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can store one additional object for each slot level above 2nd.
