# Unstoppable Motion  

This technique is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
This technique is awarded to those who usher in the memory of the second star - the era of motion - and it's accomplices.

> “May the wheel endlessly spin, and its mechanical arms ordain all.” 
> 
> — The Golden One  

---  

> **Unstoppable Motion**  
> _10th-Level Legendary Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>
> You embody the latent power of the Golden One, increasing your strength beyond imagination.  
>
> - Choose one creature or unattended object within 60 feet. The target must weigh **100,000 pounds or less**.  
> - If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or be seized by inevitability. On a failure, you hurl it up to **300 feet** in a straight line in a direction of your choice. On a success, it resists the throw but still takes **6d12 force damage** from the strain of resisting divine motion.  
> - If the target is an object, it is automatically hurled.  
> - This spell deals double damage to constructs, quadruple to objects.
>
> **Impact**: If the thrown target collides with a solid surface or creature at the end of it's trajectory, it takes **20d6 bludgeoning damage**. 
> If it collides with a creature, that creature must make a **Dexterity saving throw** or take the same damage and be knocked prone.
> On a success, they take half as much damage and are not knocked prone.
>
> **Collateral Line**: If the target is hurled through other creatures or objects during its flight, each creature in its path must succeed on a **Dexterity saving throw** or take **12d6 bludgeoning damage** and be knocked prone as the body (or object) smashes through them. 
> On a success, they take half as much damage and are not knocked prone.
> If it collides with an object and the object is destroyed by the damage, the path is uninterrupted and it keeps traveling.
>
> **At Higher Levels (11th+)**: Increase the weight limit by **+50,000 pounds** and both impact and collateral damage by **+4d6** for each level above 10th.
