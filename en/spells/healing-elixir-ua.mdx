> **Healing Elixir (UA)**
> _1st-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You create a healing elixir in an empty flask that you touch. For the next 24 hours, drinking the elixir causes a creature to regain 2d4 + 2 hit points. The elixir’s potency is lost after 24 hours. The elixir has the consistency and taste of a normal potion of healing.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 2d4 + 2 for each slot level above 1st.
