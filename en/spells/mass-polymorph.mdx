> **Mass Polymorph**
> _9th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You transform up to ten creatures of your choice that you can see within range. An unwilling target must make a Wisdom saving throw to resist the transformation. A shapechanger automatically succeeds on this saving throw. Each target assumes the form of a beast of your choice, the challenge rating of which is equal to or less than the target’s (or the target’s level, if it doesn’t have a challenge rating). The transformation lasts for the duration, or until the target drops to 0 hit points or dies. You can choose the same form or different ones for each target.
>
> Each target’s game statistics are replaced by the statistics of the chosen beast, though the target retains its alignment and personality. Each target assumes the hit points of its new form. When it reverts to its normal form, the creature returns to the number of hit points it had before it transformed. If it reverts as a result of dropping to 0 hit points, any excess damage carries over to its normal form. As long as the excess damage doesn’t reduce the creature’s normal form to 0 hit points, it isn’t knocked unconscious.
>
> The creature is limited in the actions it can perform by the nature of its new form, and it can’t speak, cast spells, or take any other action that requires hands or speech. The target’s gear melds into the new form. The creature can’t activate, use, wield, or otherwise benefit from any of its equipment.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 10th level or higher, you can transform up to two additional creatures for each slot level above 9th.
