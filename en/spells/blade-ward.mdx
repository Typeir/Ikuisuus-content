# Blade Ward

A ragged film of force snaps over your skin, deflecting blades and blows.

---

> **Blade Ward**
> _Abjuration cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
>
> You extend your hand and trace a sigil of warding in the air.
>
> You have resistance against bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage dealt by weapon attacks until the end of your next turn.
