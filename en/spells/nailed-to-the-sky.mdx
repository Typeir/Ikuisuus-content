# Nailed to the Sky / Bound to the Earth

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.  
This spell can be attained by the scions of ambition, if they ever survive the trial that comes with embracing true potential.

> “Nor heavens above or hells below will take our destiny from us.” - Nekarion, the Dreadlord

---

> **Nailed to the Sky / Bound to the Earth** 
> _12th-Level Legendary Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Melee (reach of your weapon)  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>
> You deliver an uppercut with impossible force, launching your foe into the firmament.  
> Target one creature you can effectively **grapple** and make a **melee spell attack** with your weapon.
>
> On a hit, the target takes *75 + *10d12 force damage** and must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or be hurled into the upper atmosphere, vanishing from the battlefield.
>
> While in orbit:
>
> - The target is **banished** but continues to suffer **4d6 cold damage per round** and begins **suffocating**.
> - If no open sky exists, the creature instead collides with the ceiling, taking **20d12 bludgeoning damage**, and the spell ends.
> - You may choose to follow the target; if you do, you vanish with them and remain linked until impact.
> - A creature launched this way falls naturally after **50 rounds (10 minutes)**, taking cold damage for the first 25 rounds.
>
> ---
>
> **Bound to the Earth** >_Bonus Action or Action (while concentrating and only if you followed them)_
>
> You end the spell early, dragging the target from orbit in a blazing arc to a point of your choice within **120 ft** of where they were struck.
>
> The target must make a **Constitution saving throw** or take **75 + 10d12 bludgeoning damage, plus 10d12 force damage** (half on success).
>
> All other creatures of your choice within a **60-ft-radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking the same damage on a failure or half as much on a success, and are **knocked prone** on a failure.
> 
> The main target instantly dies if brought to 0 HP or less by Bound to the Earth.
>
> You land harmlessly, standing in an unoccupied space within 5 ft of the impact zone.
