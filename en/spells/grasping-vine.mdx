# Grasping Vine

Gnarled vines erupt from the ground, snaking outward to seize and wrench at anything they touch.

---

> **Grasping Vine**  
> _4th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You conjure a vine that sprouts from the ground in an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within range. As part of casting the spell, you can direct the vine to lash out at one creature you can see within 30 feet of it. That creature must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be pulled 20 feet directly toward the vine.  
>  
> As a Minor Action on your turn, you can direct the vine to lash out at the same creature or another one.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you conjure **one additional vine** for each slot level above 4th. One Minor Action directs all of them, though each must lash a different creature.
