# Far Step

A seam in space opens beneath your feet and you reappear a short distance away.

---

> **Far Step**  
> _5th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. On each of your turns before the spell ends, you can use a Minor Action to teleport in this way again.
