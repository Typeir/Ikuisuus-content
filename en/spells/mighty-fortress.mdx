# Mighty Fortress

A complete structure imposes itself on the landscape without construction, without assembly — the world simply accommodates it, and has always done so.

---

> **Mighty Fortress**
> _8th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: 1 mile
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You conjure a fortress made of stone that appears on the ground you can see within range. The fortress is a square, with sides 120 feet long and 20 feet high, and it is composed of ten-foot-thick walls made of stone. The fortress has four towers, one at each corner, each 30 feet square and 30 feet high. The main building has a large central keep, with a roof, and a gatehouse with a portcullis. The interior is divided into rooms as you wish, but the spell can’t create furnishings or other objects. The fortress is furnished with doors, shutters, and a drawbridge, all made of stone. The fortress is impervious to nonmagical weapons and spells of 6th level or lower can’t affect it. The fortress remains until it is destroyed or you dismiss it as an action.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level, the fortress is twice as large in each dimension.
