# Revivify

A cold, blue-white light stitches flesh and bone together and breath returns to the body.

---

> **Revivify**  
> _3rd-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (diamonds worth 1,000 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> This is a jolt to a body that can still hold life, not the making of one that cannot. You touch a creature that has **died within the last 10 minutes** and whose corpse is still **functional** — head, heart, and the greater part of its flesh intact. The creature returns to life with **1 hit point**.  
>  
> Revivify cannot restore a creature reduced to pieces, ash, or ruin, nor one that died of old age, and it replaces no missing body parts. Any Grievous Wounds the creature carried in death remain with it in life.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the window in which the creature must have died extends by 1 minute for each slot level above 3rd.
