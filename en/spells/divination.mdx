# Divination

The offering goes onto the fire and the smoke does not rise straight. Someone is reading it before you are.

---

> **Divination**  
> _4th-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (incense and an offering appropriate to your god, together worth at least 25 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You burn the offering and put a question to your god — or to whichever of its granted Keys happens to be listening. Ask one question concerning a specific goal, event, or activity to occur within the next 7 days, and you receive a truthful reply. It may come as a short phrase, a cryptic rhyme, or an omen.
>
> The answer accounts for nothing that has not happened yet. A spell cast later, a companion gained or lost, a mind changed on the road — the reply was already given, and it does not revise itself.
>
> If you cast this spell two or more times before finishing your next Recovery, there is a cumulative 25 percent chance for each casting after the first that the reading comes back random instead. That roll is made in secret.
