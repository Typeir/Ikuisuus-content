# Fog

You exhale, and the breath does not disperse. The air of a dead world is always most of the way to fog already; it takes very little persuading to finish the job.

> **Fog**  
> _1st-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You create a **10-foot-radius** sphere of fog centered on a point within range. It spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured.  
>  
> As a **Minor Action** on any later turn, you can plant another fog of the same size at a point within range. Every fog belongs to this one casting and this one concentration — there is no limit on how many you can lay down, only on how many turns you are prepared to spend laying them.  
>  
> Each fog lasts until the spell ends, or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses that one. Wind takes them individually; the rest stay where they are.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, every fog you plant has its radius increased by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st.
