# Silmä

An ornate, glassy eye manifests on you. That which was hidden is now bare.

---

> **Silmä**  
> _2nd-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a bit of bat fur)  
> **Duration**: 2 hours  
>  
> For the duration, you see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you perceive the thin Folds of the world laid over the visible — things within them appear ghostly and translucent.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **4th level or higher**, the eye **detaches** and drifts as an invisible, roaming sensor for the duration, which becomes Concentration, up to 1 hour. You mentally receive visual information from the eye, which has normal vision and darkvision out to 30 feet and can look in every direction. As a Major Action, you can move the eye up to 30 feet in any direction; there is no limit to how far it can travel, but it cannot leave the Fold of the world you occupy. A solid barrier blocks its movement, but it can pass through an opening as small as 1 inch in diameter.
