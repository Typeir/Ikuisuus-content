# Wild Strike

A barbed vine of thorns lashes from your hand, snagging whatever it strikes and yanking it closer.

---

> **Wild Strike**  
> _Transmutation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: self 
> **Components**: V, S, M (the stem of a plant with thorns)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  You channel the magic of the wilds, embodying the dangers of the untamed
>  
> **Thorn.** You create a long, vine-like whip covered in thorns that you lash toward a creature within 30 feet.  Make a melee spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the creature takes 1d6 piercing damage, and if the creature is Large or smaller, you pull the creature up to 10 feet closer to you.
> 
> **Beast.** You embody a poisonous animal of your choice, your teeth or fingernails sharpen, ready to deliver a toxic attack. You then jab a creature you can see within 10 feet. The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 poison damage inmidiately, and 1d6 again at the end of its next turn. 
> 
> The spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).  
>
