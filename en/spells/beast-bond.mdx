# Beast Bond

A quiet tether snaps to the animal, and for a while you look out through borrowed eyes.

---

> **Beast Bond**  
> _1st-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a bit of fur wrapped in a cloth)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You establish a telepathic link with one willing beast you touch. The spell fails if the beast's Intelligence is 4 or higher. Until the spell ends, you can communicate with the beast telepathically while within 1 mile of it.  
>  
> As a Major Action, you can perceive through the beast's senses — seeing through its eyes and hearing what it hears — until you use a Major Action to return to your own. While doing so, you gain the benefits of any special senses the beast has, but you are blinded and deafened to your own surroundings.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can bond with one additional beast for each slot level above 1st, and may shift your perception among any bonded beasts.
