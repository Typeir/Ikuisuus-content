# Restoration

Your touch drives out what has claimed the body, and the flesh remembers how to be itself.

---

> **Restoration**  
> _2nd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S (and, for the greater working, diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You touch a creature and end one disease afflicting it, or one of the following conditions: blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **5th level or higher**, consuming the diamond dust, you can instead end one of the following on the touched creature:  
>  
> - one level of exhaustion  
> - one curse, including the creature's attunement to a cursed object  
> - one charm or petrification effect  
> - one effect reducing an ability score  
> - one effect reducing the creature's hit point maximum
