# Moonbeam

A shaft of pale light descends from whatever Kuutar is willing to lend. What burns is not the light itself but the thing the light reminds every living form that it carries.

---

> **Moonbeam**  
> _2nd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a seed of any night-blooming plant)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A silvery beam shines down in a 5-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within range. Dim light fills the cylinder until the spell ends.
>
> When a creature enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Constitution saving throw, taking [% 2d10 holy %] on a failure or half as much on a success. A shapechanger makes this save with disadvantage; on a failure it also reverts instantly to its true form and cannot take another while it remains in the light.
>
> On each of your turns after you cast this spell, you can move the beam 60 feet in any direction as a **Minor Action**.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
