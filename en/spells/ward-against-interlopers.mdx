# Ward Against Interlopers

A pale, translucent ward clings to the touched creature, pushing external influence away and steadying the target.

---

> **Ward Against Interlopers**  
> _1st-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (holy water or powdered silver and iron, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> When you cast this spell, **choose one creature type**: aberration, celestial, construct, monstrosity, null, väki, or wildlife. You cannot choose humanoid — a ward against people is not a ward, it is a wall, and this spell is not that.  
>  
> Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is warded against creatures of the chosen type. Such creatures have **disadvantage on attack rolls** against the target, and the target cannot be **charmed, frightened, or possessed** by them. If the target is already suffering one of those effects from such a creature, it has advantage on any new saving throw against it.
