# Infallible Relay (UA)

A thin, humming filament of light unspools from your hand and anchors into the air, forming an unbroken conduit that carries sight and whispers along its length.

---

> **Infallible Relay (UA)**
> _1st-level Divination T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You send a short message of twenty-five words or less to a creature with which you are familiar. The creature hears the message and can respond in a like manner immediately. The spell enables creatures with Intelligence scores of at least 1 to understand the meaning of your message.
