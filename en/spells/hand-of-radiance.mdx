> **Hand of Radiance (UA)**
> _Evocation cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 5 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You point at up to three creatures you can see within range, and they must each make a Constitution saving throw. The target takes 1d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> The spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
