# Pour

Pour the contents of a vial or potion

---

> **Pour**  
> _Conjuration cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 60 Feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M(a vial or potion)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  The spell can be used for the following effect
> Administer it to a willing creature within range. If the creature is unconcious, treat it as a spell attack. if it hits, the target drinks the potion. In any case the potion is spent.
>  
> Use up a quarter vial of acid, poison or alchemist's fire. Reduplicate and precipitate it over a creature, or two creatures within range that are within 5 feet of each other. A target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 acid, poison or fire damage, according to the material used.  
>  
> This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
