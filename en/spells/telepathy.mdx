# Telepathy

An invisible lattice of thought snaps into being, linking minds so images, words, and sensations flow between them.

---

> **Telepathy**  
> _8th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Unlimited  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 24 hours  
>  
> You create a telepathic link between yourself and a willing creature you are familiar with. The creature can be anywhere within the same Fold of the world as you. If either of you leaves it, the link does not break — it thins, and carries nothing but the knowledge that the other is still alive, until you are both back within one Fold again.  
>  
> Until the spell ends, you and the target can instantaneously share words, images, sounds, and other sensory messages with one another through the link, and the target recognizes you as the creature it is communicating with. The spell enables a creature with an Intelligence score of at least 1 to understand the meaning of your words and take in the scope of any sensory message you send to it.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level or higher, you link **one additional willing creature** for each slot level above 8th. Every linked creature can reach every other, not only you, which is a great deal harder to keep quiet than people expect.
