# Wall of Thorns

A jagged wall of thorns erupts, hooked spines ripping flesh and shredding anything that presses against it.

---

> **Wall of Thorns**
> _6th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
>
> A wall of tough, pliable, tangled brush bristling with needle-sharp thorns appears at a point you choose within range.
>
> You choose to make the wall up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick or a circle that has a 20-foot diameter and is up to 20 feet high and 5 feet thick. The wall blocks line of sight.
>
> When the wall appears, each creature within its area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 7d8 piercing damage, or half as much damage on a successful save.
>
> A creature can move through the wall, albeit slowly and painfully. For every 1 foot a creature moves through the wall, it must spend 4 feet of movement. Furthermore, the first time a creature enters the wall on a turn or ends its turn there, the creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. It takes 7d8 slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> The wall can be damaged and thus removed by normal means, but it is immune to poison and psychic damage.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.
