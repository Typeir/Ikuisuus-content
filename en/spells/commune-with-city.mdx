# Commune with City (UA)

You briefly become one with a city, attuning your senses to its streets, buildings, and inhabitants.

---

> **Commune with City (UA)**
> _5th-level Divination T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute R
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You briefly become one with a city, attuning your senses to its streets, buildings, and inhabitants. For the duration, you gain knowledge of the city’s layout, the location of significant sites, and the general state of affairs. You can sense the presence of powerful creatures, ongoing magical effects, and recent major events. The spell does not reveal the identities of individuals or provide detailed information about secret or hidden locations, but it gives you a general sense of the city’s mood and any notable occurrences within the past 24 hours.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the duration increases by 1 minute for each slot level above 5th.
