# Foresight

The Canvas is written a little ahead of where anyone is standing. For one night, the target is allowed to read their own thread a few seconds early.

---

> **Foresight**  
> _9th-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: until next dawn
>  
> You touch a willing creature and run its thread forward a few seconds along the Canvas, where the writing is already dry. For the duration, the target can’t be surprised and has advantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws. Other creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls against it.  
>  
> This spell immediately ends if you cast it again before its duration ends.
