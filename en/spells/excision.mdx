# Excision

You excise life from the shells of your enemies, leaving but husks behind.

---

> **Excision**  
> _8th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 150 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> a 30-foot-cube centered on a point you choose within range is momentarily engulfed by impenetrable darkness.
> Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw.
> Constructs are unaffected.
> A creature takes 12d8 dark damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Aberrations, Monstrosities and Celestials, take maximum damage from it.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 8th.
