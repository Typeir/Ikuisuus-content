# Starpath  

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, gifted to those who have walked the pale, ashen deserts of the now derelict Plato station, and followed on the now hidden footsteps of the heir of fate.    

> “I saw him run.  
>  Not like a man fleeing, but like something that had forgotten gravity, weight, or consequence.  
>  And for a moment, the world tore itself apart just trying to keep up.”  
>
> — The Demiurge's account of the White Homunculus's pilgrimage.  

---

> **Starpath**  
> _10th-Level Legendary Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: S, M (a dead Homunculus of at least CR 20 or level 15)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>
> You touch a willing creature and imbue them with the strength of the everlasting Light of Creation.
> For the duration of the spell, they move as a comet across the battlefield, body and light indistinguishable.  
> Their base speed for all forms of movement becomes **600 feet**.  
> Until the spell ends, they gain the following effects:  
>
> - they gain **+5 AC**.  
> - they have **advantage** on all melee attack rolls.  
> - All attack rolls made against they are made **at disadvantage**.  
> - they ignore **difficult terrain**, wind, magical impediments, and any effect that would reduce or restrict their movement.  
> - they cannot have disadvantage imposed on any roll by any effect.  
> - their melee weapon attacks deal an additional **10 fire damage** on hit.  
>
> For every **100 feet** they move, they take **10 true damage**.  
> For every **action they take**, they take **10 true damage**.   
> You cannot willingly or unwillingly end this spell early, except by dying. You concentrate on this spell even when you would be otherwise incapacitated.
>
> If their hit points are reduced below 1, they are instantly consumed, their body disintegrating into a brief line of fire that vanishes into the sky, ending the spell.
