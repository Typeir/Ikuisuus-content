# Find Familiar

A minor väki, curious about you in particular, agrees to wear the shape of a small animal for a while.

---

> **Find Familiar**  
> _1st-level Conjuration (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Hour  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (10 gp of charcoal, incense, and herbs, consumed in a brass brazier)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You gain the service of a familiar: a minor väki wearing an animal Idea — a bat, cat, crab, frog, hawk, lizard, octopus, owl, rat, raven, snake, spider, or weasel. It has the statistics of the chosen form, though it is a väki rather than a beast.  
>  
> The familiar acts independently of you, but always obeys your commands. In combat it rolls its own initiative and acts on its own turn, but it can't attack. When it drops to 0 hit points, it recedes into its Idea, leaving no form behind; it reappears when you cast this spell again.  
>  
> While the familiar is within 100 feet of you, you can communicate with it telepathically. As a Major Action, you can see through its eyes and hear what it hears until the start of your next turn, gaining the benefits of any special senses it has; during this time you are deaf and blind with regard to your own senses.  
>  
> When you cast a spell with a range of touch, your familiar can deliver the touch as if it had cast the spell, using its reaction if you cast on your turn.  
>  
> As a Major Action, you can temporarily dismiss the familiar into a crease of the world, or dismiss it forever. While tucked away, it cannot act; as a Major Action, you can call it back to any unoccupied space within 30 feet of you. Casting the spell again while a familiar serves you lets you re-shape it into a new form instead of calling a second.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher with a casting time of 1 minute, you conjure one additional temporary familiar for each slot level above 1st. Temporary familiars last 1 hour and then recede.
