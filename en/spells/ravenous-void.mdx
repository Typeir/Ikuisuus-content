# Ravenous Void

A yawning void tears open in the air, swallowing terrain and light as nearby matter collapses.

---

> **Ravenous Void**
> _9th-level Evocation DG_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 1,000 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A sphere of crushing force forms at a point you can see within range and draws all creatures and objects toward it.
>
> A 20-foot-radius sphere of destructive gravitational force forms at a point you can see within range and tugs at creatures and objects that aren’t somehow anchored. The sphere lasts for the spell’s duration, and its area is difficult terrain. Any creature that starts its turn within the sphere must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take 5d10 force damage and be pulled straight toward the center of the sphere, ending in an unoccupied space as close to the center as possible. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pulled.
>
> A creature that enters the sphere for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there must also make the saving throw.
>
> Nonmagical objects that aren’t being worn or carried are likewise pulled toward the sphere’s center.
>
> At the start of your turn, the sphere deals 5d10 force damage to any creature or object inside it, with no saving throw. If the sphere overlaps with a creature or object, the creature or object is pulled into the sphere’s center.
>
> If the sphere is in the area of an antimagic field, the sphere is suppressed for the duration of the antimagic field.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 10th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 9th.
