# Tear the Canvas

Reality tears open around the target, folding nearby matter into a jagged void.

---

> **Tear the Canvas**  
> _8th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You open a seam in the Canvas in front of one creature and let it see what is written on the other side: the Clone Worlds, running in parallel, none of them needing it. Choose one creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take [% 5d12 force %] and suffer a random effect from the Reality Break Effects table at the start of each of its turns for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw, ending the spell on itself on a success.
>
> **Tear The Canvas Effects**
>
> | d10  | Effect                                                                                                                                                |
> | ---- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
> | 1-2  | **Visions of the Clone Worlds.** The target is **staggered** until the end of its next turn.                                                          |
> | 3-4  | Rending Rift. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take [% 5d12 force %], or half as much damage on a successful save.              |
> | 5-6  | Wormhole. The target is teleported to an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within 30 feet of it.                                       |
> | 7-8  | **Grasp of the Stags.** The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take [% 5d12 frost %], or half as much damage on a successful save. |
> | 9-10 | Mindkilling Truth. The target must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take [% 5d12 psychic %], or half as much damage on a successful save.   |
