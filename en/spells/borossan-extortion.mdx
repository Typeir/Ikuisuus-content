# Borossan Extortion

The spell was popular long before there was a king of borossa. it used to be called yellow whisper for reasons lost to time 



> **Borossan Extortion**  
> _6th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: until dawn
>  
> You whisper a phrase that only one creature of your choice within range can hear.  
>  
> The target must make a **Wisdom saving throw** against your **Spell Save DC**. It automatically succeeds if it **does not share a language with you** or if it **cannot hear you**.  
>  
> On a successful save, the whisper sounds like unintelligible mumbling and has no effect.  
>  
> On a failed save, the creature is **Charmed** by you for the duration, or until you or your allies **attack or damage it**.  
>  
> While charmed in this way, the target believes you know its most mortifying secret. It obeys your commands for fear you will reveal the secret. It won’t risk its life or fight for you, unless it was already inclined to do so.  
>  
> When the spell ends, the creature has **no memory** of why it feared you.
