# Empty Skies

A globe of living blackness swallows light and sight, severing the world from sky and horizon alike.

---

> **Empty Skies**  
> _2nd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, M (bat fur and a drop of pitch or piece of coal)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> Magical darkness spreads from a point you choose within range to fill a 15-foot-radius sphere for the duration. The darkness spreads around corners. A creature with darkvision can't see through this darkness, and mundane light can't illuminate it.  
>  
> If the point you choose is on an object you are holding or one that isn't being worn or carried, the darkness emanates from the object and moves with it. Completely covering the source of the darkness with an opaque object, such as a bowl or a helm, blocks the darkness.  
>  
> If any of this spell's area overlaps with an area of light created by a spell of this spell's level or lower, the spell that created the light is dispelled.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the darkness spreads by an additional 15 feet for each slot level above 2nd. When you cast it using a spell slot of **4th level or higher**, the vault deepens: the darkness fills a 40-foot-radius sphere (plus 15 feet per slot level above 4th), magical light of the slot's level or lower is suppressed within it, and you and a number of creatures of your choice equal to the slot level minus three can see through it normally.
