# Purify Food and Drink

A clean, odorless wave washes over food and water, stripping away visible spoilage and organic taint.

---

> **Purify Food and Drink**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action R  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> All mundane food and drink within a 5-foot-radius sphere centered on a point of your choice within range is purified and rendered free of poison and disease.
