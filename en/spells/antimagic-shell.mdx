# Antimagic Shell

In their study of death and decay, the Revenants have developed a weaving of the arcane that allows them to disperse magical effects around them, a burial of all things arcane.

---

> **Antimagic Shell**  
> _5th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You envelop a willing creature in a volatile shell of counteractive energy that destabilizes hostile magic.
> When the spell is cast, you may choose a number of **temporary magical effects** currently affecting the target equal to your **spellcasting modifier** (minimum 1).  
> Each chosen effect ends immediately.
>
> For each effect ended this way, the target gains **5 temporary hit points**.
>
> While protected by *Antimagic Shell*, the creature gains **advantage on saving throws** against spells and magical effects.
>
> **Unstable Reflection (Once per turn).**  
> Once per turn, when the protected creature is **targeted by a spell or magical effect** (including being included in an area effect), roll **1d6**:
> - **1–3**: The spell functions normally.  
> - **4–6**: The spell **fails to affect** the creature.  
>
> This spell does not suppress **Heirlooms**.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **6th level or higher**, the spell’s duration increases by **1 minute** for each slot level above 5th.
