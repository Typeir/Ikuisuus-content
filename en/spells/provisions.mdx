# Provisions

Fresh provisions and a basin of clean water materialize, steaming as they settle.

---

> **Provisions**  
> _3rd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You create 45 pounds of food and 30 gallons of water on the ground or in containers within range, enough to sustain up to fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is bland but nourishing, and spoils if uneaten after three days. The water is clean and doesn't go bad.
