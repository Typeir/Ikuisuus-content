# Provisions

Fresh provisions and a basin of clean water materialize, steaming as they settle. Every wizard's is different, and every wizard is oddly proud of theirs.

---

> **Provisions**  
> _3rd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You create 45 pounds of food and 30 gallons of water on the ground or in containers within range — enough to sustain fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. It keeps for **ten days** before it spoils, which is what makes it worth carrying rather than merely worth eating. The water is clean and does not go bad at all.  
>  
> The food takes whatever form the caster habitually conjures. It is nourishing without exception and delicious without reliability.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you create **double the food and water** for each slot level above 3rd.
