# Commune with the Land

You parlay with the väki of the place, and they answer in kind — the land in roots and water, the city in crowds and stone.

---

> **Commune with the Land**  
> _5th-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You attune to your surroundings and speak with the väki that dwell in them. Outdoors, you learn of the land within 3 miles; in caves and natural underground, within 300 feet; within a settlement, within 1 mile. The place's own väki answer according to what it is — wild land speaks in soil, leaf, and current; a city speaks in streets, crowds, and the memory of stone.  
>  
> You instantly gain knowledge of up to **three facts** of your choice about the area, drawn from:  
>  
> - terrain, waterways, streets, and structures  
> - prevalent plants, minerals, beasts, peoples, trades, or factions  
> - powerful creatures present — celestials, väkis, the grave-touched, the restless dead  
> - intrusions from other Folds of the world or other Clone Worlds  
>  
> The land answers plainly; it does not interpret. What it tells you is true, but the meaning is yours to make.
