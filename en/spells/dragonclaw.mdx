# Dragonclaw

Talons split the ends of your fingers and toes, and claws sharp as the edge of the world sprout threaten your foes.

---

> **Dragonclaw**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a talon or wing-bone of a dragon)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You grow draconic talons for the duration.
>
> When you take the **Attack action**, you can rake with your talons: make two melee spell attacks, each reaching 15 feet. On a hit, a talon deals [% 3d8 slashing %]. When both talons hit the same creature, the momentum lets you either knock it **prone** or shove it 10 feet away.
> These talons count as natural weapons for the duration of the spell, and whenever you make an attack with them, they count as the **Cast** action being taken, in addition to the **Attack action**.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, each talon's damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for every two slot levels above 3rd.
