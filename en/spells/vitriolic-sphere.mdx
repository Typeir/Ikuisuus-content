# Vitriolic Sphere

A seething sphere of acid coalesces midair, hissing as its corrosive surface eats through whatever it contacts.

---

> **Vitriolic Sphere**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 150 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You point at a location within range, and a glowing 1-foot-diameter sphere of chemical streaks there and explodes in a 20-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes [% 10d4 chemical %] and [% 5d4 chemical %] at the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much initial damage and no damage at the end of its next turn.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the initial damage increases by [% 2d4 %] and the damage at the end of the next turn increases by [% 1d4 %] for each slot level above 4th.
