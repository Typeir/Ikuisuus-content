# Shutdown (UA)

Choose one construct that you can see within range. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it is paralyzed for the duration. This spell has no effect on a construct that is immune to being paralyzed.

---

> **Shutdown (UA)**
> _5th-level Transmutation T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Choose one construct that you can see within range. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it is paralyzed for the duration. This spell has no effect on a construct that is immune to being paralyzed.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can target one additional construct for each slot level above 5th. The constructs must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
