# Comprehend Languages

Chosen tongues sharpen into clear, intelligible syllables and readable script — but only the ones you asked for.

---

> **Comprehend Languages**  
> _1st-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a pinch of soot and salt)  
> **Duration**: 1 hour  
>  
> Choose **two languages you have encountered** — heard spoken aloud, or seen in written form. For the duration, you speak, understand, and read those languages. Reading takes about 1 minute per page.  
>  
> You cannot choose a language you have never encountered, and this spell doesn't decode secret messages, ciphers, or glyphs that aren't part of a written language.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, choose one additional language for each slot level above 1st.
