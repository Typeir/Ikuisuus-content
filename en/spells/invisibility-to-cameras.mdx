# Invisibility To Cameras (UA)

You or a creature you touch becomes invisible to cameras and other nonmagical sensors for the duration. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target’s person.

---

> **Invisibility To Cameras (UA)**
> _3rd-level Illusion T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 10 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You or a creature you touch becomes invisible to cameras and other nonmagical sensors for the duration. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target’s person.
