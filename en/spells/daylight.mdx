> **Daylight**
> _3rd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> A 60-foot-radius sphere of light spreads out from a point you choose within range. The sphere is bright light and sheds dim light for an additional 60 feet.
>
> The light spreads around corners. If you choose a point on an object you are holding or one that isn’t being worn or carried, the light emanates from the object and moves with it. Completely covering the source of the light with an opaque object, such as a bowl or a helm, blocks the light.
>
> If any of this spell’s area overlaps with an area of darkness created by a spell of 3rd level or lower, the spell that created the darkness is dispelled.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the radius of the light increases by 60 feet for each slot level above 3rd.
