# Wish

You speak, and the Fold answers with any working ever devised — borrowed whole, formula and all.

---

> **Wish**  
> _9th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> By speaking aloud, you cast any other spell of **8th level or lower, regardless of spell list**. You don't need to meet any requirements in that spell, including costly components. The spell simply takes effect.  
>  
> Wish reaches no further than this. The workings that rewrite reality itself belong to [_True Wish_](/en/library/spells/true-wish) — and to those willing to pay what the Canvas charges for them.
