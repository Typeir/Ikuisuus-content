# Spirit Of Death

A cold, hollow wisp tears free from the target and coalesces into a pale, drifting skull of shadow.

---

> **Spirit Of Death**
> _4th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You call forth a spirit of death to serve you.
>
> You call forth a spirit of death in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The spirit is a Medium undead. It has AC 14, 45 hit points, and immunity to necrotic, poison, and psychic damage. It is immune to the charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, and poisoned conditions. The spirit has a flying speed of 40 feet and can hover. It can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
>
> The spirit obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> The spirit makes a melee spell attack against a creature within 5 feet of it. On a hit, the target takes 3d8 necrotic damage, and the spirit regains hit points equal to half the amount of necrotic damage dealt. The spirit’s attack bonus is your spell attack bonus.
>
> The spirit disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the spirit’s hit points increase by 10 for each slot level above 4th.
