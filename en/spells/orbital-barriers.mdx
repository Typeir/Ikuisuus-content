# Orbital Barriers

The seven bodies of the sky are called down and stood on edge, in order, each one as unwilling to be crossed as the last.

---

> **Orbital Barriers**  
> _9th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 10 minutes
>
> A vertical opaque wall of stacked light forms — up to 90 feet long, 30 feet high, and 1 inch thick — centered on a point you can see within range. The wall is immobile, blocks line of sight, and remains for the duration. If you position it so that it passes through a creature's space, the spell fails and the slot is wasted. Where its area overlaps a [Perfect Barrier](/en/library/spells/perfect-barrier) or a similar effect, only the stronger applies.
>
> The wall has seven layers, ordered outward from Kultharja. Each layer harms creatures that pass through it, and each is undone by one specific kind of damage — dealt to the layer directly, from either side. Only the outermost surviving layer can be destroyed at a time; the rest are shielded behind it.
>
> **Kultharja.** Pure brilliance. [% 10d6 holy %], Wisdom save for half. Destroyed by 25 **dark** damage.
>
> **Urmela.** Blood. [% 10d6 fire %], Dexterity save for half. Destroyed by 25 **frost** damage.
>
> **Selkara.** A thin sheet of sharpened bone. [% 10d6 piercing %], Dexterity save for half. Destroyed by 25 **bludgeoning** damage.
>
> **Kalmora.** Noxious mist. [% 10d6 poison %], Constitution save for half. Destroyed by 25 **chemical** damage.
>
> **Damocles.** A mirage inhabited by every soul the world is carrying. [% 10d6 psychic %], Wisdom save for half. Destroyed by 25 **force** damage.
>
> **Itähenki and Länsihenki.** Wind and lightning. [% 10d6 lightning %], Dexterity save for half. Destroyed by 25 **slashing** damage.
>
> **Mana.** Floating crystals of ice. [% 10d6 frost %], Constitution save for half. Destroyed by 25 **fire** damage.
>
> All saving throws against the wall use your spell save DC.
>
> **Passing Through.** A creature crossing the wall makes the saving throw for each surviving layer in turn, starting from the side it entered. On a success it takes half damage and continues to the next layer. On a failure it takes full damage and is stopped — it does not cross, and it does not reach the layers beyond.
>
> **Destroying the Wall.** A destroyed layer has no further effect. When the last layer is destroyed, the wall is gone.
