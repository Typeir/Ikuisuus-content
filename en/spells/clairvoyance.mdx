# Clairvoyance

A distant occulus, channeling sight and faint sound inward.

---

> **Clairvoyance**  
> _3rd-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes  
> **Range**: 1 mile  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a focus worth at least 100 gp, either a jeweled horn for hearing or a glass eye for seeing)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 30 minutes
>
> You create an invisible sensor within range in a location familiar to you (a place you have visited or seen before) or in an obvious location that is unfamiliar to you (such as behind a door, around a corner, or in a grove of trees).
>
> When you cast the spell, you choose seeing or hearing. You can use the chosen sense through the sensor as if you were in its space. As a **Major Action**, you can switch between seeing and hearing.
>
> A creature that can see the sensor — one with truesight, or otherwise able to see the invisible — sees a luminous, intangible orb about the size of your fist.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you place **one additional sensor** for every two slot levels above 3rd. You perceive through one at a time and can switch between them as a Minor Action.
