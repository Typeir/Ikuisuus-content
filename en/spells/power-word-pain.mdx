# Power Word: Pain

A jagged current of force tears through the target, erupting their nerves into sudden, crushing pain.

---

> **Power Word: Pain**
> _7th-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
>
> You speak a word of power that causes waves of intense pain to assail one creature you can see within range. If the target has 100 hit points or fewer, it is subject to crippling pain. Otherwise, the spell has no effect on it.
>
> The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be incapacitated with excruciating pain for the duration. A target that has 101 hit points or more is unaffected.
>
> At the end of each of its turns, and each time it takes damage, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the spell ends for it.
