# Polymorph

Flesh remembers older shapes, and for a while, it wears one.

---

> **Polymorph**  
> _4th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a caterpillar cocoon)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You transform a creature that you can see within range into a beast form. An unwilling creature must make a Wisdom saving throw to avoid the effect. A shapechanger automatically succeeds.  
>  
> The new form can be any beast whose challenge rating is equal to or less than the target's (or the target's level, if it doesn't have a challenge rating). The target's game statistics are replaced by the statistics of the chosen beast, but it retains its alignment and personality.  
>  
> The target assumes the hit points of its new form. When it reverts, it returns to the hit points it had before it transformed; if it reverts from dropping to 0 hit points, any excess damage carries over to its normal form. The creature is limited in the actions it can perform by the nature of its new form, and it can't speak, cast spells, or take any other action that requires hands or speech. The target's gear melds into the new form and cannot be used.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th.  
> - **8th level or higher (Herd):** any number of willing creatures within 30 feet of a point in range instead take beast forms of challenge rating 1 or lower. The duration becomes 24 hours without concentration, and an affected creature can change its beast form as a Major Action.
