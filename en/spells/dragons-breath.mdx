# Dragon's Breath

A ragged cone of searing flame erupts from the spell's origin, roaring outward in a scalding wave.

---

> **Dragon's Breath**
> _2nd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M (a hot pepper)
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> You touch one willing creature and imbue it with the power to spew magical energy from its mouth, provided it has one.
>
> Choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison. Until the spell ends, the target can use an action to exhale energy of the chosen type in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d6 damage of the chosen type on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
