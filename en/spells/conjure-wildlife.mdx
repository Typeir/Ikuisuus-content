# Conjure Wildlife

You do not whistle and wait. You offer something, and what answers wears fur, or bark, or chitin, because that is the shape it agreed to wear.

---

> **Conjure Wildlife**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a handful of fur, seed, or gristle, offered and not returned)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You strike a bargain, and living things step into unoccupied spaces you can see within range — beast or growth, tooth or thorn; not creatures fetched from elsewhere, but **Väki stuff wearing a living shape** for as long as the bargain holds. It is a bargain, never a command.  
>  
> Their combined Challenge Rating may not exceed **twice this spell's level**. See [Summoning and Conjuration](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/summoning-and-conjuration) for the Challenge Budget, how a multitude resolves as a Swarm or Formation, command and control, and what becomes of the called when your concentration breaks.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** The Challenge Budget is always twice the level of the slot spent — a 5th-level casting calls CR 10 of wildlife.
