# Passwall

A passage appears at a point of your choice that you can see on a wooden, plaster, or stone surface (such as a wall, a ceiling, or a floor) within range and lasts for the duration.

---

> **Passwall**
> _5th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> You touch a surface and create a passage through it. The passage is 5 feet wide, 8 feet tall, and up to 20 feet deep. The passage creates no instability in a structure surrounding it. When the opening disappears, any creatures or objects still in the passage created by the spell are safely ejected to an unoccupied space nearest to the surface on which you cast the spell.
