# Lifetap

A brutal exchange of vitality, siphoning strength from a foe and converting it into raw momentum.

---

> **Lifetap**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Melee  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>
> You strike a creature within range, tearing vitality from its body.  
> Make a **melee spell attack** against the target.
>
> On a hit, the target takes **3d4 damage**, and you regain **hit points equal to the damage dealt**.
>
> Until the end of your next turn, you gain a **bonus to the next attack roll or skill check you make** equal to **half the damage dealt** by this spell, rounded down.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **2nd level or higher**, the damage increases by **1d4** per slot level above 1st.
