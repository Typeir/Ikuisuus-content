# Abominable Grasp

A spell that warps the caster’s reach into something unnatural, dragging foes closer with every successful strike.

---

> **Abominable Grasp**  
> _4th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>
> For the duration, your melee attacks gain an unnatural extension.
>
> Your **melee reach increases by 20 feet**.
>
> When you **hit** a creature with a melee attack, you may pull that creature **up to 10 feet** straight toward you.
>
> In addition, your melee attacks deal an extra **2d6 necrotic damage** on a hit.
>
> A creature pulled by this spell must end the movement in an unoccupied space and cannot be pulled through solid objects or other creatures.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **5th level or higher**, the extra necrotic damage increases by **1d6** per slot level above 4th.
