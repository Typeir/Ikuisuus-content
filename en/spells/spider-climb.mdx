# Spider Climb

Fine adhesive hairs sprout across the skin, clinging to stone and timber and allowing movement along walls and ceilings.

---

> **Spider Climb**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch gains the ability to move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving its hands free. The target also gains a climbing speed equal to its walking speed.  
> 
> ****At Higher Levels.**.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.
