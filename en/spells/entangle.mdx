# Entangle

Knotted roots and thorned vines burst from the earth, wrapping around anything in their path.

---

> **Entangle**  
> _1st-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> Grasping weeds and vines sprout from the ground in a 20-foot square starting from a point within range. For the duration, these plants turn the ground in the area into difficult terrain.  
>  
> A creature in the area when you cast the spell must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be restrained by the entangling plants until the spell ends. A creature restrained by the plants can use a Major Action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC. On a success, it frees itself.  
>  
> When the spell ends, the conjured plants wilt away.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - **2nd level or higher (Lattice):** the growth thickens into a clinging web of fiber and thorn. The duration becomes Concentration, up to 1 hour, the area is lightly obscured, and any creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must also make the Strength saving throw. The lattice is flammable: any 5-foot cube exposed to fire burns away in 1 round, dealing [% 2d4 fire %] to any creature that starts its turn in the fire. Each slot level above 2nd adds one additional 20-foot square of growth.  
> - **4th level or higher (Crush):** the growth constricts — a creature restrained by the plants takes [% 3d6 bludgeoning %] at the start of each of its turns.
