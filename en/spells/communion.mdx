# Communion

You open yourself to one domain of the living world and it passes you its impressions — not in words, for it has none, but in urges, images, and the chemistry of intent.

---

> **Communion**  
> _1st-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 30 minutes  
>  
> Choose a domain when you cast this spell. For the duration, you can commune with it:  
>  
> **Beasts.** You comprehend the beasts around you and can press impressions and urges upon them in return. Beasts do not speak and are not made clever by this — they pass you what they perceive or perceived within the past day: nearby locations, dangers, and creatures, as far as their dim awareness reaches. You might persuade a beast to a small favor, at the Game Director's discretion.  
>  
> **Flora.** You read the chemical impulses of the growing network around you — root, spore, and sap. A connected stand of plants tells you what has passed through it, and a clonal grove that runs for kilometers will tell you of everything that has walked its ground.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the duration doubles for each slot level above 1st.
