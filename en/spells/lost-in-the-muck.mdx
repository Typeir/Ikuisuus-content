# Lost in the Muck

Push a creature into the muck dimension between folds

---

> **Lost in the Muck**  
> _8th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> You banish a creature that you can see within range into a near empty dimension of grime. The target remains there for the duration or until it finds its way back.  
>  
> The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be banished. At the end of each of its turns, it can make a DC 20 cartography check. If it succeeds, it escapes, and the spell ends.  
>  
> When the spell ends, the target reappears in the space it left or, if that space is occupied, in the nearest unoccupied space.
