# Banishment

A rift opens onto brine and pressure, and the target is dragged down into it.

---

> **Banishment**  
> _4th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (an item distasteful to the target)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You try to cast a creature you can see within range out of the world. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be banished into the **Muck Dimension** — a rot-brine microchamber folded into reality, a copy of the drowned layer beneath Plato Station. There it is incapacitated, clawing through pressure and grime, until the spell ends, at which point it reappears in the space it left or the nearest unoccupied one.  
>  
> If the target is native to a different Clone World, it is instead flung back there through the Folds. If the spell lasts the full minute, that return is permanent — it does not come back.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th (the targets must be within 30 feet of each other).  
> - **9th level (Sealed):** a single target that fails the save does not return when the spell ends. The Muck closes over it, and only it can find its own way back, if it ever does.
