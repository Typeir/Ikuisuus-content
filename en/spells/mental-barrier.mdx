> **Mental Barrier (UA)**
> _2nd-level Abjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Reaction
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
> You weave a temporary shield of psychic energy around your mind, granting you protection from mental assaults.
>
> When you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make an Intelligence, a Wisdom, or a Charisma saving throw to take only half damage, you can use your reaction to take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, interposing your mental barrier between yourself and the effect.
