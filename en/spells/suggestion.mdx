# Suggestion

The words settle into the target's mind as though they had always been its own idea.

---

> **Suggestion**  
> _2nd-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, M (a snake's tongue and either a bit of honeycomb or a drop of sweet oil)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 8 hours  
>  
> You suggest a course of activity — a sentence or two — to a creature you can see within range that can hear and understand you. The suggestion must be worded to sound reasonable; asking the creature to obviously harm itself ends the spell.  
>  
> The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it pursues the course of activity you described as best it can, until the activity is completed or the spell ends. A suggestion works within the target's [disposition](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/disposition-reputation-and-attitude): no wording, however reasonable, carries a creature across its will-not line.  
>  
> If you or your companions damage the target, the spell ends.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.  
> - **6th level or higher:** up to twelve targets, the duration becomes 24 hours, and the spell no longer requires concentration.  
> - **7th level or higher:** the duration becomes 10 days.
