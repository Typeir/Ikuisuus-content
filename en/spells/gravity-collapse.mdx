# Gravity Collapse

For an instant, "down" points wherever you decide, and everything nearby obeys.

---

> **Gravity Collapse**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a black marble)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A well of crushing force opens in a **20-foot-radius sphere** centered on a point you can see within range. Each creature in the area must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes [% 5d10 force %] and is pulled in a straight line toward the center, ending in the nearest unoccupied space to it. On a success, half as much damage and no pull.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 4th.  
> - **6th level or higher (Fissure):** the collapse instead opens along a **100-foot line, 5 feet wide,** emanating from you. Each creature in the line makes a Strength saving throw against [% 8d8 force %], pulled up to 30 feet toward you on a failure; the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 6th.
