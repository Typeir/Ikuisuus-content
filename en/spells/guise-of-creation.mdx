# Guise of Creation

A pale, plated veneer crawls over the caster as flesh tightens and features harden into a cold, nonhuman mask.

---

> **Guise of Creation**  
> _6th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Uttering an incantation, you call upon cosmic magic to transform yourself, taking on aspects of one of the dragon's eyes  
>  
> You transform until the spell ends. 
> - You have a flying speed of 40 feet and can hover.  
> - You can attack twice, instead of once, when you take the Attack action on your turn.  
> - You can use your bonus action to make one weapon attack.  
> - Your weapon attacks use your spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls.  
>  
>  Choose one of the following options:  
>  
> **Päivätär**: You gain the following benefits:  
> - You are immune to fire and radiant damage.  
> - You are immune to the frightened, burning and terrified conditions.  
>  
> **Kuutar**: You gain the following benefits:  
> - You are immune to cold and necrotic damage.  
> - You are immune to the charmed, bleeding and poisoned conditions.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the duration increases by 5 minutes for each slot level above 6th.
