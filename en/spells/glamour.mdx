# Glamour

The arcane dazzles and charms those you interact with; you can make yourself more appealing and persuasive, but at a cost.

---

> **Glamour**  
> _Enchantment cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> For the duration, you have advantage on all Charisma checks directed at one creature of your choice that isn’t hostile toward you. When the spell ends, the creature realizes that you used magic to influence its mood and moves in the reputational track to the hostile range toward you.
> A creature prone to violence might attack you. Another creature might seek retribution in other ways (at the DM’s discretion), depending on the nature of your interaction with it.
