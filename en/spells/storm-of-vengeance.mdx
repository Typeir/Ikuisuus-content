# Storm of Vengeance

A vast storm condenses overhead, hammering the ground with spear-like lightning and shredding the terrain with savage winds.

---

> **Storm of Vengeance**  
> _9th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Sight  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> A churning storm cloud forms, centered on a point you can see and spreading to a radius of 360 feet. When it appears, and at the start of each of your turns thereafter, the storm advances one **tier**. Every tier it has reached applies its effect each round — the tiers **stack**, and the storm only worsens. A creature is "under the cloud" if it is beneath the cloud and no more than 5,000 feet below it.  
>  
> **Tier 1 — Thunderhead.** Each creature under the cloud must make a Constitution saving throw, taking [% 2d6 bludgeoning %] and becoming deafened for 5 minutes on a failure, or half as much and no deafness on a success.  
>  
> **Tier 2 — Caustic Rain.** Each creature and object under the cloud takes [% 1d6 chemical %].  
>  
> **Tier 3 — Spears of the Sky.** Six bolts of lightning strike six creatures or objects of your choice under the cloud. Each target makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 10d6 lightning %] on a failure, or half as much on a success.  
>  
> **Tier 4 — Hailfall.** Each creature under the cloud takes [% 2d6 bludgeoning %] from driving hail.  
>  
> **Tier 5 — Killing Gale.** The area becomes difficult terrain and is heavily obscured, ranged weapon attacks within it are impossible, and open flames are snuffed. Each creature under the cloud takes [% 1d6 frost %]. Once the storm reaches this tier it remains here, applying all five tiers each round for as long as you concentrate.
