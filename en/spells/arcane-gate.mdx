# Arcane Gate

You create linked teleportation portals that remain open for the duration.

---

> **Arcane Gate**
> _6th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 500 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You create two linked portals, each 10 feet in diameter, at locations you can see, one within 10 feet of you and one within 500 feet of you. A portal is a circular opening, which you can orient in any direction you choose. The portal remains open for the duration.
>
> If the portal would open in the space occupied by a creature, the spell fails, and the casting is lost.
>
> Any creature or object entering one portal exits from the other as if the two were adjacent to each other, passing through the same orientation. For example, if a creature enters the portal facing north, it exits from the other portal facing north. Passing through a portal counts as 5 feet of movement, regardless of the distance between the portals.
>
> A creature can only pass through a portal once per turn. If a creature tries to pass through a portal more than once on a turn, it is blocked by the portal and can't pass through.
