# Black Quill Of Oblivion

Ten feet of borrowed edge, hanging in the air with nothing holding it up. The God of Ambition's blade is a great deal longer than this. You are permitted the sliver.

---

> **Black Quill Of Oblivion**  
> _9th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You draw a sliver of the Dreamcatcher's edge into an unoccupied space you can see within range: a blade about ten feet long, an echo of True Severance and about as forgiving. It lasts for the duration.
>
> When you cast the spell, you can make up to two melee spell attacks with the blade, each against a creature or object within 5 feet of it. On a hit the target takes [% 4d12 true %]. On a critical hit it takes an extra [% 8d12 true %], for a total of [% 12d12 true %].
>
> As a **Minor Action** on your turn, you can move the blade up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see and repeat the attacks with it.
>
> The blade passes through matter as though it were not there — walls, doors, armour, the ground. It does not pass through a [Perfect Barrier](/en/library/spells/perfect-barrier), and it will not cut another severance.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 10th level or higher, the strike damage increases by [% 1d12 %] and the critical damage by [% 2d12 %] for each slot level above 9th.
