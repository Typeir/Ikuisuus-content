# Primal Savagery

You channel primal magic to cause your teeth or fingernails to sharpen, ready to deliver a corrosive attack.

---

> **Primal Savagery**
> _Transmutation cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You make a melee spell attack against one creature within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 acid damage. After you make the attack, your teeth or fingernails return to normal.
>
> The spell’s damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).
