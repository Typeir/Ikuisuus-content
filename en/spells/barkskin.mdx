# Barkskin

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 16, regardless of what its AC would otherwise be.

---

> **Barkskin**
> _2nd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 16, regardless of what its AC would otherwise be.
