# Barkskin

The target's skin hardens into fissured, bark-like plates.

---

> **Barkskin**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 hour  
>  
>  You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target’s skin assumes a bark-like appearance, and the target has an Armor Class of 17 if its AC is lower than that.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the Armor Class it sets increases by 1 for each slot level above 2nd.
