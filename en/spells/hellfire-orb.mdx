# Hellfire Orb

This spell is not innate to any vocation, but rather, granted as a boon or a blessing by a **Heirloom** or a **higher power**.
This is usually granted to those who heed the calls of the roaring black flames of the Everdark.

> “What you call fire is only the shadow of a greater flame, sealed beyond the Opaline Belt and kept from mortal hands.”

---

> **Hellfire Orb**  
> _7th-Level Legendary Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You conjure a dense orb of Everdark flame and hurl it at a point you can see.
>
> Each creature in a **20-foot-radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **Dexterity saving throw**. On a failed save, a creature takes **10d6 fire damage** and **10d6 necrotic damage**. On a success, the creature takes half as much of each type.
>
> **Collateral**: Nonmagical, flammable objects in the area are scorched to ash; unsecured flammables ignite.
>
> **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, both damage types increase by **1d6** for each slot level above 7th.
