# Destructive Wave

A concussive shock erupts from you, vaulting debris and cracking stone as a pressure wave tears outward.

---

> **Destructive Wave**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot radius)  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You strike the ground and it ruptures — a hard concussive front that vaults debris and cracks stone as it goes outward. Each creature you choose within 30 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take [% 5d6 bludgeoning %] plus [% 5d6 holy %] or dark damage (your choice on casting), and be knocked prone. On a success, half as much damage and no prone.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, **both damage rolls increase by [% 1d6 %]** for each slot level above 5th, and the radius increases by 5 feet.
