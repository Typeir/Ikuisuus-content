# Conjure Barrage

A thrown weapon multiplies into a wall of identical steel that sweeps forward and vanishes.

---

> **Conjure Barrage**  
> _3rd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (60-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (one piece of ammunition or a thrown weapon)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You throw a mundane weapon or fire a piece of mundane ammunition into the air to create a barrage of identical weapons that sweep forward and then disappear. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take [% 3d8 %] damage of the same type as the weapon or ammunition you used.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 3rd.  
> - **5th level or higher (Volley):** you may instead call the barrage down as rain — a 40-foot-radius, 20-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within 150 feet — dealing [% 8d8 %] of the weapon's type (Dexterity saving throw for half), +1d8 for each slot level above 5th.
