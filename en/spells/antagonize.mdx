# Antagonize

You attempt to goad one creature of your choice that you can see within range into attacking you. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you until the end of your next turn.

---

> **Antagonize**
> _3rd-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You attempt to goad one creature of your choice that you can see within range into attacking you. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target has disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you until the end of your next turn.
