# Festering Edge

A spell that wraps a weapon in a malignant hex, ensuring that every wound it carves refuses to close.

---

> **Festering Edge**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You touch a weapon, infusing it with a virulent blood curse. For the duration, the weapon becomes wreathed festering red hues.
>
> When a creature is **hit** by an attack made with the affected weapon, it gains **1 stack of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding)**.
