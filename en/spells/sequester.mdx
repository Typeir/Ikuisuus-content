# Sequester

By means of this spell, a willing creature or an object can be hidden away, safe from detection for the duration.

---

> **Sequester**
> _7th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Until dispelled
>
> When you cast the spell and touch a willing creature or an object no larger than 10 feet in any dimension, the target is rendered invisible, and it can't be targeted by divination spells or perceived through scrying sensors created by divination spells.
>
> If the target is a creature, it falls into a state of suspended animation. Time ceases to flow for it, and it doesn't grow older.
>
> You can set a condition for the spell to end early. The condition can be anything you choose, but it must be based on a sensory trigger, such as "after 1,000 years" or "when the tarrasque awakens." The spell also ends if the target takes any damage.
