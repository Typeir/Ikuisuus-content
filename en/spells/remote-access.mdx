# Remote Access

The mechanism does not require your hands, only your intent. Distance collapses between your will and whatever part of the device moves.

---

> **Remote Access**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 10 minutes  
>  
> Choose a mechanism, lock, or tombsteel contrivance within range that is not being worn or carried. You manipulate it at a distance as though with your hands: opening or closing doors, drawers, and containers; throwing levers and pressing catches; retrieving a loose object from an open container. You cannot pick locks, disarm traps, or activate heirlooms. The spell ends if you move more than 120 feet from the target.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - **4th level or higher (Backdoor):** with a 1-minute casting time, you instead worm a lasting connection into a complex tinker-system or ancient Empyrean mechanism you touch, bypassing its safeguards. For 1 hour (Concentration), you issue commands to it from any distance as though present. The duration increases by 1 hour for each slot level above 4th.
