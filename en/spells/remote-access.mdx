# Remote Access (UA)

You reach out with your mind to manipulate an object at a distance.

---

> **Remote Access (UA)**
> _1st-level Transmutation T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 10 minutes
>
> Choose a nonmagical object within range that is not being worn or carried. You can manipulate the object as if you were using your hands, but at a distance. You can open or close doors, drawers, or containers; press buttons; or retrieve an object from an open container. You can’t use this spell to activate magic items or perform complex tasks, such as picking locks or disarming traps. The spell ends if you move more than 120 feet away from the object.
