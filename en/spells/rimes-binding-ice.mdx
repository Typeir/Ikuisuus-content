# Rime's Binding Ice

A burst of cold energy emanates from you, forming a 30-foot cone.

---

> **Rime's Binding Ice**
> _2nd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot cone)  
> **Components**: S, M (a vial of meltwater)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 3d8 cold damage and has its speed reduced to 0 until the start of your next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and suffers no reduction to its speed.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the cold damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
