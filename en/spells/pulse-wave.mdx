# Pulse Wave

You create intense pressure, unleashing it in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 6d6 force damage and is pushed 15 feet away from you. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed.

---

> **Pulse Wave**
> _3rd-level Evocation D_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self (30-foot cone)
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You create intense pressure, unleashing it in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 6d6 force damage and is pushed 15 feet away from you. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
