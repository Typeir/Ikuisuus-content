# Frost Fingers

Needlelike ice shards lance from your fingertips, spearing outward in a spray that freezes and shatters whatever they strike.

---

> **Frost Fingers**
> _1st-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self (15-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
>
> Thin and blue, a sheet of ice erupts from your outstretched fingers.
>
> A blast of cold air erupts from your fingers. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 2d8 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The cold freezes nonmagical liquids in the area that aren’t being worn or carried.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
