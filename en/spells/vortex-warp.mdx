# Vortex Warp

You fold the space a creature stands in, and it is standing somewhere else — a corner conducting it out and away.

---

> **Vortex Warp**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You fold the space around another creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be teleported to an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within range. The chosen space must be on a surface or in a liquid that can support the target without squeezing.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** The reach and the count grow with the slot:  
>  
> | Slot Level | Targets            | Range   |
> | ---------- | ------------------ | ------- |
> | 2nd        | 1                  | 90 ft.  |
> | 3rd        | 2                  | 120 ft. |
> | 4th        | 3                  | 150 ft. |
> | 5th        | 4                  | 200 ft. |
> | 6th        | 5                  | 300 ft. |
>  
> All chosen creatures are warped at once; each unwilling creature saves separately. The destination spaces must all be within the spell's range and visible to you.
