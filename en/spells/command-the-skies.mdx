# Command the Skies

You hark upon the sky-väkis, and they briefly listen. They are not obedient so much as agreeable, and only for as long as the arrangement amuses them.

---

> **Command the Skies**  
> _4th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 300 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a whistle carved from hollow bone)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You take control of the air in a 100-foot cube you can see within range. Choose one of the following effects when you cast the spell. It lasts for the duration unless you use a **Major Action** on a later turn to switch to a different one. Only one effect can be active at a time.
>
> - **Gusts.** A wind picks up within the cube, blowing continuously in a horizontal direction you choose, at an intensity you choose: calm, moderate, or strong. While it is moderate or strong, ranged weapon attacks that pass through the cube or are made against targets inside it have disadvantage. While it is strong, a creature moving against the wind spends 1 extra foot of movement for each foot moved.
> - **Downdraft.** A sustained blast of wind blows downward from the top of the cube. Ranged weapon attacks that pass through the cube or are made against targets inside it have disadvantage. A creature that flies into the cube for the first time on a turn, or starts its turn flying there, must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be knocked **prone**.
> - **Updraft.** A sustained updraft rises from the cube's bottom edge. A creature that ends a fall within the cube takes only half the falling damage, and a creature making a vertical jump inside it can jump up to 10 feet higher than normal.
> - **Whirlwind.** A vortex forms in the cube: 10 feet wide at its base, up to 50 feet wide at its top, and as tall as the cube. Every unsecured object of Medium size or smaller within it that is not worn or carried is drawn up, carried spiralling for a round, and flung outward to land [% 2d6 %] × 10 feet from the vortex in a random direction. A creature that enters the vortex for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be lifted 20 feet and dropped, taking falling damage as normal.
