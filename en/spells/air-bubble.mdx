# Air Bubble

A clear globe of air materializes around the target, a small borrowed sky in a place that offers none.

---

> **Air Bubble**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: 24 hours  
>  
> You create a bubble of breathable air around a willing creature you can see within range. The bubble moves with the creature and allows it to breathe normally in an environment where air is otherwise unavailable — underwater, in choking smoke, or in vacuum.  
>  
> The bubble lasts for the duration or until you dismiss it as a Major Action. A creature can only be affected by one air bubble at a time.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target **two additional willing creatures** for each slot level above 2nd.
