# Air Bubble

A clear globe of air materializes around the target, isolating them from the surrounding atmosphere.

---

> **Air Bubble**
> _2nd-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 Feet
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
>
> A bubble of breathable air surrounds a creature of your choice within range, allowing it to breathe normally in an environment where air is otherwise unavailable.
>
> You create a bubble of breathable air around a willing creature you can see within range. The bubble moves with the creature and allows it to breathe normally in an environment where air is otherwise unavailable, such as underwater or in a vacuum. The bubble lasts for the duration or until you dismiss it as an action. A creature can only be affected by one air bubble at a time. If you cast this spell again, the effect of the previous casting ends.
