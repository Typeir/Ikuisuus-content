# Continuance

Tissue and bone coalesce inside the sealed vessel, knitting into a lifelike duplicate that stirs with faint pulses.

---

> **Continuance**  
> _8th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Hour  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 1,000 gp and at least 1 cubic inch of flesh of the creature that is to be cloned, which the spell consumes; a vessel worth at least 2,000 gp that has a sealable lid and is large enough to hold a Medium creature, such as a large urn, coffin, mud-filled cyst in the ground, or crystal container filled with salt water)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> This spell grows a **spare vessel** for a living creature's identity, against the day it needs one. The body forms inside a sealed container and reaches full size and maturity after 120 days. It remains inert and keeps indefinitely, so long as the container is not disturbed.
>
> At any time after the vessel's occupant matures, if the original creature dies, **its identity moves across** — provided nothing is holding that identity where it fell. What arrives is physically identical, with the same personality, memories and abilities, and none of the original's equipment.
>
> The original's remains, if any survive, go inert. They cannot afterward be restored to life, because the thing that made them a person is not in them any more. It is over there, in the urn, opening its eyes.
