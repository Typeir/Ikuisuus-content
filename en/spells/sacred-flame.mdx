# Sacred Flame

A shaft of searing flame strikes the target, scorching flesh and blackening nearby shadow.

---

> **Sacred Flame**
> _Evocation cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
>
> A flame-like radiance descends on a creature that you can see within range.
>
> The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d8 radiant damage. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw.
>
> The spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
