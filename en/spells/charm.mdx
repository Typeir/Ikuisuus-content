# Charm

A warmth spreads through the target, their muscles loosening and their gaze fixing with unnatural trust.

---

> **Charm**  
> _1st-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 hour  
>  
> You attempt to charm a creature you can see within range whose type the slot you cast this spell with can reach (see the table below). It must make a Wisdom saving throw, and does so with advantage if you or your companions are fighting it. On a failed save, it is [charmed](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#charmed) by you until the spell ends or until you or your companions do anything harmful to it. While charmed, its [disposition](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/disposition-reputation-and-attitude) toward you is locked in the Friendly range; the charmed condition does not move its true disposition, and when the spell ends the creature knows it was charmed by you.  
>  
> | Slot Level | Creature types you can target                |
> | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
> | 1st        | Humanoid                                     |
> | 2nd        | + Wildlife                               |
> | 3rd        | + Monstrosity, Construct                     |
> | 4th        | + Väki, Aberration                      |
> | 5th        | + Celestial                                  |
>  
> Each row includes the rows above it.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** For each slot level above the minimum required for your chosen target's type, you can target one additional valid creature. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
