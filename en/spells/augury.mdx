# Augury

Two pale sigils bloom above your hand, pulsing in opposing rhythms before collapsing into a single steady ember.

---

> **Augury**  
> _2nd-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (gem-inlaid sticks, dragon bones, ornate cards, or another divining tool)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You cast the sticks, roll the bones, or lay out the cards, and something answers: your god, a Hiisi, or a faith of yours that may or may not be true. You receive an omen about a specific course of action you plan to take within the next 30 minutes. One of four:
>
> - **Weal**, for good results
> - **Woe**, for bad results
> - **Weal and woe**, for both
> - **Nothing**, for results that are neither especially good nor especially bad
>
> The omen accounts for nothing that has not happened yet — a spell cast later, a companion gained or lost. It answers the question you asked, not the one you will wish you had asked.
>
> If you cast this spell two or more times before completing your next Recovery, there is a cumulative 25 percent chance for each casting after the first that the reading comes back random instead. That roll is made in secret.
