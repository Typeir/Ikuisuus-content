# Expeditious Retreat

Your limbs blur and your gait lengthens into quick, relentless strides.

---

> **Expeditious Retreat**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
>  
> This spell allows you to move at an incredible pace.  
>  
> When you cast this spell, and then as a Minor Action on each of your turns until the spell ends, you can take the Dash action.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, one additional willing creature within 30 feet of you gains the same benefit for each slot level above 1st, spending its own Minor Action to use it.
