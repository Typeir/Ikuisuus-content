# Ardent Prism

A prism forms from the surrounding luminosity, and it scatters reality into a deadly emanation.

---

> **Ardent Prism**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a bit of tallow, a pinch of brimstone, and a dusting of powdered iron)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> Choose a damage type from a stratum your slot level has unlocked (see the table below). A 5-foot-diameter sphere of that element appears in an unoccupied space of your choice within range and lasts for the duration.
>
> Any creature that ends its turn within 5 feet of the sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 1d10 %] damage of the chosen type on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
>
> As a Minor Action, you can move the sphere up to 30 feet. If you ram the sphere into a creature, that creature must make the saving throw against the sphere’s damage, and the sphere stops moving this turn. When you move the sphere, you can direct it over barriers up to 5 feet tall and jump it across pits up to 10 feet wide. The sphere ignites flammable objects not being worn or carried, and it sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet.
>
> | Slot Level | Strata unlocked                       |
> | ---------- | ------------------------------------- |
> | 2nd        | Elemental (frost, fire, lightning)    |
> | 4th        | + Somatic (poison, chemical, psychic) |
> | 8th        | + Akashic (holy, dark, force)         |
> | 12th       | + True                                |
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
