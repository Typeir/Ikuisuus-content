# Find Vehicle (UA)

A pale filament of light unspools from your hand and lodges at the vehicle's location.

---

> **Find Vehicle (UA)**
> _2nd-level Conjuration T_
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
>
> You summon a spirit that assumes the form of an unusual vehicle. Appearing in an unoccupied space within range, the vehicle takes on a form you choose: a rowboat, a chariot, a wagon, or a similar conveyance. The vehicle has the statistics of a normal vehicle of its kind, though it is celestial, fey, or fiendish (your choice) in origin. The vehicle disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The vehicle has all the normal features of its kind (including wheels, sails, oars, and so on), and it can be ridden, driven, or sailed as appropriate. The vehicle can be dismissed as an action, causing it to disappear. You can also dismiss it as a bonus action if you are within 5 feet of it. If the vehicle disappears, anything riding in it or on it is gently deposited in unoccupied spaces within 5 feet of where the vehicle disappeared.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the vehicle’s hit points increase by 10 for each slot level above 2nd.
