# Find Vehicle

A pale filament of light unspools from your hand and lodges itself in reality; the blood from the wound coagulates into a perfect machine.

---

> **Find Vehicle**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 24 hours
>
> You conjure a flood of Arkhé and let it set. It coalesces in an unoccupied space within range as a vehicle of your choosing, drawn from the table below — anything at or beneath the tier of the slot you spent.
>
> The vehicle has the statistics of an ordinary vehicle of its kind. It is **Arkhé-wrought rather than built**, and counts as an object for any effect that cares about such distinctions. It disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> | Slot Level | What You Can Set                   |
> | ---------- | ---------------------------------- |
> | 2nd        | Rowboat, cart, simple parachute    |
> | 3rd        | Coach carriage, outrigger sailboat |
> | 4th        | Horse-drawn caravan, warkite       |
> | 5th        | Empyrean warbike                   |
> | 6th        | Empyrean syracusia                 |
> | 7th        | Empyrean celestial chariot         |
>
> The vehicle has all the normal features of its kind — wheels, sails, oars, rigging — and can be ridden, driven or sailed as appropriate. You can dismiss it as a **Major Action**, or as a **Minor Action** if you are within 5 feet of it. Anything riding in or on it when it goes is set down gently in unoccupied spaces within 5 feet of where it stood.
