# Hold

Muscles seize and limbs lock, freezing the target in place.

---

> **Hold**  
> _2nd-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a small, straight piece of iron)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> Choose a creature that you can see within range whose type the slot you cast this spell with can reach (see the table below). The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the spell ends on the target.  
>  
> This spell has no effect on the undead. The dead do not answer.  
>  
> | Slot Level | Creature types you can target                |
> | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
> | 2nd        | Humanoid                                     |
> | 3rd        | + Wildlife                               |
> | 4th        | + Monstrosity, Construct                     |
> | 5th        | + Väki, Aberration                      |
> | 6th        | + Celestial                                  |
>  
> Each row includes the rows above it. Celestials are the hardest of all things to stop.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** For each slot level above the minimum required for your chosen target's type, you can target one additional valid creature. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
