# Dragon Whole

You claim an ephemeral throne besides True Terror and Creation, and your body beckons a vile transmogrification; you become fear incarnate.

---

> **Dragon Whole**  
> _7th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a scale, a fang, and a talon, all taken from one dragon)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You take on a dragon's whole shape and bearing for the duration, gaining all of the following.
>
> **Draconic Senses.** You have blindsight out to 30 feet.
>
> **Wings.** You have a flying speed of 60 feet and do not provoke opportunity attacks when you fly out of a creature's reach.
>
> **Fang and Talon.** When you take the Attack action, or as a **Major Action**, you make one bite and two talon strikes, each a melee spell attack. The bite reaches 10 feet and deals [% 2d10 piercing %]; on a hit against a creature no more than one size larger than you, that creature is **grappled** in your jaws (escape DC equals your spell save DC). Each talon reaches 5 feet and deals [% 3d8 slashing %].
>
> **Breath Weapon.** When you cast the spell, choose a damage type: fire, frost, or lightning. As a **Minor Action**, you exhale a 30-foot cone of that type; each creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 6d6 %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the breath's damage increases by [% 1d6 %] for each slot level above 7th. At the summit of its reach — a slot of 12th level — the breath may instead deal **Force** damage — the **Akashic** stratum, harder to resist than any element.
