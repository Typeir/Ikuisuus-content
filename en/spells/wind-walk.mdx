# Wind Walk

Your form unravels into a living wind, flesh dissolving into rushing air that slips through cracks and over obstacles.

---

> **Wind Walk**  
> _6th-level transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 minute  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (fire and holy water)  
> **Duration**: 8 hours  
>  
> You and up to ten willing creatures you can see within range assume a gaseous form for the duration, appearing as wisps of cloud. While in this cloud form, a creature has a flying speed of 300 feet and has resistance to damage from mundane weapons. The only actions a creature can take in this form are the Dash action or to revert to its normal form. Reverting takes 1 minute, during which time a creature is incapacitated and can't move. Until the spell ends, a creature can revert to cloud form, which also requires the 1-minute transformation.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can carry five additional willing creatures for each slot level above 6th.
