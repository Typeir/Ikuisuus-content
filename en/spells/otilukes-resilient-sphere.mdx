# Otiluke's Resilient Sphere

A shell of absolute force asserts itself around a target with the indifference of a geometric proof — what is inside remains inside; what is outside remains so.

---

> **Otiluke's Resilient Sphere**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A sphere of shimmering force encloses a creature or object of Large size or smaller within range. An unwilling creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is enclosed for the duration.
>
> Nothing—not physical objects, energy, or other spell effects—can pass through the barrier, in or out, though a creature in the sphere can breathe there. The sphere has **40 hit points** and is **resistant to all damage except force and piercing**. The sphere has a **damage threshold of 25**; any single source of damage that rolls 25 or more before resistance and threshold application pushes through. If the sphere is reduced to 0 hit points or its threshold is beaten, it shatters and the spell ends. A creature or object inside can't be damaged by attacks or effects originating from outside, nor can a creature inside the sphere damage anything outside it.
>
> The sphere is weightless and just large enough to contain the creature or object inside. An enclosed creature can use a Major Action to push against the sphere's walls and thus roll the sphere at up to half the creature's speed. Similarly, the globe can be picked up and moved by other creatures.
>
> A _dispel magic_ spell cast at 4th level or higher ends the sphere. A _dispel magic_ cast at a lower slot level forces the caster to make a **spellcasting ability check** against a DC of **13 + the spell level difference**. For example, a 3rd-level _dispel magic_ must beat DC 14; a 1st-level casting must beat DC 16. On a success, the sphere shatters.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**
