# Twilight Bolt

A bolt with two faces: dawn's light lodges in the target and marks it, or dusk's shadow settles on its hands and drags them down.

---

> **Twilight Bolt**  
> _1st-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> Choose the bolt's face, then make a ranged spell attack against a creature within range.  
>  
> **Dawn.** On a hit, the target takes [% 3d8 holy %], and the next attack roll made against it before the end of your next turn has advantage, guided by the dim light glittering on it until then.  
>  
> **Dusk.** On a hit, the target takes [% 3d8 dark %], and it has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of your next turn.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 1st.
