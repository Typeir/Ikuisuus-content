> **Sudden Awakening (UA)**
> _1st-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: 10 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You speak a sharp command, instantly rousing a creature from magical sleep.
>
> Choose one creature within range that is unconscious due to a magical effect, such as the sleep spell. That creature immediately wakes up. This spell has no effect on creatures that are unconscious for other reasons, such as being reduced to 0 hit points or being knocked out by a blow to the head.
