> **Continual Flame**
> _2nd-level Evocation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M (ruby dust worth 50 gp, which the spell consumes)
> **Duration**: Until dispelled
>
> A flame, equivalent in brightness to a torch, springs forth from an object that you touch. The effect looks like a regular flame, but it creates no heat and doesn’t use oxygen.
>
> You touch an object that is no larger than 5 feet in any dimension and cause a flame to appear on it. The flame looks like a normal flame but creates no heat and does not use oxygen. A continual flame can be covered or hidden but not smothered or quenched.
