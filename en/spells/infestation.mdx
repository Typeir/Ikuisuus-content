# Infestation

A cloud of mites, fleas, and other parasites appears momentarily on one creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 poison damage and move 5 feet in a random direction if it can move and its speed is at least 5 feet. Roll a d4 for the direction: 1, north; 2, south; 3, east; or 4, west. This movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks, and if the direction rolled is blocked, the target doesn’t move.

>
> The spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
