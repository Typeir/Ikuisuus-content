# Cloud of Daggers

A swirling cloud of razor blades materializes and slashes through flesh and cloth with a grinding metallic hiss.

---

> **Cloud of Daggers**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You fill the air with spinning daggers in a cube 5 feet on each side, centered on a point you choose within range.  
>  
> A creature takes [% 4d4 slashing %] when it enters the spell’s area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 2d4 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
