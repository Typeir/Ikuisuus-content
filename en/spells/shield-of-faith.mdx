# Shield of Faith

A thin, translucent ward snaps into being around the target, flaring at each impact and bending blows aside.

---

> **Shield of Faith**  
> _1st-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a small parchment with a bit of holy text written on it)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> A shimmering field appears and surrounds a creature of your choice within range, granting it a **+2 bonus to AC** for the duration.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the bonus to AC increases by +1 for every three slot levels above 1st (4th, 7th, and 10th).
