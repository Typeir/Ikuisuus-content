# Summon Warrior Spirit (UA)

A spectral warrior materializes beside you, its sword and shield forming from coalescing shadow.

---

> **Summon Warrior Spirit (UA)**
> _3rd-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 90 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You call forth a spirit that assumes the form of an unarmed warrior. The spirit appears in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The spirit disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The spirit is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the spirit shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> The spirit manifests as a warrior of a culture you choose. The spirit uses the Warrior Spirit stat block. When you cast the spell, choose one of the following forms for the spirit: Barbarian, Fighter, or Monk. The spirit resembles a humanoid of your choice, which determines its appearance, but not its statistics.
>
> **Warrior Spirit**
>
> | Form      | AC   | Hit Points | Speed | Attack      |
> |----------|------|------------|-------|-------------|
> | Barbarian| 14   | 50         | 30 ft.| Greataxe    |
> | Fighter  | 16   | 45         | 30 ft.| Longsword   |
> | Monk     | 15   | 40         | 40 ft.| Unarmed     |
>
> **Actions**
>
> **Multiattack.** The spirit makes a number of attacks equal to half this spell’s level (rounded down).
>
> **Attack.** Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8 + 3 + the spell’s level bludgeoning, slashing, or piercing damage (your choice for each attack).
>
> **Bonus Actions**
>
> **Warrior’s Rally (1/Day).** The spirit chooses up to three creatures it can see within 30 feet of it. Each creature regains hit points equal to 1d8 + the spell’s level.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, use the higher level wherever the spell’s level appears in the stat block.
