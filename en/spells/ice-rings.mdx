# Ice Rings

A lattice of rime blooms from your hands in needlelike shards, hardening into jagged rings of ice that clamp around limbs and freeze fast whatever they close on.

---

> **Ice Rings**  
> _2nd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (30-foot cone)  
> **Components**: S, M (a vial of meltwater)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> A burst of cold blooms from you in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes [% 3d8 frost %] and jagged rings of ice clamp its limbs, reducing its speed to 0 until the start of your next turn. On a successful save, it takes half as much damage and its speed is not reduced.  
>  
> The cold freezes mundane liquids in the area that aren't being worn or carried.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the frost damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
