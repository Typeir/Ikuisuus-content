# Healing Word

A single spoken word crosses the field and closes a wound as it lands.

---

> **Healing Word**  
> _1st-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to [% 2d4 %] + your spellcasting ability modifier. The dead are beyond it; constructs and the undead are unmoved.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**
>
> - The healing increases by [% 2d4 %] for each slot level above 1st.
> - **3rd level or higher (Chorus):** you may instead distribute the healing among up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range.
