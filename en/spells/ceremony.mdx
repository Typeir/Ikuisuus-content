# Ceremony

A pale sigil blooms at the touch and razor-fine filaments of light snap outward to bind and seal skin.

---

> **Ceremony**  
> _1st-level Abjuration (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (grave-ash and powdered silver worth 25 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You perform one of the old rites in whatever form your faith gives it. The words, the tools and the mess involved vary enormously from one god to the next, and the spell does not care which of them you are keeping. Choose one rite when you cast it; its target must remain within 10 feet of you throughout the casting.
>
> **Atonement.** You touch one willing creature whose [alignment](/en/library/rules/the-measure-of-the-self/alignment) has been shifted by sustained contrary behaviour, and make a DC 20 Wisdom (Insight) check. On a success, you reverse that shift by one step. Where the creature has also severed a bond to a granted Key, this rite is the one that mends it.
>
> **Bless Water.** You touch one vial of water and make holy water of it.
>
> **Coming of Age.** You touch one humanoid who is a young adult. For the next 24 hours, whenever the target makes an ability check, it can roll a [% 1d4 %] and add the number rolled. A creature can benefit from this rite only once.
>
> **Dedication.** You touch one humanoid who wishes to be dedicated to the service of your god. For the next 24 hours, whenever the target makes a saving throw, it can roll a [% 1d4 %] and add the number rolled. A creature can benefit from this rite only once.
>
> **Funeral Rite.** You touch one corpse. For the next 7 days nothing can animate it — no rite, no spell, no accident of the Fold will make a construct of that body.
>
> **Investiture.** You touch one willing creature. For the next 24 hours, whenever the target makes an attack roll, it can roll a [% 1d4 %] and add the number rolled. A creature can benefit from this rite only once.
>
> **Wedding.** You touch adult humanoids willing to be bound together in marriage. For the next 7 days, each gains a +2 bonus to AC while within 30 feet of its spouse. A creature can benefit from this rite only once.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can perform **one additional rite** for each slot level above 1st, on the same target or another within reach. The component is consumed once, however long the ceremony runs.
