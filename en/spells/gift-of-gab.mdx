# Gift of Gab

Your voice sharpens into a steady, magnetic cadence that fixes listeners on each word.

---

> **Gift of Gab**
> _2nd-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: Reaction
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
>
> When you speak to others, you can subtly alter the words you have just spoken.
>
> When you cast this spell, you magically alter your words to be whatever you wish, even if you have already spoken. You can change up to 6 seconds (one round) of speech that you have just spoken, replacing it with new words of your choice. Other creatures that heard the original statement forget it and remember only the new words. However, any creature that succeeds on a Wisdom saving throw is aware that your words have been magically altered.
