# Silence

Ambient sound collapses into a flat, motionless hush.

---

> **Silence**  
> _2nd-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> For the duration, no sound is created within — and none can pass into or out of — a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range. There is no saving throw; the hush simply holds.  
>  
> A creature entirely inside the sphere is **deafened**, cannot cast a spell with a verbal component, and cannot be harmed by anything that works through sound — a thunderous blast, a shattering note, a word of power all die at the sphere's edge (such sonic force deals bludgeoning damage in Damocles, and the sphere negates it).
