# Silence

Ambient sound collapses into a flat, motionless hush.

---

> **Silence**  
> _2nd-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action R  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> For the duration, no sound can be created within or pass through a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range. Any creature or object entirely inside the sphere is immune to bludgeoning damage, and creatures are deafened while entirely inside it. Casting a spell that includes a verbal component is impossible there.
