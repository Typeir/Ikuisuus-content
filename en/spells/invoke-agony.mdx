# Invoke Agony

There are words the body was never given the right to refuse. This is one of them; it does not argue with the flesh, it overrules it.

---

> **Invoke Agony**  
> _7th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> You speak a word of ruin at one creature you can see within range. Choose **Agony**; or, if you are casting the spell with a slot of 8th level or higher, you may instead choose **Rigor**.  
>  
> **Agony.** If the target has **100 hit points or fewer**, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be **incapacitated** by pain. Otherwise the spell has no effect on it. At the end of each of its turns, and whenever it takes damage, the target repeats the save, ending the effect on itself on a success.  
>  
> **Rigor.** If the target has **150 hit points or fewer**, it is **stunned** — the word itself allows no saving throw to resist. Otherwise the spell has no effect on it. The target makes a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** Casting **Agony** with a slot of 8th level or higher raises its threshold by 20 hit points for each slot level above 7th. Casting **Rigor** with a slot of 9th level or higher raises its threshold by 20 hit points for each slot level above 8th.
