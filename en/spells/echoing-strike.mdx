# Echoing Strike

A thunderous strike. Unobservant targets will be struck by a booming echo of the original trajectory.

> **Echoing Strike**  
> _Evocation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (5-foot radius)  
> **Components**: S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You brandish the weapon used in the spell's casting and make a melee attack with it against one creature within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target suffers the attack's normal effects, and an echo of the blow hangs over it waiting to land again.
>
> **At the end of the target's turn, if it is not steady, it takes [% 1d8 bludgeoning %] and the spell ends.** If it held steady, the echo goes unspent and the spell ends anyway.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** At 5th level the melee attack deals an extra [% 1d8 bludgeoning %] on a hit, and the echo increases to [% 2d8 %]. Both increase by [% 1d8 %] again at 11th level and at 17th.
