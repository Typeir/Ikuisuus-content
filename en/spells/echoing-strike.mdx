# Echoing Strike

A thunderous strike. Unobservant targets will be struck by a booming echo of the original trajectory.

> **Echoing Strike**  
> _Evocation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (5-foot radius)  
> **Components**: S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You brandish the weapon used in the spell’s casting and make a melee attack with it against one creature within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target suffers the weapon attack’s normal effects, and an echo of you will attempt to repeat the strike at the end of the creature's turn. If the creature is unsteady then, it takes [% 1d8 bludgeoning %], and the spell ends.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage to the target on a hit, and the damage the target takes for moving increases to 2d8. Both damage rolls increase by 1d8 at 11th level (2d8 and 3d8) and 17th level (3d8 and 4d8).
