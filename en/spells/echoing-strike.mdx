# Echoing Strike

A twofold strike. Unstrung targets will be struck by a trailing iteration of the original attack.

> **Echoing Strike**  
> _Evocation cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self (5-foot radius)  
> **Components**: S, M  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You brandish the weapon used in the spell’s casting and make a melee attack with it. On a hit, the target suffers the weapon attack’s normal effects, and an echo of you will attempt to repeat the strike at the end of the creature's turn. If the creature is unsteady then, it takes 1d10 slashing damage, and the spell ends. 
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d10 slashing damage to the target on a hit, and the damage the target takes for being unsteady increases to 2d10. Both damage rolls increase by 1d10 at 11th level (2d10 and 3d10) and 17th level (3d10 and 4d10).
