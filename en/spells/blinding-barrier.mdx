# Blinding Barrier

A vertical wall of blinding light snaps into being, scorching and vaporizing matter where it touches.

---

> **Blinding Barrier**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action or 1 Reaction  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a shard of mirror, silvered on both faces)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> A wall of brilliant light springs into existence at a point you choose within range. It appears in any orientation you choose — horizontal, vertical, or angled — and can be free floating or resting on a solid surface. It must be **straight**: light does not bend for you, and this barrier cannot be shaped into a ring, a curve, or a dome. You can form it up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick.
>
> The wall sheds bright light out to 120 feet and dim light for an additional 120 feet.
>
> When the wall appears, each creature in its area makes a Constitution saving throw, taking [% 4d8 holy %] and being **blinded** for 1 minute on a failure, or half as much damage and no blindness on a success. A blinded creature repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
>
> The wall blocks line of sight but not movement. Creatures and objects can pass through it, and it **cannot be crossed through the Folds**. A creature crossing the wall makes a Constitution saving throw or is blinded until the end of its turn. If you end your turn in the wall's area, you take [% 4d8 holy %].
>
> **The Beam.** Once on each of your turns, you can use a **Major Action** to launch a beam of light from the wall at one creature you can see within 60 feet of it. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit the target takes [% 4d8 holy %] and must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be **blinded** until the end of its next turn. Hit or miss, the wall is 10 feet shorter afterward; if its length reaches 0, the spell ends.
>
> **Energy barrier.** Area effects pass straight through it. See [Barriers](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/barriers).
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] and the wall can be created **20 feet** longer for each slot level above 5th.
