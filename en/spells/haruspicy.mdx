# Haruspicy

A rite that reads truth from dead flesh, revealing not meaning or fate, but responsibility.

---

> **Haruspicy**  
> _3rd-level Divination (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 minutes  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (the organs of a dead creature)  
> **Duration**: Until the next dawn  
>
> You examine the organs of a dead creature, divining the truth of its death.  
> The creature must have died within the last **7 days** and must not be undead.
>
> When you complete the rite, you learn the **exact cause of death** and the **identity of the creature’s killer**.
>
> If the killer is within **3 miles** of you, you also learn the killer’s **current location**.
>
> Until the next dawn, you have **advantage on attack rolls and skill checks** made against the killer.
>
> This spell fails if the creature died of natural causes, and it cannot be used on the same corpse more than once.
