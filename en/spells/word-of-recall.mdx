# Word of Recall

A vertical seam of compressed light opens at the caster's feet, a hard, silent pull dragging them and nearby matter through before the seam snaps shut.

---

> **Word of Recall**  
> _6th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 5 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  
> **Designating a Sanctuary.** You designate a sanctuary by casting this spell in a place consecrated to your god, or bound tightly enough to it that the distinction stops mattering. Cast this way anywhere else, the spell does nothing.  
>  
> **Recall.** You and up to five willing creatures within 5 feet of you are instantly folded to the sanctuary you designated, arriving in the nearest unoccupied spaces to the spot you marked. If you cast this spell without having prepared a sanctuary, nothing happens at all.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can take **five additional creatures** with you for each slot level above 6th.
