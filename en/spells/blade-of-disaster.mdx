# Blade of Disaster

A colossal blade of condensed force rips from the sky and slams into the earth, cleaving rock and shredding buildings.

---

> **Blade of Disaster**
> _9th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> You create a blade-shaped planar rift about 3 feet long in an unoccupied space you can see within range. The blade lasts for the duration.
>
> When you cast the spell, you can make up to two melee spell attacks with the blade, each one against a creature or object within 5 feet of the blade. On a hit, the target takes 4d12 force damage. If the attack roll is a critical hit, the target takes an extra 8d12 force damage (for a total of 12d12 force damage).
>
> As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the blade up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see and repeat the attacks with it.
>
> The blade can pass through a barrier, but it can't pass through a planar barrier.
