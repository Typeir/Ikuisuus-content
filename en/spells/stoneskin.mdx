# Stoneskin

The target's skin hardens into dull, rock-like plates, flesh taking on the texture of stone.

---

> **Stoneskin**  
> _4th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
>  
> This spell turns the flesh of a willing creature you touch as hard as stone.  
>  
> The target’s flesh turns as hard as stone. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
