# Stoneskin

The target's skin hardens into dull, rock-like plates, flesh taking on the texture of stone.

---

> **Stoneskin**  
> _4th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp, which the spell consumes)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> The flesh of a willing creature you touch turns as hard as stone. Until the spell ends, that creature has **resistance to all bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage**.
>
> **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, target an additional creature for every two levels above the 4th.
