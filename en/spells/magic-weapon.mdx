# Magic Weapon

Your touch suffuses a weapon with a pale, hard light that clings to its edge.

---

> **Magic Weapon**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You touch a weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon gains a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** For every two levels above 2nd, the bonus increases by 1.
