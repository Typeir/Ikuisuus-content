# Psychic Scream

You unleash the power of your mind to blast the intellect of up to ten creatures of your choice that you can see within range.

---

> **Psychic Scream**
> _9th-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 90 feet
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You target up to ten creatures of your choice that you can see within range. Each target must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take 14d6 psychic damage and be stunned. On a successful save, a target takes half as much damage and isn’t stunned. If a target is killed by this damage, its head explodes, assuming it has one.
>
> A stunned target can make an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the stunning effect ends.
