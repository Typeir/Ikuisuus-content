# Locate

A thread only you can feel pulls taut, and somewhere at its far end is the thing you named.

---

> **Locate**  
> _2nd-level Divination_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a forked twig)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> Name your quarry, and for the duration you sense the direction to it whenever it is within 1,000 feet of you. If it is moving, you know the direction of its movement. Choose one:  
>  
> - **A specific object** familiar to you, seen up close at least once. If an object of that kind is instead merely described, you sense the nearest one.  
> - **The nearest beast or plant** of a kind you name.  
>  
> Old wardings hold against this thread: the spell cannot find an object behind even a thin sheet of lead, and a named beast is lost to it across running water.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - The range increases by 500 feet, and the duration by 15 minutes, for each slot level above 2nd.  
> - **4th level or higher (Quarry):** you may instead sense the direction to a **specific creature** familiar to you, so long as you have seen it up close at least once. Running water at least 10 feet wide breaks the thread.
