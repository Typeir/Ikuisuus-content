# On/Off (UA)

You choose an object that you can see within range that has a mundane on/off switch, such as a lantern, a stove, or a hooded lantern. You instantly turn the object on or off. If the object is locked, stuck, or otherwise unable to be turned on or off, the spell has no effect.

---

> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
