# Sulphurous Sphere

This spell is not innate to any vocation, and can only be learnt through a pact with the lord of brimstone.

> “A globe of swirling sulfureous fumes, the very image of the world the lord of the brume envisioned.”

---

> **Sulfurous Sphere**  
> _3rd-Level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a lump of brimstone and a drop of rancid oil)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You conjure a **10-foot-radius sphere** of swirling sulfurous vapor, centered on a point you can see within range. The sphere hovers in place and can be moved up to **20 feet as a bonus action** on your turn.
>
> When a creature **enters the sphere** for the first time on a turn or **starts its turn there**, it must make a **Constitution saving throw**. On a failed save:
>
> - The creature takes **1d10 fire damage** and **1d10 acid damage**.
> - It is **blinded** and its **speed is halved** until the end of its next turn. If the creature **started its turn** inside, the effect instead lasts until the **end of that turn**.
>
> On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and suffers no conditions.
>
> The sphere heavily obscures its interior.
>
> **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, increase both the fire and acid damage by **1d10** for each slot level above 3rd.
