> **Harm**
> _6th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You unleash a virulent disease on a creature that you can see within range.
>
> The target must make a Constitution saving throw. It takes 14d6 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The damage can’t reduce the target’s hit points below 1. If the target fails the saving throw, its hit point maximum is reduced for 1 hour by an amount equal to the necrotic damage it took. Any effect that removes a disease allows a creature’s hit point maximum to return to normal before that time passes.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 6th.
