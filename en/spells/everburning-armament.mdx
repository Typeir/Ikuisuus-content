# Everburning Armament

A spell that forces a weapon beyond its material limits, flooding it with relentless, destructive heat until nothing remains but slag.

> “That which burns in the flames of the everdark is imbued with it's power, if only briefly.”

---

> **Everburning Armament**  
> _8th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a melee weapon you are wielding)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 30 seconds.
>
> You wreathe a weapon you are wielding in catastrophic, ever-hungry flame, pushing it far beyond what physical matter was meant to endure.
>
> For the duration, the affected weapon gains the following benefits:
>
> - The weapon gains a **+3 bonus** to attack and damage rolls. This bonus is in addition to any other bonuses the weapon may have.
> - The weapon’s **reach increases by 25 feet**.
> - Each time you hit a creature with the weapon, the attack deals an additional **4d6 fire damage**.
> - A creature hit by the weapon is **[_burning_](/en/library/rules/conditions#burning)** and has its **speed reduced by half** until the end of their next turn.
> - While wielding the empowered weapon, you can make **one additional weapon attack as a bonus action** on each of your turns.
>
> When the spell ends, the weapon is **destroyed**, rendered unusable by the strain of the enchantment.
>
> If the weapon is **unbreakable or indestructible**, it survives the spell, but the additional fire damage dealt by the weapon is reduced to **2d4** instead of 4d6.
