# Veil of Mana

Weave an impossibly thin gauze of ice, it covers a creature in stillness and quickly sublimates afterwards.

> **Veil of Mana**  
> _3rd-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action R  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>  
> You touch a willing creature and put it into a cataleptic state that is indistinguishable from death.  
>  
> The target is blinded and incapacitated, and its speed drops to 0. The target appears dead to all outward inspection and to spells used to determine the target’s status. The target is immune to disease and poison while under the effect of the spell, although a disease or poison already in its system is only suppressed, not neutralized.  
>  
> The spell ends if you use a Major Action to dismiss it or if the target takes any damage.
