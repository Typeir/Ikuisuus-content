# Time Ravage

You target a creature you can see within range, putting its physical form through rapid and lethal aging. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 10d12 necrotic damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, it dies and instantly turns to dust. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and does not age.
