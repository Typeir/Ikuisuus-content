# Darkvision

A pale, filmy light wells in the recipient's eyes, stripping away shadow and revealing forms in the dark.

---

> **Darkvision**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: 16 hours
>  
> You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If it already has darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can touch one additional willing creature for each slot level above 2nd.
