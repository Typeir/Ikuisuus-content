> **Darkvision**
> _2nd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If it already has darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet.
