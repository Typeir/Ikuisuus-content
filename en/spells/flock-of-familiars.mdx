# Flock of Familiars

You temporarily summon three familiars, which appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. Each familiar is a celestial, fey, or fiend (your choice) and appears in the form of a beast of your choice that is no larger than Tiny and that has the statistics of the chosen form, though it is a celestial, fey, or fiend (instead of a beast).

---

> **Flock of Familiars**
> _2nd-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> Each familiar disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. The familiars are friendly to you and your companions, and they obey your commands. You can communicate telepathically with the familiars and perceive through their senses as long as you are on the same plane of existence. While perceiving through a familiar’s senses, you can also speak through the familiar in your own voice, even if your familiar is normally incapable of speech.
>
> As an action, you can temporarily dismiss one or more of the familiars. As a bonus action while any of them are temporarily dismissed, you can cause them to reappear in any unoccupied space within 30 feet of you.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you summon one additional familiar for each slot level above 2nd.
