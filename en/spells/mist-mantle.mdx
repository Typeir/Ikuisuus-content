# Mist Mantle

A thin brume covers you, concealing your movements and subtly deflecting blows.

---

> **Mist Mantle**  
> _Abjuration cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> You weave a cloak of thin concealing fog, covering yourself in it. 
>  
> You have resistance against bleeding, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage dealt by weapon attacks until the end of your next turn.
> 
> Additionally, for the next hour, you leave behind no tracks or other traces of your passage.
