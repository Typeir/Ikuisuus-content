# Archivists' Pall

A heavy, dense curtain of concentrated thought settles over the mind, muting the world's noise and turning intrusion aside.

---

> **Archivists' Pall**  
> _3rd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 4 hours
>
> For the duration, you or one willing creature you can see within range gains:
>
> - resistance to psychic damage;
> - advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws;
> - immunity to any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, and to divination effects that target it — its mind cannot be read or scried;
> - **Feedback:** when the warded creature succeeds on a saving throw against an effect that would deal it damage, it can use its reaction to take no damage from that effect instead.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
