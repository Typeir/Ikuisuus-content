# Null Sphere

The Canvas does not tear cleanly. Where the seam gives, everything nearby leans in to look, and then keeps leaning.

---

> **Null Sphere**  
> _9th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 1,000 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a bead of lead, dropped)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A 20-foot-radius sphere of collapsing gravity forms at a point you can see within range and pulls at everything not anchored against it. The sphere lasts for the duration, and its area is difficult terrain.
>
> A creature that starts its turn inside the sphere, enters it for the first time on a turn, or ends its turn there must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take [% 5d10 force %] and be dragged straight toward the center, ending in the nearest unoccupied space to it. On a success it takes half damage and is not moved. Unattended objects are pulled the same way.
>
> At the start of your turn the sphere deals [% 5d10 force %] to every creature and object inside it, no saving throw. Anything the sphere overlaps is pulled to its center.
>
> Within the area of an [Antimagic Shell](/en/library/spells/antimagic-shell) or an [Entropic Field](/en/library/spells/entropic-field), the sphere is suppressed for as long as that effect lasts.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 10th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d10 %] for each slot level above 9th.
