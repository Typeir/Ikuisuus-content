# Burning Barrier

A vertical wall of flame erupts, scorching the air and singeing any matter it touches.

---

> **Brimstone Barrier**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> 
>  
> You create a wall of fire on a solid surface within range. You can make the wall up to 60 feet long, 20 feet high, and 1 foot thick, or a ringed wall up to 20 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 1 foot thick. The wall is opaque and lasts for the duration.  
>  
> One side of the wall, selected by you when you cast this spell, deals 5d8 fire damage to each creature when it 
> - Is inside the wall when it appears. That creature can make a Dexterity saving throw, and takes half as much damage on a successful save. 
> - Enters the wall for the first time on a turn.
> - Ends its turn within 10 feet of the chosen side or inside the wall. 
> The other side of the wall deals no damage.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
