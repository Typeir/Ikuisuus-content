# Freedom of Movement

Invisible filaments slide along the subject's joints, loosening restraint and restoring fluid motion.

---

> **Freedom of Movement**  
> _4th-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a leather strap, bound around the arm or a similar appendage)  
> **Duration**: 4 hours
>  
> You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target’s movement is unaffected by difficult terrain, and spells and other magical effects can neither reduce the target’s speed nor cause the target to be paralyzed or restrained. The target can also spend 5 feet of movement to automatically escape from restraints, such as manacles or a creature that has it grappled. Finally, being underwater imposes no penalties on the target’s movement or attacks.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can touch one additional willing creature for each slot level above 4th.
