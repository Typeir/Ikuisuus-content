# Prayer of Healing

A warm, cleansing light washes over the injured, knitting torn flesh and sealing wounds.

---

> **Prayer of Healing**  
> _2nd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 10 Minutes  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to [% 2d8 %] + your spellcasting ability modifier. Constructs are unaffected — there is nothing in them for this to knit.  
>  
> A creature that spends the full ten minutes with you also gains **the benefit of a Repose**, and cannot benefit from this part of the spell again until it finishes a Recovery. Ten minutes is a long time to stand still, and it is meant to be.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
