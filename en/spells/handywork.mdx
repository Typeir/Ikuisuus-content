# Handywork

Broken edges draw together as fine filaments stitch and fuse the material into a whole.

---

> **Handywork**  
> _Enchantment cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> Choose one of the following effects:
> 
>  **Mend.** Repair a single break or tear in an object you touch, As long as the break or tear is no larger than 1 foot in any dimension, mend it, leaving no trace of the former damage. This doesn't restore magic to constructs or heirlooms, only fixes their form. 
>  
>  **Toil.** A simple tool(axe, shovel, shears, hammer, broom...) starts floating, during the next ten minutes you can use your action to command it to perform its purpose on a target within range(axes chop trees, hammers hit nails...). The enchanted tool can't harm creatures. The enchanted tool is unfit for precise work and has an average strength. You may end this effect as an action.
>  
>  **Hearth.** Light a bonfire over sufficient lumber. It burns evenly and does not spread for eight hours, giving 30 feet of light and ten of dim light. When the effect ends the fire extinguishes completely. You may end this effect as an action.
>  
>  **Scour.** Filter water, wash a change of clothes, or clean ingredients. This is only superficial hygiene, and does not offer reliable protection from posions, curses, or illnesses.
>  
>  You can only have one of these effects active at a time.
