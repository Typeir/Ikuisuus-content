> **Poison Spray**
> _Conjuration cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 10 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and project a puff of noxious gas from your palm.
>
> The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d12 poison damage. This spell’s damage increases by 1d12 when you reach 5th level (2d12), 11th level (3d12), and 17th level (4d12).
