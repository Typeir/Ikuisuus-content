# Image

A picture hangs in the air where nothing is, patient and almost true.

---

> **Image**  
> _1st-level Illusion_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a bit of fleece)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes  
>  
> You create the image of an object, a creature, or some other visible phenomenon no larger than a 15-foot cube. The image is purely visual: sound, smell, and other sensations pass through it unchanged. As a Major Action, you can move the image to any spot within range and animate it naturally as it moves.  
>  
> Physical interaction reveals the image for what it is, as things pass through it. A creature that uses a Major Action to examine it can determine it is an illusion with an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC; on a success, the creature sees through the image.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - **3rd level or higher:** the image grows to a 20-foot cube and carries sound, smell, and temperature appropriate to what it depicts. It can hold a conversation's worth of noise, though it produces no true speech unless you direct it.  
> - **6th level or higher:** the image no longer requires concentration and lasts until dispelled.
