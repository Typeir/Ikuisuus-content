> **Unearthly Chorus (UA)**
> _1st-level Illusion_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self (30-foot radius)
> **Components**: V
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You magically impress audiences with your musical talent. Until the spell ends, you gain advantage on Charisma (Performance) checks. In addition, as a bonus action on each of your turns, you can beguile one humanoid of your choice within 30 feet of you who can hear you, making it charmed by you until the spell ends or until you or your companions do anything harmful to it. The charmed condition ends early if you or your companions do anything harmful to it. While charmed in this way, the creature idolizes you, it speaks glowingly of you to anyone who speaks to it, and it hinders anyone who opposes you, although it avoids violence unless it was already inclined to fight on your behalf. This effect ends immediately if you or your companions do anything harmful to it.
