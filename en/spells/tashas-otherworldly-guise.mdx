# Tasha's Otherworldly Guise

A pale, plated veneer crawls over the caster as flesh tightens and features harden into a cold, nonhuman mask.

---

> **Tasha's Otherworldly Guise**
> _6th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> Uttering an incantation, you call upon extraplanar magic to transform yourself, taking on aspects of a lower planar or upper planar being.
>
> You transform until the spell ends. Choose one of the following options:
>
> **Fiendish**: You gain the following benefits:
> - You are immune to fire and poison damage.
> - You are immune to the poisoned condition.
> - You have a flying speed of 40 feet and can hover.
> - You can attack twice, instead of once, when you take the Attack action on your turn.
> - You can use your bonus action to make one weapon attack.
> - Your weapon attacks are magical and you use your spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls.
>
> **Celestial**: You gain the following benefits:
> - You are immune to radiant and necrotic damage.
> - You are immune to the charmed condition.
> - You have a flying speed of 40 feet and can hover.
> - You can attack twice, instead of once, when you take the Attack action on your turn.
> - You can use your bonus action to make one weapon attack.
> - Your weapon attacks are magical and you use your spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the duration increases by 1 minute for each slot level above 6th.
