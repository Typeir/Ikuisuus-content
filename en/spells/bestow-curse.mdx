# Bestow Curse

A living shadow roots itself to the target and begins, quietly, to treat their identity as its own. It bends their motions, dulls their reflexes, and does not consider itself a guest.

---

> **Bestow Curse**  
> _3rd-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You touch a creature; it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be cursed for the duration. Choose the shape of the shadow when you cast the spell:  
>  
> | Curse       | Effect                                                                                                                    |
> | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
> | **Hobble**  | Choose one ability score. The target has disadvantage on ability checks and saving throws made with it.                    |
> | **Avert**   | The target has disadvantage on attack rolls against you.                                                                   |
> | **Stall**   | At the start of each of its turns the target makes a Wisdom saving throw; on a failure it wastes its Major Action that turn. |
> | **Mark**    | Your attacks and spells deal an extra [% 1d8 dark %] to the target.                                                        |
>
> Any effect that lifts a curse ends this one. You may devise an alternative curse in the same register, no stronger than those above.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the duration is concentration, up to 10 minutes. If you use a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the duration is 8 hours. If you use a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the duration is 24 hours. If you use a spell slot of 9th level, the spell lasts until it is dispelled. Using a spell slot of 5th level or higher grants a duration that doesn’t require concentration.
