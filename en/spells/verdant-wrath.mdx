# Verdant Wrath

Gnarled roots explode from the ground, lashing out to crush stone and tear flesh as the terrain convulses.

---

> **Verdant Wrath**  
> _5th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> You call out to the spirits of nature to rouse them against your enemies. Choose a point you can see within range. The ground in a 60-foot cube centered on that point becomes difficult terrain for your enemies. For the spell’s duration, you can cause the following effects when you cast the spell and as a Minor Action on each of your turns:  
>  
> **Grasses and Undergrowth.** Grasses and undergrowth in the area, including trees, turn the ground in the area into difficult terrain for your enemies.  
>  
> **Roots and Vines.** At the start of each of your turns, one creature of your choice that is on the ground in the cube must succeed on a Strength saving throw or become restrained until the spell ends. A restrained creature can use a Major Action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, ending the effect on itself on a success.  
>  
> **Rocks.** As a Minor Action on your turn, you can cause a loose rock in the cube to launch at a creature you can see in the cube. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes [% 3d8 bludgeoning %], and it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or fall prone.  
>  
> **Trees.** At the start of each of your turns, each of your enemies within 10 feet of any tree in the cube must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take [% 4d6 slashing %] from whipping branches.
