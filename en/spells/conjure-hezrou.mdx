# Conjure Hezrou (UA)

A hulking mass of muscle and shadow tears out of a jagged rift and smashes into the ground.

---

> **Conjure Hezrou (UA)**
> _7th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You summon a hezrou demon that appears in an unoccupied space you can see within range. The hezrou disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The hezrou is friendly to you and your companions for the duration. Roll initiative for the hezrou, which has its own turns. It obeys any verbal commands that you issue to it (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any commands to the hezrou, it defends itself from hostile creatures but otherwise takes no actions.
>
> The DM has the hezrou’s statistics.
