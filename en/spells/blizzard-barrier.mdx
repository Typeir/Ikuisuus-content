# Blizzard Barrier

A wall of solid ice rockets up from the ground, forming a tall barrier that frosts the air.

---

> **Blizzard Barrier**
> _6th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action or 1 Reaction  
> **Range**: 120 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 30 minutes
>  
>  
> A wall of ice springs into existence at a point you choose within range. You can make the wall up to 10 feet long, 10 feet high, and 1 foot thick per level of the spell. The wall lasts for the duration. If the wall cuts through a creature’s space when it appears, the creature is pushed to one side of the wall (your choice which side).  
>  
> The wall is an object that can be damaged and thus breached. It has AC 12 and 30 hit points per 10-foot section. Reducing a section to 0 hit points destroys it and leaves behind a sheet of frigid air in the space the wall occupied. A creature moving through the sheet of frigid air for the first time on a turn must make a Constitution saving throw. That creature takes [% 5d6 frost %] on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The frigid air lasts for 1 minute, though a strong wind can disperse it.  
>  
> You can shape the wall into a hemispherical dome or a sphere with a radius of up to 10 feet per level of the spell. The wall is 1 foot thick. You can also shape the wall into a flat surface made up of ten 10-foot-square panels. Each panel must be contiguous with another panel. In any form, the wall is 1 foot thick.  
>  
> If a creature tries to break through the wall, it must make a Strength saving throw. On a successful save, the creature can move through the wall as if it were difficult terrain, taking [% 5d6 frost %]. On a failed save, the creature can’t move through and takes [% 10d6 frost %].  
> 
> If you maintain concentration for the spell's whole duration **and you are still within casting range when it ends**, the wall is left behind as ordinary ice and can no longer be dispelled, melting at a regular pace (24 hours to melt completely in temperate weather). Otherwise the wall disappears when the spell ends.  
> 
> **Solid barrier.** Area effects break against it. See [Barriers](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/barriers).  
> 
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, increase the length of the wall by 10 feet, or the radius by 10 feet, for each slot level above 6th.
