# Summon Aberration

A malformed, pulsing mass of flesh and eyes appears, its slick limbs unfurling and probing the air.

---

> **Summon Aberration**
> _4th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 90 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M (a pickled tentacle and an eyeball in a platinum-inlaid vial worth at least 400 gp)
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You call forth an aberrant creature. It manifests in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. This corporeal form uses the Aberration Spirit stat block. When you cast the spell, choose Beholderkin, Slaad, or Star Spawn. The creature resembles the chosen creature, which determines certain traits in its stat block.
>
> The aberration disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. The aberration is friendly to you and your companions for the spell’s duration. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> **Aberration Spirit**
>
> *Medium aberration, neutral*
>
> **Armor Class** 11 + the level of the spell (natural armor)
>
> **Hit Points** 40 + 10 for each spell level above 4th
>
> **Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)
>
> **STR** 14 (+2)  **DEX** 14 (+2)  **CON** 14 (+2)  **INT** 13 (+1)  **WIS** 14 (+2)  **CHA** 11 (+0)
>
> **Saving Throws** Wis +4, Cha +2
>
> **Skills** Perception +4, Stealth +4
>
> **Damage Resistances** psychic; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
>
> **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 14
>
> **Languages** understands the languages you speak
>
> **Challenge** —
>
> **Proficiency Bonus (PB)** equals your bonus
>
> **TRAITS**
>
> **Aberrant Mind.** The aberration has advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.
>
> **Actions**
>
> **Multiattack.** The aberration makes a number of attacks equal to half this spell’s level (rounded down).
>
> **Beholderkin: Eye Ray.** Ranged Spell Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, range 150 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8 + 3 + the spell’s level psychic damage.
>
> **Slaad: Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d10 + 3 + the spell’s level slashing damage.
>
> **Star Spawn: Psychic Slam.** Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8 + 3 + the spell’s level psychic damage, and the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or be frightened of the aberration until the end of the target’s next turn.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, use the higher level wherever the spell’s level appears in the stat block.
