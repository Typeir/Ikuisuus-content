# Spirit Guardians

The very fibres of a domain of power split open, revealing a whirling mass of divine splinters that enact their will through you.

---

> **Spirit Guardians**  
> _3rd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (15-foot radius)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a holy symbol)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> Spirits gather and flit around you out to a distance of 15 feet for the duration. When you cast this spell, choose **holy** or **dark** — what the dead do to the living depends on which of them answered — and designate any number of creatures you can see to be left alone.
>
> An affected creature's speed is halved within the area. When such a creature enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Wisdom saving throw, taking [% 3d8 %] damage of the chosen type on a failure, or half as much on a success.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 3rd.
