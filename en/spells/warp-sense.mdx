> **Warp Sense**
> _2nd-level Divination_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You gain the ability to sense distortions in the fabric of space. For the duration, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to detect portals, teleportation effects, or areas where the boundaries between planes are thin. You can also sense the presence of such phenomena within 30 feet of you, even if they are hidden or behind barriers.
