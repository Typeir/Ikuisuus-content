> **Synchronicity (UA)**
> _4th-level Enchantment T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a willing creature and synchronize its actions with the flow of time, granting it heightened awareness and reflexes.
>
> The target has advantage on initiative rolls and can’t be surprised. In addition, the target can take one extra reaction each round. This effect ends early if the spell is cast on another creature.
