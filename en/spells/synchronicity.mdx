# Synchronicity

A touch snaps the target's movements and sounds into precise, unnerving unison with an external beat.

---

> **Synchronicity**  
> _4th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a willing creature and set its actions running with the beat instead of against it.
>
> For the duration, the target has advantage on initiative rolls, cannot be surprised, and can take **one extra Reaction each round**. That extra Reaction is a hard cap: it does not stack with any other effect granting additional reactions, and no creature ever has more than one from any source.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can touch one additional willing creature for every two slot levels above 4th.
