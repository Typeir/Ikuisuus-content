# Orbital Cascade

Seven bodies of the sky answer at once. None of them arrive gently, and none of them arrive in the same way twice.

---

> **Orbital Cascade**  
> _7th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (60-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You call the orbits down in a cone. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw; for each target, roll a d8 to see which body answered.
>
> 1. **Kultharja.** The target takes [% 10d6 holy %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
> 2. **Urmela.** The target takes [% 10d6 fire %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
> 3. **Selkara.** The target takes [% 10d6 piercing %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
> 4. **Kalmora.** The target takes [% 10d6 poison %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
> 5. **Itähenki and Länsihenki.** The target takes [% 10d6 lightning %] on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
> 6. **Mana.** On a failed save, the target is **restrained** as crystal closes over it. It makes a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On three successes the crystal breaks and the effect ends; on three failures it closes completely and the target is **petrified**. The successes and failures need not be consecutive.
> 7. **Damocles.** On a failed save, the target is **blinded** by the mirage of every soul the world is carrying. It makes a Wisdom saving throw at the start of your next turn. On a success the blindness ends. On a failure it is unmoored from the world entirely: it surfaces somewhere in the Folds, or adrift toward a Clone World, and where it surfaces is not yours to choose.
> 8. **Conjunction.** The target is struck by two bodies. Roll twice more, rerolling any 8.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, one additional body strikes each target for each slot level above 7th.
