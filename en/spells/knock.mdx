# Knock

A sharp, resonant pulse strikes a sealed barrier — and the world, asked politely enough, agrees that this was always a door.

---

> **Knock**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Choose an object that you can see within range. The object can be a door, a box, a chest, a set of manacles, a padlock, or another object that contains a mundane or magical means that prevents access.
>
> A target that is held shut by a mundane lock or that is stuck or barred becomes unlocked, unstuck, or unbarred. If the object has multiple locks, only one of them is unlocked. If you choose a target that is held shut with [_arcane lock_](/en/library/spells/arcane-lock), that spell is suppressed for 10 minutes, during which time the target can be opened and shut normally.
>
> When you cast the spell, a loud knock, audible from as far away as 300 feet, emanates from the target — at every level of casting.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** What counts as a door grows with the slot:
>
> - **4th level or higher:** any sealed portal-shaped thing — a gate, a grate, a hatch, a mine-seal, a shuttered window — opens as though it were a door, and magical seals of the slot's level or lower are suppressed for 10 minutes.
> - **5th level or higher:** you may knock on a blank wooden, plaster, or stone surface, and it opens as though it had always held a door: a passage 5 feet wide, 8 feet tall, and up to 20 feet deep, lasting **until dawn**. The passage creates no instability in the structure around it and is covered by a simple illusion of the unbroken wall, which fails to hold up to examination. When the passage closes, anything still inside is safely ejected to the nearest unoccupied space.
