# Danse Macabre

Nearby corpses convulse and rise, their limbs snapping into a relentless, jerking macabre dance.

---

> **Danse Macabre**  
> _5th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 Feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
>  
> Threads of dark power leap from your fingers into up to five Small or Medium corpses you can see within range. Each stands up as a **construct (undead)** — an animated corpse, a thing and not a person. There is no third state between living and dead, and nothing in these is anybody.  
>  
> Each rises with the statistics it had in life, except that its Intelligence becomes 3, it loses any spellcasting it had, and it gains a bonus to attack and damage rolls equal to your spellcasting ability modifier. Together they cannot exceed the **Challenge Budget** for this spell's slot — twice the slot level, as laid out in [Summoning and Conjuration](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/summoning-and-conjuration). Corpses past the budget stay where they are.  
>  
> Each undead creature created by this spell obeys your commands until the spell ends, after which it becomes inanimate once more. As a Minor Action on your turn, you can mentally command any creature you made with this spell if the creature is within 60 feet of you (if you control multiple creatures, you can command any or all of them at the same time, issuing the same command to each one). You decide what action the creature will take and where it will move during its next turn, or you can issue a general command, such as to guard a particular chamber or corridor. If you issue no commands, the creature only defends itself against hostile creatures. Once given an order, the creature continues to follow it until its task is complete.  
>  
> This is not the bonded animation of [Animate Dead](/en/library/spells/animate-dead). Those are raised for a day and answer to you; these dance for an hour and then stop dancing.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you animate up to two additional corpses for each slot level above 5th, and the Challenge Budget rises with the slot.
