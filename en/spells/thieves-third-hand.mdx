# Thieve's Third Hand

A narrow seam opens at your wrist, forming a hollow that swallows and holds small objects out of sight.

---

> **Thieve's Third Hand**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration (ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 4 hours
>  
> You flick your wrist, causing a tiny extradimensional space to open at your fingertips, which you can use to store small objects. The space can hold up to 5 pounds or an object that fits in your hand. As part of the action used to cast the spell, or as a Minor Action while the spell persists, you can place one object into the space or retrieve one object from it. You can only have one object in the space at a time. You can retrieve the object at any time, and if the spell ends, the object appears in your hand or at your feet (your choice).  
>  
> If you die while the object is in the space, it appears at your feet.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can store one additional object for each slot level above 2nd.
