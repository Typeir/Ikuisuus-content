# Conjure Aberration

You call a Hiisi by the only name it has — its obsession — and it comes, because being named is the closest thing to being fed.

---

> **Conjure Aberration**  
> _4th-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (something you wanted badly and no longer do)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> You call an **aberration** — fiend, demon, devil, Hiisi; the world keeps one word for all of them — into an unoccupied space you can see within range. Their combined Challenge Rating may not exceed **twice this spell's level**. See [Summoning and Conjuration](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/summoning-and-conjuration) for the Challenge Budget, Swarms and Formations, and command.  
>  
> **The row's danger.** An aberration obeys the letter of a command and hunts the gap in it. Should your concentration break, it is loosed with the full remaining duration to spend as it likes, and it has been paying attention to who is nearest and who is weakest — which are rarely the same creature.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** The Challenge Budget is always twice the level of the slot spent.
