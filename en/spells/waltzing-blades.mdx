# Waltzing Blades

A spell that pulls the splinters of war into a graceful yet deadly dance, forming a phalanx of domination around the caster.

---

> **Waltzing Blades**  
> _1st-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a simple or martial weapon for each die of damage the spell can deal)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You summon a swarm of ethereal dancing blades that orbit your body in tight, defensive spirals.
>
> While the spell lasts, when a creature **hits you with an attack**, the blades interpose and you reduce the damage taken by **1d4**.
>
> When a creature **misses you with an attack**, the blades lash outward, dealing **1d4 slashing damage** to that creature.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **2nd level or higher**, the reduction and retaliatory damage each increase by **1d4** per slot level above 1st (2d4 at 2nd level, 3d4 at 3rd level, and so on).
