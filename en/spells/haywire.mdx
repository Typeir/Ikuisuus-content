# Haywire

Jagged arcs of interference snap through the mechanism, and its motions stutter, seize, and fall still.

---

> **Haywire**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> You disrupt the workings of a construct or a magical device. Choose one within range; it must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it is incapacitated and its speed is reduced to 0 for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, it repeats the save, ending the effect on a success.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.**  
>  
> - You can target one additional construct or magical device for each slot level above 3rd (targets within 30 feet of each other).  
> - **5th level or higher (Shutdown):** the target is instead **paralyzed** and gets **no repeat saving throw** — it is out for the full duration. A construct immune to paralysis is unaffected by this mode.
