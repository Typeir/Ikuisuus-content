# Incite Greed

You present a gem or other precious object and use it to beguile creatures in the area.

---

> **Incite Greed**
> _3rd-level Enchantment_
> **Casting Time**: 1 action
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You present a gem or other precious object and use it to beguile creatures in the area. Choose any number of creatures within 30 feet of you that you can see. Each target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is charmed by you until the spell ends or until you or your allies do anything harmful to it. While charmed in this way, a creature can do nothing but use its movement to approach you in a safe manner. While approaching, it avoids opportunity attacks, but not area effects, such as a fireball. Before moving into dangerous terrain, such as lava or a pit, and whenever it takes damage from a source other than the spell, a creature can repeat the saving throw. If a creature’s saving throw succeeds, the effect ends for it.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
