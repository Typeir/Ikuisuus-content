> **Tree Stride**
> _5th-level Conjuration_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You gain the ability to enter a tree and move from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 500 feet. Both trees must be living and at least the same size as you. Using 5 feet of movement, you step into the tree and know the location of all other trees of the same kind within 500 feet that are also large enough. As part of the move, you may pass into one of those trees, appearing in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the destination tree. If you have movement remaining, you may repeat this process, moving from tree to tree as part of the same move. Each time you use this ability, it costs 5 feet of movement. You can use this ability as long as your concentration lasts.
