# Control Winds

You take control of the air in a 100-foot cube that you can see within range. Choose one of the following effects when you cast the spell. The effect lasts for the spell’s duration, unless you use your action on a later turn to switch to a different effect. You can have only one effect active at a time.

---

> **Control Winds**
> _5th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 300 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> **Gusts.** A wind picks up within the cube, constantly blowing in a horizontal direction that you choose. You can change the direction as an action. The wind’s speed is 100 feet per round. Any creature moving against the wind must spend 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot moved.
>
> **Downdraft.** You cause a sustained blast of strong wind to blow downward from the top of the cube. Any creature in the cube when the effect appears must make a Strength saving throw. A creature takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature must also make this save when it enters the cube for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there. The gusts cause ranged weapon attacks that pass through the area or that are made by creatures within the area to be made with disadvantage.
>
> **Updraft.** You cause a sustained updraft within the cube, rising upward from the cube’s bottom. Any creature in the cube when the effect appears must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature must also make this save when it enters the cube for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there. The updraft causes ranged weapon attacks that pass through the area or that are made by creatures within the area to be made with disadvantage.
