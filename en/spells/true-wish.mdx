# True Wish

For one sentence, the quill is yours: you rewrite a line of the rules of reality, and the Canvas exacts its price.

---

> **True Wish**  
> _12th-level Conjuration (Legendary)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> State your desire to the Game Director as precisely as possible, and reality reshapes to accommodate it. Among what has been achieved before:  
>  
> - You create one object of up to 25,000 gp in value that isn't an heirloom. The object can be no more than 300 feet in any dimension, and it appears in an unoccupied space you can see on the ground.  
> - You allow up to twenty creatures that you can see to regain all hit points, and you end all effects on them that _Restoration_ could remove.  
> - You grant up to ten creatures that you can see resistance to a damage type you choose.  
> - You grant up to ten creatures you can see immunity to a single spell or other magical effect for 8 hours.  
> - You undo a single recent event by forcing a reroll of any roll made within the last round (including your last turn). Reality reshapes itself to accommodate the new result. You can force the reroll to be made with advantage or disadvantage, and you can choose whether to use the reroll or the original roll.  
>  
> You might achieve something beyond these examples. The Game Director has great latitude in ruling what occurs; the greater the wish, the greater the likelihood that something goes wrong. The working might simply fail, the effect might be partly achieved, or you might suffer an unforeseen consequence of your exact wording. The Canvas does not grant — it settles accounts.  
>  
> **The Price.** The stress of holding the quill weakens you. Each time you cast a spell until you finish a Recovery, you take [% 1d10 dark %] per level of that spell; this damage can't be reduced or prevented in any way. Your Strength drops to 3, if it isn't lower, for [% 2d4 %] days; each of those days spent resting reduces the remaining time by 2 days. Finally, there is a 33 percent chance that the Canvas closes to you: you can never cast True Wish again.
