# Fortune's Favor

You impart latent luck to yourself or one willing creature you can see within range.

---

> **Fortune's Favor**
> _2nd-level Divination D_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: 1 hour
>
> You impart latent luck to yourself or one willing creature you can see within range. When the chosen creature makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw before the spell ends, it can roll an additional d20 and choose which die to use. Alternatively, when an attack roll is made against the creature, it can choose to roll an additional d20 and choose which die to use. Once this additional die is rolled, the spell ends.
>
> If the spell ends before the creature uses the luck, the creature gains no benefit from the spell.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
