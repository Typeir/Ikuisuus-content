# Premonition

Your senses become uniquely attuned to the motions of the world, letting you react faster to events not yet clear in the grooves of causality.

> **Premonition**  
> _1st-level Divination DC_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 16 hours
>  
> You touch one willing creature. For the duration, the target can add [% 1d8 %] to its initiative rolls.
