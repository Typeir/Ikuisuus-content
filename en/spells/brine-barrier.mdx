# Brine Barrier

A towering curtain of churning water erupts along a line, roaring outward and battering anything in its path.

---

> **Brine Barrier**  
> _3rd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You conjure up a wall of water on the ground, or over the water's surface at a point you can see within range. You can make the wall up to 30 feet long, 10 feet high, and 1 foot thick, or you can make a ringed wall up to 20 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 1 foot thick. The wall vanishes when the spell ends.
>
> The wall’s space is difficult terrain.
>
> Any ranged weapon attack that enters the wall’s space has disadvantage on the attack roll, and fire damage is halved if the fire effect passes through the wall to reach its target. Spells that deal cold damage that pass through the wall cause the area of the wall they pass through to freeze solid (at least a 5-foot-square section is frozen). Each 5-foot-square frozen section has AC 5 and 15 hit points. Reducing a frozen section to 0 hit points destroys it. When a section is destroyed, the water doesn’t fill it.
>
> Any part of the spell cast entirely over water that's at least 5 ft deep, instead is 5 feet thick, grants half cover and reduces fire damage to a quarter. Additionally, creatures must swim to cross that space, and if that section of wall is frozen, it has twice as much HP.
