# Chromatic Bloom

The shape light takes while within a prism, a scattered radiance, searching for release.

---

> **Chromatic Bloom**  
> _1st-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (15-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a shard of refined alchemical precursor worth at least 50 gp)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Choose a damage type from a stratum your slot level has unlocked (see the table below). Each creature in a 15-foot cone makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 2d8 %] damage of the chosen type on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
>
> If two of the damage dice show the same number, the bloom does not close: each creature that failed its save is **blinded** until the end of your next turn.
>
> | Slot Level | Strata unlocked                       |
> | ---------- | ------------------------------------- |
> | 1st        | Elemental (frost, fire, lightning)    |
> | 4th        | + Somatic (poison, chemical, psychic) |
> | 8th        | + Akashic (holy, dark, force)         |
> | 12th       | + True                                |
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 1st.
