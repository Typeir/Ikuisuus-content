# Arcane Hacking (UA)

Razor filaments of shimmering light lash outward, slicing arcane patterns and leaving ragged tears in the air.

---

> **Arcane Hacking (UA)**
> _2nd-level Transmutation T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
>
> You temporarily gain the ability to manipulate magical effects as if you were a master of arcane code, allowing you to alter the properties of spells and magical items.
>
> For the duration, you can use your action to attempt to alter a magical effect within 30 feet of you. Make an Intelligence (Arcana) check against a DC equal to 10 + the spell’s level or the item’s rarity (common 10, uncommon 12, rare 15, very rare 18, legendary 20). On a success, you can change one property of the effect or item, such as its color, sound, or minor function, as determined by the DM. The change lasts for the duration of the spell or until you lose concentration.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can affect one additional magical effect for each slot level above 2nd.
