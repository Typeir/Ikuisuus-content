# Spray of Cards

Razor cards erupt from your palm in a widening cone, spinning and slicing anything in their path.

---

> **Spray of Cards**  
> _2nd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (15-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S, M  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
>  
> A spray of shimmering, magical playing cards shoots from your fingertips.  
>  
> Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes [% 3d8 force %] on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 2nd.
