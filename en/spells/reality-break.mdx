# Reality Break

Reality tears open around the target, folding nearby matter into a jagged void.

---

> **Reality Break**
> _8th-level Conjuration DC_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You shatter the barriers between realities and drive a creature mad with the revelation of worlds beyond the material plane. Choose one creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 5d12 force damage and suffer a random effect from the Reality Break Effects table at the start of each of its turns for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw, ending the spell on itself on a success.
>
> **Reality Break Effects**
>
> | d10 | Effect |
> |-----|--------|
> | 1-2 | Vision of the Far Realm. The target's speed is halved, and it can't take reactions until the end of its next turn. |
> | 3-4 | Rending Rift. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 5d12 force damage, or half as much damage on a successful save. |
> | 5-6 | Wormhole. The target is teleported to an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within 30 feet of it. |
> | 7-8 | Chill of the Dark Void. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 5d12 cold damage, or half as much damage on a successful save. |
> | 9-10 | Mindscarring Visions. The target must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take 5d12 psychic damage, or half as much damage on a successful save. |
