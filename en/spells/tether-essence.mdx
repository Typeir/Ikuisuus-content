> **Tether Essence**
> _7th-level Necromancy D_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You forge a magical bond between two creatures, linking their life forces.
>
> Choose two creatures within range that you can see. Each creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. If both succeed, the spell fails. If at least one fails, the spell creates a tether between the two. While the spell lasts, whenever one of the tethered creatures takes damage, the other takes the same amount of damage. The spell ends if either creature drops to 0 hit points or dies.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, you can target one additional pair of creatures for each slot level above 7th. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
