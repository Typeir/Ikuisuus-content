# Wild Cunning (UA)

A thin, quicksilver haze wraps the target, sharpening perception and tightening reflexive motion.

---

> **Wild Cunning (UA)**
> _1st-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action R
> **Range**: 120 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You call out to the spirits of nature to aid you. Choose a point within range. Plant life within a 20-foot radius centered on that point either grows to obscure the area or is cleared away (your choice). If you choose to have the plant life obscure the area, it becomes difficult terrain for the duration. If you choose to clear the area, all ordinary vegetation in the area is cut down.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the radius of the area increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st.
