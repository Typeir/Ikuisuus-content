# Reincarnate

A seed of fate takes root in living flesh, and over a long year a tree grows that bears a single, breathing fruit.

---

> **Reincarnate**  
> _5th-level Transmutation (ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 year (a ritual tended each day)  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a seed of the Tree of Fate, and a drop of the dead one's blood or a relic that was truly theirs)  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You plant a **fate-seed** in your own living body, or that of a willing host, together with what remains of one who has died. Over the span of a year, the **Tree of Fate** takes root and grows upon and through the host, feeding on their days: for as long as the ritual runs, the host cannot regain hit points from a Recovery beyond half their maximum, and they carry the tree's mark ever after.
>
> At the ritual's end the tree bears a single fruit, and from it steps the reincarnated one, grown a **new adult body**. The dead must have been named and known to you, and no other working may already hold their fate — a will made an immovable part of reality, or one unmade, does not answer.
>
> The new body's shape is drawn by fate, not chosen: it is a living person of the world, though seldom the same face. The GM and the returning player determine the new form and its traits. The reincarnated one recalls their former life and remains who they were, taking on the capabilities of the new body in place of the old.
