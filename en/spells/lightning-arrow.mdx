# Lightning Arrow

Electrical energy condenses along the arrow, which streaks forward crackling and flinging jagged arcs at the point of impact.

---

> **Lightning Arrow**
> _3rd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Bonus Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> The next time you make a ranged weapon attack during the spell’s duration, the weapon’s ammunition, or the weapon itself if it’s a thrown weapon, transforms into a bolt of lightning. Make the attack roll as normal. If the attack hits, the target takes 4d8 lightning damage instead of the weapon’s normal damage. Whether you hit or miss, each creature within 10 feet of the target must make a Dexterity saving throw. Each of these creatures takes 2d8 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The piece of ammunition or weapon then returns to its normal form.
>
> If you draw a weapon or piece of ammunition before the spell ends, it transforms into a bolt of lightning as described above.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, both the initial damage and the secondary damage increase by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
