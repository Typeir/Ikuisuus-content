# Compelled Duel

You name a challenge aloud and something older than either of you writes it down. Their blood recognises the terms even where they do not.

---

> **Compelled Duel**  
> _1st-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> One creature you can see within range must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure it is bound to the challenge for the duration: it has **disadvantage on attack rolls against anyone but you**, and each time it tries to move to a space more than 30 feet from you it must make a Wisdom saving throw, succeeding to move freely for that turn.
>
> **The spell ends if you attack or target any creature other than your challenged foe**, or if you end your turn more than 30 feet away from it. An oath kept badly is not kept.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, both the range and the leash distance increase by **15 feet** for each slot level above 1st. A larger circle is still a circle.
