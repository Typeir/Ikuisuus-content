# Water Walk

A translucent crust forms atop water, bearing weight like stone.

---

> **Water Walk**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation (Ritual)_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a scrap of cork, or a cupful of dried brine)  
> **Duration**: 8 hours  
>  
> Up to ten willing creatures you can see within range gain the ability to move across any liquid surface — water, chemical, mud, snow, quicksand, or lava — as though it were harmless solid ground. Crossing molten lava still means crossing something that is on fire; the surface holds, the heat does not care.  
>  
> If you target a creature already submerged, the spell carries it to the surface at a rate of 60 feet per round.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target five additional willing creatures for each slot level above 3rd.
