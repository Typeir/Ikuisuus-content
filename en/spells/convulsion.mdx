# Convulsion

Invisible filaments seize the target's nervous system, wrenching limbs into violent, uncontrollable spasms.

---

> **Convulsion**  
> _6th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Choose one creature that you can see within range. The target is wracked by violent convulsions for the duration: limbs jerking, body seizing, muscles locked in spasm.  
>  
> A convulsing creature must use all its movement to thrash without leaving its space and has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws and attack rolls. While the target is affected by this spell, other creatures have advantage on attack rolls against it. As a Major Action, a convulsing creature makes a Wisdom saving throw to regain control of itself. On a successful save, the spell ends.
