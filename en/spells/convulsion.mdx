# Convulsion

Invisible filaments seize the target's nervous system, wrenching limbs into violent, uncontrollable spasms.

---

> **Convulsion**  
> _6th-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
>  
> Choose one creature that you can see within range. **It must succeed on a Constitution saving throw** or be wracked by violent convulsions for the duration: limbs jerking, body seizing, muscles locked in spasm. Something in the flesh has been misread, and the flesh is doing its best to comply.  
>  
> A convulsing creature must use all its movement to thrash without leaving its space, and has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws and attack rolls. Other creatures have advantage on attack rolls against it.  
>  
> As a **Major Action** — its whole turn's worth — a convulsing creature can make a Wisdom saving throw to take itself back. On a success, the spell ends.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can affect one additional creature for each slot level above 6th. Each saves separately, and each must buy its own way out.
