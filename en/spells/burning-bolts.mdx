# Burning Bolts

Touched arrows flare into seething flame that clings to the shaft and burns whatever they pierce.

---

> **Burning Bolts**  
> _3rd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 4 hours
>  
> You touch a quiver containing arrows or bolts. When a target is hit by a ranged weapon attack using a piece of ammunition drawn from the quiver, all damage is dealt as fire damage and the target gains the [_Burning_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#burning) condition. The spell’s magic ends on the piece of ammunition when it hits or misses, and the spell ends when six pieces of ammunition have been drawn from the quiver.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can affect six additional pieces of ammunition for each slot level above 3rd.
