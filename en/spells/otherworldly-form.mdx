# Otherworldly Form (UA)

Your flesh thins and rearranges into a jagged, otherworldly shape while the air around you ripples with cold distortion.

---

> **Otherworldly Form (UA)**
> _6th-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> You alter your physical form to become otherworldly, taking on aspects of a celestial or fiend.
>
> Until the spell ends, your body is infused with magic, and you gain the following benefits:
>
> - You are immune to the following conditions: charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, and poisoned.
> - You are immune to poison and nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
> - You have a flying speed of 40 feet and can hover.
> - You can make one attack with your spellcasting ability modifier as a bonus action on each of your turns.
> - You have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
> - You can use your action to end one spell or magical effect on yourself.
>
> When you cast the spell, choose celestial or fiend. If you choose celestial, you emit bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. If you choose fiend, you have advantage on attack rolls against fiends and undead.
