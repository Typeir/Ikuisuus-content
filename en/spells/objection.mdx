# Objection

A jagged seam of muted light lashes outward, and conjured energies shatter into dull embering motes.

---

> **Objection**  
> _1st-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Reaction, taken when you see a creature within 60 feet of you casting a spell or when a magical effect activates within range — or 1 Major Action, to unweave standing magic  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous  
>  
> **Objection (Reaction).** You thrust your refusal between a caster and their working. Make a saving throw using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals the triggering caster's spell save DC, increased by the difference between the triggering effect's level and the slot level you cast this spell at (minimum increase of 0). On a success, the spell or effect fails and has no effect.  
>  
> If the triggering effect is not a traditional spell, the DC is instead 10 + its creator's tier bonus + the difference. If the creator cannot feasibly be determined, the DC is 13 + the difference.  
>  
> **Unweaving (Major Action).** Choose one creature, object, or magical effect within range. One spell or magical effect of your choice on the target ends if its level is equal to or lower than the slot level you cast this spell at. Against a higher-level effect, make the saving throw described above; on a success, it ends.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the level difference used for the DC shrinks accordingly, and the Unweaving ends one additional spell or magical effect on the target for each slot level above 1st.
