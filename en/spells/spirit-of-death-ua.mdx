# Spirit of Death (UA)

A cold, skeletal mist pours from your hand, its tendrils latching onto flesh as warmth and movement drain into flickering grey motes.

---

> **Spirit of Death (UA)**
> _4th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 60 feet
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
>
> You call forth a spirit of death, which appears in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The spirit is an incorporeal, skeletal figure cloaked in shadow. It uses the Spirit of Death stat block. The spirit disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
>
> The spirit is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the spirit shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
>
> **Spirit of Death**
> *Medium Undead, Neutral*
>
> **Armor Class** 14 (natural armor)
> **Hit Points** 45 (6d8 + 18)
> **Speed** 0 ft., fly 40 ft. (hover)
>
> STR 6 (−2)  DEX 16 (+3)  CON 16 (+3)  INT 10 (+0)  WIS 14 (+2)  CHA 16 (+3)
>
> **Damage Resistances** acid, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> **Damage Immunities** cold, necrotic, poison
> **Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained
> **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
> **Languages** understands the languages you speak
> **Challenge** —
>
> ***Incorporeal Movement.*** The spirit can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
>
> ***Turn Immunity.*** The spirit is immune to effects that turn undead.
>
> ***Actions***
>
> ***Reaping Scythe.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 2d8 + 3 necrotic damage.
>
> ***Life Drain (Recharge 5–6).*** The spirit targets one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC or take 4d8 necrotic damage, and the spirit regains a number of hit points equal to half the amount of necrotic damage dealt.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the spirit’s hit points increase by 10 for each slot level above 4th.
