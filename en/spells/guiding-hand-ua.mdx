# Guiding Hand (UA)

A pale, spectral hand materializes above the chosen target, pressing with steady force to guide their movements.

---

> **Guiding Hand (UA)**
> _1st-level Divination_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute R
> **Range**: 5 feet
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 8 hours
>
> You create a Tiny, invisible, spectral hand that points toward a location you describe to the hand. You must describe a location you have visited or a well-known landmark, such as a city, mountain, castle, or battlefield. The hand points in the direction of the location for the duration, moving as you move, no matter the distance between you and the location. If you are unable to describe the location, the spell fails.
