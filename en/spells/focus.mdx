# Focus

Clear the mind of superfluous thought and sharpen the wit and will.

---

> **Focus**  
> _Divination cantrip_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
> 
> You touch one willing creature. If you aren't the target, close your eyes while concentrating.
> 
> Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one check or saving throw of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the ability check. The spell then ends.
