# Command

A sharp syllable lashes outward as a visible ripple, and the subject's limbs lock into a single involuntary motion.

---

> **Command**  
> _1st-level Enchantment_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: 1 round
>
> You speak a one-word command to a creature you can see within range. The word is not instruction but imposition; it does not ask, and it is not interested in being understood so much as obeyed. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or obey on its next turn. The spell fails if the target does not understand your language, or if the command is directly harmful to it.
>
> Some typical commands follow. You may issue others; if the target cannot follow the command you give, the spell ends.
>
> Approach. The target moves toward you by the shortest and most direct route, ending its turn if it moves within 5 feet of you.
>
> Drop. The target drops whatever it is holding and then ends its turn.
>
> Flee. The target spends its turn moving away from you by the fastest available means.
>
> Grovel. The target falls prone and then ends its turn.
>
> Halt. The target doesn’t move and takes no actions. A flying creature stays aloft, provided it is able to do so. If it must move to stay aloft, it flies the minimum distance needed to remain in the air.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional creature for each slot level above 1st. The creatures must be within 60 feet of each other when you target them.
