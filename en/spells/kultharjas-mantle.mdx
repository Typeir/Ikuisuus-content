# Kultharja's Mantle

A long forgotten oath-spell, now known only to those who are blessed by Kultharja's light.

> “May the sun shine upon these lands”

---

> **Kultharja's Mantle**  
> _3rd-Level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self (Aura)  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: 10 minutes.
>
> While this spell is active, the radius of an aura of your choice produced by you increases by **10 feet**.
>
> The aura **sheds bright light** throughout its area and **dim light** for an additional 30 feet beyond it. This is considered nonmagical **sunlight**
>
> **Magical darkness** is **suppressed** within the aura’s area for the duration. If an area of magical darkness overlaps the aura, the suppressed portion returns when the spell ends.
>
> **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the aura’s radius increases by an additional **5 feet** for each slot level above 3rd.
