> **True Strike**
> _Divination cantrip_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: 30 feet
> **Components**: S
> **Duration**: Concentration up to 1 round
>
> You point a finger at a target in range. Your magic grants you a brief insight into the target’s defenses.
>
> On your next turn, you gain advantage on your first attack roll against the target, provided that this spell hasn’t ended.
