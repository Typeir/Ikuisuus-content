# Gift of Alacrity

A pale electric shimmer flows through the touch, honing muscles and snapping reflexes into sharp, immediate motion.

---

> **Gift of Alacrity**
> _1st-level Divination DC_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> You touch one willing creature. For the duration, the target can add 1d8 to its initiative rolls.
