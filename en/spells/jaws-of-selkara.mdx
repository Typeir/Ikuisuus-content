# Jaws of Selkara

A spell that manifests a vast pair of ossified jaws, snapping shut in a single, grinding bite before vanishing.

---

> **Jaws of Selkara**  
> _3rd-level Conjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: Self (25-foot cone)  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> You call forth a massive pair of bone jaws that erupt outward from you in a **25-foot cone** and snap shut.
>
> Each creature in the area must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **12d4 piercing damage** on a failed save, or half as much damage on a success.
>
> Creatures that are **Large or larger** have disadvantage on the saving throw.
>
> The jaws vanish immediately after closing.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **4th level or higher**, the damage increases by **1d4** per slot level above 3rd.
