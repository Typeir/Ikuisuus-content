# Spiritual Weapon

You are not holding it and it is not there, and it will still take a man's arm off at the shoulder.

---

> **Spiritual Weapon**  
> _2nd-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minor Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 minute
>
> You shape a weapon out of nothing at a point within range. Choose any melee weapon from the weapon list that **you are proficient with**; the shaped weapon has that weapon's reach and properties, and what you choose is what you get. It lasts for the duration or until you cast this spell again.
>
> When you cast the spell, and as a **Minor Action** on each of your turns, you can move the weapon up to 20 feet and make one melee spell attack against a creature within its reach. On a hit, the target takes [% 1d8 force %] plus your spellcasting ability modifier.
>
> People shape what their god was known to carry. Followers of the Golden One or his echoes raise Rhompaia of will and null; those of Kalmora, a grave-spade, and they dig with it as often as they swing it.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** For each slot level above 2nd, the weapon's damage die advances one step along the [Dice Sequence](/en/library/rules/arms-armour-and-burden/weapons-and-dice-levels) — [% 1d8 %] to [% 1d10 %] to [% 1d12 %] to [% 2d8 %], and onward.
