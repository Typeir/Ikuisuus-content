# Magnify Gravity

For one moment you let the Canvas press down where it has always wanted to. A dead world is heavy, and it is only politeness that keeps it off you.

---

> **Magnify Gravity**  
> _1st-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 60 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 1 round  
>  
> The gravity in a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you can see within range increases for a moment. Each creature in the sphere on the turn you cast the spell must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes [% 2d8 force %], and its speed is halved until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and suffers no reduction to its speed.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 1st.
