# Sunbeam

Kultharja is very far away and leans closer for nobody. What you are given is a borrowed inch of that light, held in the hand, and an inch of it is more than enough.

---

> **Sunbeam**  
> _6th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self (60-foot line)  
> **Components**: V, S, M (a lens of polished quartz)  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> A beam of brilliant light flashes from your hand in a 5-foot-wide, 60-foot-long line. Each creature in the line must make a Constitution saving throw, taking [% 6d8 holy %] and being **blinded** until your next turn on a failure, or half as much damage and no blindness on a success.
>
> As a **Major Action** on any turn until the spell ends, you can loose a new line.
>
> For the duration, a mote of that light burns in your hand, shedding bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. **This light is sunlight** — see [Vision, Light, and Environment](/en/library/rules/the-long-road/vision-light-and-environment).
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by [% 1d8 %] for each slot level above 6th.
