# Elemental Ward

A translucent membrane of crackling energy envelops the target, forcing incoming elemental forces to arc away from its surface.

---

> **Elemental Ward**  
> _3rd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> For the duration, the willing creature you touch has resistance to **frost, fire, and lightning** damage — the whole Elemental stratum, not a choice from it.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can touch one additional willing creature for every two slot levels above 3rd.
