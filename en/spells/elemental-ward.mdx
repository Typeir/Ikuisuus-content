# Elemental Ward

A translucent membrane of crackling energy envelops the target, forcing incoming elemental forces to arc away from its surface.

---

> **Elemental Ward**  
> _3rd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>  
> For the duration, the willing creature you touch has resistance to cold, fire, and lightning damage.
