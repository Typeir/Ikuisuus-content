# Tiny Servant

You touch one Tiny, nonmagical object that isn’t attached to another object or a surface and isn’t being carried by another creature. The target animates into a creature under your control until the spell ends or the creature drops to 0 hit points.

---

> **Tiny Servant**
> _3rd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Minute  
> **Range**: Touch  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: 8 hours
>
> The servant animates at your command, using the statistics in the Tiny Servant stat block. When you cast the spell, you can mentally command the creature if it is within 120 feet of you (if you control multiple servants, you can command any or all of them at the same time, issuing the same command to each one). You decide what action the servant will take and where it will move during its next turn, or you can issue a general command, such as to fetch a key, stand watch, or stack some books. If you issue no commands, the servant does nothing other than defend itself against hostile creatures. Once given an order, the servant continues to follow it until its task is complete.
>
> When the servant drops to 0 hit points, it reverts to its original form, and any remaining damage carries over to that form.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can animate two additional objects for each slot level above 3rd.
