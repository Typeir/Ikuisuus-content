# Archivists' Barrier

A translucent dome of concentrated thought forms around the target, muting external noise and pushing away intrusive mental forces.

---

> **Archivists' Barrier**  
> _3rd-level Abjuration_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action  
> **Range**: 30 feet  
> **Components**: V  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 4 hours
>  
> For the duration, you or one willing creature you can see within range has resistance to psychic damage, as well as advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.  If it succeeds, it can use its reaction to take no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, interposing the mental barrier between itself and the effect.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
