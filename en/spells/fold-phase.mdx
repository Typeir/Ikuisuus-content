# Fold Phase

You briefly step into the unreality between the folds and your world, every movement thereafter, uncannily ethereal and swift.

> **Fold Phase**  
> _7th-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Up to 8 hours  
>  
> You step sideways into the **thin Folds** — the creases that lie over the world without quite touching it — and remain there for the duration, or until you use a Major Action to step back out. You can move in any direction; moving up or down costs an extra foot for every foot travelled. You can see and hear the world you left, but it is grey and flat from in here, and nothing more than 60 feet away resolves at all.  
>  
> While in the Folds you can affect, and be affected by, only what is in there with you. Anything outside cannot perceive you or interact with you unless something specifically grants it the ability.  
>  
> You ignore objects and effects that are not in the Folds with you, and so walk through the walls of the world as though they were the suggestion of walls. When the spell ends you return to the world in the space you currently occupy. If something solid is already there, you are shunted to the nearest space that can hold you and take force damage equal to twice the number of feet you were moved.  
>  
> The spell does nothing if you are already in the Folds when you cast it.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, you can target up to three willing creatures (including yourself) for each slot level above 7th. The creatures must be within 10 feet of you when you cast the spell.
