# Rite of Empty Skies

A rite that smothers the battlefield beneath a lightless vault, severing sight from sky and horizon alike.

---

> **Rite of Empty Skies**  
> _4th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 action  
> **Range**: 90 feet  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>
> You conjure a vast shroud of supernatural darkness centered on a point you can see within range.
>
> The darkness fills a **40-foot-radius sphere** and spreads around corners.  
> Nonmagical light cannot illuminate it, and magical light of **4th level or lower** is suppressed within the area.
>
> If any part of this spell’s area overlaps with an area of light created by a **spell of 4th level or lower**, the spell that created the light is **dispelled**.
>
> You and up to **three creatures of your choice** can see normally through this darkness.
>
> Creatures that cannot see through the darkness are **effectively blinded** while inside it.
>
> The darkness persists for the duration or until your concentration ends.
>
> **At Higher Levels.**  
> When you cast this spell using a spell slot of **5th level or higher**, you may choose **one additional creature** that can see through the darkness for each slot level above 4th.
