> **Elemental Weapon**
> _3rd-level Transmutation_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. The weapon now deals an extra 1d4 damage of the chosen type when it hits.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th or 5th level, the bonus increases to +2 and the extra damage increases to 2d4. When you use a spell slot of 6th or 7th level, the bonus increases to +3 and the extra damage increases to 3d4.
