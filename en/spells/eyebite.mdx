# Eyebite

A pair of spectral eyes flare and lock onto a target, flooding their vision with vertigo and nausea.

---

> **Eyebite**  
> _6th-level Necromancy_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
>  
> For the duration, your eyes become an inky void imbued with dread power. Until the spell ends, you can use a **Major Action** on each of your turns to fix one creature you can see within 60 feet. **That creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or suffer one effect of your choice:**  
>  
> **Asleep.** The target falls **unconscious**. It wakes if it takes any damage, or if another creature uses a Major Action to shake it awake.  
>  
> **Panicked.** The target is **frightened** of you. On each of its turns it must Dash away from you by the safest available route, unless there is nowhere to go. The effect ends if the target gets at least 60 feet from you and can no longer see you.  
>  
> **Sickened.** The target is **poisoned**. At the end of each of its turns it repeats the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.  
>  
> A creature that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to your gaze for 24 hours. Constructs, and anything that cannot be charmed, are immune to this spell entirely.  
>  
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, each use of your Major Action can fix **one additional creature** for each slot level above 6th. They must all be within 30 feet of one another, and each saves separately against the effect you chose.
