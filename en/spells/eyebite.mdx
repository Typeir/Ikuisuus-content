> **Eyebite**
> _6th-level Necromancy_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Self
> **Components**: V, S
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> For the spell’s duration, your eyes become an inky void imbued with dread power. Until the spell ends, you can use your action on each of your turns to target one creature you can see within 60 feet of you and affect it with one of the following effects:
>
> **Asleep.** The target falls unconscious. It wakes up if it takes any damage or if another creature uses its action to shake the sleeper awake.
>
> **Panicked.** The target is frightened of you. On each of its turns, the frightened creature must take the Dash action and move away from you by the safest available route, unless there is nowhere to move. If the target moves to a place at least 60 feet away from you where it can no longer see you, this effect ends.
>
> **Sickened.** The target has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks. At the end of each of its turns, it can make a Wisdom saving throw. If it succeeds, the effect ends.
>
> A creature that successfully saves against this effect is immune to it for 24 hours. Undead and creatures that can’t be charmed are immune to this spell.
