# Bolide Bombardment

You reach past the dead heaven and take hold of the Opaline Belt. What comes down is only stone until it meets the air; what the air makes of it is never the same twice.

---

> **Bolide Bombardment**  
> _9th-level Evocation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: 1 mile  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Instantaneous
>
> Four stones fall out of the Belt at four different points you can see within range. Each creature in a 40-foot-radius sphere centered on each point makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking [% 10d12 bludgeoning %] from the impact on a failed save, or half as much on a success. The spheres spread around corners.
>
> **Opaline Shift.** Each stone burns a different colour on the way down. Roll a d10 separately for each of the four impacts to determine what the stone was carrying; a creature in that sphere takes a further [% 10d12 %] damage of that type on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
>
> | d10 | Stratum   | Damage    |
> | --- | --------- | --------- |
> | 1   | Elemental | frost     |
> | 2   | Elemental | fire      |
> | 3   | Elemental | lightning |
> | 4   | Somatic   | poison    |
> | 5   | Somatic   | chemical  |
> | 6   | Somatic   | psychic   |
> | 7   | Akashic   | holy      |
> | 8   | Akashic   | dark      |
> | 9   | Akashic   | force     |
> | 10  | True      | true      |
>
> A creature caught in more than one sphere is affected only once, by the impact of its choice. The spell damages objects in the area and ignites flammable objects that aren't being worn or carried.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 10th level or higher, **one additional stone falls** for each slot level above 9th. Roll the Opaline Shift separately for every impact.
