# Protection from Ballistics (UA)

A shell of condensed force ripples into being around the touched target, causing bolts and arrows to shatter or glance off its surface.

---

> **Protection from Ballistics (UA)**
> _3rd-level Abjuration T_
> **Casting Time**: 1 Action
> **Range**: Touch
> **Components**: V, S, M
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
>
> You touch one willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to nonmagical ranged weapon attacks.
