# Paivatar's Boon

Something of the harpooned sun still leaks from where it was opened. Stand in it long enough and your flesh remembers a shape it was never given.

---

> **Paivatar's Boon**  
> _2nd-level Transmutation_  
> **Casting Time**: 1 Major Action  
> **Range**: Self  
> **Components**: V, S  
> **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 hour
>
> You draw a little of Päivätär's spilled radiance into yourself and let it finish something your bloodline left unfinished.
>
> Choose **5 Boon Points** worth of options from **your own bloodline's Boon table** — your bloodline's, no one else's. You gain those boons for the duration.
>
> You cannot choose a boon you already have, and you must meet any requirement the option lists. Unspent points are lost. A boon that changes your size does not resize what you are wearing or carrying.
>
> **At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you gain **2 additional Boon Points** for each slot level above 2nd.
