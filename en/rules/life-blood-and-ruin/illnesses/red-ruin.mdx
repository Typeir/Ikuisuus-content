# Red Ruin

Rubedo, the Red Rebis, is the source of a catastrophic affliction known as **Red Ruin**.  
Red Ruin is not a disease, curse, or toxin—it is the result of **extreme ionizing radiation exposure**, where flesh, blood, and thought unravel at the cellular level.

Rubedo spreads Red Ruin through supercritical emissions, spatial annihilation, and direct proximity to its core output.

When afflicted by **Red Ruin**, a creature must roll on the table below to determine the specific systemic failure they suffer.  
**Multiple symptoms can stack.**

As a rule of thumb, **each time you gain Red Ruin**:
- You gain **1 level of exhaustion**
- You must make **one Permanent Death Saving Throw**
  - On a failure, the save remains failed until all Red Ruin is cured

| **Roll** | **Effect** |
|--------|-----------|
| 0~5 | **Prodromal Collapse**: Severe nausea and vertigo. Disadvantage on **Dexterity checks and saving throws**; you cannot **Dash**. |
| 6~10 | **Crushing Headache**: At the start of each of your turns, take **2d10 psychic damage**. |
| 11~15 | **Capillary Rupture**: Your skin bruises and splits under stress. Whenever you take damage, take an additional **1d10 true damage**. |
| 16~20 | **Coagulation Cascade**: Your blood clots uncontrollably. Disadvantage on **Constitution saving throws**; **critical hits** against you maximize all damage. |
| 21~25 | **Bone Marrow Liquefaction**: You can no longer produce blood cells. You **cannot regain hit points** except through magical healing, and magical healing you receive is **halved**. |
| 26~30 | **Degloving Injury**: Skin separates from underlying tissue. Whenever you move more than **10 feet** on your turn, take **2d10 true damage**. Whenever you take an action, **you take 2d10 true damage**. |
| 31~35 | **Chromosomal Failure**: Cellular replication collapses. Your **maximum hit points are reduced by 4d12** (minimum 1). |
| 36~40 | **Neural Misfiring**: Your brain fails under load. At the start of each of your turns, roll a d6—on **1–2** you lose your **action**; on **3–4** you lose your **bonus action**. |
| 41~45 | **Radiation Burns**: Deep tissue burns form without heat. You take **5 true damage** at the end of each of your turns. |
| 46~50 | **Vascular Sloughing**: Blood vessels rupture internally. At the end of each **long rest**, take **6d10 true damage**. |
| 51~55 | **Immune Erasure**: Your immune system collapses. You automatically **fail saving throws against disease and poison**, and have disadvantage on **death saving throws**. If you were immune to diseases, you are not anymore. |
| 56~60 | **Osteolysis**: Bone density collapses. You become vulnerable to **bludgeoning damage**, and falling damage against you is **quadrupled** and dice are maximized. |
| 61~65 | **Wounds That Will Not Heal**: Any wound reopens endlessly. You **cannot regain hit points during short rests**, and magical healing restores the **minimum possible result**. |
| 66~70 | **Neurovascular Shock**: You are **stunned** at the start of your first turn in each combat. |
| 71~75 | **Endocrine Collapse**: Hormonal regulation fails. **–4 Constitution** (score). |
| 76~80 | **Blood Pooling**: Circulation fails. Your **speed is halved**, and you **cannot take reactions**. |
| 81~85 | **Cerebral Edema**: Brain swelling causes confusion. While below half HP, you are affected as though by [_Confusion_](/en/library/spells/confusion). |
| 86~90 | **Multi-Organ Failure**: Heart, lungs, and kidneys begin to fail. You have disadvantage on **all checks, saves, and attack rolls**. |
| 91~95 | **Structural Fragility**: Your tissues tear like wet paper. You become vulnerable to **all damage**. |
| 96~98 | **Neural Death**: Large portions of the brain shut down. You are affected by [_Feeblemind_](/en/library/spells/feeblemind); this effect cannot end while you have any Red Ruin. |
| 99 | **Terminal Syndrome**: Every time you make a roll, increase the **DC of all future rolls by 1** and reduce your **maximum hit points by 1**. |
| 100 | **Systemic Collapse**: At the end of each of your turns, make a **DC 5 Constitution saving throw**. Each time you make this save, the DC increases by **5**. On a failure, you fail **one Permanent Death Saving Throw**. |

## Curing Red Ruin

Red Ruin is **not** a disease or curse.

- **Lesser Restoration**: No effect  
- **Remove Curse**: No effect  
- **Greater Restoration**: Removes **one** Red Ruin effect and **one** exhaustion level  
- **Wish**, **True Resurrection**, or equivalent magic: Removes all Red Ruin and clears Permanent Death Saving Throws  

If Red Ruin kills a creature, **Raise Dead** is insufficient—the body is biologically unsalvageable.
