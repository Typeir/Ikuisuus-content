# Poison of the Mind

The lord of Sulphur and madness, Xanthous, is the source of a terrible disease known as **Poison of the Mind**.
This illness is caused by his very pressence, the act of worship to the miracle, and the interaction with some of his servants.

When a character is afflicted, they must roll on the table below to determine the specific symptom they suffer.
Multiple symptoms can stack.

| **Roll** | **Effect**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0~5      | **Memory Erosion**: The affected individual forgets a crucial piece of information, suffering a -2 penalty on Intelligence checks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 6~10     | **Self-Hatred**: The individuals gains the impulse to hurt themselves. They take damage equal to their proficiency modifier every time they deal damage.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| 11~15    | **Fleeting Triumph**: Their next roll of a d20 becomes a natural 20 without rolling. Five next rolls after that, are rolled with disadvantage. This effect resets after a short rest.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| 16~20    | **Pig's tongue**: One of the creatures languages is replaced by Pigspeak, an useless language to communicate simple ideas with pigs.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 21~25    | **Loss of coordination**: They lose a part of their subconscious. Every time an attack roll lands on a "1", they hit themselves instead.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| 26~30    | **Paranoia of Betrayal**: Gain disadvantage on Persuasion, Insight and Animal handling checks, additionally, cannot be affected by the help action.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| 31~35    | **Chronic Regret**: The individual suffers a -1 penalty on Wisdom saving throws as they are distracted by their past decisions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 36~40    | **Compulsive Rituals**: Must perform a specific action before taking a long or short rest. Failure to do so results in exhaustion.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 41~45    | **Muscle Memory Loss**: The creatures mind-muscle connection becomes less stable, their strength score drops by two.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| 46~50    | **Sulphurous Thoughts**: They become obsessed with sulfur, and must ingest at least one portion of it once a day or suffer a level of exhaustion. Ingesting sulfur poisons them.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| 51~55    | **Obsessive Comparison**: They develop a -1 penalty to all ability checks, believing they can never measure up to others.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 56~60    | **Temporal Displacement**: They lose their sense of time, resulting in disadvantage to initiative rolls as they struggle to react.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 61~65    | **Loss of Recall**: They become disoriented, suffering disadvantage on Intelligence saving throws and Insight checks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |

| 100      | **Annihilation of the self**: The affected individual's conscience is detached from their body, excised like an alien object. ## Curing Poison of the Mind

Like any sickness of magical nature, it can be removed by the usage of the [_Lesser Restoration_](/en/library/spells/lesser-restoration), [_Greater Restoration_](/en/library/spells/greater-restoration) or [_Remove Curse_](/en/library/spells/remove-curse) spells, or similar magic.
