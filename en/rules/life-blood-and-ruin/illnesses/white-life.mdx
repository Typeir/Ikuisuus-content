# White Life

_Also known as the Fractal Curse or the Bloom of Albedo._

When exposed to Albedo’s flesh, a creature becomes infected with **White Life**—a corruption that forces the body to _grow_, _branch_, and _divide_ endlessly.  
When a creature contracts White Life, roll on the table below. Multiple results can stack.

| 06–10    | **Shrimp Hands**: The creature’s fingers elongate and segment, becoming shrimp-like appendages. | 16–20    | **Webbed Skin**: Thin membranes form between fingers, elbows, and knees. The creature’s speed is reduced by 5 feet, but it gains a swim speed of 20 feet.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| 21–25    | **Shell Patches**: Plates of hard chitin grow across the body’s joints. They stiffen movement and grind when the creature bends, giving it disadvantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |

## Curing White Life  
White Life is a magical disease. It can be removed by [_Lesser Restoration_](/en/library/spells/lesser-restoration), [_Greater Restoration_](/en/library/spells/greater-restoration), [_Remove Curse_](/en/library/spells/remove-curse), or an equivalent act of divine intervention.  
If not cured within seven days, roll again on this table at each dawn as the body continues to blossom out of control.
