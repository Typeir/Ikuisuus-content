# Flametongued

A Flametongued caster channels the destructive and purifying power of flame and radiance.
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,
along with other spells they may know. A flametongued creature is permanently affected by the [_Ashardalon's Stride_](/en/library/spells/flame-stride) spell.
