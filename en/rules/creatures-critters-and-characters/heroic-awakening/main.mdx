# Heroic Awakening

At the start of combat, when initiative is rolled, each creature also rolls a **Fate Die (d20)**.  
If the result meets or exceeds the creature’s **Heroic DC**, that creature becomes **Heroically Awakened**.

A Heroically Awakened creature immediately:

- Maximizes all hit dice.
- Increases its proficiency bonus, AC, and saving throws by +1.
- Gains an **Affix** from the table below, rolled.

This effect triggers only once per combat, during initiative.

---

## Heroic DC by CR

| CR    | Heroic DC |
| ----- | --------- |
| 1–5   | 15        |
| 6–10  | 16        |
| 11–15 | 17        |
| 16–20 | 18        |
| 21+   | 19        |

## Heroic Affix Table

When a creature becomes **Heroically Awakened**, roll **1d10** on the table below to determine its Affix.

If the result is **10**, roll again, but roll an extra **Affix**.  
The creature gains both resulting Affixes and is considered **Legendary**.  
While Legendary, it gains a **+2 bonus** to it's proficiency bonus, saving throws, and AC, and gains 2 HP per CR or level.

If either of those rerolls results in another 10, the creature gains a third Affix instead, becomes **Mythic**, and its bonus increases to **+3**,
and gains an additional 3 HP per CR or level.  
No further increases are made beyond this point, and the creature cannot gain more than 3 Affixes.
