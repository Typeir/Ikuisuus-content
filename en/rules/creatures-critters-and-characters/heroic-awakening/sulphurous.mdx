# Sulphurous

A Sulphurous Caster channels decay, venom, and corrosive alchemy — the rot that eats through flesh and steel alike.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,  
along with any other spells they may know.

A Sulphurous creature is **permanently affected by [Stinking Cloud](/en/library/spells/stinking-cloud)**, centered on itself.  
The cloud moves with the creature but does not obscure its own vision.
