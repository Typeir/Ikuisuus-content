# Psionic

A Psionic Creature can hold dominion over the minds of others with thought alone.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,  
along with any other spells they may know.

A Psionic creature is **permanently affected by [Mind Blank](/en/library/spells/mind-blank)** for the duration of its existence.
