# Stormbound

A Stormbound Caster wields the power of storms, wind and sea.
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,
along with other spells they may know. A stormbound creature is permanently affected by the [_Warding Wind_](/en/library/spells/warding-wind) spell.

| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Cantrips (0)** | Lightning Lure, Thunderclap,                                                                                                                                  |
| **1st**          | [Fog](/en/library/spells/fog), [Thunderwave](/en/library/spells/thunderwave), [Witch Bolt](/en/library/spells/witch-bolt)                                     |
| **2nd**          | [Shatter](/en/library/spells/shatter), [Skywrite](/en/library/spells/skywrite)                                                                                |
| **3rd**          | [Call Lightning](/en/library/spells/call-lightning), [Sleet Storm](/en/library/spells/sleet-storm), [Lightning Bolt](/en/library/spells/lightning-bolt)       |
| **4th**          | [Storm Sphere](/en/library/spells/storm-sphere), [Command The Seas](/en/library/spells/command-the-seas)                                                      |
| **5th**          | [Maelstrom](/en/library/spells/maelstrom), [Destructive Wave](/en/library/spells/destructive-wave), [Command The Skies](/en/library/spells/command-the-skies) |
| **6th**          | [Chain Lightning](/en/library/spells/chain-lightning), [Wind Walk](/en/library/spells/wind-walk)                                                              |
