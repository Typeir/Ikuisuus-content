# Monster Senses, Vulnerabilities, Resistances, Immunities, and Proficiencies

## Overview

This page explains how the Senses, Vulnerabilities, Resistances, Immunities, Languages, and Proficiency entries in a monster stat block work. Full rules for darkvision, blindsight, and truesight are at [Vision, Light, and Environmental Hazards](/en/library/rules/the-long-road/vision-light-and-environment). Damage resistance and vulnerability resolution is at [Damage](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/damage).

## Mechanics

### Vulnerabilities, Resistances, and Immunities

These entries modify how a monster takes damage or is affected by conditions. See [Damage](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/damage) for full resolution rules.

- **Vulnerability**: takes double damage from the listed type.
- **Resistance**: takes half damage from the listed type.
- **Immunity**: takes no damage from the listed type, or is unaffected by the listed condition.
- Resistance or immunity to **nonmagical attacks** requires the source to be magical (a spell, magic item, or other magical source) to bypass it.

### Senses and Passive Perception

The Senses entry lists the monster's passive Wisdom (Perception) score (10 + its Perception modifier) and any special senses. Special senses are defined below.

### Darkvision

Sees in darkness as dim light within the listed range; cannot discern colour in that darkness. See [Vision, Light, and Environmental Hazards](/en/library/rules/the-long-road/vision-light-and-environment).

### Blindsight

Perceives surroundings without relying on sight within the listed range, bypassing darkness and invisibility. See [Vision, Light, and Environmental Hazards](/en/library/rules/the-long-road/vision-light-and-environment).

### Truesight

Within the listed range: sees through magical darkness, invisibility, illusions, and shapechanged or magically transformed forms; also perceives the Fold. See [Vision, Light, and Environmental Hazards](/en/library/rules/the-long-road/vision-light-and-environment).

### Tremorsense

Detects and pinpoints vibrations within a specific radius, provided the monster and the source share contact with the same ground or substance. Cannot detect flying or incorporeal creatures.

### Telepathy

A magical ability allowing mental communication with a creature within the listed range.

- The target must understand at least one language but need not share one with the monster.
- A creature without telepathy can receive and respond to messages but cannot initiate contact.
- The monster can initiate or terminate contact without using an action.
- Incapacitation ends active contact and prevents initiating new contact.
- Contact ends when creatures leave range, or when the monster contacts a different creature.
- Antimagic fields block telepathic communication entirely.

### Languages

Spoken languages are listed alphabetically. If a monster understands a language but cannot speak it, this is noted. A dash (—) means the creature neither speaks nor understands any language.

### Armor, Weapon, and Tool Proficiencies

A monster is assumed proficient with any armor, weapons, and tools listed in its stat block. If equipment is changed, the GM decides whether proficiency applies. See [Monster Stat Blocks](/en/library/rules/creatures-critters-and-characters/monster-stat-blocks) for equipment recovery and usability notes.
