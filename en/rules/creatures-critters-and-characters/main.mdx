# Creatures, Critters, and Characters

In the world of Damocles, the abstract representation of what a creature can do or be is a stat block.
Characters are considered creatures. So are critters normally invisible to play such as ants. Everything that is not an Object, is represented by the same structure.

A stat block is a collection of statistics and traits that define a creature's capabilities, behaviors, and interactions within the game world. These rules cover how to read and interpret stat blocks, how to use them in play, and how they interact with the core mechanics of the game.

Creatures intended for use as enemies are often referred to as "monsters", but this is not a taxonomical distinction with any mechanical implication.
