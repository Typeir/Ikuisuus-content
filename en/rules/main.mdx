# Rules

The rules of Damocles are arranged by domain. Each section covers a specific area of play — from the fundamentals of ability scores and dice to the management of combat, survival, and confrontation with exceptional creatures.

## Sections

- **[The Measure of the Self](en/library/rules/the-measure-of-the-self)** — Ability scores, modifiers, skill checks, saving throws, and how rolls resolve.
- **[Steel and Strife](en/library/rules/steel-and-strife)** — Combat procedures: initiative, attacks, actions, conditions, damage types, and movement.
- **[Arms, Armour, and Burden](en/library/rules/arms-armour-and-burden)** — Weapons, weapon properties, weapon mastery, gear, and encumbrance.
- **[Life, Blood, and Ruin](en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin)** — Healing, death saves, resting, poisons, illnesses, and madness.
- **[Arcana and the Fold](en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold)** — Spellcasting, Heirlooms, the nature of magic in Damocles.
- **[The Long Road](en/library/rules/the-long-road)** — Travel, traps, vision, light, mounts, and environmental hazards.
- **[Hearth and Station](en/library/rules/hearth-and-station)** — Downtime, lifestyles, languages, backgrounds, and social systems.
- **[Creatures, Critters, and Characters](en/library/rules/creatures-critters-and-characters)** — Monster anatomy, Legendary Deeds, Heroic Awakening, and exceptional encounters.
