# Tools and Trinkets

## Overview

Gear carried on an adventure divides into two categories with different homes in the rules.

**Tools** — artisan's tools, kits, musical instruments, and the starting equipment packs that contain them — are the instruments of craft and trade. Their proficiency rules, cost table, and pack inventories live in [Tools and Trades](en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-and-trades). Rules for producing goods with those tools during downtime are in [Crafting and Creation](en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/crafting-and-creation).

**Trinkets** — consumables and adventuring items with active mechanical uses (lamps, lanterns, torches, oil, tinderboxes, poisons, potions, ropes, manacles, locks, and similar gear) — are catalogued as individual items. Each entry lists its effects, action cost, range, and any saving throw DCs.
