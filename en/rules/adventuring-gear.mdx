## Interactions

- Containers and pack entries indicate volume or weight capacity for storing gear; use the listed cubic-foot or pound limits when adjudicating encumbrance or stowage.
- Items that are grouped (for example: arrows (20), crossbow bolts (20), blowgun needles (50), sling bullets (20)) are presented with their packaged counts and listed weight/cost as shown.
- Monetary values and weights are reproduced exactly; entries with an em dash (—) indicate the original source lists no weight for that item.
