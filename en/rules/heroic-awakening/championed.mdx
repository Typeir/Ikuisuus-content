# Championed

A Championed creature embodies mastery of combat; the apex of martial discipline.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, it gains the following traits in addition to any other abilities it possesses.

---

## Features

- **Critical Mastery.** The creature’s critical hit range is **reduced by 1**.
- **Extra Attack.** The creature can make one additional attack whenever it takes the Attack action on its turn.
- **Legendary Resistance (1/Day).** If the creature fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.
- **Combat Maneuvers.** The creature gains access to the following maneuvers. It can use each maneuver **once per short or long rest**, regaining all expended uses when it finishes a rest.
  - **Pushing Attack.** When the creature hits a creature with a weapon attack, it can force the target to make a Strength saving throw (**DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Strength modifier**) or be pushed up to 15 feet away.
  - **Tripping Attack.** When the creature hits a creature with a weapon attack, it can force the target to make a Strength saving throw (**DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Strength modifier**) or be knocked prone.
  - **Disarming Attack.** When the creature hits a creature with a weapon attack, it can force the target to make a Strength saving throw (**DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Strength modifier**) or drop one item of its choice that it is holding. The object lands at its feet.
  - **Riposte.** When a creature misses it with a melee attack, the creature can use its reaction to make one melee weapon attack against that creature.
