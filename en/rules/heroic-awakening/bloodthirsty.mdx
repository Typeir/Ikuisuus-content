# Bloodthirsty

A Bloodthirsty creature fights through rage and pain, thriving in the heart of violence.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, it gains the following traits in addition to any other abilities it possesses.

The creature’s **Strength score becomes at least 10 + its proficiency bonus** (if not already higher).

---

## Features

- **Rage.**  
  As a bonus action, the creature can enter a rage that lasts for 1 minute.  
  While raging, it gains the following benefits:

  - Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
  - Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
  - Its melee weapon attacks deal an additional amount of damage equal to its proficiency bonus.
  - The rage ends early if the creature is incapacitated or has not attacked a hostile creature since its last turn.  
    The creature can rage **twice per long rest**.

- **Relentless Fury.**  
  When the creature is reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, it can make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw.  
  On a success, it drops to 1 hit point instead.  
  Each time it uses this feature after the first, the DC increases by 5.  
  The DC resets after a long rest.

- **Reckless Attack.**  
  When the creature makes its first attack on its turn, it can choose to attack recklessly, gaining advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls using Strength during this turn.  
  Attack rolls against the creature have advantage until the start of its next turn.
