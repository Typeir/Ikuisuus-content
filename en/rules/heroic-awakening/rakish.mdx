# Rakish

A Rakish creature embodies precision, cunning and opportunism.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, it gains the following traits in addition to any other abilities it possesses.

A Rakish creature’s **movement speed increases by 15 feet** and it permanently gains the abilities below.

- **Cunning Action.** On each of its turns, the creature can use a bonus action to take the **Dash**, **Disengage**, or **Hide** action.
- **Sneak Attack (1/Turn).** The creature deals extra damage (see table below) when it hits a target with an attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when an ally is within 5 feet of the target and not incapacitated.
- **Uncanny Dodge.** When a creature the Rakish one can see hits it with an attack, it can use its reaction to halve the attack’s damage.
