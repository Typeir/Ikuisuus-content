# Crusading

A Crusader channels conviction into radiant destruction, embodying relentless zeal and divine endurance.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, it gains the following traits in addition to any other abilities it possesses.

The creature’s **Charisma score becomes at least 10 + its proficiency bonus** (if not already higher).  
It is also considered a **half caster** for the purpose of determining spell slots and regains all expended slots after a long rest.

---

- **Divine Smite.**  
  When the creature hits another creature with a melee weapon attack, it can expend one spell slot to deal radiant damage in addition to the weapon’s damage.  
  The extra damage is 2d8 for a 1st-level slot, plus 1d8 for each spell slot level higher than 1st, to a maximum of 6d8.  
  The damage increases by 1d8 if the target is undead or a fiend.

- **Lay on Hands.**  
  The creature gains a healing pool equal to 5 × its proficiency bonus × 2.  
  As an action, it can touch a creature (including itself) to restore hit points from this pool.  
  It can also expend 5 points to cure a disease or neutralize a poison affecting the target.  
  The pool replenishes after a long rest.

- **Aura of Protection.**  
  The creature and all friendly creatures within 10 feet gain a bonus to all saving throws equal to the creature’s Charisma modifier (minimum +0).  
  At CR 16 or higher, the aura extends to 30 feet.
