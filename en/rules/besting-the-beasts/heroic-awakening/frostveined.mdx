# Frostveined

A Frostveined Caster channels the stillness and cruelty of winter, shaping ice and cold into weapons of control and endurance.  
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,  
along with any other spells they may know.

A Frostveined creature is **automatically affected by [Armor of Agathys](http://www.dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:armor-of-agathys)** at the start of each of its turns.
The spell is cast as though a spell slot of the creature's proficiency bonus was used.
Creatures of less than 2 CR cast it as a **first level spell**
