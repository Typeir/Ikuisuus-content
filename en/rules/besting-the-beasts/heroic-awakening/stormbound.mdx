# Stormbound

A Stormbound Caster wields the power of storms, wind and sea.
Whenever a Heroic Creature gains this affix, they gain the following spells,
along with other spells they may know. A stormbound creature is permanently affected by the [_Warding Wind_](http://www.dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:warding-wind) spell.
