# Besting the Beasts

Not all adversaries are created equal, and not all confrontations are decided by who swings first. These rules cover the anatomy of monsters, the Legendary Deeds system that governs exceptional encounters, and the Heroic Awakening mechanics available to characters who have pushed past ordinary limits.
