# Legendary Creatures

## Exceptional Monsters

Some creatures are genuinely exceptional — ancient in the way that erodes memory, powerful beyond what their stat blocks suggest, and capable of effects that ordinary encounters cannot produce. These creatures are not defined by a mechanical template; they are defined by the weight they carry in the world.

For the mechanical systems that govern how players confront and overcome legendary creatures, see [Legendary Deeds](/library/rules/besting-the-beasts/legendary-deeds) and [Heroic Awakening](/library/rules/besting-the-beasts/heroic-awakening).

## Monster Equipment

The Game Director may equip monsters with additional gear as appropriate to the creature's role and history. Consider in advance how much of a monster's equipment is recoverable after it is slain, and whether any of that equipment remains usable. Armour or weapons crafted for a creature's body are rarely serviceable for a different creature.

## Material Components for Spellcasting Monsters

If a spellcasting monster's abilities require material components, assume the monster already possesses those components unless a specific scenario dictates otherwise. The Game Director need not track this granularly — it is a conceit of narration, not resource accounting.
