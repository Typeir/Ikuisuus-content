# Edaphite

Twice extinct, edaphites are a resilient and adaptable people.
Sparsely referred to as "humans" by creatures other than Empyreans, they are the second most widespread sapient species on the planet.
They are known for their ambition, curiosity, and determination.
What weakness they carry in frames, they mend with their spirit.

Edaphites are found in nearly every corner of the world, and they have a wide range of cultures and traditions, all of which stem from their predecessors,
the [_Empyreans_](/en/library/character-creation/bloodlines/empyrean.bloodline).

Although some see them as vermin, degraded versions of their once godlike ancestors,
edaphites have proven time and again that no matter the cruelty the black cradle may throw at them, they can return to sender, thousandfold.

## Boons

You have a budget of **10 Boon Points**.

<Collapsible open>

##### Mind <span>5 BP - Choose One</span>

Pick one of the following:

<Collapsible>

###### Urban Explorer <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites are often **at home in cities and towns**, where they can navigate **complex social structures and urban environments**.

You gain proficiency two skills of the following: _Perception_, _Investigation_, _Persuasion_, or _Sleight of Hand_.

_Notes._

> When they could no longer overpower their foes with **brute strength**, edaphites learned to **outthink them instead**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Tradesmith <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites are known for their **craftsmanship and ingenuity**, often excelling in **various trades and professions**.

You gain proficiency with **one artisan's tool of your choice**.
Whenever you fail an ability check using the chosen tool, you can **re-roll the die** and must use the new roll.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a **short rest**.

_Notes._

> Edaphites quickly **dominated the mercantile societies** after the fall of the Four, creating **vast networks of trade and craftsmanship**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Learned Tongue <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites are known for their **linguistic diversity and adaptability**, often learning multiple languages to **communicate and trade**.
You gain proficiency in **one skill of your choice**.
You also learn **two additional languages of your choice** that are common in your upbringing.

_Notes._

> Edaphites often find themselves in **multicultural environments**, where the ability to **speak multiple languages** is a valuable asset.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Quick Hands <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites are said to possess the best hand-eye coordination. A myth perhaps, but one with some basis in reality.

You can use the **Use an Object** action as a free action once per round.

_Notes._

> It is said that before edaphites, most potions were large vials, unwieldy and slow to drink. Edaphites popularized smaller flasks and quick-draw pouches, allowing them to drink on the move.

</Collapsible>
_Notes._

> When strength failed them, edaphites learned systems: cities, crafts, words, and social leverage.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible open>

##### Matter <span>5 BP - Choose One</span>

Pick one of the following:


<Collapsible>

###### Mind over Might <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites are **ill adapted to physical challenges**, but their **ingenuity and willpower** often make up for it.

You gain proficiency in **one saving throw of your choice**.

_Notes._

> This boon reflects the **edaphite capacity to overcome physical limitations** through sheer force of will.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Dutiful Warrior <span>5 BP</span>

In combat, edaphites often optimize for tools that can be used in a **variety of situations**, such as **crossbows, guns, and versatile weapons**.

You gain proficiency with **crossbows, firearms, or one martial weapon of your choice**.

You do **not suffer disadvantage** on ranged attacks against creatures within 5 ft. of you.

_Notes._

> This boon reflects the **edaphite tendency to adapt** their combat style to a variety of situations, **favoring versatility over specialization**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Tireless Spirit <span>5 BP</span>

**Determined, resilient, and stubborn to a fault**, edaphites can push themselves **beyond their limits** when the situation demands it.

You can choose to **ignore the effects** of [_exhaustion_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#exhaustion), [_charmed_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#charmed), [_frightened_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#frightened) and [_poisoned_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#poisoned) for a number of minutes equal to your **proficiency bonus**.
When this duration ends, you gain **one level of exhaustion**.

You can use this feature **once per long rest**.

_Notes._

> It is said the **Golden One resembled an edaphite**, even though none of his kin had walked the earth yet.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Adapted to Extremes <span>5 BP</span>

Edaphites can be found **almost anywhere**, integrating themselves into wildly **different enviroments**.

When **climbing**, **swimming**, or **crawling**, your **movement penalty** becomes 1/2 a feet for every feet traveled, instead of 1.
You gain resistance to **nonmagical fire and cold** damage.

_Notes._

> Edaphites have spread far and wide, carrying a stubborn conviction that anywhere they can survive, they will thrive in.

</Collapsible>

_Notes._

> Edaphites endure violence not by superiority, but by refusal to fall, clever armament, and stubborn survival.

</Collapsible>
