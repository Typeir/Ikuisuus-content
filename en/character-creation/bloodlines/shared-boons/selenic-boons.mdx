##### Selenic Boons <span>Varies</span>

<Collapsible>

###### Settled Shape <span>5 BP</span>

Choose one of the results from the above boon. You get the effects of it every midnight.

_Notes._

> Some of the moon's children are blessed in the fortune of a birth under an auspicious, unobscured moon, and the tides that sway their nature are but a settled, fathomless lake.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Otherworld Luck <span>5 BP</span>

You have 3 luck points. Whenever you make an **attack roll**, an **ability check**, or a **saving throw**, you can spend one **luck point** to roll an additional d20. You can choose to spend one of your luck points after you roll the die, but before the outcome is determined. **You choose** which of the d20s is used for the attack roll, ability check, or saving throw.

You can also spend one luck point when an **attack roll is made against you**. Roll a d20 and then choose whether the attack uses the attacker's roll or yours.

If more than one creature spends a **luck point** to influence the outcome of a roll, the points **cancel each other out**; no additional dice are rolled.

You regain your expended **luck points** when you finish a long rest.

_Notes._

> What if not luck managed to land the pieni in the derelict Taiva and not in the burning soot and vile pits of rancorous Kalmora?

</Collapsible>

###### Mercurial Nature <span>4 BP</span>

Each midnight, you commune with the moon, either awake or asleep. Roll a d6 and get the following result from the table:

| d6  | Ability      | Effect                                                                                                 |
| --- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Strength     | Your carrying capacity doubles.                                                                        |
| 2   | Dexterity    | At the start of your first turn of combat, your speed increases by 5 feet until the end of that turn.  |
| 3   | Constitution | Gain 2d6 temporary hit points.                                                                         |
| 4   | Intelligence | You can accurately recall anything you have seen or heard within the past week.                        |
| 5   | Wisdom       | You have a +5 bonus to your passive perception score.                                                  |
| 6   | Charisma     | You can speak telepathically to any creature you can see within 30 feet of you in a language you know. |

You benefit from the associated effect. Additionally, once during that period, you may choose to gain advantage on an **attack roll**, an **ability check**, or a **saving throw** made with the associated ability.
The effects last until the next midnight.
_Notes._

> The moon's influence is capricious, the tears shed by her evercorpse eternally swaying like the lost tides of the Hidden Kingdom, and it is in that sway that her chosen find their strength.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Tide of Divinity <span>2 BP</span>

Once each long rest, during a short rest you may change a number of your prepared spells equal to your proficiency bonus for other prepared spells. You may do this once per long rest.

You do not need alchemical precursor in order to cast spells that you have changed in this way.

_Notes._

> Those of divine ascendance, even if diluted, are uniquely attuned to the folds of the world, and their thoughts can shape the very fabric of reality, even if slightly.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Scholar of Kuutar <span>1 BP</span>

You gain advantage on intelligence checks related to Kuutar or the Hidden Kingdom.

_Notes._

> Nothing like motherly love and a hearthy home to make a scholar of the world’s mysteries. — Old Dwarven Saying.

> The moon's children are as varied as the phases of the moon itself, and the facets that have held their crow, the fathomless Kuutar, and the hateful Red Queen.
