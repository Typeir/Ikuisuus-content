# Empyrean

Empyreans came from far beyond the Black Cradle, self-made beings of immense ambition. They often refer to themselves as "Humans", in contrast to the [_Edaphites_](/en/library/character-creation/bloodlines/edaphite).
They were the first and only creatures to cross the flames of the Everdark, intruding upon the derelict paradise of Taiva and forever changing the course of history.

Empyreans are ever-young, marble-skinned humanoids of uncanny beauty, distinguished by their disproportionately long limbs.
They are said to be children of stars, born from the very essence of creation itself—though this belief did not survive them.

Empyreans were once human. Through forbidden will-rites, they altered their vessels to transcend mortality.
Now they walk the world as living monuments to their own hubris, their empires having collapsed time and time again, until they persist only as oddities amid the pastoral landscape of Damocles.

### Blade and Brush

You gain proficiency with **one martial weapon** of your choice, and one artisan's tool of your choice.

The Empyreans of yore believed there were two strikes to be mastered in life: the strike of the blade, and the stroke of the brush.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write **Common** and **Empyrean**.

## Boons

You have a budget of **10 Boon Points**.

<Collapsible>

###### Extended Reach <span>6 BP</span>

Your limbs are longer than those of most humanoids.

Your melee weapon attacks have a reach of **5 feet greater than normal**.

_Notes._

> While most Empyreans have overlong limbs, most do not have truly inhuman proportions.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Starcharting Precision <span>6 BP</span>

When you have advantage on an attack roll using **Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma**, you can reroll one of the dice once.

You must use the new roll.

Additionally, you gain proficiency in the Perception skill.

_Notes._

> The Empyreans of yore used to spend a great deal of time navigating the stars from within their great observatories. This honed their senses to a razor's edge.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Daedal Oath <span>5 BP</span>

As a **bonus action**, you engage your daedal machinery for **10 minutes**, during which:

- Once per turn, When you deal damage to a creature with an attack or a spell, you can deal extra **true damage** equal to your level.
- After you deal this extra damage, you gain extra movement speed equal to half your walking speed until the end of your next turn.
- opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage.

You can use this feature once per **long rest**.

_Notes._

> Daedal machinery like this is often only found in Empyreans of pure lineage that were assigned the roles of personal guardians for the five families.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Heavenpiercing Aim <span>5 BP</span>

Your critical hit range for weapon attacks is increased by **1**.

_Notes._

> Many times over the eons have Empyrean fencers fallen into self absorbed journeys of perfection, in hopes to attain a personal heaven of their own making.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### First Step <span>5 BP</span>

As a **bonus action**, you can move up to **30 feet** to an unoccupied space you can see. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.
When you finish this movement, you can make one melee weapon attack with advantage against a creature within your reach.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **proficiency bonus**, regaining all expended uses when you finish a **long rest**.

_Notes._

> Empyreans are renowned for their uncanny agility. Often being spoken of as spectres of sorts, they can appear and disappear in the blink of an eye.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Soma <span>4 BP</span>

Your body hardens against damage through sheer force of will.

Choose one damage type when you gain this boon: **necrotic, radiant, psychic, or force**.
You gain **resistance** to the chosen damage type.

Additionally, when you take damage of the chosen type, you have advantage on the **first saving throw, ability check or attack roll** you make before the end of your next turn.

_Notes._

> Some empyreans regularly dosed on small amounts of primordial wax, believing it would enhance their resilience.
> A belief was widely held by them; What breaks lesser beings is often simply… refused.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Last Step <span>3 BP</span>

When you take the **Dodge** action, you can move up to half your speed as a **reaction**.

You may attempt to Hide while **lightly obscured** by environmental effects (such as dim light, foliage, or heavy rain).

_Notes._

> In modern times, Empyreans are often seen as elusive and mysterious figures, able to vanish from sight when they so choose, some even claim to be ghosts.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Lucid Will <span>3 BP</span>

You have **advantage on saving throws against being [_charmed_](/en/library/rules/conditions#charmed)**.

Magic cannot put you to sleep.

When you rest, you only need to do so for half the normal time.

_Notes._

> Empyreans optimized their bodies to avoid unnecessary resource expenditure. Some even claim that they can will themselves to stay awake indefinitely.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Will Rites <span>3 BP</span>

You learn **one cantrip** of your choice.

Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

_Notes._

> In their hubris, some Empyreans tampered with the splinters of creation, eternally binding a lesser fragment of power to their vessel.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Pureblood <span>3 BP</span>

You are a purebred Empyrean, your lineage untainted by the passing of time.

You are immortal, and do not age; you cannot be aged magically or otherwise.
You remain physically and mentally stable indefinitely.

You can otherwise die as normal from injury or disease.

_Notes._

> Immortality is a rare gift among Empyreans, as most have deteriorated into humanity over the centuries.
> Pureblood Empyreans are often treated as nobility among their kind, and as ill omens among others.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Pioneer of the End <span>2 BP</span>

Your movement speed increases by **5 feet**.

While [_frightened_](/en/library/rules/conditions#frightened), you can still move towards the source of your fear at a normal rate, without penalties.

_Notes._

> It is said that Empyreans were the first to venture into the Everdark; No matter how frightening the journey was, they pressed on regardless.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Starbound observer <span>1 BP</span>

You gain proficiency with the **Navigator's Tools**.

Additionally, you have advantage on **skill and ability checks of any kind** made to plan and navigate a course over long distances.

_Notes._

> Empyreans used to spend a great deal of time in vessels they called "great observatories", charting the stars and navigating the void between worlds.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Great Machinator <span>1 BP</span>

You gain proficiency with the **Tinkerer's Tools**.

You have advantage on **skill and ability checks** related to machinery, both ancient and modern.

_Notes._

> What is man if not a machine of flesh and bone? Empyreans often sought to understand the workings of their own bodies through the study of machinery and constructs.

</Collapsible>
