# Silent One

Silent Ones are the warped remnants of Päivätär's envy — beings birthed not by love or natural order, but by a divine desire to overshadow.
Made from spite against the moon goddess Kuutar, their forms are uncanny and unsettling, but sometimes beautiful.
Each and every one of them a different beast altogether, some aberrant, some elegant, all of them spiteful in some way or another.
The demons from the Hidden Kingdom operate in contracts and social logic beyond that of the inhabitants of Damocles, which often lands them in secluded places, the wrong company, or worse.

### Physiology

Silent Ones must eat, drink, and sleep like other living creatures, though what they consider "nourishment" varies wildly from individual to individual.
Some subsist on normal food, while others can sustain themselves on ash, marrow, fruit rinds, blood, flowers, charcoal, crushed insects, or even strange alchemical mixtures.
Their needs are unpredictable, and no two Silent Ones share the same diet.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write **Common** and **Hidden** (the script of the Hidden Kingdom).
Silent Ones often speak in murmurs, which still satisfy verbal spell components.

_Notes._

> Silent Ones are Hiisi. "Demon" is the surface-world designation; "Hiisi" is the old-tongue classification. The distinction is cultural, not biological.

###### Frame <span>Variable BP - Choose One</span>

Pick one of the following:

| Option         | Size   | Effect                                                             | Cost |
| -------------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---: |
| Powerful Build | Medium | Count as one size larger for carrying, pushing, dragging, lifting. |    1 |
| Large Frame    | Large  | Your space is 10 ft. Equipment must be made to fit.                |    3 |

- Large creatures must use the Squeeze action to fit through gaps of 5 ft or less.
- The Squeeze action gives advantage to attackers and grants disadvantage on skill checks, attack rolls, saving throws, and halves movement speed.
- Large creatures may use Large weapons without disadvantage, and Huge weapons at disadvantage.

_Notes._

> Hiisi size and mass are not chosen, cultivated, or grown into. Silent Ones are born at random scales; most bodies collapse under their own disproportions and perish shortly after birth. Those that survive do so by chance alone, their frames merely functional enough to persist.

| Darkvision (120 ft) | Darkvision  | 120 ft |    2 |

###### Elemental Resilience <span>2 BP</span>

Choose one damage type: fire, cold, acid, thunder, or lightning.
You have resistance to that damage type, and ignore resistance to it for damage you deal.
If you would ignore resistance from another source, then creatures with immunity count as having resistance instead.

_Notes._

> Silent Ones bear an obsession which often manifests around the inert prima materia that forms our world. The elements call to them, if they listen.

###### Undaunted Weaver <span>4 BP</span>

- You have advantage on Constitution saving throws to maintain concentration on a spell.
- If you have advantage on these saving throws from a source other than this feature and you fail one, you can choose to succeed instead.
- You can use this a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, regaining all uses when you finish a long rest.

_Notes._

> Silent Ones that persist do so because their bodies and minds happen to hold together under strain. Magic does not obey them; it merely fails to tear them apart.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Fold Sense <span>4 BP</span>

You are uniquely attuned to the energies between the seams of reality.
When one interacts with them next to you, you can react quicker than most.

You gain the following benefits:

- When you hit a creature with a melee attack, and that creature makes a concentration saving throw as a result, they do so at disadvantage.
- When a creature you can see within 5 feet of you forces you to make a saving throw against a spell, you do so at advantage.

If you cause disadvantage on concentration saves with melee attacks or gain advantage on saving throws against spells in this way from a source other than this feature,
you can also make an opportunity attack at a creature when it casts a spell within your reach.

_Notes._

> Traces of divinity manifest within the silent ones as a crude, natural attunement to the folds of reality.

</Collapsible>

_Notes._

> Silent Ones operate in bizarre contractual terms, often misunderstood by others. Some may even externalize this instinctively as magic.
