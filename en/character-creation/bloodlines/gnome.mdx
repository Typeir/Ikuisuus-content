# Gnome

Gnomes are volatile Pieni lineages known for misdirection, close-range audacity, and abrupt disappearance.
In Hidden Kingdom, gnomes are known as The Shifted: descendants of Pieni Lineages warped by the Interlocking, with wide and unstable anatomical variance and high mortality.
Those who survive infancy often join the ranks of the "Blood Gnomes", a loosely affiliated network of berserking pillagers, hell bent on a prophetic death wish at the hands of their
God-Empress the Red Queen. In the wider world, gnomes are often found in small, tight-knit communities where their skills in subterfuge and improvisation are valued for survival and social standing.

### Shifted Anatomy

Your body may present atypical proportions or organ placement common to Interlocking-affected lineages.
Whenever you take a long rest, roll a d20. if the result is less than or equal to 10, your size is Small until your next long rest.
If the result is greater than 10, your size is Medium until your next long rest.

### Mimicry

You can accurately mimic sounds you have heard, including voices.
A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations with a successful Wisdom (Insight) check contested by your Charisma (Deception) check.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write **Common** and **one additional language of your choice**.
Many gnome communities also preserve dialects of the **Hidden** tongues.

## Boons

You have a budget of **10 Boon Points**.

<Collapsible>

###### BLOOD FOR THE BLOOD GOD <span>4 BP</span>

You have advantage on attack rolls against creatures with the [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding) condition.

Whenever you use a [_Masterful Blow_](/en/library/rules/weapon-properties#masterful-blows) against a creature, you also give the creature 1d4 stacks of the [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding) condition.

If a creature is immune to the [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding) condition, you deal additional damage to it equal to your proficiency bonus when you hit it with a masterful blow.

_Notes._

> The Red Queen's followers are known for their reckless, berserking fighting style. When drums are heard, they charge into battle with abandon, seeking gruesome glory in the spilling of blood; that of their victims as well as theirs.

</Collapsible>

###### Dreams of Greatness <span>3 BP</span>

Whenever your Shifted anatomy causes you to change size, you can choose to gain one of the following benefits until your next long rest, per the size you assume:

| Small Size                                                                             | Medium Size                                                          |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks and Saving Throws                              | Advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks and Saving Throws           |
| Can squeeze through spaces as narrow as 1 foot wide                                    | Can choose to become Large instead of medium                         |
| Treat creatures of size small as 1/2 cover, and of medium size or larger as full cover | Can attempt to grapple creatures of up to two sizes larger than you  |
| Opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage when you are Small          | You have advantage on attack rolls against creatures grappled by you |

You gain only one of the benefits for each size, not the full list.

_Notes._

> It is said the Shifted experience vivid nightmares and dreams when their body turns.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Skirmisher's Exit <span>3 BP</span>

You can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of your turns.

_Notes._

> Most gnome fighting manuals teach departure before commitment. The trick is deciding which strike counts as the commitment.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Quiet Casting Discipline <span>3 BP</span>

You have trained to reduce your audible and visible casting profile.

When you cast a spell in combat, creatures more than 40 feet away cannot identify you as the source by sight or sound alone.
Creatures within 40 feet must succeed on an Intelligence (Arcana) check against a DC of 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier to identify you as the source.

If the spell you cast lacks verbal components or lacks somatic components, those creatures make this check with disadvantage.

This feature does not prevent creatures from noticing visible projectile trajectories, impact points, or obvious magical effects.

_Notes._

> Gnome casters are taught concealment as a practical survival method, not as ritual doctrine.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

###### Trinket Improviser <span>1 BP</span>

Once per long rest, you can craft one temporary trinket weighing 1 pound or less during a short rest.
The trinket lasts until your next short rest, until it is consumed by use, or until it is destroyed.

If a feature from your class, background, or gear calls for a saving throw caused by this trinket, the save DC is 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier unless another DC is explicitly higher.

_Notes._

> Improvised gnome devices are often single-use by design. Reliability is traded for speed and deniability.

</Collapsible>

###### Knack for Trickery <span>1 BP</span>

You learn one cantrip of your choice from the following list:

- Minor Illusion
- Mage Hand
- Friends

Choose one 1st-level spell from the following list:

- Catapult
- Disguise Self
- Grease

You can cast the chosen 1st-level spell once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.

Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for spells learned through this boon.

_Notes._

> Gnome spellcraft favors leverage, confusion, and sudden positional advantage.

</Collapsible>

- Sleight of Hand

_Notes._
