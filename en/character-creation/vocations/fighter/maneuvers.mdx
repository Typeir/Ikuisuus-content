# Maneuvers

Maneuvers are tactical techniques that allow you to exploit openings, control the battlefield, and direct your allies through practiced martial skill.

Each maneuver requires you to expend **one Superiority Die**.

---

> ### Ambush
>
> When you make a **Dexterity (Stealth)** check or roll **Initiative**, you can expend a Superiority Die and **add the result** to the roll.

---

> ### Bait and Switch
>
> While within **5 feet** of a willing creature, you can expend a Superiority Die to **switch places** with it. This movement does not provoke **Opportunity Attacks**.
>
> Roll the Superiority Die. You or the other creature (your choice) gains **AC equal to the roll** until the start of your next turn.

---

> ### Commander’s Strike
>
> When you take the **Attack** action, you can replace one attack to direct an ally.
>
> Expend a Superiority Die. A willing creature that can see or hear you uses its **Reaction** to make an attack, adding the Superiority Die to the **damage roll** on a hit.

---

> ### Commanding Presence
>
> When you make a **Charisma (Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion)** check, you can expend a Superiority Die and **add the result** to the roll.

---

> ### Disarming Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and the target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or drop an object of your choice.

---

> ### Distracting Strike
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**. The next attack against the target by a creature other than you has **Advantage** if made before your next turn.

---

> ### Evasive Footwork
>
> As a **Bonus Action**, you can expend a Superiority Die and take the **Disengage** action.
>
> Add the die to your **AC** until the start of your next turn.

---

> ### Feinting Attack
>
> As a **Bonus Action**, you can expend a Superiority Die to feint.
>
> Choose a creature within **5 feet**. You gain **Advantage** on your next attack against that creature this turn. On a hit, add the Superiority Die to the **damage roll**.

---

> ### Goading Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and the target must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw** or have **Disadvantage** on attacks against targets other than you until the end of your next turn.

---

> ### Lunging Attack
>
> As a **Bonus Action**, you can expend a Superiority Die and take the **Dash** action.
>
> If you move at least **5 feet in a straight line** before hitting with a melee attack this turn, add the die to the **damage roll**.

---

> ### Maneuvering Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and choose an ally who can see or hear you. That ally may use its **Reaction** to move up to **half its Speed** without provoking **Opportunity Attacks** from the target.

---

> ### Menacing Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and the target must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw** or become **Frightened** until the end of your next turn.

---

> ### Parry
>
> When you are hit by a **melee attack**, you can use your **Reaction** and expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Reduce the damage by the die result + your **Strength or Dexterity modifier** (your choice).

---

> ### Precision Attack
>
> When you **miss** with an attack roll, you can expend a Superiority Die and **add the result** to the attack roll, potentially turning it into a hit.

---

> ### Pushing Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and if the creature is **Large or smaller**, it must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or be pushed **15 feet directly away** from you.

---

> ### Rally
>
> As a **Bonus Action**, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Choose an ally within **30 feet** who can see or hear you. That ally gains **Temporary Hit Points** equal to the die result + **half your Fighter level** (rounded down).

---

> ### Riposte
>
> When a creature **misses you with a melee attack**, you can use your **Reaction** and expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Make a melee attack against the creature. On a hit, add the Superiority Die to the **damage roll**.

---

> ### Sweeping Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with a **melee attack**, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Choose another creature within **5 feet** of the target and within your reach. If your original attack roll would hit the second creature, it takes damage equal to the Superiority Die roll of the same type as the original attack.

---

> ### Tactical Assessment
>
> When you make an **Intelligence (History or Investigation)** or **Wisdom (Insight)** check, you can expend a Superiority Die and **add the result** to the check.

---

> ### Trip Attack
>
> When you hit a creature with an attack, you can expend a Superiority Die.
>
> Add the die to the **damage roll**, and if the target is **Large or smaller**, it must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or fall **Prone**.
