# Rimelord

Rimelords, like their astral patron, walk near the edge of total annihilation, they wield the frigid power of the Seventh Winter, a cold so absolute that it can halt the very passing of time.
With that very cold, they shape weapons of ice and frost to rend their enemies, and call upon dancing glacier blades to slay those who would oppose them.

## How one becomes a Revenant

You froze to death. Something inside the Seventh Winter noticed the quiet in your heart and refused to let it stop. You rose at the edge of the snowdrift—alive, but carrying a cold that was not yours.

## 1st level - Rimeforged Armament
When you choose this specialization, the cold of the Seventh Winter infuses your weapon.
At the end of each of your turns in which you take the Attack action, you may reshape the weapon you are holding into one of the following frost-forged forms. The chosen form lasts until the end of your next turn and affects only your next melee weapon attack.

**Impaling Lance — Piercing** :

- Your next melee weapon attack deals piercing damage
- The attack gains +5 feet reach.

**Cleaving Edge — Slashing** :

- Your next melee weapon attack deals slashing damage.
- If the attack misses, the target still takes slashing damage equal to your Strength modifier.

**Crushing Maul — Bludgeoning** :

- Your next melee weapon attack deals bludgeoning damage.
- On a hit, the target is pushed back 5ft.

**Frostbite Brand — Cold** :

- Your next melee weapon attack deals cold damage.
- On a hit, the target’s speed is reduced by 5 feet until the end of its next turn.

</Collapsible>

## 3rd level - Flash Freeze
You gain **resistance to cold damage**, and **ignore resistances to it** when dealing damage.
additionally, whenever you deal cold damage **with an attack roll that hit** or **saving throw** you can deal your **Execution damage as cold damage** to **one** affected creature, provided you have not used execution this round.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 3rd level - Cold Parade
You are unimpeded by **difficult terrain** or **speed impediments** caused by snow, ice, or similar effects, and gain a **climbing speed** equal to your walking speed on **icy terrain**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 6th level - Winter's caress
Whenever you take the attack action, you can make **one additional attack**, But only up to **two attacks total**.
When you do so, **all the damage** from your second attack is **treated as cold damage**, as an icy reprise of your swing shatters against your target.
Additionally, You may use your **Strength** in place of your **Intelligence** as a **spellcasting modifier** for spells and features that deal cold damage.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th level - Avalanche Blade
You may transform your Abundance and decay into a **gargantuan**, quasi-intangible ice blade of pure entropy as a **bonus action**. The blade appears in it's place.
The blade **cannot normally move by itself** and automatically swings on creatures of your choice that enter it's space, once per turn.
The blade is treated as an Object, and has the following properties:

> #### Avalanche Blade
>
> | **Armor Class** | **Hit Points**                       | **Damage Threshold**  |
> | --------------- | ------------------------------------ | --------------------- |
> | 8 + your STR    | Revenant level x 2, + your CON score | 10+ your CON modifier |
>
> - **Size**: Gargantuan object (20 ft. section)
> - **Material**: Ice
> - **Resistances**: **Slashing, Piercing**
> - **Vulnerabilities**: **Force**
> - **Immunities**: **Poison**, **Psychic**, **Necrotic**, **Radiant**, **Cold**
> - **Reactive Aura**: The Avalanche can take one reaction per turn.
> - **Reactions — Cold Cleave.**  
>   _Melee Weapon Attack:_ 8 + your Strength to hit, reach 5 ft.; one target.  
>   **Hit:** 8d6 + your STR modifier necrotic cold. Target's speed is reduced to 0 ft. until the end of their next turn.

You may **dismiss** the blade at will with **no cost**.
You may only summon one **Avalanche Blade** at a time.
While your Avalanche Blade is **active**, the benefits from your Rimeforged armament are **doubled**.
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **Strength modifier**, and you gain all expended uses after a **long rest**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 18th level - Dance of Mana
Your frostcraft ebbs and follows like the endless dance of the cold Ringworlds of Mana,
whenever you gain the benefits of **Rimeforged armament**, you may choose **three benefits instead of one**, choosing your preferred damage type from said benefits.

Once per turn, whenever you deal **cold damage** to a creature, your Avalanche blade may move up to **your speed** towards that creature, forcing creatures on it's path to make a
DEX saving throw or take damage equal to the blade's **Cold Cleave**.

</Collapsible>
