# Mistreaver

The collision of Länsihenki and Itähenki—the West and East Wind giants—creates a doctrine of distance and disruption.
Mistreavers embrace their dichotomy to strike from afar, conceal themselves in brumes, and weave their execution into the perfect orbit of obliteration.

## How one becomes a Revenant

You drowned, or vanished in a fog no one returns from. Days later, you stumbled back into the world —breathing, but wrong. Your memories warp and collide when you reach for them; the mists remember you more clearly than you remember yourself.

## 1st Level — Distant Execution

Your mastery of entropy extends to ranged implements of death.

**Execution** can now be used with **ranged weapons** wielded with **two hands**.

- The range for Execution is **the weapon's normal range** (not long range).
- When you hit a target that is behind cover (half or three-quarters), you deal extra damage equal to the AC bonus the cover provides (2 for half cover, 5 for three-quarters cover).
- If any of your features make your ranged attacks ignore cover, you do not benefit from this extra damage.

---

## 3rd Level — Ghastly Aim

You excel at finding angles through impossible defenses.

- **Total cover** is treated as **three-quarters cover** for you when determining if you can target a creature.
- Objects of a higher density or mass than **15 inches of brickwork** still provide total cover.
- Your **Retinue** provides **half cover** to you while it is within **5 feet** of you.

---

## 3rd Level — Brume Steps

Your mastery of stillness makes you a patient, unseen hunter.

- You can use your **Intelligence modifier** instead of **Dexterity** for **Stealth** checks.
- You ignore **disadvantage** on Stealth checks caused by **armor**.

---

## 6th Level — Twin Orbits

You manifest dual zones of decay, representing Itähenki and Länsihenki.

When you create your **Abundance and Decay**, you also create a second area called **Länsihenki's Shroud**.

**Länsihenki's Shroud:**
- A **5-foot-radius** area you place anywhere within **30 feet** at the same time as your Abundance and Decay.
- This area is **heavily obscured** to all creatures except you and your summons.
- Does not reduce AC but it counts as **"standing on your abundance and decay"** in order to provide healing.
- At **18th level**, Länsihenki's Shroud becomes a **10-foot-radius** area to match Abundance and Decay.

You can move **each area independently** up to **10 feet** as part of the same bonus action.

---

## 14th Level — Winds of Desolation

The winds and tides carry your execution further than ever before.

You can use **Execution** at your weapon's **long range** (instead of only normal range).

Additionally, your **Retinue** becomes an extension of the twin winds' power, gaining the following abilities:

**Wind's Edge:**
- Your Retinue can make a ranged attack instead of **Grave Swipe**.
- _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ your spell attack modifier to hit, range 30/60 ft.; one target.
- **Hit:** 1d4 + your INT modifier necrotic damage. Target's speed is reduced by 5 ft. until the end of their next turn.

**Galeforce Slam:**
- When **you** hit with **Execution**, you may use **your bonus action** to have the Retinue unleash a powerful gust at a creature within **30 feet** of it.
- The target must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** against your spell save DC or be knocked **prone**.
- This does not use the Retinue's action.

---

## 18th Level — Twinned Death

The cycle of perfect storms comes to completion within you. You embody the recurring clash of Itähenki and Länsihenki, and your mastery over their forces allows you to manipulate their zones of power in devastating ways.

**Extreme Reach:**
- When you create your **Abundance and Decay**, you can choose to place it at a point you can see within **120 feet** (instead of 30 feet).

**Vortex Swap:**
- As an **action**, you can **swap** all creatures between your two zones—creatures in Abundance and Decay teleport to Länsihenki's Shroud, and vice versa.
- **Willing** creatures are teleported automatically.
- **Unwilling** creatures must succeed on a **Charisma saving throw** or be teleported.

**Collision Event:**
- As an **action**, you can cause both zones to **crash together**, moving them to collide at a point of your choice, then immediately dissipate.
- All creatures within **5 feet** of either zone when they collide must make a **Constitution saving throw**.
- On a failed save, a creature takes **12d10 necrotic damage** and is **stunned** until the end of your next turn.
- On a success, a creature takes **half damage** and is not stunned.
- After using Collision Event, you cannot create Abundance and Decay again until you finish a **short rest**.

You can use **Collision Event** and **Vortex Swap** one time each, regaining the use after a **short rest**.
