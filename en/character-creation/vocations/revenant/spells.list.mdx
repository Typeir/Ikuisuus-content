# Revenant Spell List

The following spells are available to Revenants. As a Revenant, you prepare spells from this list during a Recovery. Your spell save DC and spell attack bonus use your Intelligence modifier.

## Preparing Spells

You can prepare a number of Revenant spells equal to your Intelligence modifier + half your Revenant level (rounded down, minimum of one spell). You must have the spell slots to cast the spell.

For example, if you are a 5th-level Revenant with an Intelligence of 16, your list of prepared spells can include six spells of 1st or 2nd level, in any combination, chosen from the Revenant spell list.
