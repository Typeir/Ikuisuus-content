# Warrior

Warriors rule many battlefields. Questing knights, royal champions, elite soldiers, and hardened mercenaries—as Warriors, they all share an unparalleled prowess with weapons and armor. They are well acquainted with death, both meting it out and defying it.

Warriors master various weapon techniques, and a well-equipped Warrior always has the right tool at hand for any combat situation. Likewise, a Warrior is adept with every form of armor. Beyond that, each Warrior specializes in certain styles of combat. Some concentrate on archery, some on fighting with two weapons at once, and some on augmenting their martial skills with magic. This combination of broad ability and extensive specialization makes Warriors superior combatants.

## Becoming a Warrior

### As a Level 1 Warrior

- Gain all the traits in the **Core Warrior Traits** table.
- Gain the level 1 features listed in the **Warrior Features** table.

### As a Mixed Warrior

- Gain the following from **Core Warrior Traits**: Hit Point Die, proficiency with Martial weapons, and training with Light and Medium armor and Shields.
- Gain the Warrior’s level 1 features listed in the **Warrior Features** table.

<Collapsible>
## 1st Level – Fighting Style

You gain a **[Fighting Style feat](/en/library/character-creation/feats/fighting-styles)** of your choice. **Defense** is recommended. When you gain a Fighting Style this way, you do not increase an ability score.

Whenever you gain a Warrior level, you may replace your chosen Fighting Style feat with a different one.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 1st Level – Second Wind

As a **Minor Action**, you regain hit points equal to **1d10 + your Warrior level**.

- You may use this feature **twice**.
- You regain **1 use on a Repose**, and **all uses on a Recovery**.

Refer to the **Second Wind Uses** column in the Warrior Features table for scaling.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 1st Level – Weapon Mastery

Choose **three Simple or Martial weapons**. You can use the **Mastery properties** of those weapons.

- When you finish a **Recovery**, you may change one of these choices.
- Your number of mastered weapons increases at higher levels (see the Warrior Features table).

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 2nd Level – Action Surge

When you use this feature, you gain **one additional Action**, which you can take as a **Major Action**, regardless of if you have already taken a Major Action or not,
This action can be used ad libitum for any valid purpose.

- You regain this feature after a **Repose or Recovery**.
- At **level 17**, you may use it **twice** per rest (but only once per turn).

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 2nd Level – Tactical Mind

When you fail an **ability check**, you may expend a use of **Second Wind** to reroll a **d10** and add it to the check.

- If the check still fails, this use of Second Wind is **not expended**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible open>
## 3rd Level – Warrior Specialization

You gain a Warrior specialization. Options include:

| Warrior Specializations                                                                            |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Battle Master](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/warrior/battle-master.specialization)     |
| [Champion](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/warrior/champion.specialization)               |
| [Eldritch Knight](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/warrior/eldritch-knight.specialization) |
| **Psi Warrior**                                                                                    |
| [Sword Saint](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/warrior/sword-saint.specialization)         |

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 4th Level – Ability Score Improvement

You gain the **[Ability Score Improvement feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:ability-score-improvement)** or another **[feat of your choice](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all)**.

You gain this feature again at levels **6, 8, 12, 14, and 16**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 5th Level – Extra Attack

When you take the **Attack action**, you may attack **twice** instead of once.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 5th Level – Tactical Shift

When you activate **Second Wind**, you may move up to **half your Speed** without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 9th Level – Indomitable

When you fail a **saving throw**, you may reroll it and add a bonus equal to your **Warrior level**.

- You must use the new roll.
- You may use this feature **once per Recovery**.
- At **level 13**, use it **twice** per rest.
- At **level 17**, use it **three times** per rest.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 9th Level – Tactical Master

When you attack with a weapon you have **Mastery** of, you may replace its property with **Push**, **Sap**, or **Slow** for that attack.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 11th Level – Two Extra Attacks

When you take the **Attack action**, you may attack **three times** instead of once.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 13th Level – Studied Attacks

When you make an **attack roll** against a creature and miss, you gain **Advantage** on your next attack roll against that creature before the end of your next turn.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 19th Level – Epic Boon

You gain an **[Epic Boon feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all)** or another feat of your choice.

**[Boon of Combat Prowess](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:boon-of-combat-prowess)** is recommended.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

### Level 20 – Three Extra Attacks

When you take the **Attack action**, you may attack **four times** instead of once.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>

### Level 22 – Masterful Warrior

You have fought battles unending, mastering every form of combat.

- You gain **Mastery** of **all Simple and Martial weapons**.
- Once per turn When you use **Tactical Master**, you may apply two different properties to the same attack, or apply a property from \*_Tactical Master_ in addition to your weapons own mastery.

</Collapsible>
