# Pilgrim

Pilgrims draw power from the gods themselves — dead, distant, or merely uninterested — and harness it to work miracles anyway. Blessed by a god, a pantheon, or something else that has outlasted its own worship, a Pilgrim channels that power directly to bolster allies and battle foes.

Pilgrims typically associate themselves with temples dedicated to the force that unlocked their magic. Not every priest is a Pilgrim—some serve simply through prayer and ritual—but a true Pilgrim can marshal divine power in miraculous ways.

---

## Core Pilgrim Traits

| Trait                          | Description                                                                                           |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Primary Ability**            | Wisdom                                                                                                |
| **Hit Point Die**              | d8 per Pilgrim level                                                                                  |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Wisdom and Charisma                                                                                   |
| **Skill Proficiencies**        | Choose 2: History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, or Religion                                         |
| **Weapon Proficiencies**       | Simple weapons                                                                                        |
| **Armor Training**             | Light and Medium Armor, Shields                                                                       |
| **Starting Equipment**         | Choose A or B:<br/>(A) Chain Shirt, Shield, Mace, Holy Symbol, Priest's Pack, and 7 gp<br/>(B) 110 gp |

---

## Becoming a Pilgrim

### As a Level 1 Character

- Gain all **Core Pilgrim Traits**.
- Gain the Pilgrim’s level 1 features.

---

### As a Mixed Character

- Gain the Pilgrim’s **Hit Point Die**.
- Gain **Light and Medium Armor**, **Shields**, and Pilgrim level 1 features.
- Use the [**Mixing Spellcasting**](/en/library/rules/arcana-and-the-fold/mixing-spellcasting) rules to determine spell slots.

## 1st Level – Spellcasting

You have learned to cast spells through prayer and divine connection.

- **Spell List**: [_Cleric spell list_](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/pilgrim/spells)
- **Spellcasting Ability**: Wisdom
- **Spellcasting Focus**: Holy Symbol

---

### Cantrips

- Know **3 cantrips** at level 1.
- Learn additional cantrips at **4th** and **10th level**.
- Cantrips may be replaced on level up.

---

### Spell Slots

Use the **Pilgrim Features Table** for your spell slots.  
You regain all expended slots after a **Recovery**.

---

### Prepared Spells

- You prepare a number of Pilgrim spells equal to the **Prepared Spells column** in the Pilgrim Features table.
- Prepared spells must be of a level for which you have spell slots.

You may change your prepared spells after a **Recovery**.

</Collapsible>

## 2nd Level – Channel Divinity

You can channel divine energy to fuel magical effects.  
You begin with **two uses** and regain:

- **One use on a Repose**,
- **All uses on a Recovery**.

At higher levels, your uses increase (see table).

---

### Divine Spark

As a **Magic action**, point your **Holy Symbol** at a creature within **30 feet**.

- Roll **[% 1d8 %] + Wisdom modifier**.
- Choose to either **heal** or deal **Dark or Holy damage** (your choice).
- On a failed save, the target takes full damage. On a success, it takes half.

Additional dice at:

| Level | Dice |
| ----- | ---- |
| 7     | [% 2d8 %]  |
| 13    | [% 3d8 %]  |
| 18    | [% 4d8 %]  |

---

### Turn Undead

As a **Magic action**, present your **Holy Symbol**.

- Each Undead in **30 feet** must make a **Wisdom saving throw**.
- On failure: the creature is **Frightened and Incapacitated** for **1 minute** and tries to flee.
- The effect ends early if the creature takes damage, you become **Incapacitated**, or you die.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 4th Level – Ability Score Improvement

Gain the **[Ability Score Improvement feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:ability-score-improvement)** or another **[feat of your choice](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all)**.

Repeat at **8th, 12th, and 16th level**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 5th Level – Sear Undead

When you use **Turn Undead**, you may roll a number of **d8s equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 1)**.

- Undead that fail their save take **Holy damage equal to the total**.
- This damage **does not end the Turn effect**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 7th Level – Blessed Strikes

Choose one:

### Divine Strike

- Once per turn, when you hit with a weapon attack, deal an extra **[% 1d8 Dark %] or Holy damage**.

---

### Potent Spellcasting

- Add your **Wisdom modifier** to **Pilgrim cantrip damage**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 10th Level – Divine Intervention

As a **Magic action**, choose a **5th-level Pilgrim spell or lower** and cast it **without expending a spell slot** or requiring **Material components**.

- You can’t use this feature again until you finish a **Recovery**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th Level – Improved Blessed Strikes

Your **Blessed Strikes** option improves:

### Divine Strike

- Extra damage increases to **[% 2d8 %]**.

---

### Potent Spellcasting

- When you deal damage with a Pilgrim cantrip, choose a creature (yourself or an ally) within **60 feet**.
- That creature gains **Temporary Hit Points equal to twice your Wisdom modifier**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 19th Level – Epic Boon

Gain an **[Epic Boon feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all)** or another feat of your choice.

**[Boon of Fate](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:boon-of-fate)** is recommended.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 20th Level – Greater Divine Intervention

When you use **Divine Intervention**, you may choose **[Wish](/en/library/spells/wish)**.

- If you do, you cannot use **Divine Intervention** again until you finish **[% 2d4 %] Recovery**.

</Collapsible>
