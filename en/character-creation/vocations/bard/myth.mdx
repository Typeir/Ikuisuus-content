# Myth

_Sing the Deeds That Shape the World._

Myth singers preserve and reenact the great deeds upon which history turns. They do not merely recount legends—they perform them into being, ensuring that heroism, terror, and fate remain vivid forces in the world.

Wherever a Myth Bard fights, their actions echo those of ancient heroes. Blades strike as remembered, victories unfold as foretold, and enemies falter beneath the weight of stories older than themselves.

## 3rd Level – Mythic Deeds
A creature that has a **Bardic Inspiration die** from you can use it for one of the following effects:

### Tale of the Unbreakable

When the creature is **hit by an attack**, it can use its **Reaction** to roll the Bardic Inspiration die and **add the number rolled to its AC** until the end of the turn. This may cause the attack to miss.

### Tale of the Mighty

Immediately after the creature **hits a target with an attack roll**, it can roll dice according to the table below, and add them to the attack's damage, as Psychic damage.

| Bard Level | Bonus Damage              |
| ---------- | ------------------------- |
| 3rd        | 2 Bardic Inspiration dice |
| 5th        | 3 Bardic Inspiration dice |
| 10th       | 4 Bardic Inspiration dice |
| 15th       | 5 Bardic Inspiration dice |

### Tale of the Cunning

When a creature misses an **attack roll** they can use their **Reaction** to roll the Bardic Inspiration die and **add the number rolled to the attack and damage rolls**, potentially turning a miss into a hit.

- You can use a **Simple or Martial weapon** as a **Spellcasting Focus** for your Bard spells.

## 6th Level – Extra Attack
When you take the **Attack** action, you can attack **twice** instead of once.

## 6th Level – Tales of Othertime
You can spend an use of your **Bardic Inspiration** to use **Mythic Deeds** on yourself, as a bonus action.
You do not benefit from standard **Bardic Inspiration** die rolls while using this feature, you can only use the die for **Mythic Deeds**.

Additionally, Whenever you use your **Tale of the Mighty** feature to deal extra damage on a hit, you can may choose to have the added damage be **halved** (rounded down).
If you do so, the target must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw** against your **Spell Save DC** or suffer an additional effect, determined as follows:

- **Good**: The target is **[_Charmed_](/en/library/rules/conditions#charmed)** by you until the **end of your next turn**.
- **Evil**: The target is **[_Frightened_](/en/library/rules/conditions#frightened)** of you until the **end of your next turn**.
- **Neutral**: The target is **[_Silenced_](/en/library/rules/conditions#silenced)** until the **end of your next turn**.

On a successful saving throw, the target suffers no additional effect.

A creature affected by this feature is immune to it until the **end of your next turn**.

</Collapsible>
