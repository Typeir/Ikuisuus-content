# Metamagic Techniques

Metamagic Techniques are arcane manipulation methods that allow a spellcaster to reshape spells at the moment of casting.

**Metamagic Techniques are optional spell alterations.**  
They are declared **when you cast a spell**, consume **Sorcery Points**, and modify that spell’s parameters or resolution.

Unless stated otherwise, **only one Metamagic Technique can be applied to a spell**.

---

> ### **Careful Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell that forces creatures to make a saving throw  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** Choose a number of affected creatures up to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1).  
> Chosen creatures **automatically succeed** on their saving throw against the spell, and **take no damage** if the spell would normally deal half damage on a successful save.

---

> ### **Distant Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:**  
> • If the spell has a range of **5 feet or greater**, **double its range**.  
> • If the spell has a range of **Touch**, its range becomes **30 feet**.

---

> ### **Empowered Spell**
> **When:** You roll damage for a spell  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** Reroll a number of the spell’s damage dice up to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1).  
> You **must use the new rolls**.  
>  
> You may use this technique **even if another Metamagic Technique was already applied** to the spell.

---

> ### **Extended Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell with a duration of **1 minute or longer**  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** Double the spell’s duration, up to a maximum of **24 hours**.  
>  
> If the spell requires **Concentration**, you have **Advantage on saving throws** made to maintain it.

---

> ### **Heightened Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell that forces a creature to make a saving throw  
> **Cost:** 2 Sorcery Points  
> **Effect:** One target of the spell has **Disadvantage on saving throws** against the spell.

---

> ### **Quickened Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell with a casting time of **1 action**  
> **Cost:** 2 Sorcery Points  
> **Effect:** The spell’s casting time becomes **1 bonus action** for this casting.  
>  
> **Restriction:** You cannot modify a spell this way if you have already cast a **1st-level or higher spell** on this turn, nor can you cast another **1st-level or higher spell** after using this technique.

---

> ### **Seeking Spell**
> **When:** You miss with a spell attack roll  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** Reroll the d20 and **use the new result**.  
>  
> You may use this technique **even if another Metamagic Technique was already applied** to the spell.

---

> ### **Subtle Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** The spell is cast **without verbal, somatic, or material components**, except for materials that are **consumed** by the spell or that have a **listed cost**.

---

> ### **Transmuted Spell**
> **When:** You cast a spell that deals acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder damage  
> **Cost:** 1 Sorcery Point  
> **Effect:** Change the spell’s damage type to **another type from the same list**.
