# Circle of the Land

_Celebrate Connection to the Natural World._

The Circle of the Land comprises mystics and sages who safeguard ancient knowledge and rites. These Druids meet in sacred circles of trees or standing stones to whisper primal secrets in Druidic. The wisest among them preside as the chief priests of their communities.

## 3rd Level – Circle of the Land Spells
Whenever you finish a **Long Rest**, choose one **type of land**: **arid**, **polar**, **temperate**, or **tropical**.  
You always have the spells from that land’s list prepared, for your **Druid level and lower**.

## 3rd Level – Land’s Aid
As a **Magic action**, expend a use of **Wild Shape** and choose a point within **60 feet** of you.

- **Vital flowers and draining thorns** bloom in a **10-foot-radius Sphere**.
- Each creature of your choice in the sphere must make a **Constitution saving throw** (DC = your spell save DC).
  - **Failure**: Takes **2d6 Necrotic damage**.
  - **Success**: Takes half damage.
- One creature of your choice in the area regains **2d6 Hit Points**.

---

### Scaling

Damage and healing increase by **1d6** at:

| Level | Dice |
| ----- | ---- |
| 10    | 3d6  |
| 14    | 4d6  |

## 6th Level – Natural Recovery
You can cast **one spell from your Circle Spells list** without expending a spell slot.  
You must finish a **Long Rest** to do so again.

---

### Spell Slot Recovery

When you finish a **Short Rest**, you may recover expended spell slots.

- Combined levels equal to **half your Druid level (round up)**.
- Slots of **6th level or higher** cannot be recovered.
- Once you use this feature, you must finish a **Long Rest** before using it again.

</Collapsible>

## 10th Level – Nature’s Ward
You are **immune to the Poisoned condition**, and gain **Resistance** to the following damage type based on your current land choice:

| Land Type | Resistance |
| --------- | ---------- |
| Arid      | Fire       |
| Polar     | Cold       |
| Temperate | Lightning  |
| Tropical  | Poison     |

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th Level – Nature’s Sanctuary
As a **Magic action**, expend a use of **Wild Shape** to create spectral trees and vines in a **15-foot Cube** on the ground within **120 feet** of you.

- The sanctuary lasts for **1 minute**, or until you are **Incapacitated** or die.
- **Allies gain Half Cover** while in the area.
- Allies in the area also gain the **Resistance** from your current **Nature’s Ward**.

---

### Move the Sanctuary

As a **Bonus Action**, you can **move the Cube up to 60 feet**, to ground within **120 feet** of you.

</Collapsible>
