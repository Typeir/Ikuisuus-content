# Oath of Devotion

_Uphold the Ideals of Justice and Order._

The Oath of Devotion binds Paladins to Justice and Order. These Paladins embody the archetype of the knight in shining armor, holding themselves—and sometimes the world—to the highest standards of conduct.

Many swear this oath before gods of law and goodness, while others revere angelic ideals, weaving symbols of wings into their armor and heraldry.

**Tenets of Devotion**:

- Let your word be your promise.
- Protect the weak and never fear to act.
- Let your honorable deeds be an example.

## 3rd Level – Sacred Weapon
When you take the **Attack action**, you can expend one use of **Channel Divinity** to imbue a **Melee weapon** you’re holding with positive energy.

For **10 minutes**, or until you use this feature again:

- Add your **Charisma modifier** (minimum +1) to **attack rolls** made with the weapon.
- Each time you hit, the weapon deals either its **normal damage type** or **Radiant damage** (your choice).
- The weapon emits **Bright Light** in a **20-foot radius** and **Dim Light** for an additional 20 feet.

You can end this effect early (no action required). It ends if you’re no longer carrying the weapon.

</Collapsible>

## 7th Level – Aura of Devotion
You and your allies are **immune to the Charmed condition** while within your **Aura of Protection**.

If a Charmed ally enters the aura, the condition has no effect while they remain inside.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 15th Level – Smite of Protection
Whenever you cast **[Divine Smite](/en/library/spells/divine-smite)**, you and your allies gain **Half Cover** while in your **Aura of Protection**.

This benefit lasts **until the start of your next turn**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 20th Level – Holy Nimbus
As a **Bonus Action**, you can imbue your **Aura of Protection** with radiant power. This lasts for **10 minutes** or until you end it (no action required). You can’t use this feature again until you finish a **Long Rest**, unless you expend a **5th-level spell slot** to restore it.

### Benefits:

**Holy Ward.**  
You have **Advantage on saving throws** forced by Fiends or Undead.

**Radiant Damage.**  
Whenever an enemy **starts its turn in your aura**, it takes **Radiant damage equal to your Charisma modifier + Proficiency Bonus**.

**Sunlight.**  
Your aura emits **Bright Light** that counts as **sunlight**.

</Collapsible>
