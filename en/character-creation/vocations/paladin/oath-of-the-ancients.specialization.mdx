# Oath of the Ancients

_Preserve Life and Light in the World._

The Oath of the Ancients is as old as the first elves.  
Paladins who swear this oath cherish life and light, valuing the beauty of the natural world more than rigid principles of honor or justice. They often adorn themselves with images of growing things—leaves, antlers, or flowers—to symbolize their connection to the living world.

**Tenets of the Ancients**:

- Kindle the light of hope.
- Shelter life.
- Delight in art and laughter.

<Collapsible>
## 3rd Level – Nature's Wrath
As a **Magic action**, you can expend one use of **Channel Divinity** to conjure spectral vines.

Each creature of your choice that you can see within **15 feet** must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or become **Restrained** for **1 minute**.

A restrained creature repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

</Collapsible>

## 7th Level – Aura of Warding
Ancient magic surrounds you, forming a mystical ward.  
You and your allies in your **Aura of Protection** gain **Resistance** to **Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant damage**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 15th Level – Undying Sentinel
When you are reduced to **0 Hit Points** and not killed outright, you can choose to drop to **1 Hit Point instead**, then immediately regain additional Hit Points equal to **three times your Paladin level**.

Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

Additionally, you:

- Cannot be aged magically.
- Cease visibly aging.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 20th Level – Elder Champion
As a **Bonus Action**, you can empower your **Aura of Protection** with primal energy for **1 minute** or until you end the effect (no action required).

Once you use this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a **Long Rest**, unless you expend a **5th-level spell slot** to restore it.

### Benefits:

**Diminish Defiance.**  
Enemies in the aura have **Disadvantage on saving throws** against your **spells** and **Channel Divinity** options.

**Regeneration.**  
At the start of each of your turns, you regain **10 Hit Points**.

**Swift Spells.**  
When you cast a spell that has a casting time of **1 action**, you can cast it using a **Bonus Action instead**.

</Collapsible>
