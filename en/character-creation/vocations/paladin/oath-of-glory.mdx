# Oath of Glory

_Strive for the Heights of Heroism._

Paladins who take the Oath of Glory believe they and their companions are destined to achieve greatness through deeds of heroism. They train relentlessly, readying themselves and inspiring others for the moment when destiny calls.

**Tenets of Glory**:

- Endeavor to be known by your deeds.
- Face hardships with courage.
- Inspire others to strive for glory.

<Collapsible>
## 3rd Level – Inspiring Smite
Immediately after you cast **[Divine Smite](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:divine-smite)**, you can expend one use of **Channel Divinity** to distribute **Temporary Hit Points** to creatures of your choice within **30 feet** of yourself (which can include you).

The total Temporary Hit Points equal **2d8 + your Paladin level**, divided among the chosen creatures as you choose.

</Collapsible>

## 3rd Level – Peerless Athlete
As a **Bonus Action**, you can expend one use of **Channel Divinity** to augment your physical prowess.

For **1 hour**, you gain:

- **Advantage** on **Strength (Athletics)** and **Dexterity (Acrobatics)** checks.
- Your **Long Jump** and **High Jump** distances increase by **10 feet** (this extra distance still costs movement as normal).

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 7th Level – Aura of Alacrity
Your **Speed increases by 10 feet**.

In addition, whenever an ally enters your **Aura of Protection** for the first time on a turn or starts their turn there, that ally’s **Speed increases by 10 feet** until the end of their next turn.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 15th Level – Glorious Defense
You can turn defense into a sudden strike.  
When you or a creature you can see within **10 feet** of you is hit by an attack roll, you can use your **Reaction** to grant a bonus to the target’s AC against that attack equal to your **Charisma modifier** (minimum +1), potentially causing the attack to miss.

If it misses, you may make **one weapon attack** against the attacker as part of the same Reaction, if the attacker is within your weapon’s range.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **Charisma modifier** (minimum once), regaining all expended uses when you finish a **Long Rest**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 20th Level – Living Legend
As a **Bonus Action**, you can empower yourself with the legends—true or exaggerated—of your heroic deeds.  
This transformation lasts **10 minutes**.

Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**, unless you expend a **5th-level spell slot** to restore it.

While transformed, you gain:

- **Charismatic.** You have **Advantage** on all Charisma checks.
- **Saving Throw Reroll.** When you fail a saving throw, you can use your **Reaction** to reroll it. You must use the new result.
- **Unerring Strike.** Once on each of your turns, when you make a weapon attack and miss, you can cause that attack to **hit instead**.

</Collapsible>
