# Diviner

_Learn the Secrets of the Multiverse_

The counsel of a Diviner is sought by those who desire clarity about the past, present, and future. As a Diviner, you strive to part the veils of space, time, and consciousness. Your studies focus on spells of discernment, remote viewing, supernatural knowledge, and foresight.

<Collapsible>
## 3rd Level – Divination Savant
Choose two Wizard spells from the Divination school of level 2 or lower and add them to your spellbook for free.

Additionally, whenever you gain access to a new spell level, you can add one Divination spell of that level to your spellbook for free.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 3rd Level – Portent
Glimpses of the future press upon your awareness. Whenever you finish a Long Rest, roll **two d20s** and record the results.

You can replace any d20 Test made by you or a creature you can see with one of these rolls. You must choose to do so before the roll, and you can replace a roll in this way only once per turn.

Each foretelling roll can be used once. When you finish a Long Rest, you lose any unused rolls.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 6th Level – Expert Divination
Casting Divination spells comes easily to you. When you cast a Divination spell using a spell slot of level 2 or higher, you regain one expended spell slot. The regained slot must be of a lower level than the slot you expended and cannot be higher than level 5.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 10th Level – The Third Eye
You can enhance your perception. As a Bonus Action, choose one of the following benefits. The benefit lasts until you start a Short or Long Rest. You can't use this feature again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

- **Darkvision.** You gain Darkvision with a range of 120 feet.
- **Greater Comprehension.** You can read all written languages.
- **See Invisibility.** You can cast [See Invisibility](/en/library/spells/see-invisibility) without expending a spell slot.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th Level – Greater Portent
Your visions become more intense and accurate. You roll **three d20s** for your Portent feature instead of two.

</Collapsible>
