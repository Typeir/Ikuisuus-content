# Eldritch Invocations

## Agonizing Blast

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock, a Warlock Cantrip That Deals Damage_

Choose one of your known Warlock cantrips that deals damage. You can add your Charisma modifier to that spell’s damage rolls.

**Repeatable.** You can gain this invocation more than once. Each time you do so, choose a different eligible cantrip.

## Armor of Shadows

You can cast [Mage Armor](/en/library/spells/mage-armor) on yourself without expending a spell slot.

## Ascendant Step

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock_

You can cast [Levitate](/en/library/spells/levitate) on yourself without expending a spell slot.

## Devil’s Sight

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You can see normally in Dim Light and Darkness—both magical and nonmagical—within 120 feet.

## Devouring Blade

_Prerequisite: Level 12+ Warlock, Thirsting Blade Invocation_

The Extra Attack of your Thirsting Blade invocation confers two extra attacks rather than one.

## Eldritch Mind

You have Advantage on Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain Concentration.

## Eldritch Smite

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock, Pact of the Blade Invocation_

Once per turn when you hit a creature with your pact weapon, you can expend a Pact Magic spell slot to deal an extra 1d8 Force damage per slot level (minimum 1d8) and knock the target Prone if it is Huge or smaller.

## Eldritch Spear

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock, a Warlock Cantrip That Deals Damage and Has a Range of 10+ Feet_

Choose one of your known Warlock cantrips that deals damage. Its range increases by 30 × your Warlock level.

**Repeatable.** You can gain this invocation more than once. Each time you do so, choose a different eligible cantrip.

## Fiendish Vigor

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You can cast [False Life](/en/library/spells/false-life) on yourself without expending a spell slot, always gaining the maximum result.

## Gaze of Two Minds

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock_

As a Bonus Action, you can perceive through the senses of a willing creature you touch until the end of your next turn. You can use a Bonus Action on subsequent turns to maintain this effect. While perceiving, you benefit from the creature’s special senses and can cast spells as if you were in either space if you're within 60 feet of each other.

## Gift of the Depths

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock_

You can breathe underwater, gain a Swim Speed equal to your Speed, and cast [Water Breathing](/en/library/spells/water-breathing) once per Long Rest without expending a spell slot.

## Gift of the Protectors

_Prerequisite: Level 9+ Warlock, Pact of the Tome Invocation_

A page in your _Book of Shadows_ can hold names (up to your Charisma modifier). When a named creature is reduced to 0 Hit Points but not killed outright, it drops to 1 HP instead. This effect refreshes after a Long Rest.

## Investment of the Chain Master

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock, Pact of the Chain Invocation_

Your familiar gains:

- Fly or Swim Speed of 40 ft.
- Can attack as a Bonus Action.
- Can deal Necrotic or Radiant damage.
- Uses your spell save DC.
- Can gain Resistance to damage once per turn using your Reaction.

## Lessons of the First Ones

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You gain one [Origin feat](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/feat:all) of your choice.

**Repeatable.** You can take this invocation multiple times.

## Lifedrinker

_Prerequisite: Level 9+ Warlock, Pact of the Blade Invocation_

Once per turn when you hit with your pact weapon, deal an extra 1d6 Necrotic, Psychic, or Radiant damage and heal yourself for 1 Hit Die + Constitution modifier.

## Mask of Many Faces

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You can cast [Disguise Self](/en/library/spells/disguise-self) at will.

## Master of Myriad Forms

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock_

You can cast [Alter Self](/en/library/spells/alter-self) at will.

## Misty Visions

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You can cast [Silent Image](/en/library/spells/silent-image) at will.

## One with Shadows

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock_

In Dim Light or Darkness, you can cast [Invisibility](/en/library/spells/invisibility) on yourself at will.

## Otherworldly Leap

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock_

You can cast [Jump](/en/library/spells/jump) on yourself at will.

## Pact of the Blade

As a Bonus Action, conjure a pact weapon (Simple or Martial Melee) or bond with a magic weapon. You can use your Charisma modifier for attack and damage rolls and change the weapon’s damage to Necrotic, Psychic, or Radiant.

## Pact of the Chain

You learn [Find Familiar](/en/library/spells/find-familiar), castable as a Magic action. Your familiar can be an Imp, Pseudodragon, Quasit, Skeleton, Slaad Tadpole, Sphinx of Wonder, Sprite, or Venomous Snake. You can forgo one attack to let your familiar attack as a Reaction.

## Pact of the Tome

Conjure a _Book of Shadows_ containing three cantrips and two 1st-level rituals from any vocation. You can use the book as a Spellcasting Focus.

## Repelling Blast

_Prerequisite: Level 2+ Warlock, a Warlock Cantrip That Requires an Attack Roll_

When you hit a Large or smaller creature with the chosen cantrip, you can push the creature up to 10 feet away.

**Repeatable.** You can take this invocation multiple times.

## Thirsting Blade

_Prerequisite: Level 5+ Warlock, Pact of the Blade Invocation_

You gain Extra Attack with your pact weapon.

## Visions of Distant Realms

_Prerequisite: Level 9+ Warlock_

You can cast [Arcane Eye](/en/library/spells/arcane-eye) at will.

## Whispers of the Grave

_Prerequisite: Level 7+ Warlock_

You can cast [Speak with Dead](/en/library/spells/speak-with-dead) at will.

## Witch Sight

_Prerequisite: Level 15+ Warlock_

You gain Truesight out to 30 feet.
