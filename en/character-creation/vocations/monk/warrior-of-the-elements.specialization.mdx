# Warrior of the Elements

_The World Moves as You Move._

You follow a monastic tradition that teaches you to harness the elements. By focusing your focus, you align with the forces of creation—bending earth, air, fire, and water to your will. Some monks dedicate themselves to a single element, but others weave all four into one flowing practice.

Many monks of this tradition tattoo their bodies with representations of their focus powers—coiling dragons, phoenixes, waves, or mountains that shift as they fight.

## 3rd Level – Disciple of the Elements
You learn magical disciplines that harness the power of the four elements. Some disciplines require you to spend focus points.

- You learn **two elemental disciplines of your choice** from the list below.
- You learn **two additional disciplines** at **6th**, **11th**, and **17th level**.
- Whenever you learn a new discipline, you may replace one you know with another from the list.

### Casting Elemental Spells

When you cast a spell via a discipline:

- It is cast at its **lowest level**, unless specified otherwise.
- You **do not provide material components**.
- At **5th level**, you can spend **additional focus points** to upcast a discipline spell that has enhanced effects.  
  For each additional focus point spent, the spell’s level increases by 1.
- After using a discipline, you may make **one unarmed strike as a bonus action** or use **Flurry of Blows**.

## 3rd-Level Disciplines

- **Become the Teapot.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [absorb elements](/en/library/spells/absorb-elements).

- **Effortless Step.** Your **jump distance and height are doubled** (quadrupled with **Step of the Wind**). You have **advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks** related to jumping.

- **Enduring Mountain Stance.** When you take the **Dodge action**, you cannot be **moved, pushed, grappled, terrified, or knocked prone** until the start of your next turn. You must be **standing on the ground**.

- **Fangs of the Fire Snake.** When you make an **unarmed attack**, you may choose to **extend your reach by 5 feet** and deal **fire damage** instead of bludgeoning. On a hit, you may spend **1 focus point** to deal **+1d10 fire damage**.

- **Fang of the Frost Wolf.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [ice knife](/en/library/spells/ice-knife).

- **Fist of Four Thunders.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [thunderwave](/en/library/spells/thunderwave).

- **Fist of Unbroken Air.** When you make an **unarmed attack**, you may extend your reach by **10 feet**. On a hit, spend **1 focus point** to force the creature to make a **Strength saving throw** or be **pushed 10 feet** and **knocked prone**.

- **Golden Snake’s Icy Path.** When you **Dash**, you leave a **trail of ice** behind you. The ice is **difficult terrain** and lasts until the start of your next turn.

- **Rumbling Badger.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [earth tremor](/en/library/spells/earth-tremor).

- **Rush of the Gale Spirits.** Spend 2 focus points to cast [gust of wind](/en/library/spells/gust-of-wind).

- **Shape the Raincloud.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [create or destroy water](/en/library/spells/create-or-destroy-water).

- **Sweeping Cinder Strike.** Spend 1 focus point to cast [burning hands](/en/library/spells/burning-hands).

- **Water Whip.** Spend 1 focus point as a **bonus action** to whip a creature within **30 feet**. The creature makes a **Dexterity saving throw**. On a failure, it takes **your Martial Arts die + Wisdom modifier** in bludgeoning damage plus **1d10 per extra focus point** spent. You may **pull the creature up to 25 feet** or **knock it prone**.

## 17th-Level Disciplines

- **Avatar of the Elements.** Spend 5 focus points to activate a **1-minute avatar state**

  ## Avatar of the Elements

  As an ultimate display of your mastery of the elements, you can spend **5 focus points as an Action** to surround yourself with a sphere of elemental energy, channeling earth, fire, air, and water in harmony.

  This transformation lasts for **1 minute** and grants you the following benefits:
  - You gain **resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder damage**.
  - You gain a **burrow, fly, and swim speed** equal to your walking speed.

  ### Bonus Action: Elemental Assault

  While Avatar of the Elements is active, you can use one of the following effects as a **Bonus Action** each turn:
  - **Earthquake Burst.**  
    Each creature within **15 feet of you** must make a **Dexterity saving throw**. On a failed save, a creature takes **1d6 bludgeoning damage** and is **knocked prone**. On a success, the creature takes half damage and isn’t knocked prone.

  - **Line of Flame.**  
    You create a **15-foot line** of fire that is **5 feet wide**, extending from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **3d6 fire damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

  - **Swirling Wind Vortex.**  
    Choose a point you can see within **60 feet**. Each creature in a **15-foot cube** centered on that point must make a **Constitution saving throw**, taking **1d10 bludgeoning damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success. **Large or smaller creatures** that fail are also **pushed 10 feet away from the cube’s center**.

  - **Cone of Ice Shards.**  
    You unleash a **15-foot cone** of freezing shards. Each creature in the cone must make a **Constitution saving throw**, taking **2d6 cold damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success. A creature that fails also has its **speed halved until the start of your next turn**.

- **Breath of Winter.** Spend 5 focus points to cast [cone of cold](/en/library/spells/cone-of-cold).

- **Eyes of Fire.** Cast [see invisibility](/en/library/spells/see-invisibility) for free. Spend 2 focus as a **bonus action** to gain **truesight until end of turn**.

- **Eye of the Hurricane.** Spend 5 focus points to cast [control winds](/en/library/spells/control-winds).

- **Mold the Mountain.** Spend 5 focus points to cast [transmute rock](/en/library/spells/transmute-rock).

- **Swirling Crab’s Revenge.** Spend 5 focus points to cast [maelstrom](/en/library/spells/maelstrom).

- **Wave of Rolling Earth.** Spend 5 focus points to cast [wall of stone](/en/library/spells/wall-of-stone).
