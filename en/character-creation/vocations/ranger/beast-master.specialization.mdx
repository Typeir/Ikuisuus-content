# Beast Master

_Bond with a Primal Beast_

A Beast Master forms a mystical bond with a special animal, drawing on primal magic and a deep connection to the natural world.

<Collapsible>
## 3rd Level: Primal Companion
You magically summon a primal beast, which draws strength from your bond with nature. Choose its stat block:

- **Beast of the Land**
- **Beast of the Sea**
- **Beast of the Sky**

You also determine the kind of animal it is, choosing a form appropriate for the stat block. Whatever beast you choose, it bears primal markings indicating its supernatural origin.

---

### The Beast in Combat

- The beast acts on your turn.
- It can move and use its Reaction on its own.
- The only action it takes is the **Dodge** action unless you take a **Bonus Action** to command it to take an action in its stat block or another action.
- You can forgo one of your attacks when you take the **Attack** action to command the beast to take the **Beast’s Strike** action.
- If you have the **Incapacitated** condition, the beast acts on its own and isn’t limited to the Dodge action.

---

### Restoring or Replacing the Beast

If the beast has died within the last hour, you can take a **Magic action** to touch it and expend a spell slot. The beast returns to life after **1 minute** with all its Hit Points restored.

Whenever you finish a **Long Rest**, you can summon a different primal beast, which appears in an unoccupied space within **5 feet** of you. You choose its stat block and appearance. If you already have a beast from this feature, the old one vanishes when the new one appears.

<Collapsible>
## 7th Level: Exceptional Training
When you take a **Bonus Action** to command your Primal Companion to take an action, you can also command it to take the **Dash**, **Disengage**, **Dodge**, or **Help** action using its **Bonus Action**.

In addition, whenever it hits with an attack roll and deals damage, it can deal your choice of **Force damage** or its normal damage type.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 11th Level: Bestial Fury
When you command your Primal Companion to take the **Beast’s Strike** action, the beast can use it **twice**.

In addition, the first time each turn it hits your [_Prey_](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/ranger/lay-of-the-land#prey), the beast deals extra **Force damage** equal to half your ranger level.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 15th Level: Share Spells
When you cast a spell targeting yourself, you can also affect your Primal Companion with the spell if the beast is within **30 feet** of you.

</Collapsible>
