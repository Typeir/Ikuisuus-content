# Telekineticist

The quintessential manipulator of matter, the Telekineticist bends the physical world to their will. With a thought, they lift, hurl, and reshape objects and creatures alike. For them, no creature is too strong, no structure is too big.

### 3rd Level – Extended Manipulation
You can cast _Mage Hand_ at will. The hand has a range of 60 feet.
If you already knew mage hand, you learn another cantrip of your choice from the [_Esper spell list_](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/esper/spells.list).

As an **action**, you can strike a creature you can see within range of your mage hand.
Make a ranged spell attack. On a Hit, the target takes **force damage** equal to **two rolls** of your **Psionic Dice** and is **pushed 10 feet away** from you in a straight line.
The damage increases when you reach higher levels in this vocation:  
at **5th level**, the damage becomes **three rolls** of your Psionic Dice;  
at **11th level**, **four rolls**;  
and at **17th level**, **five rolls**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 6th Level – Kinetic Shear
Your telekinesis exploits structural stress points with ruthless efficiency.
Whenever a spell or vocation feature you use **deals damage** to a **structure or unattended object**, it deals **additional force damage** equal to **four** rolls of your **Psionic Dice**.

Whenever you make an intelligence (Investigation, History) check to analyze the integrity of a structure or object, you can roll your **Psionic dice** and add it to the result.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 10th Level – Telekinetic Shield
Whenever you are hit by an attack, an unseen kinetic force stops part of the blow.
Subtract your **intelligence modifier** (minimum 1) from the damage taken from **all attacks,** as long as you are not **Incapacitated**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 10th Level – Telekinetic Slam
When your forced movement causes a creature to collide with a **solid surface**, they take force damage equal to a roll of your **Psionic Dice per 5** feet of movement remaining after the collision, up to a maximum of **5 rolls**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 14th Level – World Mover
Whenever you would push or pull a creature or object with a spell or vocation feature, you can **double the distance** you move it.
You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **Intelligence modifier** (minimum of once).
You regain all uses when you finish a **Long Rest**.

</Collapsible>
