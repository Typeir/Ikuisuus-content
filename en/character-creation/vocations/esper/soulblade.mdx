# Soulblade

A warrior who shapes psionic energy into a weapon of pure thought, striking with precision and devastating force. The mind, the ID, is yet another battlefield.

## 3rd Level – Soulblade Spells

You know the [_Blade Burst_](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:blade-burst) cantrip. Additionally, you know the following spells, depending on your Esper level:

These spells count as Esper spells for you, but they don’t count against the number of Esper spells you know.
When you cast them, you may choose to deal psychic damage instead of their normal damage type.

### 3rd Level – Psychic Blade

You can manifest a psychic blade as an extension of your will.  
This blade is a **simple melee weapon** that uses the **rapier** as its base for **weapon masteries and properties**.
You are prificient with this weapon, and are considered as having mastery with it.

- The blade deals **psychic damage** equal to **two rolls of your Psionic Dice**.
- You can use your **Intelligence or Constitution modifier** for attack and damage rolls with this weapon.
- The blade can be **summoned or dismissed at will**, requires no action, and can be **invisible**.
- You **cannot be disarmed** of this weapon.
- Any checks made to conceal or smuggle the blade **automatically succeed**, unless a creature can perceive or identify psychic manifestations.
- The blade counts as **magical** for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks.

### 6th Level – Mind-Sundering Edge

Your psychic blade cuts past mental defenses.

- You **ignore resistance** to psychic damage dealt by your psychic blade.
- When you deal psychic damage to a creature that is **immune** to psychic damage, you can instead deal **force damage** equal to **one roll of your Psionic Dice** (no modifier).

### 6th Level – Extra Attack

You can attack **twice**, instead of once, whenever you take the **Attack** action on your turn.

### 10th Level – Perfect Focus

Your psychic blade sharpens your defensive discipline.

When you use a **Mind Path** as a reaction, you gain a bonus to your Armor Class equal to the number rolled until the end of this turn.

While your Armor Class is increased by a Mind Path, **opportunity attacks against you are made at disadvantage**.

### 14th Level – Soul Overdraw

When you cast a spell using your **action**, you can take the **Attack** action as a **bonus action**, making up to **two attacks** with your psychic blade.

When you do so, you may overextend your blade with raw psionic force. As part of the bonus action, you can expend a spell slot to empower your attacks until the start of your next turn:

- The **reach** of your psychic blade increases by **5 feet**, plus an additional **5 feet for each level of the spell slot expended**.
- The **next time you hit a creature** with your psychic blade before the start of your next turn, the attack deals **additional force damage** equal to **1d4 per level of the spell slot expended**, to a maximum of **12d4**.

This additional damage is applied **once**, regardless of the number of attacks made.
