# Soothsayer

A seer who redistributes harm rather than erasing it. A song of empathy sang in wavelengths of pain and healing, heard only by those attuned to the mind’s deeper currents.

<Collapsible>
### 3rd Level – Soothing words
You know the [_Cure Wounds_](/en/library/spells/cure-wounds) spell .  
It counts as an Esper spell for you, but it doesn’t count against the number of Esper spells you know.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 3rd Level – Resonant Sustenance
While you are **concentrating on a spell**, whenever you **restore hit points or grant temporary hit points** to a creature, you can intensify that effect as a **bonus action**.

Choose one creature you can see within **60 feet**. That creature regains hit points equal to a number of **rolls of your Psionic Dice** equal to the **spell slot level** of the spell you are concentrating on.

- For example, if you are concentrating on a **3rd-level spell**, the creature regains **3 rolls of your Psionic Dice**.
- This healing can be applied to **only one creature per bonus action**.

When you use this feature, the spell you are concentrating on **cannot have its concentration last longer than 1 minute**.  
If the spell’s normal duration exceeds 1 minute, it instead ends when this limit is reached.

This feature does not function if you are concentrating on a spell with a duration **shorter than 1 minute**.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 6th Level – Guided Reposition
Whenever you **restore hit points or grant temporary hit points** to one or more creatures, you can immediately **teleport** as a **bonus action**.

- You teleport up to **30 feet** to an unoccupied space within **5 feet** of one creature you affected.
- This teleport does not provoke opportunity attacks.
- You must be able to see the destination.

This teleport occurs **after** the healing or temporary hit points are applied.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 10th Level – Mind Cushion
Your foresight prevents excess vitality from being wasted.

Whenever you **restore hit points** to a creature and the healing would cause it to exceed its maximum hit points, the excess healing is converted into **temporary hit points equal to twice the excess amount**.

- These temporary hit points last for **1 minute**.
- Temporary hit points gained this way do not stack with themselves.

This feature does not trigger from temporary hit points already being granted — only from **actual healing** that would overflow.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 14th Level – Overflowing Synapses
You can convert unrealized survival into immediate violence.

As an **action**, you choose a **willing creature** you can see within **30 feet** that has temporary hit points.

You remove any number of those temporary hit points (up to **10 × your Intelligence modifier**) and deal **force damage** equal to the amount removed to a creature you can see within **60 feet**.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your **Intelligence modifier** (minimum of once), and you regain all uses when you finish a **long rest**.

</Collapsible>
