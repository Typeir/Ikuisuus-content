# Telepath

A telepath is a master of mind gates and psychic communication, able to reach into the thoughts of others and influence their actions. They excel at mental manipulation and telepathic connection.

<Collapsible>
### 3rd Level – Awakened Mind
Your Psionic Link expands beyond conventional limits.

Whenever you establish telepathy using **Psionic Link**, its range becomes **60 feet × the total rolled** instead of 20 feet × the total rolled.

In addition:

- You do not need to share a language with the creature.
- The creature does not need to be willing.
- The creature does not need to be able to understand a language.

The telepathy functions regardless of the target’s capacity for speech or comprehension.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 6th Level – Intrusive Thought
When a creature you can see uses or declares an **action** of any kind, **bonus action**, or **reaction**, you can use your reaction to assault its mind.

The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your Esper spell save DC.  
On a failure, the creature takes **Psychic damage** equal to **two rolls** of your **Psionic Dice**, and the triggering action, bonus action, or reaction is **wasted** and has no effect.
Any resource the creature spent during the triggering action is refunded.

Once a creature is affected by this feature, it becomes **immune** to it until the end of its next turn.

You can use this feature a number of times per Long Rest equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once).

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 10th Level – Distributed Cognition
As a bonus action, you can designate one creature you are currently linked to via **Psionic Link**.

Until the start of your next turn, that creature can trigger **one of your [_Mind Paths_](/en/library/character-creation/vocations/esper/mind-paths.reference)** on its turn **without using an action or reaction**.

- The Mind Path still follows its normal triggering conditions.
- The Mind Path still counts against your once-per-round limit.
- The creature uses your Psionic Dice and Esper spell save DC, if applicable.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
### 14th Level – Borrowed Cognition
As a bonus action, you enter a state of heightened telepathic synchronization with one creature you are linked to via **Psionic Link**. This state lasts for **1 minute**.

While this state is active, **once per round**, when either you or the linked creature makes an **attack roll, saving throw, or ability check**, you can choose one of the following options before the roll is made:

- **Cognitive Override.** You make the roll **in place of** the creature, using your own ability scores, modifiers, and proficiencies.
- **Cognitive Delegation.** The linked creature makes the roll **in place of you**, using its own ability scores, modifiers, and proficiencies.

The roll is treated as if made by the original creature for all effects, targets, and consequences.

Once you use this feature, you cannot do so again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

</Collapsible>
