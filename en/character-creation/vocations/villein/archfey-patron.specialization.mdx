# Archfey Patron

_Bargain with Whimsical Fey_

Your pact draws on the Hidden Kingdom. When you choose this specialization, you might strike a deal with one of the great **väki** — an Idea old enough and fed enough to have opinions — or with a Hiisi that keeps a court and expects it observed. Or you might call on a spectrum of lesser väki instead, weaving a web of favours and debts nobody has written down. Whoever they are, your patron deals in terms rather than affection, and holds you to them exactly.

<Collapsible>
## 1st Level – Steps of the Fey
You can cast [Misty Step](/en/library/spells/misty-step) without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a Recovery.

Whenever you cast [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step), you can choose one of the following additional effects:

- **Refreshing Step.** You or one creature you can see within 10 feet gains [% 1d10 %] Temporary Hit Points.
- **Taunting Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of the space you left must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC = your spell save DC) or have **Disadvantage** on attack rolls against creatures other than you until the start of your next turn.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 6th Level – Misty Escape
You can cast [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step) as a Reaction in response to taking damage.

Additionally, you gain two new options for Steps of the Fey:

- **Disappearing Step.** You gain the **Invisible** condition until the start of your next turn or until you attack, deal damage, or cast a spell.
- **Dreadful Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of the space you left or the space you appear in (your choice) must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take [% 2d10 %] **Psychic** damage.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 10th Level – Beguiling Defenses
You are immune to the **Charmed** condition.

Additionally, when a creature hits you with an attack roll, you can use your Reaction to halve the damage and force the attacker to make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = your spell save DC). On a failed save, the attacker takes **Psychic** damage equal to the damage you took. You can use this feature once per Recovery, or restore it by expending a Pact Magic spell slot (no action required).

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th Level – Bewitching Magic
Immediately after you cast an **Enchantment** or **Illusion** spell using a Major Action and a spell slot, you can cast [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step) as part of the same Action and without expending a spell slot.

</Collapsible>
