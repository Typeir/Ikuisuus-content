# Great Old One Patron

_Unearth Forbidden Lore of Ineffable Beings_

When you choose this specialization, you might bind yourself to something that was never meant to be addressed: a Terror of the Vastness, whose attention is a weather condition rather than a decision; something in the Everdark that has been counting since before there was anything to count; or a Null that held together long enough to want. Or you might call on several without yoking yourself to any one of them. Their motives are not incomprehensible so much as irrelevant — yours are not part of the arrangement — and the thing you have bound yourself to may well be indifferent to your existence. The secrets you have learned work anyway.

<Collapsible>
## 1st Level – Awakened Mind
You can form a telepathic connection between your mind and the mind of another. As a Minor Action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. You and the chosen creature can communicate telepathically while within a number of miles equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of 1 mile). To understand each other, you each must mentally use a language the other knows.

The telepathic connection lasts for a number of minutes equal to your Villein level. It ends early if you use this feature to connect with a different creature.

</Collapsible>

## 3rd Level – Psychic Spells
When you cast a Villein spell that deals damage, you can change its damage type to Psychic.

In addition, when you cast a Villein spell that is an Enchantment or Illusion, you can do so without Verbal or Somatic components.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 6th Level – Clairvoyant Combatant
When you form a telepathic bond with a creature using your Awakened Mind, you can force that creature to make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save:

- The creature has Disadvantage on attack rolls against you.
- You have Advantage on attack rolls against that creature for the duration of the bond.

Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Repose or Recovery unless you expend a Pact Magic spell slot (no action required) to restore your use of it.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 10th Level – Eldritch Hex
You always have the [Hex](/en/library/spells/hex) spell prepared. When you cast Hex and choose an ability, the target also has Disadvantage on saving throws of the chosen ability for the spell's duration.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 10th Level – Thought Shield
- Your thoughts can’t be read by telepathy or other means unless you allow it.
- You have Resistance to Psychic damage.
- Whenever a creature deals Psychic damage to you, it takes the same amount of damage that you take.

</Collapsible>

<Collapsible>
## 14th Level – Create Thrall
When you cast [_Conjure Aberration_](/en/library/spells/conjure-aberration), you can modify it so that it doesn’t require Concentration. If you do so:

- The spell's duration becomes 1 minute.
- The Aberration has Temporary Hit Points equal to your Villein level plus your Charisma modifier.

In addition, the first time each turn the Aberration hits a creature under the effect of your [Hex](/en/library/spells/hex), it deals extra Psychic damage equal to the bonus damage of that spell.

</Collapsible>
