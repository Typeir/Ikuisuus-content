# Defensive Duelist

_Prerequisite: **Dueling** style, **Dexterity 13**_

Your training in one-on-one combat has taught you to read your opponent's body language and turn aside their strikes with a flick of your blade.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Parry.** When you are wielding a **finesse weapon** with which you are proficient and another creature hits you with a melee attack, you can use your **reaction** to add your **Proficiency Bonus** to your AC until the start of your next turn — potentially causing the attack to miss.
