# Ironeye

_Prerequisite: **Strength** or **Dexterity** 17, proficiency with at least one ranged weapon, the [\_Sharpshooter_](/en/library/feats/sharpshooter). feat\_

No target is too distant for you, your aim is true and your vision is keen.

---

Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits:

Your long range is doubled for any ranged weapon you are proficient with.

When using a weapon with the loading property that you are proficient with, you can choose to perform an aimed shot, which has the following benefits:

- Your critical hit range with that weapon increases by 2.
- Your attacks deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus.

If you chose to perform an aimed shot, you cannot ignore the loading property of a weapon when doing so.
