# Sharpshooter

_Prerequisite: **Archery** Fighting Style_

You can make shots that others find impossible, striking true through distance, cover, and chaos.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Bypass Cover.** Your ranged weapon attacks ignore **half cover**, and treat **three-quarters cover** as **half cover**.

- **Long Shots.** Attacking at **long range** doesn’t impose **disadvantage** on your ranged weapon attack rolls.
