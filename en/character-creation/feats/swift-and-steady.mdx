# Swift and Steady

_Prerequisite: **Extra Attack** feature, **Dexterity 15**_

You have trained to combine disciplined stillness with explosive efficiency — when you plant your feet, you attack faster.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Planted Assault.** While you are in the [**Steady**](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/movement#steady) state, you may make **one additional attack** as part of the **Attack** action on your turn.

  This bonus attack follows all normal rules for additional attacks granted by Extra Attack. It triggers on-hit effects normally.
