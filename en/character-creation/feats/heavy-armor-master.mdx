# Heavy Armor Master

_Prerequisite: **Defense** Fighting Style, **Proficiency with heavy armor**_

Your discipline in wearing heavy armor is total. You have trained yourself to turn aside punishment that would fell lesser warriors.

---

Increase your **Strength or Constitution score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Damage Reduction.** While wearing **heavy armor**, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage you take from **non-magical weapons** is reduced by **3**.
