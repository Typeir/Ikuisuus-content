# Epic Boon: Blessings of the Archivist  

Through eons of hoarding and documenting the vile and beautiful lands of Damocles,
your connection to the Archivist and his goals have granted you knowledge abound of the world’s inner workings — The tools, trades, tricks and tongues of its inhabitants.  

> “To have lived is to be documented; to document the living is to live.”

---

Increase your **Intelligence** or **Wisdom** score by 1, up to a maximum of 30.

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits: 

- Gain *expertise in all tools*.  
- Gain *proficiency in one skill of your choice*.  
- Gain *expertise in another skill of your choice*.  
- Learn *three languages of your choice*.  
- If you already had expertise in a tool, you now have *advantage* on ability checks using that tool.
