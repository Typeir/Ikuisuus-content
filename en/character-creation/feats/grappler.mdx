# Grappler

_Prerequisite: **Strength 13 (or higher)**_

You learned close-quarters grappling from the Graspbrood of the Black Cradle, mastering holds and locks taught in its iron yards.

---

Increase your **Strength score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Advantage on grappled attacks.** You have advantage on attack rolls against a creature you are grappling.
- **Pin.** You can use your action to try to pin a creature grappled by you. To do so, make another grapple check. If you succeed, you and the creature are both restrained until the grapple ends.
