# Healer

_Prerequisite: **Wisdom** 13 or higher_

You are an able physician, allowing you to mend wounds quickly and get your allies back in the fight.

---

Increase your Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- You gain proficiency with the **Medicine** skill, if you do not already have it. If you are already proficient, you gain **Expertise** in it instead.

- As an action, you can expend **one use of a healer’s kit** to tend to a creature and restore **Hit points equal to your Medicine bonus**, plus additional hit points equal to the creature’s Level or Challenge Rating.  
  A creature can’t regain hit points from this benefit again until it finishes a **Recovery**.

- When you use a healer’s kit during a **Repose** to restore hit points, you can treat a number of creatures up to your **Tier Bonus**, instead of one.  
  Each creature can benefit from this treatment **only once per Repose**.
