# Lunar Curse

_Origin Feat_

Through forbidden rites of rebirth, lunar fleshcraft, a failed moonblessing—or something worse—you have learned to briefly reshape your body at will.

---

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- As a bonus action, you can enter a **lunar shifting** state. This transformation lasts for **1 minute**, until you die, or until you end it early as a bonus action.

- When you enter lunar shifting, you gain **temporary hit points equal to 2 × your proficiency bonus**.

- While shifted, you gain **one Lunar Adaptation** of your choice from the list below. You choose the adaption when you take the feat.

- Additionally, while sifted, you gan darkvision out to a range of **60 feet**, and, if you already have darkvision, its range increases by **30 feet**.

- You can use lunar shifting a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

## Lunar Adaptations

**Beasthide.** You gain **1d6 additional temporary hit points**, and while shifted, you gain a **+1 bonus to Armor Class**.

**Longtooth.** While shifted, you can make an unarmed strike with elongated fangs as a bonus action. On a hit, the target takes **1d6 + your Strength modifier piercing damage**.

**Swiftstride.** While shifted, your walking speed increases by **10 feet**. In addition, when a creature ends its turn within 5 feet of you, you can use your reaction to move up to **10 feet** without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Wildhunt.** While shifted, you have **advantage on Wisdom checks**, and no creature within **30 feet** of you can make attack rolls with advantage against you unless you are incapacitated.
