# Power Attacker

_Prerequisite: **Strength 18** or **Dexterity 18**_

You commit to precision or extreme force, launching strikes so difficult to land their damage would be disastrous.

---

Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- Choose one weapon type you are proficient with, including natural weapons.
  When wielding that weapon you may use the following features:
- At the **start of your turn**, you may declare a **Power Attack Round**.

  - Until the start of your next turn, **all attack rolls you make** suffer a **–5 penalty**.
  - Until the start of your next turn, **all damage rolls you make** with your preferred weapon gain a **+10 bonus**.

- You cannot take this decision back. What is done is done.
