# Dual Wielder

_Prerequisite: **Two-Weapon Fighting** style_

You have mastered the fluid art of the twin blades, removing the clumsiness that limits other dual wielders.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Unrestricted Off-Hand.** You can use two-weapon fighting even when the one-handed melee weapons you are wielding aren't light.

- **Quick Draw.** You can draw or stow two one-handed weapons when you would normally be able to draw or stow only one.

- **Defensive Rotation.** While you are wielding a melee weapon in each hand, you gain a **+1 bonus to AC**.
