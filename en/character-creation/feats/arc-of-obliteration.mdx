# Arc of Obliteration

_Prerequisite: **Extra Attack** feature, **Strength 17** or **Dexterity 17**_

You have mastered the art of the single decisive strike — collapsing all your potential attacks into one overwhelming blow.

---

Increase your **Strength or Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Obliterating Strike.** When you take the **Attack** action, you may choose to make a single attack in place of all attacks granted by **Extra Attack**. If you do:
  - You forgo all additional attacks.
  - Add the **weapon's damage dice** one additional time **for each attack you forgo** (before applying modifiers). These extra damage dice are not multiplied on a critical hit.
  - Add a **+2 bonus to the attack and damage rolls** for each attack you forgo.

_Example: A Fighter with Extra Attack (2 total attacks) who uses Obliterating Strike forgoes 1 attack, rolls the weapon damage dice twice, and adds +2 to the attack roll._

_Example: A Fighter with Two Extra Attacks (3 total attacks) who uses Obliterating Strike forgoes 2 attacks, rolls the weapon damage dice three times, and adds +4 to the attack roll._
