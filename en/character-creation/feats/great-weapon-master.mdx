# Great Weapon Master

_Prerequisite: **Great Weapon Fighting** style_

You have learned to put the momentum of a heavy weapon to devastating use — every swing carries the full weight of your strength and will.

---

Increase your **Strength score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Cleave Through.** When you score a **critical hit** with a melee weapon or reduce a creature to 0 hit points with one, you can make one melee weapon attack **as a bonus action**.

- **Grand Blows** When you deal damage to a creature using a heavy weapon, you deal extra damage equal to your **proficiency bonus**.
