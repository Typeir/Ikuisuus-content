# Quickdraw

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with any ranged martial weapon**_

You maintain lethal efficiency with ranged weapons even under pressure, relying on speed, discipline, and practiced handling.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- You **ignore the loading property** of ranged weapons with which you are proficient.

- Being within **5 feet of a hostile creature** doesn’t impose **disadvantage** on your ranged weapon attack rolls.
