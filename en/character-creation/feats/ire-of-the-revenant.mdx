# Ire of the Revenant

_Prerequisite: **Constitution**, **Strength** or **Dexterity** 19._

You have fallen in combat countless time, allowing you to muster strength before losing consciousness.

---

Increase your Constitution, Strength, or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you select this feat you gain the following benefits:

- Once per short rest, when you drop below 1 hit point but are not killed outright, you immediately take a full turn.

- During this turn, you have advantage on every roll.
 
- After this turn ends, you fall unconscious as normal unless you have more than 0 hit points.
 
- You can use this feature once per long rest.
