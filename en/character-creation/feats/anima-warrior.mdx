# Anima Warrior

A splinter of the primordial forces of the world is lodged within your will, granting you the ability to briefly call upon the mantle of existence to bolster your attack.

---

Increase **any** of your scores by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Anima Assault**: whenever you deal damage from any source, provided the target is within 100 feet of you, you may choose to expend one use of this feature to bolster the damage one of the following effects:

**Scorching Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d10 fire damage, and must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be affected by the [Burning](/en/library/rules/conditions#Burning) condition.
**Sleet Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d6 cold damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw, or its speed is reduced to 0 until the start of your next turn.
**Storm Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d6 lightning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw, or it has disadvantage on attack rolls until the start of your next turn, and is [Staggered](/en/library/rules/conditions#Staggered).
**Stone Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet from you in a straight line.
**Severing Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d10 slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be affected by the [Bleeding](/en/library/rules/conditions#Bleeding) condition.
**Skewering Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d10 piercing damage. If you can effectively grapple the target, it must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be [Grappled](/en/library/rules/conditions#Grappled) by you.
**Sulfuric Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d8 acid damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain the [Sundered](/en/library/rules/conditions#Sundered) condition until the end of their next turn.
**Serpentine Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d12 poison damage, and must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain the [Poisoned](/en/library/rules/conditions#Poisoned) condition until the start of your next turn.
**Synaptic Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d8 psychic damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or fall [Prone](/en/library/rules/conditions#Prone).
**Sonic Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d10 force damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw, or become [Deafened](/en/library/rules/conditions#Deafened) until the start of your next turn.
**Seraphic Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d6 holy damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be [Blinded](/en/library/rules/conditions#Blinded) until the start of your next turn.
**Shadow Strike**. The target takes an extra 1d8 dark damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be [Frightened](/en/library/rules/conditions#Frightened) until the start of your next turn.
**Supreme Strike**. All the damage from your attack becomes **true damage**, and it deals extra damage equal to your tier bonus.

The saving throw DC for these effects equals 8 + your tier bonus + the ability modifier of the stat you increased when you took this feat.

You can use this feat a number of times equal to your tier bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
