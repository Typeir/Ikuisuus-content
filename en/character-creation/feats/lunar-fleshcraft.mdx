# Lunar Fleshcraft

_Prerequisite: [Lunar Blessing](/en/library/character-creation/feats/lunar-blessing)_

The flesh is clay, and the moon whispers the secret paths of its shaping. You have learned to hear that distant voice and mold your body through sheer will.

---

Increase your Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- When you **enter lunar shifting**, its duration becomes **10 minutes × your proficiency bonus**, instead of 1 minute.

- Each time you enter lunar shifting, you may choose **one additional [Lunar Adaptation](/en/library/character-creation/feats/lunar-blessing#lunar-adaptations)** from the Lunar Adaptations list granted by **[Lunar Blessing](/en/library/character-creation/feats/lunar-blessing)**. You gain that benefit for the duration of the shift, in addition to the adaptation you normally choose.

  You can choose a different additional adaptation from the [_Lunar Adaptations_](/en/library/character-creation/feats/lunar-blessing#lunar-adaptations) each time you shift.
