# Two-Weapon Fighting

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with any martial melee weapon**_

You have trained to fight with a blade in each hand, coordinating your strikes to land in rapid succession.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can **add your ability modifier** to the damage of the **off-hand attack**.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- Whenever you **miss** with an attack roll while using Steady, you can immediately **make an attack roll** against another creature within 5 feet of the original target, provided that creature is **within your reach**.
- You have disadvantage on this attack roll.
