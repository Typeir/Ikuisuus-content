# Defense

You train to keep yourself protected at all times.

---

Increase your **Constitution score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- While you are wearing armor, you gain a **+1 bonus to AC**.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- When an attacker misses you with a melee attack, they must make a Strength save (DC 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Constitution modifier) or fall prone.
