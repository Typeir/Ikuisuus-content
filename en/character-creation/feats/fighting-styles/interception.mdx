# Interception

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with shields** or **a free hand**_

You intercept blows aimed at your allies, putting your own body or blade between them and harm.

---

Increase your **Constitution score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- When a creature you can see **hits a target other than you** that is within 5 feet of you with an attack, you can use your **reaction** to reduce the damage the target takes by **1d10 + your Proficiency Bonus + your level**.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- Your reaction range extends to **15 feet**. Additionally, you can move yourself to any point within **5 feet of the target** as part of using this technique.
