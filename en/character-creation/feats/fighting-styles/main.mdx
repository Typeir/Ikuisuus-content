# Fighting Styles

**Fighting Style** feats define a martial approach trained through years of focused practice. They are available as feats at character creation or through martial vocation features.

**Vocations that grant a Fighting Style** (Fighter, Ranger, Paladin, and similar) handle the ability score bonus according to their own rules — most vocations that grant a Fighting Style do not provide the ability score improvement, only the combat benefits. When you select a Fighting Style as a standalone feat at character creation, you gain both the ability score improvement and the combat benefits.

---
