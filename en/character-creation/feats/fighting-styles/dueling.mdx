# Dueling

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with any martial melee weapon**_

You train in the art of the one-on-one duel, attacking in tight controlled strikes.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- When you are wielding a **melee weapon in one hand** and **no other weapons** (a shield does not count as a weapon), you gain a **+2 bonus to damage rolls** with that weapon.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- After you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can move up to **5 feet**. This movement provokes opportunity attacks as normal.
