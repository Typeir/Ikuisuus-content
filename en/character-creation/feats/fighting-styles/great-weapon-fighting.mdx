# Great Weapon Fighting

_Prerequisite: **Strength 15**_

You train to channel your full weight and power into sweeping, devastating blows.

---

Increase your **Strength score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- When you roll a **1 or 2** on a damage die for an attack with a **two-handed or versatile weapon** (held in two hands), you can treat them as a **3**.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- Your melee attack range with two-handed and versatile weapons extends by **5 feet**.
