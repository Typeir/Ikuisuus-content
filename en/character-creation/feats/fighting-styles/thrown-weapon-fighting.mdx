# Thrown Weapon Fighting

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with any thrown weapon**_

You have mastered the art of hurling weapons with lethal precision.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- You can **draw a thrown weapon as part of the attack** you make with it.
- You gain a **+2 bonus to damage rolls** with thrown weapon attacks.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- When you make a thrown weapon attack, you can bounce the weapon toward another creature you can see within 60 feet. That target takes damage equal to your ability modifier.
