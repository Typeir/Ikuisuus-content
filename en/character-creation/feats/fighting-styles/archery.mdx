# Archery

_Prerequisite: **Proficiency with any ranged martial weapon**_

You have trained extensively in the discipline of ranged combat.

---

Increase your **Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefit:

- You gain a **+2 bonus to attack rolls** you make with ranged weapons.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- Designate a **5 foot sphere** that you can see within your range. Enemies that move through it without starting their turn there provoke opportunity attacks from you.
