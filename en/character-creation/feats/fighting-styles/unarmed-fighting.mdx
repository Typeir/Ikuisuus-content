# Unarmed Fighting

You have developed your body as a weapon through rigorous training.

---

Increase your **Strength or Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- Your unarmed strikes deal an additional **1d4** damage of the corresponding type. This damage is in addition to any other damage they may already deal.
- If you have both hands free and aren't wielding a weapon, the die increases to **1d6**.

When you use Steady, you gain access to the following technique:

- When you successfully **start or maintain a grapple**, you can deal damage equal to your unarmed strike damage to the creature.
