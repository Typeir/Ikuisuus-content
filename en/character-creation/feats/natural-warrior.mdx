# Natural Warrior

_Prerrequisite: **Strength 13** or **Dexterity 13**, a set of natural weapons._

Your body is a weapon, honed to perfection.

---

Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

- When you take this feat you gain the following benefits:

- You gain mastery with natural weapons.
- Your natural weapons are considered martial melee weapons.
- Your natural weapons gain an increase in their dice level, as shown in the rules for [_Weapons And Dice Levels_](/en/library/rules/weapons-and-dice-levels).

- Your natural weapons now have the following [_mastery properties_](/en/library/rules/weapon-properties), depending on their damage type:

| Damage Type | Mastery | Masterful Blow |
| ----------- | ------- | -------------- |
| Bludgeoning | Push    | Tackling       |
| Piercing    | Vex     | Hemorrhaging   |
| Slashing    | Sap     | Hamstringing   |
| Other       | Graze   | Sundering      |
