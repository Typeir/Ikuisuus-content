# Shield Master

_Prerequisite: **Interception** or **Protection** Fighting Style_

You have trained to exploit the full tactical potential of your shield — using it not just to deflect blows but to throw enemies off balance.

---

Increase your **Strength or Constitution score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Shield Shove.** If you take the **Attack** action on your turn, you can use a **bonus action** to try to **shove a creature** within 5 feet of you with your shield.

- **Block.** If you are subjected to an effect that allows a **Dexterity saving throw** to take only half damage, you can use your **reaction** to take **no damage** on a successful save, interposing your shield between you and the source.

- **Fortified Stance.** While you are holding a shield, you gain a **+1 bonus to saving throws**.
