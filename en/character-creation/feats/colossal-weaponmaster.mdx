# Colossal Weaponmaster

_Prerequisite: **Strength 17**_

You excel in wielding weapons that were never meant for you. You do so not cleanly, but decisively.

---

Increase your **Strength score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- You may choose to ignore any source of advantage on an attack roll you make with a weapon of a size larger than you. Make this choice before rolling. If you do, you gain a +2 bonus to the **attack roll and the damage roll**. This choice applies even if that advantage would otherwise be canceled by disadvantage.

## Colossal Extremes

Once per turn, when you make an attack with a weapon of a size larger than you, apply the first matching result below.

- **Perfect Contact.** When you roll at **disadvantage** and **both dice would hit**, you do not roll the weapon’s damage dice. Instead, you treat the weapon’s **base damage dice as if they rolled their maximum value**.

- **Shockwave.**  When you roll at **disadvantage** and **neither die would hit**, you immediately unleash a shockwave at the point of impact.
  Each creature in a **15-foot cube centered on the target** must make a Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Strength modifier).  
  On a failed save, a creature takes **2d8 bludgeoning damage** and is **pushed 10 feet directly away from you**.
