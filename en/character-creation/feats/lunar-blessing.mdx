# Lunar Blessing

_Prerequisite: No creature type other than humanoid in addition to being a Bloodboil Revenant of at least level 5, or the [Lunar Curse](/en/library/character-creation/feats/lunar-curse) feat, or communion with an entity capable of granting such boon._

You have been granted higher status by an entity of great power, be it the Red Queen, Kuutar, or a powerful enough moon-dweller.

---

Increase your Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Speed.** Your walking speed increases by 5 feet.

- **Darkvision.** You gain Darkvision with a range of 60 feet, if you already had or gain darkvision equal to or above this range from a different feature, you instead gain blindsight with a range of 15 feet.

- **Spider Climb.** You have a Climb Speed equal to your Speed. You can move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and along ceilings while leaving your hands free, as if perpetually under the effect of the [_Spider Climb_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:spider-climb) spell.

- **Blood siphon.** You gain the ability to siphon the life force of your enemies with your blows.
  You can make your unarmed strikes deal piercing damage instead of bludgeoning damage, and you can choose to add your constitution modifier to the damage roll instead of strength.
  Whe when you hit a creature that is not a **Construct, Elemental, Plant or is an Undead or Ooze variant of any kind with them**, you can use the following traits:

  **Drain.** You regain Hit Points equal to the Piercing damage dealt.

  **Strengthen.** You gain a bonus to the next ability check or attack roll you make within the next minute; the bonus is equal to the Piercing damage dealt.

  You can empower yourself with this trait a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

And the following disadvantages:

- **Red tides.** At least once per day, you must consume a quantity of blood equal to your proficiency bonus in litres order to stave off the red tides that threaten to consume you. If you fail to do so, you suffer one level of exhaustion and lose the ability to benefit from this feat until you consume the required amount of blood.

- **Lunar vulnerability.** You now count as mixed creature, your creature type is both humanoid and monstrosity, and effects that would harm monstrosities now affect you as well.

- **Sunlight sensitivity.** You have disadvantage on attack rolls and Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.
