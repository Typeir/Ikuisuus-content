# Mounted Combatant

_No attribute prerequisite._

You have forged a battle-bond with your mount through countless skirmishes across the lands of Damocles. When mounted, you and your steed move as one — a single instrument of war.

---

Increase your Constitution, Dexterity, Strength, or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Mounted Superiority.** You have advantage on melee attack rolls against any unmounted creature that is smaller than your mount.

- **Rider's Ward.** When your mount is targeted by an attack, you can force the attack to target you instead.

- **Mounted Evasion.** If your mount is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.

- **Steady Bond.** You can command your mount to use the [Steady](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/movement#steady) action. When you do so, you both use it and both count as if steady.
