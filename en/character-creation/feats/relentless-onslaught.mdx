# Relentless Onslaught

_Prerequisite: **Extra Attack** feature_

You have learned to press your attacks relentlessly when focused on a single target, never letting up until the fight is decided.

---

Increase your **Strength or Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Press the Target.** When you take the **Attack** action and **all** your attacks target the **same creature**, you may make **one additional attack** against that creature as part of the same action.

  This extra attack uses your normal attack bonus and weapon. It does not benefit from features that would give you an additional attack on a miss (such as Cleave), but it does trigger on-hit effects normally.
