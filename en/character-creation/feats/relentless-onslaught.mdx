# Relentless Onslaught

_Prerequisite: **Extra Attack** feature_

You have learned to press your attacks relentlessly when focused on a single target, never letting up until the fight is decided.

---

Increase your **Strength or Dexterity score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Press the Target.** Whenever you land subsequent attacks against the same target, each deals additional damage equal to half your **tier bonus** (rounded down).
  This bonus damage is applied to each attack after the first, and it stacks with itself for each additional attack you make against the same target on your turn.

- **Endless Barrage.** If you only attack a single target on your turn, you may make use your reaction to make one additional attack against that target.
  You can use this reaction a number of times equal to your **tier bonus**, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Recovery.
