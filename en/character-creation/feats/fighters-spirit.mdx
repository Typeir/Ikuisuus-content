# Fighter's Spirit

_Prerrequisites: **Strength 13** or **Dexterity 13**, proficiency with at least one martial weapon._

You are one with the blade, your mind focused entirely on the art of combat. 

---

Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

When you select this feat, you gain the following benefits:

Once per short rest, as a bonus action, you can enter a state of heightened combat awareness until the start of your next turn. While in this state, you gain the following benefits:

- You have Advantage on all attack rolls.

- You gain a +2 bonus to AC.

- You gain advantage on dexterity saving throws.

- You immediately end one effect on yourself that is causing you to be charmed or frightened.

- You gain temporary hit points equal to your level.
