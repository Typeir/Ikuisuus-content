# Tough

_No attribute prerequisite._

You have pushed your body past its limits through years of punishment, emerging harder and more resilient.

---

Increase your **Constitution score by 1**, to a maximum of **20**.

When you take this feat, you gain the following benefits:

- **Fortified Frame.** Your hit point maximum increases by **1 for each level you have** when you take this feat, and it increases by **1 each time you gain a level thereafter**.

- **Iron Recovery.** When you spend a **Hit Die** to recover hit points during a short rest, you add **1** to the result of the die roll (before adding your Constitution modifier).
