# Väkis

Väkis are **quasi-physical entities** that manifest **only if they will it**.
They sprout from **Ideas** in the *Platonic* sense—when the **Demiurge** carves an idea into being from the thick **prima materia**, **splinters** are left behind.  
These splinters are **Väkis**.

They are **not born from people or objects**, but from the **act of shaping a concept into reality** itself.

Most Väkis **do not care** about anything in particular. They simply **persist**—passive **echoes of repetition or concept**. Yet over time, the **Ideas they reflect degrade** through history and use.  
As memory distorts, so do they: a Väki becomes a **reflection of how its Idea has been feared, abused, or misremembered**.

For instance, a **Väki of flame** might accidentally **burn the living** or **dance among charred corpses**—not from cruelty, but because it **remembers fire through trauma and ritual**.  
Some rare Väkis act **chaotically or erratically**, such as the **Väki of ladders**, which might **kick a climber off** before vanishing for centuries.

Väkis are **not companions**, nor are they **good or evil** — They **simply are**.
