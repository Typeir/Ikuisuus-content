# Mellificator

_Very rare_  
_Musket (Martial, Two-Handed, Ranged 40/120, Mastery: Slow)_

When Kensa returned to the Order, his descendants found not a savior but a madman—an archaic mage demanding a war no one believed could be won.
The Order endured through secrecy, while Kensa spoke openly of setting slate clean ablaze. Fearing he would expose them all, they bound him and sealed him away.

A century later, the Order learned the cost of that decision. Kensa had spent decades sowing quiet fractures among the sunborn.
Tradition buckled; dissent spread; and when the bindings finally broke, he emerged with a weapon born of heresy.
Tahmura became the first to undergo mellification, a necessary sacrifice for Kensa’s renewed crusade against the Usurper of Death.

> “Within the corpse-god lie hives most pernicious. In moments they render flesh into sacred honey. Cruel, perhaps—but excellent on toast.” — Kensa of the Sweet Blossoming.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Musket (Martial, Two-Handed, Ranged 40/120, Mastery: Slow)
- **Damage**: 1d12 necrotic
- **Weight**: 10 lbs.

## Special Features

- **Honeyed Ammunition**  
  Cartridges for this weapon are special containers filled with a viscous, blood-red and amber-honey draught.
  Normal ammunition cannot be used with this weapon. Each of these cartridges holds up to 50 shots, each shot expending a small portion of the honey within.
  In order to create ammunition for this weapon, one must have access to alchemy supplies, be proficient with alchemist's supplies, and spend 1 hour creating a new cartridge, using
  500 gp worth of red amber honey and other alchemical ingredients, along with a single red bee cygote per shot.

- **Greater Preserver**  
  Any creature reduced to 0 hit points by a shot from this weapon has its body mass mummified and infused in honey. The mellified creatures can’t become undead controlled by Nigredo.

- **Antagonistic Reaction**  
  Leaving the weapon in direct contact with tombsteel or wielding it while using [_tombsteel armor_](/en/library/items/heirlooms/tombsteel-armor) for 1d4 minutes destroys the weapon and releases its magic in an explosion that fills a 60-foot aura originating from itself.  
  Each other creature in the area makes a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw**, taking 8d8 necrotic + 8d8 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

## Effects

- **Arcane Reactor**  
  This weapon is considered magical for overcoming resistances and immunities. It's functions are nullified by antimagic features, and it jams when affected by them.

- **Consuming Swarm**  
  This weapon deals necrotic damage instead of it's normal damage type. When you hit a creature resistant or immune to necrotic damage, that creature takes an extra **2d6 acid damage**.

- **Antibiotic Field**  
  The weapon has a **5-foot aura** originating from itself.
  - All creatures that end their turn in the aura may heal from any number of [Black Rot](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/black-rot) effects and gain **1 level of [exhaustion](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions) per effect cured**.
  - Any microorganism, small plant, or fungus in the aura withers and dies within seconds.

## Weapon Mastery

- **Slow** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Muskets)_  
  When this weapon deals damage, the target’s movement speed is reduced by **10 feet until the start of your next turn**.

- **Sweet Chains** (1/Short rest, or 3/Short rest if the wielder has mastery with Muskets):  
  When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with a Sweet Chains shot.
  - Make a special ranged attack with this weapon at advantage.
  - On a hit, the target’s movement speed becomes 0 until the end of its next turn.
  - This attack deals no damage.
