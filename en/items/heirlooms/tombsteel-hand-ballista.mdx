# Tombsteel “Hand” Ballista

_Legendary_  
_Siege Weapon +3 (Heavy, Ranged 150/600, Loading, Special, Mastery: Slow)_  

A massive tombsteel ballista used to slay Päivätär during the first god-slaying crusades of the Empyrean era. Now an unwieldy and hazardous relic of that time.
Impossible to wield by a single person, these siege engines were mounted on large warships and crewed by teams of soldiers.
Yet perhaps a creature of immense strength and skill could manage to operate one alone.

> “These machines return the cold death that their winds brought to my homeland.” — Apatheria, Thief of Winds

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Siege Weapon (Heavy, Ranged, Loading, Special, Nonmagical)  
- **Damage**: 4d10 (2d10 piercing + 2d10 cold)  
- **Range**: 150 / 600 ft.  
- **Weight**: 220 lbs  

---

## Special Features

- **(Special, Loading)** it can only be fired once per round, regardless of other factors.

- **Misfire**:  
  On a natural 1, the firing mechanism locks.  
  The wielder must use an **Action** and succeed on a **DC 15 Strength check** to reset it.

---

## Effects

- **Nonmagical Weapon**: The “Hand” Ballista is not magical; its effects cannot be dispelled and ignore magic resistance.  

- **Siege Impact**: Damage from this weapon is **doubled against constructs, objects and structures**.  

- **(Special) Great Equalizer**:  
  When the “Hand” Ballista hits or misses a target, the projectile detonates in a burst of tombsteel shards.  
  - All creatures within a **10-foot radius** of the impact point (minus the main target) must make a **DC (11 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier) saving throw**.  
  - On a failure, a creature takes **2d10 piercing damage, plus 2d10 cold damage**;  
  - Objects and structures automatically fail the save.  

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Push** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Siege Weapons)_: On hit, the target is pushed 10 feet away from you.

- **Hookshot (1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has Mastery with Siege Weapons)**:  
  As an action, you may fire a Godslaying Harpoon at a creature or surface within range.  
  - On a **hit**, the target is **impaled and grappled**.
  - The grappled creature has its movement speed halved.  
  - If the target is **smaller than you**, you may **pull it** up to **30 feet** directly toward you.  
  - If the target is **larger than you**, you may instead **reel yourself** up to **30 feet** directly toward it.  
  - The movement from this effect **does not provoke opportunity attacks.**  
  - If the target is an object or surface, you move the same distance toward the point of impact.  
  - The chain does not retract until the creature escapes or you use a bonus action to recall it.
