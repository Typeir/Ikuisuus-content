# Legacy of the Seeker

_Legendary (requires attunement by a Nekarion or by someone who has killed a Nekarion)_  
_Rapier (Martial, Finesse, Special, Magical)_

---

Legacy of the Seeker is a weapon formed by pure and accumulated will.  
Maybe it once had a physical form in a long-lost iteration of the Dread Lord, but only glimmers of pure obsession remain.  
Each mote of this disturbing light represents one who wasn’t worthy to claim the title of the Dread Lord.

> “Every failure illuminates. Every death instructs. This blade is my education, and I am a dutiful student.” — Nekarion, the Dreadlord.

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Rapier (Martial, Finesse, Special, Magical)
- **Damage**: — (special)
- **Weight**: —

---

## Special Features

- **Seeking Aurora.** As a free action, the blade can vanish or materialize in the wielder’s hand as flickers of sickly light converge.
- **Ever Present.** Abilities of this weapon can be used even if the blade is not materialized.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon.** +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls. Counts as magical.
- **Meticulous Diligence.** The weapon has no damage dice except on a critical hit.
- **Dancing Daylights.** You can make one attack as a bonus action. For this attack only, the weapon gains the **Thrown** property (range 60/90). The weapon returns to your hand immediately after the attack.
- **The Shame of the Wanderer.** On a hit, you may mark a creature with a **Mark of Shame** (marks accumulate, lasting 10 minutes or until consumed).
  - As a bonus action, consume a mark to teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 ft. of the target, as long as the target is within 1000ft of you.
  - On a critical hit you may add two Marks of Shame to the target or consume all marks on that target. If you choose to consume the marks, the weapon’s damage die becomes **(number of marks + 1)d12**.
- **Annihilation of the Infinite Self.** Critical hits with this weapon cannot be rerolled or altered (not even by _Wish_). Critical damage ignores resistance, immunity, or anti-crit protections.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Vex.** On a hit, you gain advantage on your next attack against the same creature.
- **Contemplate the End (3/short rest).** As an action, double the marks of shame on any creature fighting you. If you are **Doomed**, instead triple the marks.
