# Dreaded Defender

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Consumable (Medallion)_

A dense, blackened medallion of an unknown alloy, warm to the touch and heavy far beyond its size. 
Shallow grooves score its surface like stress marks, subtly rippling with no predictable pattern. 
When several are worn, they resonate faintly, as though straining against a shared burden.

> “The best form of crowd control? Death.” — Nekarion, the Dreadlord.

---

## Special Features

- **Linked Artifact**:  
  The Dreaded Defender only functions while **at least two creatures** are attuned to a Dreaded Defender.  
  Creatures attuned to any Dreaded Defender are referred to as **the bearers**.

- **Binding Designation**:  
  When you attune to the Dreaded Defender, you designate one creature you can see as **the target**.
  - The target does not need to be willing, attuned, or aware.
  - You cannot change the target while attuned.
  - If the target is dead or on a different plane, all effects of the Dreaded Defender are suppressed.

- **Proximity Constraint**:  
  All features of the Dreaded Defender **except Chain of Position** only function while you are **within 60 feet of the target**.

- **Shared Charges**:  
  The Dreaded Defender has **4 shared charges** across all bearers.  
  All expended shared charges are regained on a **short or long rest**.

---

## Effects

- **Chain of Position**:  
  _Reaction, free once per bearer_  
  When any creature’s turn begins or ends, you may pull or push **one willing bearer** of a Dreaded Defender **5 feet** toward or away from you in a straight line.
  - This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.
  - Line of effect is required.

- **Parasitic Recuperation**:  
  _Reaction_  
  When the target regains hit points, you gain **temporary hit points equal to the amount regained**.

- **Shared Wound**:  
  _Reaction_  
  When you take damage, you may split that damage:
  - You take **half the damage (rounded up)**.
  - The target takes the remaining half.  
  This effect is **not resistance**. Damage dealt to the target by this feature is **true damage**.

- **Borrowed Damnation**:  
  _Action_  
  As an action, you prepare to steal fate. For the next **1 minute**, the **next time you are required to make a saving throw**, you may use the **target’s saving throw bonus** instead of your own.
  - You still roll the d20.
  - This effect ends immediately after it is used.
  - **Only one bearer may benefit from Borrowed Damnation at a time**. If another bearer activates this feature, any unused instance immediately ends.

- **Self Balancing Pain** _(Costs 1 shared charge)_:  
  _Legendary Deed_
  As a legendary deed, at the end of a creature’s turn, **all bearers** gain **resistance** to the damage type dealt by the **target's next damage roll**.
  - The resistance lasts **until the end of the target’s next turn**.
  - If the damage roll includes multiple damage types, only the **highest damage type** applies.

- **Last Edict** _(Costs 1 shared charge)_:  
  _Reaction_  
  When **one or more bearers take damage from the same source**, the bearers may choose to **redistribute that damage among themselves**, up to a maximum of **five bearers**.
  - Damage is divided as evenly as possible.
  - **Redistribution occurs before resistances are applied**.
  - Each bearer then applies their own resistances, immunities, and vulnerabilities to their assigned portion.
