# Ruler Of Everything

_Legendary (requires attunement by a paladin)_  
_Weapon +3 (Any Martial Weapon, Superior Quality, Dual Mastery)_

During the early empyrean civil war, the alchemists of the city of Thealas found out the lord of rot grew in strength when the empIre used tombsteel.
As a countermeasure, newly produced weaponry was blessed in the blood-sap of the divine emperor of the clear skies, king Aeridas, heir to nothing.

> “Will and motion; naught but the Mechanical hands of this wicked world.” — Nigredo, lord of rot and flies.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Any Martial Weapon (chosen on attunement)
- **Damage**: As base weapon (upgraded by one die step due to superior quality)
- **Weight**: As base weapon

---

## Special Features

- **Form Shifter**:  
  As a **bonus action**, you may change this weapon’s type to another Martial weapon type. You must choose these two weapon types on attunement. You retain attunement when swapping.
  Some restrictions apply, such as not being able to swap between light and heavy weapons, or one handed, versatile and two handed weapons. Both weapons must be of the same category in this regard.
  other properties such as reach and special are generally unaffected by these restrictions (although DM discretion is advised)

- **Dual Mastery**:  
  This weapon has **two mastery properties** determined at creation or DM discretion. Both are available to the wielder while attuned, and both activate every hit.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  This weapon has a **+3 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical** for overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Enhanced Materials**:  
  This weapon has an extra **die level** due to its superior quality (e.g., a 1d8 weapon becomes 1d10).

- **Blessed Tombsteel Frame**:  
  This weapon deals an additional **2d8 radiant damage** on a hit.

- **Engineered Retributuion**
  Whenever you use a _Smite_ spell, this weapon concentrates on it on your behalf, freeing your concentration.

- **Purifying flames**:
  This weapon deals double damage to constructs.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Swap-Strike** _(Active Mastery, 1/Short Rest per Mastery, or 3/Short Rest if you have mastery with the current weapon type)_:  
  As part of an attack with this weapon, you may trigger its clockwork mechanism, venting white-hot steam:
  - The target must make a **Constitution saving throw (DC = 11 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier)**.
  - **Failure**: The target is **stunned until the start of your next turn** and takes an additional **25 radiant damage**.
  - **Success**: The target takes half the radiant damage and is not stunned.
