# Golden Nerve Mesh

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Monstrous Graft (Skin, Chest)_

When the Order of the Wheel located their predecessor's tomb, they were surprised by the baroque machinery that kept their ancestor in an unholy stasis for millennia. The contraption was destroyed to preserve the sanctity of the cycle, but its precious metals were used to reconstruct the castigated flesh of the necromancer within.

> “So, this is our creator? A desiccated wretch called Cream Puff? The Black One is quite an insidious joker.”  
> — Tahmura, the Finder

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Monstrous Graft (Skin, Chest)
- **Weight**: —

## Attachment

Attaching this monstrous graft to a humanoid requires an **8-hour surgical procedure** using:

- **Alchemist's Supplies, Surgical Tools, and Magical Healing** (one healing spell per failed check to stabilize the patient)
- A surgeon with proficiency in Medicine and the necessary tools

During the procedure:

- The subject must succeed on a **DC 15 Constitution saving throw** before every Medicine check.
- On a failure, the subject's maximum hit points are permanently reduced by **4d6**. This reduction is irreversible, even if the graft is removed or destroyed.

Each hour of the procedure requires a **DC 30 Medicine check**.

- On a failure, the subject takes **5d6 necrotic damage** and must repeat the check.
- If interrupted, the graft is rejected permanently.

This graft is **permanent until surgically removed** (which destroys it). It is **nonmagical in nature** and cannot be healed or mended magically if botched.

## Effects

- **False Vigor**:  
  You suffer none of the weakness of old age and can't be aged magically. You can still die of old age, however.

- **Pulseless Heart**:  
  You can ignore the effects of having 1 level of exhaustion, and you can finish a short rest in half the time.

- **Direct Synapsis**:  
  Pain has become simple data for you. You are immune to the **Power Word Pain** spell and once per rest you may add your proficiency modifier to an Athletics or Acrobatics check.
