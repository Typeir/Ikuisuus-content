# Filigree

_Very Rare (requires attunement)_  
_Dagger +2 (Light, Finesse, Thrown 20/60, Returning, Special, Mastery: Vex)_

**Filigree** was once the favored knife of **Master Seamstress Petunya**, used to skin beasts and stitch together the hides of her own making.  
Its edge was never reforged, only resharpened, until it learned what to cut by memory alone.  
The knife hums faintly when drawn, as if recalling the rhythm of thread pulled through flesh.

> “All that is torn can be mended. All that is mended can be torn again.”  
> — Petunya, Master Seamstress

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Dagger (Simple, Light, Finesse, Thrown 20/60, Magical, Special: Dual Function)  
- **Damage**: 1d6 piercing  
- **Weight**: 1 lb  

---

## Special Features

- **(Special) Dual Function**:  
  Filigree can be used as a set of artisan’s tools for **skinning, tailoring, and leatherworking**.  
  When used this way, you have **advantage** on any relevant Dexterity or Wisdom checks.

- **Returning**:  
  After each throw, Filigree reappears instantly in your hand in a curl of silver and gold thread.  
  If you have multiple attacks, you can throw Filigree more than once per round.


- **Seamcutter**:  
  When you critically hit with Filigree, you may cut through seams or bindings.  
  The target’s worn armor, clothing, or restraints are partially severed, reducing their **AC by 1 (to a maximum reduction of 5)** until repaired or replaced.

---

## Effects

- **Enhanced Materials**: This weapon has an extra **die level** due to it's premium quality.

- **Magical Weapon**: Filigree grants a **+2 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and counts as magical for overcoming resistances and immunities.  

- **Threaded Return**:  
  Once per short rest, when you throw Filigree, you may cause it to drag a **thread of seekersilk** back along its path.  
  You may use this to retrieve a small to medium object (up to 5 lbs), trip a creature (DC 15 Strength save or fall prone / pull 10ft), or close a wound (granting the target 10 temporary hit points).

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Vex**: On hit, you have **advantage** on the next attack you make against the same target before the end of your next turn.  

- **Mastery: Seamline Reprise (1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has mastery with daggers)**:  
  As a bonus action after hitting a creature with Filigree, you may trace the wound’s outline remotely.  
  - The creature takes **10 flat piercing damage** and gains **7 stacks of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions)**.
  - Both flat damage and stacks are doubled if the attack was a critical hit.
