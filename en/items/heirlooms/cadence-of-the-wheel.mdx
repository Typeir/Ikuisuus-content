# Cadence of the Wheel

_Legendary (requires attunement by a school of necromancy wizard)_  
_Staff +0 (Focus, Versatile, Mastery: None)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

Originally a weapon of entropy wielded by a foolish minion of Nigredo, fate and time transformed it into a beacon of perdurance and enmity towards the Lord of Flies and Rot.

On one end, a fossilized skeletal hand beckons death as a friend, crumbling the illusion of eternity. Wrapped along the shaft, thin layers of parchment hold the memories and legacy of a whole dynasty who danced with entropy and perdured, a testimony of their arcane wielders who studied for millennia the secrets that keep the wheel of change moving.

If someone unattuned holds this heirloom, an overwhelming dissonance creeps into their senses. It seems that only those who deeply comprehend the mysteries of entropy can aspire to keep up with the rhythm of the cycle.

> “To be forgotten is the only true death. I will bind our knowledge in the flesh of our fallen to make our struggles a lesson of beauty and perseverance. This will be our legacy.”  
> — Keyumar, the Scribe of Flesh

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Staff (Versatile, Special)
- **Damage**: 1d4 bludgeoning (1d6)
- **Weight**: 1 lb

## Special Features

- **Spellcasting Focus**:  
  May be used as a focus for casting spells and cantrips.

- **Bizarre Spellbook**:  
  The staff functions as a spellbook for the wielder. It always contains all necromancy spells of the wizard spell list from level 1 to 9, as if they were always inscribed on its sheets of engraved parchment. 
  The staff also rejects spells of 3rd level or lower that aren't from the necromancy school—the texts simply vanish as they are being copied onto its sheets.

- **Unsavory Tune**:  
  The staff vibrates with a dissonant pulse that unnerves unattuned holders. 
  Any unattuned wielder loses the ability to use reactions until they drop the staff.

- **Finale of Beauty**:  
  Any creature reduced to 0 hit points by a necromancy spell cast by you is decomposed and rapidly covered in moss, fungi, and small flora. The decomposed creatures can't become undead.

## Effects

- **Adaptive Power**:  
  You gain a bonus to spell attack rolls and spell save DC of the necromancy school spells equal to half your proficiency bonus, rounded down.

- **Unfit for Brutality**:  
  Weapon damage dice are decreased by one step and the weapon lacks a mastery.

- **Unpredictable Sway**:  
  While concentrating on the spell **Danse Macabre**, you gain a bonus to your AC and saving throws equal to the number of undead summoned by the spell that are still under your control.  
  In addition, you can take a **Magic action** to cast the **Danse Macabre** spell without expending a spell slot. Once you cast the spell in this way, you can't do it again until the next dusk.

- **False Grace**:  
  While under the **False Life** spell, your Armor Class becomes **13 + your Dexterity modifier** and any critical hit against you counts as a normal hit instead.

- **The Dance Oblige**:  
  While attuned to the staff, you can't cast spells of 3rd level or lower that aren't from the necromancy school. 
  In return, every spell you cast from the necromancy school is treated as being cast one level higher than the level you declare.
