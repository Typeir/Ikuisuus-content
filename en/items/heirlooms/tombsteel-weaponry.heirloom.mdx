# Tombsteel Weaponry

_Rare_  
_Weapon +1 (Any Weapon)_

The late empyrean empire believed those that came from beyond the folds had to be excised from the world like a tumor.
To that end, they struck a tenuous bargain with the lord of rot and flies, the heart of the sun, for the purity of their realm.

> “No gods or demons, only men.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Any weapon
- **Weight**: 1.5 times the base weapon, as tombsteel is extremely dense

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  This weapon has a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical** for overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Tombsteel Edge**:  
  When you hit with this weapon, it deals an additional **1d4 cold damage**.
