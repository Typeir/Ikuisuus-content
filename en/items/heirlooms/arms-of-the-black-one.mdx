# Arms of the Black One

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Halberd +1 (Melee, Reach, Two-Handed, Mastery: Slow)_

When the black one replaced it's body with the cold mechanisms of tombsteel, it's very own fate was severed.
They became a beast of war, famine and time, endless.

> “Death, unto infinity, for the arms of the Black One are endless.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Halberd (Martial, Reach, Two-Handed, Special, Magical)
- **Damage**: 1d12 slashing (superior quality)
- **Weight**: 18 lbs

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  This weapon has a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical** for overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Enhanced Materials**:  
  This weapon has an extra **die level** due to its superior quality (damage die is **1d12**).

- **Hurled Attack**:  
  This weapon may be thrown with a range of **60/120 ft.**  
  When hurled:
  - Critical hits occur on a roll of **18–20**.
  - Critical hits deal **quadruple damage** instead of double.
  - The weapon does **not** return to the wielder and must be retrieved normally.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Slow** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Halberds)_:  
 On hit, the target's movement speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

- **Black One's Breath** _(1/Short rest, or 3/Short rest if the wielder has mastery with Halberds)_:  
 As an action, unleash a torrent of superheated steam and rot:
  - All creatures in a **15-foot cone** must make a **Dexterity saving throw (DC = 9 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier)**.
  - **Failure**: 3d6 fire + 1d6 necrotic damage
  - **Success**: Half damage
