# Whitelight

_Legendary (requires attunement by a creature proficient in heavy armor and of good or lawful alignment)_  
_Heavy Armor (Any, +3)_

Whitelight is a blessed suit of **tombsteel armor**, silver-plated and engraved with **birds of war**—a relic of the Empyrean forges once used to clothe the Godslaying Battalions.  
The alloy’s pale gleam recalls the white birds of prey that flock ashore on the cold Thulean dawns.
It is for that very gleam that seamstress Petunea decided to fix this battered tombsteel armor and turn it into a messenger of the lost birds of war.
To don Whitelight is to bear another’s burden, to stand so that others may fall.

> “The bird of war no longer carries song. Only the memory of flight.”

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Heavy Armor (Any)
- **Weight**: +15 lbs over base
- **Stealth**: Disadvantage

---

## Effects

- **Cold Eternal**  
  While wearing Whitelight, any creature **without resistance or immunity to cold damage** must make a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw** at the **end of each hour** of continuous wear.

- **On failure**: Gain **1 level of exhaustion.**  
  This effect applies even while **unconscious or asleep.**

If a creature's base Constitution saving throw is 12 or more, they ignore this feature.

- **Unyielding Defense**  
  Gain a **+3 bonus to AC** due to the unnerving rigidity and supernatural precision of the alloy.

- **Stalwart Frame**  
  You have **advantage on saving throws** against **being pushed, pulled, or knocked prone.**

- **Radiant Sunrise** _(Passive – 10 charges)_  
  The armor stores **up to 10 charges**, regaining **2 + 1d4 charges** at **sunrise.**  
  When a creature hits you with a **melee weapon attack**, you may expend **1 charge** to deal **20 radiant damage.**

- **Under The White Bird's Wings** _(1/Short Rest)_  
  Once per short rest, you may command the armor to shield another being from harm.

  As an **action**, the armor casts **[_Heroism_](/en/library/spells/heroism)** on a willing creature within **30 feet**, requiring no components.  
  While the spell lasts:
  - **Whitelight maintains concentration**, not you.
  - The spell ends early only if you dismiss it (no action required).

- **Sanctified Alloy**  
  You have **resistance to necrotic damage.**

- **Radiant Echo**  
  If you are reduced to **0 hit points** or less while attuned to Whitelight, the armor releases a pulse of searing radiance.  
  Each creature of your choice within **20 feet** must make a **DC 18 Constitution saving throw**, taking **6d10 radiant damage** on a failure or half as much on a success.
