# Iridescent Lantern of the Brilliant Will

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Wondrous item (Lantern, Spellcasting Focus)_

A lantern of crude iron forged by the ancestors of Wil'beraht the Smith. Within it dwells an iridescent flame — practically infinite in colors and forms. This flame blazes with intensity each time its originating sorcerer channels their primordial magic.

At a material level, this lantern functions as a hooded lantern.

> “I will make this flame my own, so that its primordial radiance answers to my will.”  
> — Wil'beraht the Smith

---

## Special Features

- **Unguiding Flames**
  This lantern interacts with spells of the following categories: a spell that deals fire damage, heals, or is from the school of abjuration.
  These will be refered to as "Brilliant Spells" for the purposes of determining this item's abilities and effects.

- **Canvas of Flames**:  
  The lantern acts as a spellcasting focus and can store up to **3 charges**. Each time the bearer casts a Brilliant Spell of **1st level or higher**, the lantern gains one charge.

- **Blazing Feedback**:  
  The first time the lantern gains a charge from each type of Brilliant Spell, it returns one **sorcery point** to the bearer. This ability recharges for each type at dawn.

---

## Effects

- **Color of Creation**:  
  When the bearer casts a Brilliant Spell that heals or deals fire damage, they may consume charges to empower it. For each charge consumed, the **effective spell level increases by 1**.

- **Iridescent Will**:  
  The bearer receives a bonus to their magical attack rolls and spell save DC depending on the number of charges held before casting:

  | Charges | Bonus |
  | ------- | ----- |
  | 0       | +0    |
  | 1–2     | +1    |
  | 3       | +2    |

### Spellcasting Order

When casting a spell:

1. Apply the bonus from **Iridescent Will**
2. Decide whether to spend charges with **Color of Creation**
3. Cast the spell with the bonus applied
4. Gain the charge generated by the spell itself
