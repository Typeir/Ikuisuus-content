# Despondency

_Rare (requires attunement)_  
_Amulet_

This amulet is but a mere iron locket with no markings, pictures or inscriptions. Yet it has been found time and time again in the possesion of those who have ended their own lives.
Those struck by its psychic resonance feel the will to fight drain from their limbs, like leaves dropping from a dying tree.
The despair it inflicts is not an illusion; it is a mirror held to the soul's deepest fractures.

---

## Effects

- **Crushing Despair**:  
  When you deal psychic damage to a creature, it must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw** or become **Hopeless** until the end of its next turn. A creature that succeeds the saving throw is immune to this effect for the next 24 hours.

---

## Hopeless Condition

While a creature is **Hopeless**:

- Any Hopeless creature that targets you with an attack roll must choose a new target or lose the attack or spell.
- A Hopeless creature **automatically fails** any check to escape a grapple or restraint.
