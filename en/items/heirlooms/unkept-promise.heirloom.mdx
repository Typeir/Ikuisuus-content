# Unkept Promise

_Mythic Artifact (requires attunement)_  
_Consumable, Trinket_

In the wilds of Taiva, at the foot of the stairway to the heavens, the first pilgrim had one final conversation with an old sage from an age long past.
The sage knew the white homunculus could not be stopped, so he offered the pilgrim to sit down with him and share a drink instead. 
When their conversation drew to a close, the pilgrim turned his back on the sage, and, without a word, dropped a single bullet from his weapon.
Maybe it was an act of respect, maybe a final warning, or a promise.

> “If fate is to rise, then I hope a will most powerful is to best it, just as fate intends to best the world.” — The First Pilgrim.


You may invoke **each Promise once per long rest**.  
**Nameless Promise** may be invoked **once ever**; when you do, the artifact is destroyed.

**Spell Save DC for this artifact is 20.**  
If a Promise replicates a spell effect, it does not require material components.

---

## Promise of the Murk  
_1/Long Rest_  
As an **action**, you force a creature to make a charisma saving throw (DC 20). On a failure, the creature gains four stacks of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/erosion).

---

## Promise of the Black Star  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (1 minute)_  
As an **action**, you cast [_Waltzing Blades_](/en/library/spells/waltzing-blades) on yourself as if using a 5th-level slot.  
You must **concentrate** on this effect.

---

## Promise of the Aimless Arrow  
_1/Long Rest_  
As a **bonus action**, mark one melee attack you will make before the end of your next turn.  
- Your next hit with that attack becomes an **automatic critical hit**  
- It deals an additional **10 force damage**

If the attack misses or is not made by the end of your next turn, the effect ends.

---

## Promise of the Master  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (normal spell duration)_  
As an **action**, you cast [_Maze_](/en/library/spells/maze).  
This version **requires your concentration**.

---

## Promise of Gates  
_1/Long Rest_  
As an **action**, you and up to **8 willing creatures** you can see teleport to a point you visualize within **1,000 feet**.  
As a bonus action on your next turn, you and all creatures teleported may return to your original locations, taking **4d6 force damage** in the process.

---

## Promise of Storms  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (special)_  
As an **action**, choose a willing creature you can touch.  
For as 1 + 1d4 rounds, that creature gains:

- The benefits of [_Blade Ward_](/en/library/spells/blade-ward)
- The benefits of [_Feather Fall_](/en/library/spells/feather-fall)
- The benefits of [_Blur_](/en/library/spells/blur)  
- The benefits of [_Mirror Image_](/en/library/spells/mirror-image)
- The benefits of [_Freedom of Movement_](/en/library/spells/freedom-of-movement)
- The benefits of [_Haste_](/en/library/spells/haste)  
- The benefits of [_Fly_](/en/library/spells/fly)

**Collision:** When your concentration ends for any reason, or when the spell ends, the creature takes **80 true damage**.

---

## Promise of Rot  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (1 minute)_  
As an **action**, choose up to **3 creatures** within 60 feet.  
Each must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw (DC 20)**, or be affected by the effects of [_Bane_](/en/library/spells/bane) and [_Hex_](/en/library/spells/hex), targeting Constitution.

You must **concentrate** on this Promise.

---

## Promise of Bones  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (1 minute)_  
As an **action**, choose up to **3 creatures** within 60 feet.  
Each must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw (DC 20)**, or be affected by the effects of [_Slow_](/en/library/spells/slow),
and take **1d4 force damage per 5 feet** of willing movement.

You must **concentrate** on this Promise.

---

## Promise of Freedom  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (1 minute)_  
As an **action**, choose any number of willing creatures within 60 feet.

For as long as you maintain concentration:  
- They cannot be **grappled, restrained, paralyzed, or petrified**  
- They have advantage on saves against **charmed** and **frightened**  
- Immediately upon invoking this Promise, each chosen creature may use its **reaction** to move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks

---

## Promise of Wills  
_1/Long Rest, Concentration (1 minute)_  
As an **action**, choose a willing creature within 60 feet.  
For as long as you maintain concentration:

- The creature gains **advantage on all Strength checks and Strength saving throws**  
- The creature ignores **difficult terrain**  
- The creature's carrying capacity is **quadrupled**
- The creature cannot be **pushed, pulled, knocked prone, or restrained**  
- The creature makes **opportunity attacks with advantage** and those attacks deal **+1d10 force damage**

---

## Promise of Fate  
_1/Long Rest_  
As an **action**, cast the [_Power Word Kill_](/en/library/spells/power-word-kill) spell.

---

## Nameless Promise  
_Once ever — destroys the artifact_  
As an **action**, you invoke the final vow sealed within the bullet.  
You cast [_Wish_](/en/library/spells/wish).  
After resolving the Wish, **Unkept Promise is destroyed** and all other Promises vanish with it.
