# Clockwork Tombsteel Weapon

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Weapon +2 (Any Martial Weapon, Superior Quality, Dual Mastery)_

Forged in the final years of the Empyrean Empire, these weapons combine tombsteel cores with intricate clockwork gearing, allowing the wielder to shift its form mid-battle.
The mechanisms vent bursts of white-hot steam when activated, searing foes and staggering them under its weight.

> “No matter what the Empire ever did to stall it, the death of this world was engineered long ago.” — The Archivist

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Any Martial Weapon (chosen on attunement)
- **Damage**: As base weapon (upgraded by one die step due to superior quality)
- **Weight**: As base weapon

---

## Special Features

- **Form Shifter**:  
  As a **bonus action**, you may change this weapon’s type to another Martial weapon type (GM’s discretion which are available).

  - You retain attunement when swapping.
  - Mastery effects change to match the new form.

- **Dual Mastery**:  
  This weapon has **two mastery properties** determined at creation or GM discretion. Both are available to the wielder while attuned.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  This weapon has a **+2 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical** for overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Enhanced Materials**:  
  This weapon has an extra **die level** due to its superior quality (e.g., a 1d8 weapon becomes 1d10).

- **Clockwork Tombsteel Frame**:  
  This weapon deals an additional **2d6 cold damage** on a hit.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Swap-Strike** _(Active Mastery, 1/Short Rest per Mastery, or 3/Short Rest if you have mastery with the current weapon type)_:  
  As part of an attack with this weapon, you may trigger its clockwork mechanism, venting white-hot steam:
  - The target must make a **Constitution saving throw (DC = 10 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier)**.
  - **Failure**: The target is **stunned until the start of your next turn** and takes an additional **10 fire damage**.
  - **Success**: The target takes half the fire damage and is not stunned.
