# Last Laugh

_Legendary (requires attunement by Nekarion)_  
_Heavy Armor (Plate)_

A blackened tombsteel plate with perfect finishes and a somber appearence, it is a feat of Empyrean engineering like no other. 
The Dreadlord grew disilusioned with the extreme protection it provided, as it ruined any chance of an entertaining encounter.
Eventually he started hacking away pieces of the armor and replacing them with traditional sellsword motifs, which would finally devolve into simple jester cowls and taunting bright colors, as if he were taunting death itself. 

> “A tough crowd makes for a mute finale.” — Old Brumean proverb.

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Heavy Armor (Plate)  
- **Weight**: 60 lb  
- **Stealth**: Disadvantage  

---

## Effects

- **Dark Humour**
  This armor has a -3 modifier to AC, but it adds your Dexterity modifier to it.

- **Defiant Masquerade**  
  You gain **+1 hit point per character level** while wearing this armor.

- **Jester's Gait**  
  You gain proficiency in performance, or **expertise if your alredy have performance**.

- **Dramatic Blow**  
  As a **reaction**, when **you or an ally within 30 feet** take damage from an attack, you may reduce that damage by an amount equal to your **Performance modifier**.

- **Countercue**  
  When **you or an ally within 30 feet** take damage, you may choose to transform that damage into **true damage** and make the target become **vulnerable** to that damage type for this attack.  
  The source of the damage then takes an equal amount of **true damage**.
  If true damage reduces any of the two creatures to 0 HP, the comically explode in a red mist of viscera and entrails, instantly dying.  
  You may use this feature **once per short rest**.

- **Grand Finale**  
  When **you or an ally within 30 feet** score a **critical hit**, you may add your **Performance modifier** to the total damage rolled.

- **The Fourth Wall**  
  You cannot be **silenced** or **knocked prone** by any means.  
  Attempts to still your voice or interrupt your movement simply fail.

- **Onlooker's disdain**  
  Any creature that targets you from further than **60 feet** suffers the effects of [_Bane_](/en/library/spells/bane) until the end of its turn.
