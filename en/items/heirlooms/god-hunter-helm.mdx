# God Hunter Helm

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Armor, Helmet_

The slits of this dark plate helm are barely big enough to see through, and the interior is pitch black and cold like a starless sky.

> “To hunt a god, one must become a godless thing.”

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Armor (Helmet)
- **Weight**: 6 lbs

---

## Special Features

- **Reinforced Tombsteel**:  
  This helm is forged from tombsteel and counts as an object with **AC 20 and 50 hit points** for the purposes of being sundered or destroyed.

---

## Effects

- **Cold Eternal**  
  While wearing a piece Tombsteel Armor, any creature **without resistance or immunity to cold damage** must make a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw** at the **end of each hour**:

  - **On failure**: Gain **1 level of exhaustion**
  - This effect applies even while **unconscious or asleep**

- **Heartless**:  
  Once per long rest, when you are subjected to a **charmed** or **frightened** effect, you may choose to **succeed automatically** instead of rolling a saving throw.

- **Sightless**:  
  While wearing this helm, you are **immune to divination magic** and cannot be perceived through magical scrying sensors.
  - You cannot be targeted by spells or effects that would determine your location, alignment, or thoughts unless you wish it.
