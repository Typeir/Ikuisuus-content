# Robes of the Grand Starfall

_Mythic Artifact (requires attunement by a spellcaster)_  
_Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)_

The teardrops that fell from the heavens at the end of the fourth star eventually coalesced into a mundane, silken thread. This thread was then repurposed by a rogue norn into
the robes of the first starcaller, _Väinar the Starry-eyed_, who foretold the end of the fifth star and the return to a new, gentler era of creation, or so the story goes.

> “This age is but a hum, a single teardrop. Gone with the rise of our star, like morning dew”  — Väinar, the Starry-eyed.

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)  
- **Armor Class**: 15 + Dexterity Modifier
- **Weight**: 3 lb  

---

## Effects

- **Grand Convergence**  
  You gain a **+4 bonus** to spell attack rolls, spell save DC, and spell damage rolls.  

- **Song of the Stars**
  While wearing the robes, you may cast three **Legendary Spells** bound to the fabric of their creation:  
  [_Starpath_](/en/library/spells/starpath), [_True Light_](/en/library/spells/true-light), and [_Earthmove_](/en/library/spells/earthmove).  
  Each may be cast **once per long rest**.  
  These spells cannot be upcast and consume **Hit Dice** instead of spell slots.  
  For each spell level, you lose the corresponding Hit Dice and take an equal amount of **irrecoverable necrotic damage**.  
  This damage heals by **1 Hit Die** after each long rest.  

| Spell | Cost |
| ------ | ---- |
| [_Starpath_](/en/library/spells/starpath) | 10 (4 Hit Dice) |
| [_True Light_](/en/library/spells/true-light) | 11 (5 Hit Dice) |
| [_Earthmove_](/en/library/spells/earthmove) | 12 (6 Hit Dice) |

- **Stellar Discipline**  
  When you deal damage with a spell of 1st level or higher, you may add your **proficiency bonus** to one damage roll.  

- **Solar and Lunar Harmony**  
  You gain resistance to **fire** and **cold** damage.  

- **Astral Flow**  
  Whenever your fail a concentration saving throw, you may choose to take necrotic damage **equal to the DC** and succeed instead.  

- **Celestial Wound**  
  When reduced to 0 hit points or less while wearing these robes, you emit a pulse of light in a **30-foot radius**.  
  Each creature of your choice in the area must make a **Constitution saving throw** (DC = 12 + your proficiency bonus + your spellcasting modifier) or take **20 (4d8) radiant** and **20 (4d8) cold damage**.  
  You then stabilize automatically. You can only use this **feature once per long rest**.
