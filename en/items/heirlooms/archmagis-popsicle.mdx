# Archmagis Popsicle

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Large Greatsword +4 (Melee, Heavy, Two-Handed, Special, Magical, Mastery: Graze)_

This outrageous contraption is a torture device of cruelty proper of crimes forgotten to the old ages. While the iced blade shatters and crumbles in the heat of the battlefield, the encased empyrean feels its body crumble again and again until the consequence of freedom becomes more terrifying than the everlasting agony. In an ironic twist, the immense power of the jailed individual seems related to the eternal resilience of the blade. It seems that even hell can be tailored for the ones that foolishly tried to reach heaven.

> “Your reflection in silver knows how to love, your reflection in glass knows how to thrive, but never see your reflection in ice — it only knows how you will die.”  
> — Old Binturian Proverb

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Greatsword (Martial, Heavy, Two-Handed, Magical, Special, Large)
- **Damage**: 4d6 slashing
- **Weight**: 10 lbs

---

## Special Features

- **The Ice is Hungry**:  
  While wielding this weapon, any creature without resistance or immunity to cold damage suffers **4d6 cold damage** at the start of its turn within melee range.

- **Entropic Anchor**:  
  Extreme cold always surrounds a **10-foot Emanation** that originates from the weapon. This can freeze liquid or affect creatures at a normal pace.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  Archmagis Popsicle is a **+4 weapon**. You gain a **+4 bonus to attack and damage rolls** made with it, and attacks count as magical for overcoming resistance and immunity.

- **The Shattering Blade**:  
  After hitting a creature with Archmagis Popsicle, the blade shatters — becoming a **-4 weapon** — and you gain **4d6 temporary hit points** as the ice fragments begin orbiting around you.  
  The blade reforms after **1d4 rounds** at the start of your turn.

- **Icebound Acumen**:  
  After each time the blade shatters, your **Intelligence score temporarily increases by 2**, to a maximum of 24, until the end of the current combat. This effect ends early if you are knocked unconscious, or if your turn ends and you haven't attacked a hostile creature, forced an enemy to make a saving throw, or taken damage since your last turn.

- **Merciless Jailer**:  
  Once per short rest, as a **reaction**, you can transfer the negative effects of a saving throw you failed to the empyrean trapped in the icy blade. The prisoned mage will cry in frustration as no amount of harm may end its torment.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Graze**:  
  If your attack roll with this weapon misses a creature, you can deal damage to that creature equal to the ability modifier you used to make the attack roll. This damage is the same type dealt by the weapon, and the damage can be increased only by increasing the ability modifier.

- **Cryogenesis** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  As a **bonus action**, call upon the eternal cold of the seventh winter to restore the Archmagis Popsicle, becoming a **+4 weapon** again instantly.
