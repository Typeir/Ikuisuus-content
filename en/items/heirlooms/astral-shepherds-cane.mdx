# Astral Shepherd's Cane

_Legendary (requires attunement by a Druid)_  
_Quarterstaff +2 (Melee, Versatile, Magical, Large)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

This staff was but a mundane piece of wood in the past star, but in the age of fate it serves to guide those who wish to outwit the One True Fiend.

> “In eternal motion we find the truth of our world. All is but a cycle, and we shall return to greatness” — Väinar the Starry-eyed.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Quarterstaff (Martial, Versatile, Magical, Large)
- **Damage**: 2d6 + 2 bludgeoning (2d8 + 2)
- **Weight**: 4 lbs

---

## Special Features

- **Everbark**
  <br />
  If the staff is broken or destroyed, it leaves a small sapling in it's place
  that can be grafted onto any tree to instantly grow a new Astral Shepherd's
  Cane, siphoning the tree's life force to do so.

---

## Effects

- **Astral Attunement**  
  While you hold this staff, you gain a **+2 bonus to spell attack rolls** and a **+2 bonus to your spell save DC**.

- **Spell Absorption**  
  While holding the staff, you have **advantage on saving throws against spells**.  
  When a creature casts a spell that **targets only you**, you may use your **reaction** to absorb it:
  - The spell is **negated**, and
  - The staff regains charges equal to the spell’s level  
    If this would bring the staff above **50 charges**, excess energy dissipates harmlessly.

- **Magical Weapon**  
  The staff has a **+2 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical for overcoming resistances and immunities**.

- **The roots of the tree**  
  When you cast [_Shillelagh_](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:shillelagh) on this staff:
  - The base die becomes 2d8 instead of 1d8.
  - Because this is a large-creature staff, all weapon damage dice are doubled again, making the final Shillelagh die 4d8.  
    The staff’s +2 bonus still applies normally.

- **Astral Chains**

  While attuned to the staff, you may weave luminous chains of starlight between creatures.
  These chains have 50 charges.
  - As an action, you may **link up to 5 willing creatures** you can see within 60 feet of you.  
    These links last for an hour, or you dismiss them (no action).

  - Whenever a linked creature makes a **saving throw** or **ability check**, the creature may use their **reaction** to have replace its **bonus** to that roll with the bonus of another linked creature within 10 feet of them.  
    The creature must choose to do this **before** the roll is made. Doing so consumes a charge.

  - Whenever you cast [_Guiding Bolt_](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:guiding-bolt), or when your **Starry Form: Archer** makes an attack, the attack may **retarget a new creature** within 30 feet of the previous target **after each hit**, so long as none of the attacks miss.  
    The effect ends immediately on the first missed attack. Every time you select a new target, you consume a charge.

  You may only maintain one set of Astral Chains at a time, and you may only link creatures once per short or long rest.

## Weapon Mastery

- **Topple** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Quarterstaffs)_  
  When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can force it to make a **Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier)**.  
  On a failed save, the creature is **knocked prone**.
- **Astral Snare** _(1/Short rest, or 3/Short rest if the wielder has mastery with Quarterstaffs)_  
  As an **action**, you slam the staff into the ground, casting [_Entangle_](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:entangle) with a 60 foot square, instead of the usual 20 foot square.
