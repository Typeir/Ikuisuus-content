# Serpentine Dreams

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Longsword +3 (Melee, Large, Versatile, Special, Mastery: Push)_  
_Can be used as a spellcasting focus when attuned_

When Bloodboil Ludwig dreamt of the moonlit sea, he saw a blade of shifting light and shadow, forged from the essence of the lunar godess herself.
In fear of his master, the Red Queen, he kept his vision secret, until the night he was betrayed.  
With his dying breath, he whispered a plea to the moon, and the blade appeared in his hand.

> “Oh Mother Moon, May your Cerulean light and Cinereous shadow guide us through this long night” — Ludwig, during the Empyrean invasion of Anelka.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type:** Longsword (Martial, Versatile, Special, Magical)
- **Damage:** 2d8 force (2d10)
- **Weight:** 1 lbs

---

## Special Features

- **Moonlit Edge:**  
  While under moonlight, the blade emits **dim cerulean light** in a 10-foot radius.

- **Lunar Dissolution:**  
  As a **bonus action**, the weapon can vanish or reappear in your hand as a ripple of light and mist.

---

## Effects

- **Large**:  
  This weapon deals damage as if it were one size larger, and medium creatures use it at disadvantage.

- **Dreamer’s path:**  
  When attuned, this weapon uses your **highest ability score modifier** for attack and damage rolls instead of Strength.

- **Magical Weapon:**  
  You gain a **+3 bonus to attack and damage rolls** made with this weapon.

- **Special: Ethereality**  
  The weapon deals **force damage** instead of slashing.

- **Frozen night:**  
  When you hit a creature with this weapon, it deals an additional **2d4 cold damage**.

---

## Dreamer’s Reversal

When a **melee attack hits you**, you may use your **reaction** to contest the attacker’s roll with an **attack roll of your own** made using the Serpentine Dreams.

- If your roll **equals or exceeds** the attacker’s original result, the attack **misses**, and you may make one **Serpentine Dreams attack** against that creature as part of the same reaction.
- If your roll is **lower**, the attack resolves normally.
- For every successful Dreamer’s Reversal, you accrue a -1 penalty to future reversal contests until you miss one.

### Interactions

If you possess or benefit from the following features or spells, you can declare a combined use of a single Dreamer’s Reversal per round.
You may apply **only one of these interaction benefits** to each contest.  
The combination interacts as follows:

- **Monk – Deflect Missiles / Deflect Attacks:**  
  Add your **Martial Arts die** to your contest roll.

- **Wizard (Bladesinger) – Song of Defense:**  
  When using **Song of Defense**, you may expend a spell slot to bolster your awareness instead of reducing damage.  
  Add **+2 × the slot’s level** to your contest roll for this Dreamer’s Reversal.

- **Rogue – Uncanny Dodge:**  
  Reduce the attacker’s contest roll by **3**.

- **Maneuver – Parry:**  
  Add your **Superiority Die** to your contest roll.

- **Maneuver – Riposte:**  
  If you win the contest, add your **Superiority Die** to the counterattack’s damage.  
  (The die is only expended if you win the contest.)

- **Feat – Defensive Duelist:**  
  If the triggering attack misses you due to **Defensive Duelist**, your Dreamer’s Reversal **automatically hits**, but you do do not get your reaction back.

- **Spell – Shield:**  
  If the triggering attack misses you due to **Shield**, your Dreamer’s Reversal **automatically hits**.

- **Spell – Silvery Barbs:**  
  You may cast **Silvery Barbs** before resolving the contest to force the attacker to **reroll** its hit.  
  Regardless of the reroll’s result, you may still take the **Dreamer’s Reversal** contest afterward.

- **Spell – Absorb Elements:**  
  If the attack deals an elemental damage type that **Absorb Elements** can affect, reduce the attacker’s contest roll by **5**.

You can combine Dreamer’s Reversal with a feature or spell in this way a number of times equal to your **proficiency bonus**, regaining all uses after a **long rest**.

## Weapon Mastery

- **Cleave:** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Longswords)_  
  When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can make a **second attack** with the same weapon against another creature within 5 feet of the original target and within your reach.  
  This secondary attack uses the same attack bonuses as the original attack, but not the damage bonuses.

- **Mooncleave:** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rests if the wielder has mastery with Longswords)_  
  As an action, you may unleash a **sweeping crescent of force and frost**.  
  All creatures in a **30-foot cone** originating from you must make a **Dexterity saving throw**  
  (**DC = 11 + your proficiency bonus + your highest ability modifier**) Taking 4d8 cold damage plus 4d8 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.  
  This attack counts as one weapon attack for all vocation features and abilities.
