# Cinerity, Left Arm of the Baku

_Legendary Monstrous Graft (requires attunement)_  
_Natural Weapon (Melee, Special)_  
_Martial, Magical_

Among the many war machines the Empyrean's forged in ages before this time, few sparked as much controversy as the Baku, the mechanical god warrior of failure, rejection and dreams.
When it was decomissioned shortly before the First Pilgrimage, each part was sealed away within the observatories of the five great families.

> Dream, not of what you are... but of what you want to be.”

---

## Attachment

Attaching this _major monstrous graft_ to a humanoid requires an **impossible surgical procedure** lasting **6 hours**.

**Requirements**

- **Surgical Tools**, **Smith’s Tools**, and **Alchemist’s Supplies**  
- A surgeon with **proficiency in Medicine** and **Arcana**  
- A minimum of **three healers** maintaining constant magical stabilization

**Procedure**

Each hour requires a **DC 28 Medicine check**.  
Before each check, the subject must succeed on a **DC 18 Constitution saving throw**.

- On a **failed saving throw**, the subject’s heart ruptures and the graft **consumes the torso**, killing them instantly.  
- On a **failed Medicine check**, the subject takes **10d10 force damage** and must repeat the check.  
- If the procedure is **interrupted**, the graft becomes inert and cannot be used again.

### Exception — Moonlighter Distillation

If the subject is under the effects of **true distillation of the Cerulean Dreamer’s barley (“Moonlighter”)**,  
the graft automatically attaches itself instantly without requiring surgery or checks. 
Moonlighter is consumed upon completion.

Once attached, the graft fuses permanently and cannot be removed without killing the host.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type:** Natural Weapon (Martial, Melee, Special)  
- **Damage:** 1d6 + Strength or Dexterity modifier (force)  
- **Range:** Melee (5 ft.)

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon:**  
  Unarmed strikes made **with this hand** are **magical** and have a **+3 bonus** to attack and damage rolls.

- **Titan Grip:**  
  You can wield Large weapons in **this hand** without disadvantage, even if you are a Medium creature.  

- **Enhanced Load:**  
  Your **carrying capacity, lift, and shove limits are doubled.**

- **Lucid Reflex:**  
  You gain **one additional reaction** each round.  

- **Deathbed Dream:**  
  When this graft is attached, your **maximum hit points are permanently reduced** by an amount equal to **one level’s worth of hit points**  
  (your hit die + Constitution modifier + any bonuses).  
  This reduction is **irreversible**, even if the graft is removed or destroyed.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Slow:** _(Only available if you have mastery with Unarmed Strikes)_  
  On a hit, the target’s **speed is reduced by 10 ft.** until the start of your next turn.

- **Lucid Break (1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest with mastery):**  
  When you take the **Attack action**, you may **replace one of your attacks** with a devastating strike from the arm.  
  - The target must make a **DC (18 + Proficiency Bonus) Strength saving throw.**  
  - **Failure:** 6d10 force damage and the target is **staggered**, losing its reaction until the end of its next turn.  
  - **Success:** Half damage, no stagger.
