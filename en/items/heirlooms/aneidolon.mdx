# Aneidolon

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Handgun +3 (Heavy, Ranged 30/90, Loading, Special, Mastery: Slow)_

The **Aneidolon** series of firearms were forged during the late Empyrean empire, each carefully brought to life by **Master Delphion** within the soot-stained,
petrol-fumed forges of the industrial city of **Orpholea**. 
This city once lay beneath the current borders of the **Bladed Crags**, between the what is now southern **Puebla Amapola** and the shrouded **Karvasuola**.

Every discharge from Aneidolon is so potent the weapon turns a glowing white-hot star on the verge of collapse.
The air around it shimmers with intense heat, barely a degree below melting point.
For that reason, the _Delphionic Manual of Radiant Arms_ specifies that, while the weapon lacks a traditional reload mechanism and is thus theoretically semi-automatic, there must be a mandatory three-second vent period before a subsequent shot.


> “May the sun burn away the filth that has taken root upon these wretched lands.”  
> — Excerpt from "To Forge Hope Itself", by **Delphion**, Master Gunsmith

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Handgun (Martial, Light, Loading, Magical, Special: Overheat)  
- **Damage**: 2d10 piercing  
- **Range**: 60 / 180 ft.  
- **Weight**: 7 lbs  

---

## Special Features

- **Overheat**:  
  Aneidolon can safely fire **twice per round**.  
  Firing a **third time before the start of your next turn** overheats the weapon.  
  - You immediately take **50 fire damage**.  
  - Aneidolon’s attack and damage bonus is reduced to **+1** until it is reforged by a master Gunsmith with Access or knowledge of _To Forge Hope Itself_.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**: Aneidolon grants a **+3 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and counts as magical for overcoming resistances and immunities.  

- **Enhanced Materials**: This weapon has an extra **die level** due to it's premium quality.

- **Oversized**: Aneidolon is larger than most handguns, doubling it's damage dice but imposing disadvantage on all attacks, unless wielded by a large or larger creature, or in two hands.  

- **Recoil**:  
  Due to the immense power of Aneidolon, when you fire it, you can move 5 feet in the opposite direction to your target, without provoking oportunity attacks.

## Weapon Mastery

- **Slow** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Handguns)_: On hit, the target’s movement speed is reduced by **10 feet** until the end of its next turn.

- **Niobean Punishment** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has mastery with handguns)_:  
  When you fire Aneidolon, you may do so beyond its safety cycle, invoking a **Niobean Punishment**—a discharge so precise and violent that even the wielder is struck by it.  
  - The attack deals **40 flat piercing damage** (plus relevant modifiers), doubled on a critical hit and ignores all cover and full cover up to 10ft thick of all materials short of dense tombsteel, and all resistances and immunities to nonmagical damage.  
  - The target must succeed on a **DC (11 + Proficiency bonus + Dexterity modifier) Constitution saving throw** or be **blinded** and **stunned** until the end of its next turn.
  - After the attack resolves, you take **15 fire damage**, and Aneidolon overheats.  
  - You cannot fire Aneidolon again until the end of your next turn.
