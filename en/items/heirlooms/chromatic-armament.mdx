# Chromatic Armament

_Very rare (requires attunement by a homunculus)_  
_Large Greatsword +2 (Melee, Magical, Mastery: Vex)_

Undecided between a weapon, a tool and a relic deserving of an opulent exhibition, the Chromatic Armament is said to have been designed by
Heirloom designed by Plato Moumtzis, Damocles's most devoted Homunculi scholar and preservationist. It is unkown why Plato settled on the design for this greatsword,
the stained glass mosaics serving no aparent purpose and throwing the blade wildly off balance in the wrong hands.

Still, the armament has found it's place in the reperoire of many heroic homunculi, which has led most to the suspicion that the Chromatic Armament is not a singular piece, but a series of them.
Alas, the records are as sparce as are the surviving pieces, and the truth may never be known.

> “It is that which shines through the colors of the cosmos that leads us home, my friend.” — Plato Moumtzis, to Cierto Psaltis, before he left for the first time.

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Greatsword (Martial, Magical, Large)
- **Damage**: 4d6 slashing
- **Weight**: 10 lbs

---

## Special Features

- **Rite of Kindling**:  
  By focusing on the Armament for **10 minutes**, a homunculus can light the Armament with their homunculus flame.

- **Shifting Form**:  
  When in lantern form, the Armament behaves like a hooded lantern, with the only particularity being that the color of the light emanated depends on the color of the flame within the lantern. It can change back and forth to its greatsword form at no cost.

- **Emergency Flame**:  
  If a homunculus wielding the Armament would have its flame extinguished, the Armament's flame can extinguish in its place. The Armament turns into a regular greatsword until the **Rite of Kindling** is performed on it again.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  This weapon has a **+2 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical** for overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Stoke the Flame**:  
  The weapon deals an extra **1d6 damage** on impact, determined by the color of the homunculus flame. Additionally, it grants a **Hit Dice pool** equal to double the wielder's proficiency bonus. These dice can be used to empower dice rolls according to the flame's color.

  | Flame Color | Damage Type |
  | ----------- | ----------- |
  | Red         | Fire        |
  | Blue        | Lightning   |
  | Green       | Acid        |
  | Purple      | Psychic     |
  | Black       | Force       |
  | Golden      | True Damage |

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Vex** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  On hit, the target has **disadvantage** on its next attack roll before the start of your next turn.

- **Enkindle** _(1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  After making an attack with the weapon — regardless of whether the attack hits — a **5-foot wide, 50-foot long line** blasts from the point of impact. Each creature in the line must make a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **6d6 damage** of a type depending on the active flame on a failure, or half damage on a success.  
  After this, roll **1d6** — on a result of **1 or 2**, the homunculus flame is snuffed out.
