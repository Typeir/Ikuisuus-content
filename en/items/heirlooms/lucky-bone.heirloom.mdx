# Lucky Bone

_Very rare (requires attunement by a sunborn spellcaster)_  
_Amulet_

This strange amulet holds the fossilized pinkie of a feline sunborn, carefully adorned with platinum filigree.

> “The secret of our survival? Luck and persistence, but mostly luck.”  
> — Hushang, the Hermit King


---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Amulet
- **Weight**: —

## Special Features

- **Sunborn Heritage**:  
  Only sunborn spellcasters can attune to this item.

## Effects

- **Fortune's Blessing**:  
  Whenever you cast a spell of the necromancy school, roll a d4. On a 4, you are subjected to the **greater restoration** spell.
