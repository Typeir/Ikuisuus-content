# Vestments of Brocadian Shahtoosh

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Studded Leather Armor +2_

A leather vestment made of **Seekersilk**, **demon leather**, and the heat-conductive Shahtoosh wool shorn from the manes of **lunar manticores**.  
The resulting garment can stop a spear yet folds like smoke between the fingers.  
Few robes of such quality exist, for each demands a great sacrifice, be it willing or not.

> “There is finery in punishment and beauty in suffering.”  
> — Master Seamstress Petunya

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Light Armor (Studded Leather)
- **Armor Class**: 14 + Dexterity modifier
- **Weight**: 9 lb
- **Stealth**: Advantage

---

## Effects

- **Demonic Brocadian Lamination**  
  The layered weave absorbs kinetic shock.  
  When you are hit by a melee or ranged attack, you may use your **reaction** to gain resistance to attack’s damage.  
  You can use this feature **three times per long rest**, and regain all expended uses after a long rest.

- **Dressed to Kill**  
  While you are wearing this armor, you gain a **+2 bonus to initiative rolls**.

- **Shahtoosh Insulation**  
  You are **immune to nonmagical cold damage** and ignore all penalties from extreme cold environments.  
  You are **resistant to magical cold damage**.

- **Feather Descent**  
  While wearing this armor, you can cast [_Feather Fall_](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:feather-fall) targeting only yourself, at will and without expending a spell slot or using components.

- **Threaded Silence**  
  The fabric dampens movement and sound.  
  You have **advantage** on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to avoid detection.

- **High Maintenance**  
  The Brocadian Shahtoosh cannot be repaired by mundane means, and requires a master robesmaker familiar with both **silk** and **leather** techniques.
