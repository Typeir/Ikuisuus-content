# Inverted Kratteous Core

_Legendary Monstrous Graft (requires attunement)_

---

When the empyreans crossed the Everdark, Daedal mechanisms kept their husks alive beyond flesh. Against a creature as vital as the Dread Lord, this generator inverts its function, channeling overflowing energy into herculean feats.

Once implanted, the core fuses with your chest and becomes part of your body.

> “I am in control; even the beat of my heart is of my own volition. Can you say the same?” — Nekarion, the Dreadlord.

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Monstrous Graft (Heart, Chest)
- **Weight**: —

---

## Attachment

Attaching this **major monstrous graft** to a humanoid requires a **16-hour surgical procedure** using:

- **Alchemist’s Supplies**, **Surgical Tools**, and **Magical Healing** (one healing spell per failed check to stabilize the patient)
- A surgeon with **proficiency in Medicine** and the necessary tools.

During the procedure:

- The subject must succeed a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** before **every Medicine check**.
  - On a failure, the subject’s body **permanently rejects** this graft.
  - If self-applied, they also **die instantly and violently**, as their body rots away.

Each hour of the procedure requires a **DC 40 Medicine check**.

- On a failure, the subject takes **10d6 necrotic damage** and must **repeat the check**.
- If interrupted, the graft is **rejected permanently**.

This graft is **permanent** until surgically removed (which destroys it).  
It is **nonmagical** in nature, and cannot be healed or mended magically if botched.

Alternatively, you may simply stab your heart with the core, taking **100d4 true damage** in the process. The graft roots into your chest permanently.  
Removing it deals **100d4 true damage**.

---

## Effects

- **Infuse Vitality.** Your Constitution score becomes 25.

- **Crucible Organ.** _(Activates only if you already have Constitution 25 or higher without the Core.)_
  - You gain maximum HP per level (as though every hit die rolled its maximum).
  - Immune to sickness, and instant-death effects that deal no damage, except exhaustion.
  - Your exhaustion tracker becomes 12 instead of 6, and each level gives you an increasing -1 to all d20 rolls.
  - When regaining HP, treat dice as their maximum values.
  - Lost body parts regrow at the end of your next turn if you have at least 1 HP.
  - You may take 5 true damage to gain **+1 to a Strength check**, up to **+15**.
