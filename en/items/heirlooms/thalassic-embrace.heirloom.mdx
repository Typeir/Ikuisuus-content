# Thalassic Embrace

_Legendary (requires attunement by a creature proficient in heavy armor)_  
_Heavy Armor (Plate, +3)_

**Hecatoncheire Petunya** forged the Thalassic Embrace from the remnants of monsters slain during Everstanding Andromeda’s hunts.  
As those same beasts shattered and dented her armor in battle, Petunya began to replace its seams and inner paddings with their flesh and sinew.  
Over time, the armor became a living, self-reconstructing **quasi-organism**—a shell that grows and heals upon its wearer like a second skin.

> “The deep giveth, and the deep taketh away.” — Master Seamstress Petunya

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Heavy Armor (Plate)
- **Weight**: 60 lb
- **Stealth**: Disadvantage

---

## Effects

- **Magical Flesh**:  
  The armor grants a **+3 bonus to AC** and is immune to any effect that would **lower its AC**, as the sinew and bone of the creatures from which it was forged regrow and adapt to protect its wearer.

- **Sea Mantle**:  
  The **Vaki of the depths** dances around you, encasing you in a spiraling sheath of conceptual water. While wearing this armor:

  - You have **resistance to fire damage.**
  - You gain a **swim speed equal to your walking speed** and can **breathe underwater or survive without breathing.**
  - **Open flames** within **10 feet** of you are **automatically extinguished.**
  - Against **magical fire effects**, your touch acts as **[_Dispel Magic_](/en/library/spells/dispel-magic)** cast at **9th level.**
  - This includes any **non-instantaneous fire effects** that come into contact with you.
  - Even if a magical flame persists, you take **no damage** from it.
  - Any **flaming weapon** that strikes you has its fire **suppressed until the end of the round.**
  - You have **three-quarters cover** against any effect that **originates from more than 10 feet** away.

- **Pressure of the Deep**:  
  You are **immune to nonmagical bludgeoning damage**, your body hardened by the weight of the ocean floor.
