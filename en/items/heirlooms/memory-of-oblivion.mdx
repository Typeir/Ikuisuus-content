# Memory of Oblivion

_Legendary_  
_Consumable (Memory)_

Beyond the opaline belt, ahead of the all consuming flames of the Everdark, lies the will of world;
ever in struggle against the blackened flames — to peer into it is to forget but the most primal state of the world, oblivion.

> “To forget is to be free.”

---

## Effect

After rolling and confirming damage for an attack or damaging spell you have just made:

- Choose a creature you hit. The damage becomes true damage.
- The creature has **vulnerability to all damage** from that attack.
- The damage is permanent and cannot be healed or restored by any means.
- If the creature is reduced to 0 hit points by this damage, it is **erased from existence**.
  No physical or magical means can restore it, and all memories of it are lost to those who knew it.

You may decide to use this ability after you see the final damage rolled.

---

## Cost

- Permanently take a **–1 penalty** to **Arcana, History, and Religion** checks.
- Permanently forget a specific memory, chosen by you (the player). This memory cannot be restored by any means.
- After the third use, every time you use this feature, you lose proficiency in one skill and tool of your choice.
- If you do not have proficiency in any skill or tool, you are instantly consumed in the black flames of oblivion.
