# Tombsteel Shield

_Armor (shield)_  
_rare_

One of the very first applications of tombsteel was that of crafting rudimentary shields to protect empyrean warriors from the Silent Ones they hunted.
It was during this hunts that the term "God Challenger" was coined, as the warriors eventually ran out of demons to fight, and turned their blades against the intruding gods.

> “In the face of great power, greater cruelty is required.” — An ancient empyrean proverb

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Shield (Heavy)
- **Damage**: 1d4 bludgeoning
- **Weight**: 9 lbs.

---

## Effects

- **Entropic Guard**:
  When a **willing creature you can see within 5 feet of you** is subjected to a **magical effect** that requires a **saving throw**, you can use your **reaction** to interpose the shield.
  If you do, the protected creature gains **advantage on the saving throw** against that effect. You must declare this reaction **after the effect is identified but before the saving throw is rolled**.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Push** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with shields)_:  
  After you successfully land an attack on a target, you can push that target up to **10 feet** away from you.

- **Muzzling** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with shields)_:  
  After you successfully land an attack on a target, the target can cast **only cantrips** (no leveled spells) until the **end of their next turn**.
