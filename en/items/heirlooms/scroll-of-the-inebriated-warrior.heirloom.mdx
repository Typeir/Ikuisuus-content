# Scroll of the Inebriated Warrior

_Legendary, nonmagical_  
_Scroll_

---

## Inebriation Tracker

Your mastery of inebriation has become an artform. You now track your **Inebriation Level**, which ranges from **0 to 36**.

- **Drinking alcohol** (such as a flask, bottle, or skin) counts as a **bonus action**, and increases your Inebriation Level by **1d6** (maximum 36).
- At the **end of each of your turns**, your Inebriation Level **decreases by 6**, unless you drink again.

---

## Hammered Sweet Spot: Inebriation Level 12+

While your Inebriation Level is **12 or higher**, you gain the following benefits:

- You can make **one additional attack** as part of your **Attack action** (stacks with Extra Attack).  
- Your **unarmed strikes deal an additional 1d4 force damage**, and deal extra damage equal to your **Wisdom modifier**.  
- You have **advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics)** and **Charisma (Performance)** checks.  
- You gain **+1 to AC** and **+1 to all saving throws**.

---

## Overdrunk & Exhaustion

As your Inebriation Level rises above 18, you begin to suffer the following effects:

| Inebriation Level | Effect                                           |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| 24                | Disadvantage on Wisdom checks and saving throws. |
| 30                | Gain **1 level of exhaustion.**                  |
| 36                | Gain **2 levels of exhaustion.**                 |

You cannot exceed a level of 36. Your body simply cannot contain more.

---

## Sober Up

You can spend **1 ki point** and use an **action** to forcibly purge your alcohol and reset your Inebriation Level to **0**.  
When you do, you take **1d10 poison damage**, ignoring resistance and immunity.  
Alternatively, a **short rest** also resets the level and removes any exhaustion gained from this feature.

---

## Gourd-Borne Techniques

If you are attuned to the **Gourd of the Eternal Drunkard**, you gain access to the following techniques, each can be used a number of times per long rest equal to your **proficiency bonus**.

- **Breath of the Drunkard – Fire Belch**:  
  _Requires Inebriation Level 12+_  
  You may use your action to exhale fire in a **15-foot cone**.  
  **Dexterity save (DC = 8 + proficiency + Wisdom modifier)**  
  - On failure: **5d6 fire damage** and the target is set on fire (1d10 fire damage at the start of each turn).  
  - On success: Half damage, not set on fire.  
  - **Cost**: 6 Inebriation points.

- **Belch of the Deep Still – Toxic Sphere**:  
  _Requires Inebriation Level 24+_  
  You may use your action to unleash a **20-foot-radius sphere** of intoxicating vapor.  
  **Constitution save (DC = 8 + proficiency + Wisdom modifier)**  
  - On failure: **5d6 poison damage**, the target is **poisoned (1 minute)**, and must use its reaction to stumble 10 ft away from the cloud.  
  - On success: Half damage, no poison.  
  - **Cost**: 6 Inebriation points.  
  - The cloud remains for **1 minute**, dealing its damage to creatures entering it for the first time.  
  - The cloud **heavily obscures** its area and blocks all light.

- **Secret Technique – Drunkard’s Heaven**:  
  Once per short rest, if you use **Breath of the Drunkard** against your **Belch of the Deep Still** cloud, it immediately detonates.  
  All creatures within a **30-foot sphere** must make a **Dexterity save (DC = 8 + proficiency + Wisdom modifier)**, taking **20d6 radiant damage** on a failure, or half as much on a success.  
  The explosion dissipates the cloud.
