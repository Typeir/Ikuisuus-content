# Fatespun Garment

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)_

Woven in the ripples of eternity, beyond the reach of the sands of time, no one knows who seamed these robes, it's fated threads recursing unto infinity.

> “Every path leads somewhere, most lead back to where they began.” — The One True Fiend.

---

## Armor Properties

- **Type**: Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)
- **Armor Class**: 13 + Dexterity Modifier
- **Weight**: 2 lb
---

## Effects

- **Stranded destiny**  
  While wearing the Fatespun Garment, you may cast three Legendary Spells bound into the weave itself. 
  Each casting costs **1 charge** and ignores all normal downsides or drawbacks of the spell.

| Spell                                                 | Charge Cost |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| [_Eternal Return_](/en/library/spells/eternal-return) | 1           |
| [_Prophecy_](/en/library/spells/prophecy)             | 1           |
| [_Fate Tangle_](/en/library/spells/fate-tangle)       | 1           |

- **Fate Reservoir**  
  The garment holds **4 charges** and regains **1 expended charge** after each long rest.

- **Foretold Step**  
  You have advantage on ability checks made to read omens and understand prophecies, myth and legend.

- **Unraveling Grace**  
  Once per long rest, when you would be reduced to 0 hit points or less, you instead drop to **1 hit point**, as the garment briefly rewrites the moment of impact.
