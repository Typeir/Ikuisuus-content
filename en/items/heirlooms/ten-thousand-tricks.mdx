# Ten Thousand Tricks

_Rare_  
_Tool Set_

A set of trinkets made of Chrobalt, a bizarre alloy of chromed tombsteel and cobalt — small tools, implements, and curiosities forged with a strange resilience that defies their unassuming appearance.
Each piece can be reshaped by a skilled smith, and the whole set can be recalled through an ancient Edonian burial prayer.

---

## Special Features

- **Chrobalt Resilience**:  
  Checks to break these trinkets are made at **disadvantage**, and the tools have **resistance to all damage**.

- **Reforge**:  
  You may attempt to reforge one or more trinkets into a different shape (**DC 16 Smithing check**, once per short rest per trinket). If the check fails, the trinket becomes useless until successfully reforged. The set of trinkets must maintain the total weight of **10 lbs**.

---

## Effects

- **Battle Prayer**:  
  By reciting the Edonian burial prayer while holding one of the tools (**1 minute**), you may recall the Ten Thousand Tricks. They are magically cleaned, repaired, and return to you. Expendable materials such as oil, flash salt, and similar consumables will not be recovered.
