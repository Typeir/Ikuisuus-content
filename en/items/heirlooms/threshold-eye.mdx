# Threshold Eye

_Uncommon_  
_Consumable (Magical Eye Replacement)_

This magical eye replaces a real one that was lost or removed. While the Threshold Eye is embedded in your eye socket, you can see through the tiny orb as though it were your natural eye and any undead in your field of vision glows faintly unless they are protected against divination.

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Consumable (Eye Replacement)
- **Weight**: —

## Special Features

- **Eye Socket Integration**:  
  You can insert or remove the Threshold Eye as a **Magic action**, and it can't be removed against your will while you are alive.

## Effects

- **Vision Restoration**:  
  While embedded, you can see through the orb as though it were your natural eye.

- **Undead Sight**:  
  Any undead in your field of vision glows faintly unless they are protected against divination magic.
