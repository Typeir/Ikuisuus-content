# Cloak of the Black One

_Very rare (requires attunement)_  
_Clothing, Cloak_

A cloak made of small tombsteel scales, held together by a network of fuming, black fungus.
It writhes as if alive, yet it is naught but death.

> “The black winds brought only death”

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Clothing (Cloak)
- **Weight**: 10 lb

---

## Effects

- **Enhanced protection**:
  - While you wear this cloak, you gain a +1 bonus to saving throws.
- **Black Wind Step**:  
  A number of times per long rest equal to your **proficiency bonus**, you may move up to 10ft in a straight line.
  - This movement does **not provoke opportunity attacks**.
  - Creatures you move through must succeed on a **Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier)** or fall **prone**.
  - This movement ignores difficult terrain.
