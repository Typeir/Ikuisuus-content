# Heirlooms

Legendary items and artifacts of power, each with its own history and mystical properties. Browse the collection below to discover weapons, armor, and relics that have shaped the fate of heroes and kingdoms.

### Rarity Guide

- **Common** _(recommended for adventurers of all levels)_: Readily available magical items with minor effects.
- **Uncommon** _(recommended for adventurers of all levels)_: Notable items with useful magical properties, not particularly rare.
- **Rare** _(recommended for adventurers of level 3 or above)_: Powerful items, though there may be many like them.
- **Very Rare** _(recommended for adventurers of level 7 or above)_: Weapons and artifacts of significant power, often unique or scarce.
- **Legendary** _(recommended for adventurers of level 15 or above)_: World-changing items of immense power, mostly one-of-a-kind, or a small series of them.
- **Mythic Artifact** _(recommended for adventurers of level 20 or above)_: Unique items of divine or cosmic significance. Always a singular, named item.
