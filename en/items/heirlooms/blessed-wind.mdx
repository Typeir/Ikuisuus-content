# Blessed Wind

_Legendary (requires attunement)_  
_Curved longsword +3 (Finesse, Melee, Versatile, Mastery: Graze)_

Born from the last breath of the **Storm Lord Ukkonhemmo**, the Blessed Wind is not so much a weapon as it is a splinter of the very idea of wind and storm.  
Eternally humming a song both beautiful and forlorn, this blade attracts **Vakis of the storm**, who dance in silence around it.

> “The wind knows no master—only motion.”

---

## Weapon Properties

- **Type**: Curved Longsword (Martial, Versatile, Finesse, Magical)
- **Damage**: 1d8 slashing (1d10)
- **Weight**: 0 lbs
- **Range**: 15 ft (Melee)

---

## Special Features

- **Purifying Currents**  
  As a **bonus action**, you may **dissipate fog, smoke, or similar obscuring effects** within a **10-foot radius** around you.

- **Mastery of the Wind**  
  You are **immune to wind-based effects**, such as _Gust_, _Control Winds_, _Wall of Wind_, and similar effects.

---

## Effects

- **Magical Weapon**  
  This weapon has a **+3 bonus to attack and damage rolls** and is considered **magical for overcoming resistances and immunities**.

- **Light as Air**  
  While attuned, you suffer **no penalty for jumping or falling**, and are **resistant to fall damage**.

- **Harmony with the Gale**  
  The Blessed Wind may replace your **Unarmed Strikes**, **Flurry of Blows**, and **Martial Arts** attacks.  
  It seamlessly integrates into a monk’s techniques, allowing strikes with the blade to count as unarmed.

- **Wind Breaker (PB/Long rest)**  
  If you **spend Ki** as part of your turn, the Blessed Wind emits a **protective current**, granting you **temporary hit points equal to 5 × the amount of Ki spent**.  
  This feature can be used a number of times equal to your **proficiency bonus**, and regains all expended uses after a long rest.

---

## Weapon Mastery

- **Graze**  
  When you **miss** a creature with an attack using this weapon, you still deal your ability modifier as **slashing damage**.

- **Echoing Strikes**  
  Each strike leaves behind a **lingering afterimage**, dealing **thunder damage equal to your Wisdom modifier**.

- **Crescent Gale (1/Short Rest, or 3/Short Rest if the wielder has mastery with Longswords)**  
  As an **action**, you unleash a **crescent-shaped slash of wind** in a **5-foot wide, 60-foot long line**.  
  All creatures in the path must make a **Dexterity saving throw (DC = 11 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Wisdom modifier)**.
  - **Failure**: 6d10 slashing damage and pushed 10 ft back.
  - **Success**: Half damage and not pushed.  
    This attack deals **double damage** to **constructs and inanimate objects**.
