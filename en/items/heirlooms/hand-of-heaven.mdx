# Hand of Heaven  

_Legendary Armor (requires attunement)_  

---

Even when reality diverged into annihilation, a worthless rock remained unchanged.  
This unremarkable pebble endured the Interlocking untouched; cursed — or blessed — to exist in the eye of the storm.  
Forged into a gauntlet, it became Nekarion’s emblem: a compass of truth, an anchor for impossible certainty.  

> “Abandon your wicked doubts; join me and witness the one truth.”  — Nekarion, the Dreadlord.

---

## Item Properties  

- **Type**: Heirloom (Gauntlet)  
- **Weight**: —  

---

## Effects  

- **Unstoppable Hand of the Dread Lord.** +3 bonus to attack rolls with unarmed strikes of that hand.  
  - Creatures have disadvantage on checks to escape your grapple.  
  - Whenever a creature grappled by you hits you, it takes force damage equal to half the damage you suffered.  
- **Hand of Ambition.** While attuned, your proficiency bonus increases by 1.  
- **Hand of the Spawns of Truth.** You may cast *Time Stop* once per short rest. It cannot be counterspelled and ignores antimagic.
- **Hand of the Queen.** You may spend your hit die to heal others during rests.  
- **Hand of the Demons.** You are immune to curses and hexes from creatures of CR 31 or lower.  
- **Hand of the Archivist.** You may fleetingly recall information about a topic you are not knowledgeable in. you may add your proficiency modifier to an Arcana, History or Religion check once per day.
