# Astral Stonework

_Rare (requires attunement by a Druid of Stars)_  
_Focus_

A slab of dense astral shale resembling a worn grave marker, carved with drifting constellations that seem to rearrange themselves whenever the stone is held.
Its glyphs are unmistakably written by Väkis, though whatever message they once carried is long lost.


> “☐☐☐☐ ☐☐☐☐☐☐☐☐☐ ☐☐☐ ☐ ☐☐ ☐☐ ☐☐☐☐☐ ☐☐☐☐ ☐☐☐☐☐☐☐☐” — Some Väki, forgotten in time.

---

## Starry Form Enhancements

While attuned to the Astral Stonework, whenever you activate Starry Form you immediately gain **temporary hit points equal to your druid level + your Wisdom modifier**.

Each constellation is additionally enhanced as follows:

---

### Archer — Twin Lumen Volley

When you use the Archer constellation’s bonus-action attack:

- You fire **two astral arrows**, each with its own attack roll.

---

### Chalice — Overflowing Chalice

When the Chalice constellation triggers and you target a creature with a healing spell:

- The secondary healing from the constellation always restores the **maximum possible amount**.

---

### Dragon — Birth of the Cradle

While in the Dragon constellation, you may use a bonus action to unleash a **15-foot cone of black astral flame**.

- Creatures in the area make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC.  
- On a failure, they take **1d10 force damage**.  
- On a success, they take half as much.
