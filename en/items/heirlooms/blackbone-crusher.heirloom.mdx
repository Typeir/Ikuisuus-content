# Blackbone Crusher

_Mythic Artifact (requires attunement)_  
_Greatsword +4 (Two-Handed, Large, Heavy, Magical, Mastery: Push, Enhanced Sunder)_  
_Cannot be copied, reforged, or recreated by any divine or arcane method._

---

Forged from the brutalized corpses of void-giants, it's core formed by a **Dreamcatcher shard** whose will has been shattered, and blessed with the still-beating **Heart of the Golden One**, the Blackbone Crusher is the blade meant to end ambition itself.  
It is a massive, cathedral-like slab of blackened bone and iron — **too large for a mortal to wield without divine purpose.**

When swung, the core of the blade, which is formed by a large, black spine, extends outward in a whip-like fashion, allowing it to reach further away than any regular weapon.

It lacks an edge, it is a thing made for bludgeoning rather than cutting. Its surface is carved with sculpted figures: humanoids scrambling up the blade, their flesh sloughing away until they are skeletons near the hilt, where they are ground into nothing, and the mortar
from their bones forms the cathedral-like structure of the guard, hilt and pommel.  
The weapon thwarts sounds around it when swung, stilling even the loudest, most vibrant of lights.
A dark, sickening throb follows each strike, as if the world itself had been rended with it's passing.

> “Pave your path with corpses, build your castle with bones, little one.” — The Golden One, to the white Homunculus.

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Greatsword (Two-Handed, Large, Heavy)
- **Damage**: 4d8 bludgeoning (superior quality, Large weapon)
- **Reach**: 25 ft
- **Weight**: 45 lbs

---

## Attunement

Any attempt to attune triggers a **DC 28 Strength [RR](/en/library/rules/the-measure-of-the-self/rolling-and-special-rolls) saving throw**, as the blade tries to absorb the wielder into itself.

- **Failure**: The creature is trapped within the heart of will's event horizon — it dies instantly, leaving but a faint, echoing scream that lasts for 1 minute.
- **Success**: The creature manages to wield the blade normally.

---

## Special Features

- **Indestructible**:  
  This weapon cannot be broken, sundered, or melted by any effect, much like the will of the world is unbreakable, so is it's blade.

- **Light Devourer**:
  This blade turns all light within 30ft around it into dim light, and dim light into darkness.

---

## Effects

- **Pristine Edge**:  
  +4 to attack and damage rolls. Counts as magical for the purposes of overcoming resistances and immunities.

- **Superior Quality**:  
  Weapon damage dice are increased by one step and doubled for size (4d8 base).

- **God-Slayer’s Burden**:  
  Wielding this weapon halves your base movement speed and imposes **disadvantage on all saving throws** unless you have Strength 24 or higher.

- **Anchor of Wills**:  
  You cannot be affected by magical fear, charm, or compulsion effects while attuned.

- **Heart of motion**:
  You cannot be moved against your will, nor can you be knocked prone or pushed while attuned.

- **Undefeatable**:  
  When you fail a saving throw, you can instead succeed.
  You can use this feature twice per long rest, and, in order to use it again, you must feed the blade a +3 or higher weapon during that rest.
  In order to feed the blade, you must throw the desired item at the heart of wills situated at the gaping hole in the crescent edge of the blade.
  - The item is instantly destroyed, and the blade absorbs its will.

- **Judgment Saves**:  
  While attuned to Blackbone Crusher, you no longer make individual ability-based saving throws.
  - Instead, you may use the following saving throws:
    Fortitude (STR or CON, pick highest)
    Reflex (DEX or INT, pick highest)
    Will (WIS or CHA, pick highest)

- **Weight of Ambition**:  
  Blackbone Crusher deals **double damage to Constructs and Fiends**, and **quadruple damage to objects and structures**.

## Weapon Mastery

- **Enhanced Sunder** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  When you hit a creature, it must succeed on a **Strength saving throw (DC = 12 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier)** or have its AC reduced by 1 until the end of its next turn. This can stack indefinitely. If a creature reaches 0 AC, it is immediately devoured and killed by the heart of wills.

- **Push** _(Only available if the wielder has mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  On a hit, you may push a creature of any size less than Titanic 20ft away.

- **Heart of Gold** _(1/Short Rest or 3/Short Rest if you have mastery with Greatswords)_:  
  The Blackbone Crusher splits in two, glowing like molten gold, and you bring them together in a cataclysmic cross-chop.
  - Choose a point within your reach (25 ft.).
  - All creatures in a **30-foot radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **Strength saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier)**.
  - **Failure**: Take **8d12 bludgeoning damage + 8d12 radiant damage**, are **knocked prone**, and are pulled to the center point.
  - **Success**: Take half damage and are not moved.

  When creatures are pulled to the center, each creature that collides with another creature or solid object takes an additional **1d12 bludgeoning damage** (rolled separately for each collision).

## Stratagem – **Will of the World**

_(recharge at sunrise)_
You focus the will of the world through the Heart of Wills embedded in the blade.

The Heart of wills emits a pulse of everburning dark flames, engulfing the weapon and the wielder in a shroud of black fire, and slowly manifesting a shard of the golden one.
This action is declared at the end of your turn, and takes effect at the start of your next turn.

When the action is resolved, the golden one's shard manifests from the heart of wills,
a golden chariot pulled by two burning horses, bearing a radiant figure clad in golden armor and wielding two blazing curved swords.
The image of the golden one rides forth in a 100ft long, 40ft wide line. All creatures in that line must make a **DC 28 Dexterity saving throw** or take **10d8 radiant damage** and **10d8 fire damage**, and be blinded and deafened for 1 minute.
Creatures that succeed take half damage and are not blinded or deafened.
When the chariot reaches the end of the line, you may choose to manifest from the flaming chariot and instantly critically strike a creature that failed the saving throw.

> “The edict standeth unchanged, naught precedeth wills.”
