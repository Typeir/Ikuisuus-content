# Tombsteel Skeleton

_(Monstrous Graft), legendary, nonmagical_

The lord of rot and flies has seeded tendrils all through the lands of Damocles, his dominion over death ever present.
The mycelial network of *Stachybotrys ventralis* spreads through all matter, living or inert, and fouls it into tombsteel—indiscriminate as entropy.

> “They were never the strongest of the Hiisi; they needed not be. They merely needed to be the last.”
---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Monstrous Graft (Skeleton, All)
- **Weight**: —

## Acquisition

Unlike most monstrous grafts, the Tombsteel Skeleton cannot be surgically attached.  
It manifests only as the result of **Nigredo’s curse of ossification**.  
Those afflicted endure agonizing weeks of decay and failed saving throws until their skeleton stabilizes.  
Survivors carry this graft forever.

---

## Affliction Effects

- You gain **3 permanent levels of exhaustion**.  
- Your **speed is reduced by 10 ft.** after exhaustion halving.  
- **Magical healing you receive is halved.**  
- You cannot gain **temporary hit points from spells.**  
- You **do not lose exhaustion from resting**, though magical effects that remove exhaustion still function.

- **Ossification Trial**:  
  At the end of each **short rest**, or at **dawn** if no rest was taken, you must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw [RR](/en/library/rules/the-measure-of-the-self/rolling-and-special-rolls)**.  
  - **On a failure**: You gain **1 level of exhaustion**, and the short rest has no effect on you or any of your features.  
  - **On a success**: Mark **1 progress.** At **3 progress**, the body stabilizes and all exhaustion from this feature is removed, and the affliction turns into the heirloom.  
  - Effects that enhance metabolic functions such as [_Catnap_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:catnap) can turn this roll into a normal roll instead of a Raw Roll, making it qualify for non-persistent modifiers such as the [_Bless_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:bless) spell.

---

## Heirloom Effects

When the ossification stabilizes, you permanently gain the following traits:

- **Entropic Nature**:  
  - You gain **resistance to necrotic and cold damage.**  
  - You gain a **+1 bonus to AC and saving throws**, and your natural armor baseline becomes **13** instead of 10.  
  - You have **advantage on saving throws** against spells and other magical effects.  
  - You have **disadvantage** on saving throws or checks made to resist **counterspells, dispels, or antimagic effects**, and cannot have **advantage on them from any source**.  
  - When you attempt to **attune or unattune** from an heirloom or artifact, the process requires **twice the normal time** and you make the roll at **disadvantage.**
  - Rituals take twice as long to perform when you are involved.

- **Inert Core**:  
  Magical healing and temporary hit points from spells you receive are **halved.**

- **Unmalleable Flesh**:  
  You cannot be polymorphed or otherwise transformed against your will (you automatically succeed on such saving throws).

- **Dead Weight**:  
  Tombsteel is vastly heavier than bone.  
  - If you have **Strength 15 or higher**, your **speed is permanently reduced by 5 ft.**  
  - If you have **Strength 14 or lower**, your **speed is halved.**  
  - You have **advantage on saving throws** against being pushed, pulled, knocked prone, or otherwise forcibly moved.  
  - If forced movement requires no save or contest, the distance is **halved against you.**  
  - You count as **one size larger** for the purposes of carrying capacity, push/drag/lift limits, and resisting forced movement.

---

## Entropic magic

- **Blurred Veil**:  
  Your tombsteel skeleton disrupts your magical capabilities and skills. Your maximum spell slots decrease by **1 for every level**, to a minimum of one. Whenever you gain new spell slots, this limit is reapplied.

- **Overcharged Casting**:  
  In order to overpower the nullifying energies in your bones, you must overcharge your spells.  
  - Every spell you cast is treated as being cast **one level higher** than the level you declare.

- **Nullify magic**:  
  Once per dawn, you may end **all magical effects currently affecting you**—positive and negative alike.  
  You then become **immune to all such effects** (both beneficial and harmful) for **1 hour.**  
  For each effect you absorb this way, you gain **10 temporary hit points**, which fade after 1 hour.  
  These hit points ignore halving effects from this item.
