# Chains of Andromeda  

_Legendary (requires attunement by a Paladin of the Oath of Devotion)_  
_Chains +3 (Martial, Magical, Reach (30 ft), Special, Mastery)_  

An artifact that defied its apparent function — the final creation of a Pieni artisan who rejected inevitability itself. 
The Chains of Andromeda are not tools of binding, but of liberation: forged to protect those surrendered to the world’s cruelty and to remind them that even in despair, there is choice.  

> “Each sunrise is a gift, let us not waste it bound in chains.”  
> — Andromeda, the Ever Standing  

---

## Weapon Properties  

- **Type**: Chains (Martial, Magical, Reach (30 ft), Special)  
- **Damage**: 4d4
- **Weight**: 50 lbs  

---

## Special Features  

- **Chains of Sacrifice**:  
  The weapon binds itself to your wrists, the metal fusing with your flesh. Once attuned, you cannot doff or be disarmed of the Chains unless you willingly **forsake your Oath of Devotion**.

- **Chains of Freedom**:  
  While attuned to this weapon, your movement cannot be reduced by any effect, and you ignore difficult terrain.  
  You are **immune** to the **prone, paralyzed, grappled, petrified, and restrained** conditions.  

---

## Effects  

- **Magical Weapon**:  
  The Chains have a **+3 bonus** to attack and damage rolls and are considered **magical for the purpose of overcoming resistances and immunities**.  

- **Unstoppable Roil**:  
  When you hit a creature with this weapon, you may immediately make another melee attack against a different creature within reach.  
  You can chain up to **three total attacks** in this way per turn.  

- **Savior of the disempowered**:  
  On a **critical hit**, all creatures currently affected by your **Aura of Protection** regain hit points equal to **1d10 + your Paladin level**.  
  You then gain **temporary hit points** equal to the **total amount healed**.  

---

## Weapon Mastery  

- **Sap**:  
  On hit, the target has **disadvantage on its next attack roll** before the start of your next turn.  

- **Vortex Shield** _(1/Short rest, or 3/Short rest if the wielder has mastery with heavy whips)_:  
  When a creature affected by your **Aura of Protection** makes a saving throw, you may use your **reaction** to weave the chains into a dome of deflective force.  
  - Affected creatures that must make a **Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution** saving throw to take half damage instead take **no damage on a success** and **half damage on a failure**.
