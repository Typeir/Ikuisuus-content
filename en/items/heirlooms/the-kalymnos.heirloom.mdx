# The Kalymnos

_Legendary (requires attunement by a creature with Strength 24 or higher)_  
_Clothing (Hat)_

An impossibly large, slanted hat with a vast wing and a conical top, made of layered hide and cloth.
Its craftsmanship seems unremarkable, yet for some reason, most who witness it cannot shake the feeling that this object is the very embodiment of **“hatness,”** if such a thing exists.
Perhaps it is yet another splinter from the idea of the thing it represents—the Hat.

> “It is unknown where the association between wizards and pointy hats comes from, yet it is proven right time and time again.” — Loitsunme, of the Hidden Society

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Clothing (Hat)
- **Weight**: 600 lb
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Attunement**: Required (Strength 24 or higher)

---

## Effects

- **Arcane Conduit**:  
  While wearing **The Kalymnos**, you gain a **+4 bonus** to **spell attack rolls**, **spell damage rolls**, **spell save DCs**, and **attack and damage rolls** made using the hat.

- **Heavy Is the Hat**:  
  To wear **The Kalymnos**, your Strength score must be **24 or higher**.  
  - If it is lower, upon attempting to don the hat, it collapses forward and the creature is **knocked prone and restrained** beneath it until removed.  
  - Removing the hat from a restrained creature requires a **DC 25 Strength (Athletics)** check made as an action.

- **Massive Fit**:  
  **The Kalymnos** can only be worn by creatures **Large or larger**.  
  Smaller creatures attempting to wear it automatically fail and are considered **crushed** (restrained) beneath its weight until freed.

- **Indestructible Fabric**:  
  The hat cannot be resized, cut, altered, or destroyed by any known means.

- **Bouncing Hat (1/Short Rest)**:  
  As a **bonus action**, you may throw **The Kalymnos**, transforming it into a spinning hazard.  
  You are considered **proficient** with the hat, and you may use your **Intelligence** or **Dexterity** modifier for the attack roll.  
  - **Ranged Weapon Attack**: range 60 ft., one target.  
    **Hit**: 95 (10d10 + 40) slashing damage.  
  - After the first impact, **The Kalymnos** **ricochets** up to 60 ft. in a straight line toward the nearest creature.  
  - Each 20-foot stretch of the ricochet becomes **hazardous terrain** until the end of the next round, as sharp, glasslike blades erupt and swirl.  
    - Entering that space deals **26 (2d10 + 15) slashing damage**, and moving through it deals the same damage per 5 ft.  
    - The area counts as **difficult terrain**.  
  - The hat may **ricochet up to 3 times**.  
  When it can no longer ricochet, **The Kalymnos** automatically returns to its wielder’s head, dealing its full damage to any creatures it passes through on the way back.
