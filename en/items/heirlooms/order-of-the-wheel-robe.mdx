# Order of the Wheel Robe

_Uncommon_  
_Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)_

This fashionable black robe has been created with materials obtained from the Order of the Wheel's fallen brethren. Its somber and simple design hides reinforced patches, fungicidal infusions, and a hood that allows you to breathe through treated fabrics.

---

## Item Properties

- **Type**: Clothing (Robes, Unarmored)
- **Weight**: 6 lbs

## Effects

- **Black Rot Protection**:  
  While wearing this robe, you have **Advantage on saving throws against the Black Rot** and the DC of those saving throws can't be higher than 20.

- **Saturation Limit**:  
  After ten consecutive rounds of exposure, the robes become saturated and unable to offer any protection, losing their properties until treated again with chemicals for a whole week.
