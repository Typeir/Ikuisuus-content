# Hooded Lantern

Adventuring Gear

An enclosed lantern with a sliding metal hood for controlling light output.
Once lit, it burns for 6 hours on a flask (1 pint) of oil.
It casts bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet.
As an action, you can lower the hood, reducing the light to dim light in a 5-foot radius.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (light source, concealable)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: 2 lb.
