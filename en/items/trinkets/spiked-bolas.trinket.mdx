# Spiked Bolas  
Adventuring Gear  

Weighted cords ending in ceramic balls with protruding, hooked spikes.
As an action, you can throw the bolas up to 40 feet, treating them as a thrown weapon.
Make a ranged attack against a creature; on a hit, the target takes piercing damage and must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or become prone and have their movement speed set to 0.
While restrained, the creature gains 1d4 rounds of the [_bleeding_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions) at the start of their turn, until it or an ally uses an action to free them. If the Bolas break,
the target is automatically freed.

**Damage**: 1d4  
**Damage Type**: Piercing  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (prone, bleed)  
**Range**: 40/80  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
