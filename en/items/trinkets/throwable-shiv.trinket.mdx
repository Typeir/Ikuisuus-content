# Throwable Shiv

Adventuring Gear

A crude knife carved from simple materials.
When you hit with this weapon, the targetthe creature gains 1d4 rounds of the [_bleeding_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions).
The bleeding ends early if it receives healing or it or another creature passes the [_bleeding_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions) medicine check to staunch the wound.
Critical hits also double the amount of bleeding.

**Damage**: 1  
**Damage Type**: Slashing  
**Properties**: Improvised, Special (bleed)  
**Range**: 20/60  
**Weight**: ½ lb.
