# Bone Net  
Adventuring Gear  

A heavy net woven from tough fabrics and weighted with metallic embelishments.
As an action, you can throw the net up to 30 feet.
Make a ranged attack against a creature; on a hit, the target is restrained (escape DC 16 Strength).

**Damage Type**: Piercing  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (restrain)  
**Range**: 30/60  
**Weight**: 3 lb.
