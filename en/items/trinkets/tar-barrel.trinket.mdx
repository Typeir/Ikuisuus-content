# Tar Barrel  
Adventuring Gear  

A heavy container of thick tar.
As an action, you can roll or place the barrel within 5 feet.
Creatures moving through the affected 10-foot-radius area have to expend 4 extra feet per feet they move, and the terrain becomes flammable for 1 minute.
If ignited, the tar burns for 3 rounds, dealing 1d10 fire damage to creatures entering or starting their turn in the area.

> A large creature, or one with 18 strength or more, may throw the barrel up to it's specified range.

**Damage**: 3d6 (burning)  
**Damage Type**: Fire  
**Properties**: Special (hazardous terrain)  
**Range**: 10 (placement) 10/30 (thrown)
**Weight**: 15 lb.
