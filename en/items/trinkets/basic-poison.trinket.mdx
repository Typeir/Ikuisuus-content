# Basic Poison

Adventuring Gear

A vial of extracted venom or alchemical toxin for coating weapons.
As an action, you can coat one slashing or piercing weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition.
The poison retains potency for 1 minute before drying.
A creature hit by a poisoned weapon or ammunition must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or take 1d4 poison damage.

**Damage**: 1d4  
**Damage Type**: Poison  
**Properties**: Special (coating, consumable)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: —
