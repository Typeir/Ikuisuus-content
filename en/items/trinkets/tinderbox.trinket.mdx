# Tinderbox

Adventuring Gear

A small container holding flint, fire steel, and tinder (usually dry cloth soaked in oil).
Using a tinderbox to light a torch or anything else with abundant, exposed fuel takes an action.
Lighting other fires that require care typically takes about 1 minute.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (fire-starting)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
