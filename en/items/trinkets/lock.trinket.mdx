# Lock

Adventuring Gear

An iron padlock supplied with a matching key.
Without the key, a creature proficient with thieves' tools can pick the lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity check.
The GM may rule that better locks exist at higher prices, with different DCs.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (security)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
