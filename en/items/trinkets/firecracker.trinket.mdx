# Firecracker  
Adventuring Gear  

A small clay tube packed with powder and fuse.
As an action, you can throw the firecracker up to 30 feet.
When lit, it explodes with light and sound.
Creatures within 15 feet that can see or hear it must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened until the end of their next turn.
If thrown directly at a creature, it must suceed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, or take 3d4 fire damage.
Creatures immune to fear take 1 thunder damage instead.

**Damage**: 1 (if immune to fear)  
**Damage Type**: Thunder  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (frighten)  
**Range**: 10/30  
**Weight**: ½ lb.
