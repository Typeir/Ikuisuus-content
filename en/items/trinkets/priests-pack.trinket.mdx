# Priest's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for clerics, devotees, and those who conduct religious rites in the field.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 19 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A backpack
- A blanket
- 10 candles
- A tinderbox
- An alms box
- Two blocks of incense
- A censer
- Vestments
- 2 days of rations
- A waterskin

**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~29 lb.
