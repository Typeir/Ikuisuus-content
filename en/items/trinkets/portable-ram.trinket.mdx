# Portable Ram

Adventuring Gear

A heavy iron-tipped battering log with carrying handles.
When used to break down a door, you gain a +4 bonus on the Strength check to force it.
One other character can help you use the ram, granting advantage on that Strength check.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (forced entry)  
**Range**: melee  
**Weight**: 35 lb.
