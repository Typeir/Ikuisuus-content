# Smoke-Flask  
Adventuring Gear  

A glass flask containing oil and soot.
As an action, you can throw it up to 40 feet, shattering it on impact.
The flask releases dense black smoke that fills a 15-foot-radius sphere for 1 minute or until dispersed by moderate wind.
The area is heavily obscured.
Drawing and throwing the flask counts as an object interaction.

**Properties**: Thrown, Area (15-ft. radius), Obscuring, Consumable
**Range**: 40  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
