# Bullseye Lantern

Adventuring Gear

A focused lantern that concentrates light into a directed cone.
Once lit, it burns for 6 hours on a flask (1 pint) of oil.
It casts bright light in a 60-foot cone and dim light for an additional 60 feet.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (light source, directional)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: 2 lb.
