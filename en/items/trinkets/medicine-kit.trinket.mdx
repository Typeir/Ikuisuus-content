# Healer's kit  
Adventuring Gear  

A compact kit containing bandages, salves, and tools for treating wounds.
As a Major Action, you can use the healer's kit to stabilize a creature that is dying or to tend to wounds.
Using the kit to stabilize a dying creature instantly clears their death saving throws and stabilizes them.
Alternatively, you can use the kit during a Repose to restore hit points equal to your **Medicine** bonus, provided you are proficient in the medicine skill.
In order for a creature to benefit from this healing, they must spend at least one hit dice during the Repose.
You may only use a healer's kit once per Repose, and a creature may only benefit from one healer's kit during a Repose.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special
**Range**: touch  
**Weight**: 2 lb.
