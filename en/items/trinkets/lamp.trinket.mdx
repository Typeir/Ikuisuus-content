# Lamp

Adventuring Gear

A clay or metal vessel burning oil to cast steady light.
Once lit, a lamp burns for 6 hours on a flask (1 pint) of oil.
It casts bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet.
As an action, you can lower the hood (where applicable), reducing the light to dim light in a 5-foot radius.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (light source)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
