# Magnifying Glass

Adventuring Gear

A polished convex lens set in a wire frame for inspection and fire-starting.
Grants advantage on any ability check made to appraise or inspect an item that is small or highly detailed.
Can substitute for flint and steel to start fires via focused sunlight, requiring sunlight-level bright light, tinder, and about 5 minutes of focused time.
