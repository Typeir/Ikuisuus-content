# Grease Bottle  
Adventuring Gear  

A fat-bellied glass bottle of rendered slick — soap-boiler's dregs, tallow, and a splash of lamp oil.
As a Major Action, you can throw the bottle up to 20 feet, where it shatters and slicks the ground in a 10-foot square.
The area becomes difficult terrain for 1 minute. Each creature standing in the slick when it spreads, and any creature that enters the area or ends its turn there, must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (difficult terrain, prone)  
**Range**: 20  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
