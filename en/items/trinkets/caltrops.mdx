# Caltrops (Bag of 20)
Adventuring Gear  

A small bag of spiked metal fragments.
As an action, you can spread caltrops to cover one 5-foot square.
The area becomes difficult terrain.
Any creature moving into the area must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 1 piercing damage.
Until the creature regains at least 1 hit point, its walking speed is reduced by 10 feet.
A creature moving through the area at half speed doesn't need to make the saving throw.

**Damage**: 1d6  
**Damage Type**: Piercing  
**Properties**: Special (difficult terrain)  
**Range**: 5 (placement)  
**Weight**: 2 lb.
