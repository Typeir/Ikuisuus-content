# Spike Trap  
Adventuring Gear  

A collapsible trap fitted with prongs and spikes.
As an action, you can place the trap in an adjacent 5-foot square.
When a creature enters that square, it must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 7 (2d6) piercing damage and become restrained.
Disarming the trap requires a DC 15 Dexterity check with thieves’ tools.

> A large creature, or one with 18 strength or more, may throw the trap up to it's specified range.

**Damage**: 2d6  
**Damage Type**: Piercing  
**Properties**: Special (restrain, trap)  
**Range**: 5 (placement) 10/30 (thrown)
**Weight**: 5 lb.
