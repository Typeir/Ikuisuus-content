# Grease Barrel  
Adventuring Gear  

A wooden drum filled with viscous grease.
As an action, you can spill its contents in a 10-foot radius.
The ground becomes difficult terrain.
Creatures entering or starting their turn there must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.
If the area is ignited, it burns for 2 rounds, dealing 1d10 fire damage to creatures entering or starting their turn in the area.

**Damage**: 1d10 
**Damage Type**: Fire  
**Properties**: Special (terrain, flammable)  
**Range**: 10 (placement)  
**Weight**: 10 lb.
