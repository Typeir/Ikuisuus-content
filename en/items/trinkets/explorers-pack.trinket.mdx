# Explorer's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for wilderness travel, long marches, and self-sufficient overland expeditions.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 10 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A backpack
- A bedroll
- A mess kit
- A tinderbox
- 10 torches
- 10 days of rations
- A waterskin
- 50 feet of hempen rope strapped to the side of the pack

**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~59 lb.
