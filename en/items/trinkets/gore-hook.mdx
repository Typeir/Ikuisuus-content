# Gore-Hook  
Adventuring Gear  

A hooked blade on a cord.
The cord is 30ft long unless stated otherwise.
As an action, you can use it as a melee or thrown weapon.
On a hit, the target takes slashing damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or become grappled.
As a bonus action, you can pull a grappled creature up to 5 feet closer to you.
The grappled creature takes 4 extra feet of movement per feet of movement spent.

**Damage**: 1d6  
**Damage Type**: Slashing  
**Properties**: Melee or Thrown, Special (grapple, pull)  
**Range**: 10 (melee) / 20 (thrown)  
**Weight**: 2 lb.
