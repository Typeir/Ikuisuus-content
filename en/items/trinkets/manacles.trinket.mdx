# Manacles

Adventuring Gear

Heavy iron shackles that bind a Small or Medium creature.
Escaping the manacles requires a successful DC 20 Dexterity check.
Breaking the manacles requires a successful DC 20 Strength check.
Each set comes with one key. Without the key, a creature proficient with thieves' tools can pick the lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity check.
Manacles have 15 hit points.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (restraint)  
**Range**: melee  
**Weight**: 6 lb.
