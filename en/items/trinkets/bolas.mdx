# Bola  
Adventuring Gear  

A set of weighted cords designed to entangle.
As an action, you can throw the bola up to 30 feet, treating it as a thrown weapon.
Make a ranged attack against a large or smaller creature; on a hit, the target must succed on a  (escape DC 14 Strength) or have it's movement speed set to zero and falls prone.
The target must use an action to free itself, it can also be freed by an ally or will be freed automatically if the Bola breaks.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (restrain, trip)  
**Range**: 30/60  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
