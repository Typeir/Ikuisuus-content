# Potion of Healing

Adventuring Gear

A stoppered vial of bright red fluid that smells faintly of copper and crushed herbs.
Drinking or administering a potion of healing takes an action.
It restores 2d4 + 2 hit points.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Special (healing, consumable)  
**Range**: touch  
**Weight**: 0.5 lb.
