# Shatter-Pot  
Adventuring Gear  

A brittle clay vessel filled with reactive salts, rubble and powdered glass.
As an action, you can throw the pot, shattering it on impact.
Each creature within 10 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 2d6 piercing damage and be blinded until the end of its next turn.

**Damage**: 2d6 (secondary ignition)  
**Damage Type**: piercing  
**Properties**: Thrown, Consumable  
**Range**: 30  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
