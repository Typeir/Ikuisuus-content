# Oil Flask  
Adventuring Gear  

A glass container filled with slick oil.
As an action, you can throw this flask up to 30 feet, shattering it on impact.
The oil covers a 5-foot square, making the area difficult terrain.
A creature entering the area must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.
If the area is ignited, it burns for 2 rounds, dealing 1d10 fire damage to creatures entering or starting their turn in the area.

> A large creature, or one with 18 strength or more, may throw the barrel up to it's specified range.

**Damage**: 1d10 (burning)  
**Damage Type**: Fire  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (terrain, flammable)  
**Range**: 30/60  
**Weight**: 1 lb.
