# Entertainer's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for performers, social travellers, and those who rely on appearance and disguise.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 40 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A backpack
- A bedroll
- Two costumes
- 5 candles
- 5 days of rations
- A waterskin
- A disguise kit

**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~38 lb.
