# Burglar's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for infiltration, lock work, and navigating dark or confined spaces.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 16 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A backpack
- A bag of 1,000 ball bearings
- 10 feet of string
- A bell
- 5 candles
- A crowbar
- A hammer
- 10 pitons
- A hooded lantern
- 2 flasks of oil
- 5 days of rations
- A tinderbox
- A waterskin
- 50 feet of hempen rope strapped to the side of the pack

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~48 lb.
