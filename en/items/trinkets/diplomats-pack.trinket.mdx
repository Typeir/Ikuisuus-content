# Diplomat's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for formal travel, correspondence, and social engagements.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 39 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A chest
- Two cases for maps and scrolls
- A set of fine clothes
- A bottle of ink and an ink pen
- A lamp
- Two flasks of oil
- Five sheets of paper
- A vial of perfume
- Sealing wax
- Soap

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~36 lb.
