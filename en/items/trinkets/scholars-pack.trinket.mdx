# Scholar's Pack

Adventuring Gear

A pre-assembled kit suited for researchers, sages, and those who rely on written records and careful study.
Purchasing the pack outright costs 40 gp, which is cheaper than buying the contents individually.

Includes:

- A backpack
- A book of lore
- A bottle of ink and an ink pen
- 10 sheets of parchment
- A bag of sand
- A small knife

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Container (starting equipment pack)  
**Range**: —  
**Weight**: ~12 lb.
