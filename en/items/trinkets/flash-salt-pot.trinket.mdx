# Flash-Salt Pot  
Adventuring Gear  

A brittle cermaic pot filled with volatile magnesium salts.
As an action, you can throw the pot up to 20 feet, where it bursts in a flash of blinding light.
Each creature within 5 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn.
On a success, creatures are blinded until the start of their next turn.

**Damage**: —  
**Properties**: Thrown, Special (stun, blind)  
**Range**: 20  
**Weight**: ½ lb.
