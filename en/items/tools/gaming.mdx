# Gaming

Gaming covers the instruments of structured play — dice sets, card decks, game boards, and the pieces that go with them. Across the Lands of Damocles, games of chance and skill serve as social lubricant, a mechanism for settling disputes, and a reliable way to separate the unwary from their coin. A skilled player reads the game as much as they read the table.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Gaming lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving the games of a chosen kit — play, analysis, cheating, and detecting cheating.

## Specializations
