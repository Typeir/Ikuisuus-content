# Calligraphy

Calligraphy is the trade of the written word — fine nibs, inks of various weights and compositions, paper stocks, and the knowledge of how letterforms carry meaning beyond their content. A skilled calligrapher can produce official documents, illuminate manuscripts, and inscribe arcane seals with equal facility.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Calligraphy lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving precise inscription, document analysis, and the preparation of written materials.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Calligraphy, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
