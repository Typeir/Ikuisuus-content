# Jewellery

Jewellery is the trade of fine metalwork and gemcraft — loupes, graving tools, precision scales, and the practiced eye that separates true stones from imitations. Jewellers operate at the intersection of wealth, aesthetics, and the mineral world; their skills extend from the counter to the mine.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Jewellery lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving the identification, crafting, and appraisal of jewellery, gemstones, and fine metalwork.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Jewellery, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
