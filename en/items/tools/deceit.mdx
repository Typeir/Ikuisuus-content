# Deceit

Deceit is the trade of false identity — cosmetics, binding agents, prosthetics, inks and seals, and the practised knowledge of how to become someone else, or make a document appear to be something it is not. It is favoured by infiltrators, investigators, and anyone whose profession requires maintaining a fiction.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Deceit lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving physical disguise, document fabrication, and the detection of the same.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Deceit, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
