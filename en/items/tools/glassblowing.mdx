# Glassblowing

Glassblowing is the trade of heat and form — blowpipes, marvers, shears, and a portable furnace capable of working silica into vessels, lenses, and architectural elements. Glass is at once a material of commerce and science, found in cathedral windows and laboratory equipment alike.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Glassblowing lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving the identification, crafting, and analysis of glass objects and their contents.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Glassblowing, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
