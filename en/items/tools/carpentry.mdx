# Carpentry

Carpentry is the trade of wood — from rough framing and load-bearing construction to fine joinery and decorative carving. A single proficiency covers structural work and decorative shaping alike, since both rely on the same material, edge tools, and trained eye for grain.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Carpentry lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving wood construction, woodworking, and the assessment and repair of wooden structures or objects.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Carpentry, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
