# Herbalism

Herbalism is the trade of the field healer and the wilderness scholar — drying racks, a mortar and pestle, small glass containers, and the knowledge of what grows where and what it does. Herbalists occupy a position between the physician and the naturalist, and their work is found in rural communities where trained healers are rare.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Herbalism lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving plant identification, the preparation of herbal remedies, and the assessment of natural compounds.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Herbalism, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
