# Painting

Painting is the trade of recorded vision — pigments, oils, binding media, brushes, and the practiced ability to translate what the eye sees into a form that outlasts the moment. Painters document events, honour the living and dead, map the natural world in colour, and move through courts as craftspeople whose work speaks for those who commission it.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Painting lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving the creation, identification, and analysis of visual art and painted objects.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Painting, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
