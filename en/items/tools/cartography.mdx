# Cartography

Cartography is the trade of spatial knowledge — compasses, sextants, parallel rules, and the accumulated experience of translating a living landscape into a document that others can read. A good map is a form of power; a good navigator knows how to survive without one.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Cartography lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving map-making, route-finding, spatial reasoning, and the interpretation of geographical information.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Cartography, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.

| Identification | Investigation | You have advantage on Investigation checks to identify a map's age, likely author, and assess its accuracy against known landmarks.                                                                                               | —                  |
