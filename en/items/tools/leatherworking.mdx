# Leatherworking

Leatherworking is the trade of hide and skin — shaping leather into armour, bags, and weapon harnesses, and the footwear that keeps those who wear the results moving. A single proficiency covers bench work and shoemaking alike, since both rely on the same material, edge tools, and stitching technique.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Leatherworking lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving the identification, crafting, and repair of leather goods, footwear, and textile-adjacent equipment.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Leatherworking, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
