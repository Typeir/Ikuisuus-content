# Alchemy

Alchemy is the trade of transformation — glassware, reagents, a portable heat source, and the precise measures that separate a useful extract from a dangerous one. Its practitioners are found in apothecaries, wizards' laboratories, and the kit bags of soldiers who know that the right compound changes a fight.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Alchemy lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving alchemical preparation, identification, and analysis. The GM determines which ability score applies based on the task.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Alchemy, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.

| Specialty       | Skill         | Benefit                                                                                                                                                                | Items                                       |
| --------------- | ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Identification  | Investigation | You have advantage on Investigation checks to identify alchemical substances, estimate their potency, and determine their likely components.                           | —                                           |
| Alchemy         | Arcana        | You have advantage on Arcana checks to identify magical substances and analyze the alchemical properties of magical items and reagents.                                | —                                           |
| Brines          | Nature        | You have advantage on Nature checks to locate and identify mineral-rich water sources, salt deposits, and naturally occurring chemical compounds.                      | —                                           |
| Explosives      | —             | During a Repose or Recovery, you can craft one alchemist's fire or smoke flask using materials worth 10 gp. At 5th level or higher, you can also craft a thunderstone. | Alchemist's fire, Smoke flask, Thunderstone |
| Acids           | —             | During a Repose or Recovery, you can craft one vial of acid using materials worth 15 gp.                                                                               | Acid                                        |
| Pharmaceuticals | Medicine      | You have advantage on Medicine checks to analyze the medical applications of alchemical compounds and identify substances used as medicines or poisons.                | —                                           |
| Transmutation   | Arcana        | You have advantage on Arcana checks related to transmutation magic, alchemical transformation, and the theoretical limits of matter-altering processes.                | —                                           |

## Items and Output

Items crafted with alchemist's supplies during rests require the raw materials listed in each specialization's **Items** column. Raw materials typically cost half the market value of the finished product unless otherwise noted.
