# Brewing

Brewing is the trade of patient chemistry — fermentation vessels, airlocks, hydrometers, and a working knowledge of how sugar, yeast, and time produce something worth drinking. Brewers are found wherever morale needs maintaining.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Brewing lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving fermentation, beverage identification, and the preparation of consumable liquid compounds.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Brewing, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
