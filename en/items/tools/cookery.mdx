# Cookery

Cookery is the trade of nourishment — knives, ladles, a portable pot, and the experience to turn almost anything edible into something worth eating. A skilled cook on campaign is worth their weight in iron rations, and the difference between a well-fed company and a miserable one is often nothing more than someone who knows what to do with a fire and an hour.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Cookery lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving food preparation, ingredient identification, and the maintenance of nutrition under field conditions.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Cookery, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
