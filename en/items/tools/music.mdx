# Music

Music spans the full breadth of the Cradle's sonic traditions — from the resonant percussion of southern harvest festivals to the precise string work of urban conservatories. Proficiency with Music grants a working understanding of theory, performance under pressure, and the social role that music plays in every culture, alongside practical command of a chosen instrument.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Music lets you add your proficiency bonus to Performance checks made using a chosen instrument, and to checks related to musical theory, composition, and cultural identification.
