# Pottery

Pottery is the trade of clay — wheels, paddles, kiln tongs, and the practised sense of how earthenware behaves under heat. It is one of the oldest crafts in the Cradle, and skilled potters understand a great deal about the people who made the vessels they find in ruins.

> **Design note**: Two additional specializations for this trade are currently design-incomplete and will be added in a future update. Do not include placeholder entries in player reference materials.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Pottery lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving the identification, crafting, and assessment of ceramic and earthenware objects.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Pottery, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
