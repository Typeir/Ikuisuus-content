# Electrics

Electrics is the trade of infrastructure and artificial systems — wire strippers, terminal connectors, insulated grips, and the knowledge of how charge flows and where it should not. Its practitioners are found wherever automata are maintained, power is distributed, or machines must be kept alive rather than simply operated.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Electrics lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving electrical systems, circuit diagnosis, and the installation or repair of electrically powered mechanisms.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Electrics, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.

| Specialty      | Skill         | Benefit                                                                                                                                                                                                   | Items |
| -------------- | ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----- |
| Identification | Investigation | You have advantage on Investigation checks to identify electrical components, diagnose faults in circuits, and estimate when a system was last serviced.                                                  | —     |
| Diagnostics    | Medicine      | You have advantage on Medicine checks to treat injuries caused by electrical or lightning damage, and to stabilize a creature electrocuted to 0 hit points.                                               | —     |
| Shock hazards  | Arcana        | You have advantage on Arcana checks to identify and neutralize magical traps that trigger electrical effects.                                                                                             | —     |
| Wiring         | —             | In 10 minutes, you can install a simple electrical circuit (bell wire, pressure switch, contact alarm). You have advantage on checks to conceal wiring in walls and structures.                           | —     |
| Robotics       | —             | Automata you have maintained during your last rest ignore the disabled condition from electrical damage until your next long rest. You can maintain a number of automata equal to your proficiency bonus. | —     |
