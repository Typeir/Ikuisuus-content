# Trades

Trades are bodies of craft and professional knowledge — each one a set of techniques, a working vocabulary, and the discipline to apply them. Across the Cradle, a person known for their trade is someone who works, not merely someone who watches.

This section contains individual pages for every trade, including their specialization tables, crafting outputs, and notes for GMs.

For the rules governing how proficiency, expertise, and specialization interact, see [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts).
