# Tinkering

Tinkering is the trade of mechanical ingenuity — fine screwdrivers, pliers, a portable soldering kit, and the knowledge of how small moving parts interact to produce large effects. Tinkers repair what others cannot, build what others cannot conceive, and maintain the devices that everyone else depends on.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Tinkering lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving mechanical assembly, clockwork mechanisms, and the identification and repair of complex devices.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Tinkering, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
