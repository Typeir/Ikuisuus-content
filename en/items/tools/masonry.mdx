# Masonry

Masonry is the trade of stone — chisels, mallets, levels, and the experience of reading rock as a material with grain, weakness, and load. Masons build the walls that cities depend on and know better than anyone how to bring them down.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Masonry lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving stonework construction, structural assessment, demolition, and the identification of worked stone.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Masonry, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
