# Weaving

Weaving is the trade of textile craft — shuttles, frames, needles, and the accumulated knowledge of how fibre can be transformed into fabric, armour, and architecture. Weavers understand structure at a tactile level: the relationship between tension and strength, between pattern and meaning.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Weaving lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving textile identification, crafting, and the assessment of fabric goods, rope, and woven structures.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Weaving, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
