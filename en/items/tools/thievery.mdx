# Thievery

Thievery is the trade of access — tension wrenches, picks, a small mirror, and the practised patience of someone who understands that most locks are a question, not an answer. It is practised by locksmiths, infiltrators, investigators, and anyone whose work requires getting into places that were designed to keep them out.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Thievery lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving lock manipulation, trap assessment, and security bypass.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Thievery, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
