# Vehicles

Vehicle operation covers both land and water craft — the knowledge of how a vehicle responds, where its tolerances lie, how to keep it running under field conditions, and how to use its mounted systems effectively. Vehicle specializations are currently incomplete.

> **Design note**: Vehicle specializations are design-incomplete. The entries below represent a working draft. Do not use in player reference materials without further review.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Vehicles lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving the operation, maintenance, and navigation of land or water vehicles.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Vehicles, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
