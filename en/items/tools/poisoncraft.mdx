# Poisoncraft

Poisoncraft is the trade of those who understand that what kills need not announce itself — extraction vessels, stabilising agents, delivery mechanisms, and the body of knowledge required to produce compounds that affect the living in controlled and specific ways. It is practised by assassins, physicians, and anyone who needs to neutralise a threat without raising an alarm.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Poisoncraft lets you add your proficiency bonus to ability checks involving the identification, preparation, and delivery of toxins and pharmaceutical compounds.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Poisoncraft, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
