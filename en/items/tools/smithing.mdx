# Smithing

Smithing is the trade of metal — hammers, tongs, files, and the knowledge of how iron and steel behave under heat, force, and time. Smiths are essential to any settlement of consequence, and their skills range from the purely functional to the finely decorative.

## Using This Trade

Proficiency with Smithing lets you add your tier bonus to ability checks involving metalwork identification, crafting, and the assessment and repair of metal objects.

## Specializations

When you gain proficiency with Smithing, choose one specialization. When you gain expertise, choose a second. See [Tools, Trades and Crafts](/en/library/rules/hearth-and-station/tools-trades-and-crafts) for the full rules on specializations.
