# Moonblessed Warrior

_Medium Humanoid (Sunborn), Lawful Evil_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| --------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 18 (half plate) | 127 (15d8+60)  | 40 ft.    |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) |

- **Saving Throws**: STR +8, CON +8, WIS +6
- **Skills**: Athletics +8, Intimidation +5
- **Damage Resistances**: **cold**
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
- **Languages**: Empyrean, Common
- **Challenge**: 9 (5,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Bloodgift of the Moon

At the start of each of its turns, the warrior gains **10 temporary hit points**. If it begins its turn with no temporary hit points, it may teleport up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.

#### Moon Step Technique

If the Moonblessed Warrior forgoes all movement on its turn, it regains **10 hit points** and gains **+1 to AC, saving throws, and skill checks** until the start of its next turn.

#### Innate Spellcasting

The Moonblessed Warrior can use its Alfanjón as a spellcasting focus and casts the following spells innately (save DC 16):

- At will: [_Lesser Mooncleave_](/en/library/spells/lesser-mooncleave), [_Thaumaturgy_](/en/library/spells/thaumaturgy)
- 1/short rest: Mooncleave (weapon feature, 30 ft. cone, DC 17)

#### Lunar Dissolution

As a bonus action, the Alfanjón can vanish or reappear in its hand in a shimmer of silver mist.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Moonblessed Warrior makes three attacks with the Alfanjón of the Crescent Moon.

- **Alfanjón of the Crescent Moon**
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ **1d12 + 4 slashing damage.** On hit, the target’s movement speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn (Slow mastery).

#### Moon Step

The warrior teleports up to 20 feet to a space it can see, then immediately makes one Alfanjón attack against a creature within range. This attack has advantage.

#### Moonflare (Recharge 6)

The warrior sweeps the Alfanjón in a killing arc. Each creature of its choice within 15 feet must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **4d8 cold damage** and becoming **blinded** until the start of the warrior’s next turn on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

---

## Bonus Actions

#### Cantrip Surge (1/Turn)

If the warrior uses Moon Step Technique, it may cast a cantrip as a bonus action.
