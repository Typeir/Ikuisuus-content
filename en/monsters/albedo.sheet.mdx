## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Albedo has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of her turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)

When Albedo fails a saving throw, she can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Titanic Godmass

The listed statistics represent **Albedo’s control nucleus** (roughly horse-sized), extruded from a miles-wide quasi-liquid body. Only the nucleus can be targeted. The rest of Albedo functions as terrain and sources of spawns created by her abilities.

- The nucleus can’t be grappled or knocked prone.
- If the nucleus would be pushed or pulled, Albedo may instead shift the ground beneath her and remain in place.

#### Cancerous Proliferation

Albedo **spawns** when anything bleeds or breaks near her.

- **When Hit**: Whenever Albedo takes **20 or more damage** from a single source, **spawn 1 Petal** in an empty space within 15 ft. of the attacker (**2 Petals** on a critical hit).
- **On Hit**: Whenever Albedo hits a creature with a melee attack, the target is **seeded** with **1 Parasite** (see _Reflected Parasite_).
- **End of Each Turn**: If Albedo damaged **two or more** different creatures since her last turn, **spawn 1 Petal** adjacent to each damaged creature.
- **Brood Cap (Soft 20)**: At the end of each round, for each spawn beyond **20**, **merge** the two lowest-HP Petals into **1 Bloom**.
- **At the start of each turn**: if three **Blooms** are present within 10 ft. of one another, they automatically **merge into 1 Flower** at the end of the round.
- **At the start of each turn**: if three **Flowers** are present within 10 ft. of one another, they automatically **merge into 1 Bouquet** at the end of the round.

#### Self-Fertilization

_As a bonus action_, Albedo **consumes** up to **3** allied spawns within 30 ft. For each creature consumed, permanently **increase the damage die size of all her melee and area attacks by one step** on this scale (no maximum):

> d4 → d6 → d8 → d10 → d12 → **2d8** → **2d10** → **2d12** → **4d8** → **4d10** → **4d12** → **8d8** → **8d10** → **8d12** → … (continue increasing die count in the same pattern)

Additionally, each spawn gains a **+1 to all attack and damage rolls**, also stacking infinitely.

Albedo loses all such bonuses if she is reduced to 0 HP, or if she consumes no spawns during her turn.

Additionally, whenever Albedo rests for longer than 1 hour, she loses all such bonuses.

#### Endless Reef

Ground Albedo has affected this combat becomes **semi-living reef**:

- Counts as **difficult terrain** for creatures other than Albedo.
- A creature that walks across Endless Reef takes **7 (2d4) acid** per foot, unless it willingly walks at half its speed.
- Certain effects convert areas into Endless Reef as noted.

#### White Life

Albedo floods the surrounding area with its **endless reef**, a mass of pale coral-like growths that spread and pulse with vile intent.  
When a creature touches the reef, starts its turn within **30 feet** of Albedo, or is struck by one of its attacks, it must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or roll on the [_White Life_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/white-life) table.  
Creatures **immune to disease and curses** that would roll in the table ignore this effect and instead take **2d4 acid damage** as the coral burns against their skin.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Albedo makes **two** **Mitotic Rends** and **one** **Exuvia Sweep**. Each may be replaced with either **Enzyme Torrent** or **Consume the Brood**.

- **Mitotic Rend**
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ **16 (3d6 + 5)** slashing plus **8 (3d4)** acid.  
  **On hit**: the target is **seeded** with **1 Parasite**.

- **Exuvia Sweep**
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 20 ft., up to two targets.  
  _Hit:_ **13 (3d4 + 5)** bludgeoning plus **5 (2d4)** necrotic.  
  Each creature hit must succeed on a **DC 22 Strength** saving throw or be **pushed 15 ft.** and **knocked prone**. If at least one creature fails, **spawn 1 Petal** adjacent to one failed target’s space.

- **Enzyme Torrent (Recharge 5–6)**
  A **60-ft. cone** of dissolving enzymes. Creatures in the area make a **DC 23 Dexterity** saving throw, taking **23 (9d4)** acid + **23 (9d4)** necrotic on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
  Creatures that fail are **seeded with 1 Parasite**. All squares in the cone become **Endless Reef**.

- **Consume the Brood (1/Turn)**
  Choose up to **5** spawned creatures within 30 ft.; they **die** as they are reabsorbed into Albedo’s mass.  
  For each creature consumed, choose one effect:

- **Petal**: Albedo **heals 15 HP**, or **creates 1 Bloom**, consuming **2 Petals** per Bloom.
- **Bloom**: Albedo **heals 40 HP**, or **creates 1 Flower**, consuming **2 Blooms**.
- **Flower**: Albedo **heals 100 HP**, or **creates 1 Bouquet**, consuming **2 Flowers**.
- **Bouquet**: Albedo **heals 250 HP** and erupts with regenerative sludge; all other spawned creatures within 30 ft. regain **30 HP**.

All merges occur immediately in spaces formerly occupied by the consumed creatures.

## Reactions

### **Shedding Wake**

When a creature **ends its movement within 10 ft.** of Albedo's Godmass, **spawn 1 Petal** adjacent to that creature (once per creature per round).

### **Exuvial Guard**

When Albedo would be hit by a melee attack, growths interpose. The attacker must succeed on a **DC 22 Dexterity** saving throw or the attack **misses**.  
Whether it hits or misses, **spawn 1 Petal** adjacent to the attacker.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

Albedo can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Spawn Petal.** (Costs 1 Deed) Create **1 Petal** in an empty space within 15 ft.
- **Detonate Parasites.** (Costs 2 Deeds) Each **Parasite** on creatures within 60 ft. **detonates** for **10 (4d4)** acid + **10 (4d4)** psychic damage.
- **Reef Stride.** (Costs 2 Deeds) Albedo **teleports up to 60 ft.** to a square of **Endless Reef** she can sense.
- **Merge Swarm.** (Costs 3 Deeds) Choose up to **3 Petals** within 20 ft.; they merge into **1 Bloom** at full HP.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Albedo can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. She cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

### **Womb Weeps**

**Spawn 1d4 Petals** adjacent to each hostile creature that **moved** since the previous round.

### **Crawlspace**

Choose up to **three 10-ft. squares** within 120 ft.; they become **Endless Reef** and immediately **spawn 1 Petal** each.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

### **Bloodied (50%) — Knots of Flesh**

At the end of each of Albedo’s turns, **merge automatically**: for every **3 Petals** within 10 ft. of each other, create **1 Bloom** (consuming those Petals).

### **Doomed (25%) — Critical Mass**

The nucleus destabilizes; replication overruns structure.

- At the start of each round, **spawn Petals until there are exactly 10 active**.
- Then, **merge every three Petals** within 30 ft. into **one Bloom**.
- Each time a **Bloom forms**, Albedo **uses Self-Fertilization** as a **free action**, consuming **1 Petal** if any remain.
- All ground within **120 ft.** becomes **Endless Reef**.
