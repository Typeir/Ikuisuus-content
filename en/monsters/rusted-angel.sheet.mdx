## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

The Rusted Angel has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Black Ice Consumption

When the Rusted Angel moves into the space of a **black ice fragment** or starts its turn within 5 feet of one, it can consume it.  
When it does, it gains **20 temporary hit points**, gains **1 Black Ice Charge**, and immediately recharges one feature of its choice that normally requires **1 Black Ice Charge** to use.
Its maximum number of **Black Ice Charges** is **3**.  
The Rusted Angel can consume only one fragment per turn.

#### Black Ice Charges

The Rusted Angel fuels its dead winter techniques with harvested black ice.  
It starts combat with **0 Black Ice Charges**.  
Whenever it consumes a **black ice fragment**, it gains **1 Black Ice Charge**.  
It may spend these charges to use certain actions. If an action requires a charge and none remain, it cannot use that action.

#### Eroding Presence

At the start of each of the Rusted Angel’s turns, each creature of its choice within 10 ft. takes **7 (2d6)** acid damage and must succeed on a **DC 21 Constitution saving throw** or gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

#### Rimeforged Armament

At the end of each of its turns in which it takes the Attack action, the Rusted Angel reshapes its blade into one of the following forms. The chosen form lasts until the end of its next turn and affects its next **Ruinblade** or **Glacial Judgment** attack.

- **Impaling Lance — Piercing.** The attack deals piercing damage instead of slashing and gains **+5 feet reach**.
- **Cleaving Edge — Slashing.** The attack deals slashing damage. If the attack misses, the target still takes slashing damage equal to the Rusted Angel’s **Strength modifier**.
- **Crushing Maul — Bludgeoning.** The attack deals bludgeoning damage instead of slashing. On a hit, the target is pushed **10 feet**.
- **Frostbite Brand — Cold.** The attack deals cold damage instead of slashing. On a hit, the target’s speed is reduced by **10 feet** until the end of its next turn.

#### Corroded Sword Saint

The Rusted Angel scores a critical hit on a roll of 19–20; this threshold is lowered by 1 per stack of **[_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** the target has.

#### Construct Nature

The Rusted Angel does not require air, food, drink, or sleep.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Rusted Angel makes three attacks with any combination of its weapons.

- **Ruinblade.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 25 (3d10 + 8) slashing damage plus **9 (2d8)** cold damage.  
  The target gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

- **Ice Blade Volley.**  
  The Rusted Angel summons a storm of dancing glacier blades in a **60-foot line that is 10 feet wide**.  
  Each creature in the area must make a **DC 21 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **45 (10d8)** cold and slashing damage on a failure or half as much on a success.  
  Creatures that fail also gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** and have their **speed reduced by 10 feet** until the end of their next turn.

- **Glacial Judgment (Costs 1 Black Ice Charge).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 33 (4d10 + 8 plus 2d8) slashing and cold damage.  
  The target must make a **DC 21 Constitution saving throw** or become **restrained** as black ice spears erupt through its limbs until the end of the Rusted Angel’s next turn.  
  A creature restrained this way takes **9 (2d8)** cold damage at the start of each of its turns and gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

**Falling Cathedral Step.**  
_Special Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, range 20–40 ft., one target.  
The Rusted Angel launches itself in a screaming arc of rust and frost, landing within 5 ft. of its target.  
_Hit:_ 21 (3d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage plus **9 (2d8)** cold damage.  
If the target is Large or smaller, it must make a **DC 21 Strength saving throw** or be **knocked prone**.  
A target that takes damage also gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

- **Black Ice Meteor (Costs 1 Black Ice Charge).**  
  The Rusted Angel conjures and hurls a screaming mass of dead winter at a point on the ground it can see within **120 feet**, provided that point is at least **60 feet** away from it.  
  Each creature within a **15-foot-radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **DC 21 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **35 (10d6)** cold and bludgeoning damage on a failure or half as much on a success.  
  Creatures that fail also gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** and are **knocked prone**.  
  After the impact, a **black ice fragment** forms in the center of the area.

- **Avalanche Blade (Costs 2 Black Ice Charges).**  
  The Rusted Angel manifests a gargantuan, quasi-intangible ice blade in an unoccupied space it can see within **30 feet**.

> **Cold Cleave.**  
> _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
> _Hit:_ **36 (8d6 + 8)** cold and necrotic damage. The target’s speed becomes **0 feet** until the end of its next turn.

The blade lasts for **1 minute**, until the Rusted Angel dismisses it, or until it is destroyed. It cannot normally move on its own.  
Once per turn, when a creature of the Rusted Angel’s choice enters the blade’s space or starts its turn there, the blade makes **Cold Cleave**.

- **Black Star** (Costs 3 Black Ice Charges).

The rusted angel howls to the fathomless stars above, calling down a shard of the seventh winter's hatred.

The shard strikes a point on the ground the Rusted Angel can see within **150 feet**, creating a **20-foot-radius sphere** of black ice and frost.

Creatures in the area must make a **DC 21 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **37 (8d8)** cold damage plus **37 (8d8)** bludgeoning damage on a failure or half as much on a success.
Creatures that fail the saving throw also gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** and are **knocked prone**.
Creatures struck by the shard are immediately [slowed](/en/library/rules/conditions#slowed) for 1 minute, and have their erosion stacks, if any doubled.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Rusted Angel can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Ruinblade Cut.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Rusted Angel makes one **Ruinblade** attack.
- **Black Ice Call.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Rusted Angel conjures **one black ice fragment** in an unoccupied space it can see within 30 ft.
- **Mana Ring Drift.** (Costs 2 Deeds) One **Avalanche Blade** the Rusted Angel created moves up to **30 feet** in a straight line. Each creature in its path must succeed on a **DC 21 Dexterity saving throw** or take **18 (4d8)** cold damage.
- **Wingless Ascension.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Rusted Angel moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Creatures whose spaces it moves adjacent to during this movement take **7 (2d6)** cold damage.
