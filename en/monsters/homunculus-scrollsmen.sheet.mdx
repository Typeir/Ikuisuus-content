# Scrollsman

_Small Humanoid (Homunculus), Any Alignment_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| --------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 14              | 99 ([% 12d8 +48 %]) | 35 ft.    |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 8 (–1)  | 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) | 12 (+1) | 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) |

- **Saving Throws**: Constitution +7, Wisdom +7
- **Skills**: Arcana +4, Insight +7, Medicine +7
- **Damage Resistances**: Fire
- **Condition Immunities**: Exhaustion
- **Senses**: Passive Perception 14
- **Languages**: Common, plus one taught by its creator
- **Challenge**: 8 (3,900 XP)
- **Tier Bonus**: +3

---

## Traits

#### Living Flame

The Scrollsman carries a magical flame atop its body.

- It emits **bright light** in a **10-foot radius** and **dim light** for an additional **20 feet**.
- It automatically fails Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in darkness.
- It can hood the flame, reducing the light to dim light only (5 ft.) and rolling Dexterity (Stealth) checks at disadvantage instead of automatically failing.
- If the flame is **extinguished**, the Scrollsman rolls **all attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws at disadvantage**.
- Relighting the flame requires a **Repose** or magical flame from a spell, item, or similar source.
- The flame ignites flammable objects that come into contact with it.

---

#### Wax Physiology

The Scrollsman is an artificial being animated by primordial wax.

- It has **advantage on saving throws** against effects that would reduce its movement speed or inflict exhaustion.
- This advantage does **not** apply against paralysis, petrification, or similar total incapacitation effects.

---

#### Core Flame: Green (The Resilience of Kalmora)

The Scrollsman's flame burns a steady emerald, attuned to endurance and survival.

- When the Scrollsman or a creature it can see within **30 feet** makes a saving throw, the Scrollsman can spend **two of its Hit Dice** to empower the roll.
- Roll **one** of the spent Hit Dice. The target adds the result to its saving throw (2 HD → 1 HD bonus).
- The Scrollsman can use this trait **once per round**, and can do so **after seeing the roll but before knowing the outcome**.
- The Scrollsman has **12 Hit Dice** (d8). Spent Hit Dice are regained after a **Repose**.

---

#### Scroll-Bound Mind

The Scrollsman was purpose-built to handle spell scrolls. It can read and use **any spell scroll** regardless of class or spell list restrictions. If the spell is of a higher level than it can normally cast, it must still succeed on the required Arcana check.

---

#### Cartouche of Scrolls

The Scrollsman wears a waxed-leather bandolier across its chest, holding a rotating stock of pre-written scrolls — its life's work and sole purpose.

As a **Minor Action**, the Scrollsman can pluck and cast one of the following spells from the cartouche without expending a spell slot:

- [_Blur_](/en/library/spells/blur) — the Target's form blurs, imposing disadvantage on attack rolls against it
- [_Mirror Image_](/en/library/spells/mirror-image) — three illusory duplicates shield the target from harm
- [_Magic Weapon_](/en/library/spells/magic-weapon) — a touched weapon becomes a +1 magical weapon
- [_Haste_](/en/library/spells/haste) — an ally's speed doubles, gains +2 AC, and an extra action

Each spell can be cast this way **once**, and all uses are regained after a **Repose**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Scrollsman makes two **Stylus Dagger** attacks.

- **Stylus Dagger.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 5 ([% 1d4 +2 piercing %]).

---

#### Spellcasting

The Scrollsman is a **10th-level Pilgrim**. Its spellcasting ability is **Wisdom** (spell save DC **15**, +**7** to hit with spell attacks). It has the following spells prepared from its scroll collection:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Focus_](/en/library/spells/focus), [_Sacred Flame_](/en/library/spells/sacred-flame), [_Thaumaturgy_](/en/library/spells/thaumaturgy)
- **1st level (4 slots)**: [_Bless_](/en/library/spells/bless), [_Healing Word_](/en/library/spells/healing-word), [_Shield of Faith_](/en/library/spells/shield-of-faith)
- **2nd level (3 slots)**: [_Aid_](/en/library/spells/aid), [_Spiritual Weapon_](/en/library/spells/spiritual-weapon), [_Warding Bond_](/en/library/spells/warding-bond)
- **3rd level (3 slots)**: [_Beacon of Hope_](/en/library/spells/beacon-of-hope), [_Mass Healing Word_](/en/library/spells/mass-healing-word), [_Motivational Speech_](/en/library/spells/motivational-speech)
- **4th level (3 slots)**: [_Aura of Life_](/en/library/spells/aura-of-life), [_Denial_](/en/library/spells/denial), [_Freedom of Movement_](/en/library/spells/freedom-of-movement)
- **5th level (2 slots)**: [_Dawn_](/en/library/spells/dawn), [_Greater Restoration_](/en/library/spells/greater-restoration)

## Reactions

#### Protective Lantern

When a creature the Scrollsman can see targets it with an attack, it flares its flame as a reaction. The attacker must succeed on a **DC 15 Constitution saving throw** or be **blinded** until the start of its next turn and take **8 (2 × CON mod) fire damage**.

The Scrollsman can use this reaction a number of times equal to its **tier bonus (3)**, regaining all uses after a **Recovery**.
