# Hound of Tindalos – Gaper

<BlendedImage
  src='/library/images/monsters/hound-of-tindalos-gaper.webp'

Where ordinary Hounds of Tindalos devour space, **Gapers rewrite it**. Their large, yawning torsos contain both flesh and prophecy — divine code etched into unknowable space.
They are the whispering agents of the Canvas, and the only prayer they offer is annihilation.

_Medium Celestial (Fold), Lawful Neutral_

- **Damage Immunities**: Psychic

- **Challenge**: 3 (700 XP)
- **Tier Bonus**: +1

---

## Traits

#### Gaping Maw of Revelation

Creatures that start their turn within 5 ft. must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or become **Charmed** until the end of their next turn. If already they are instead **Paralyzed** for 1 round.

#### Fold-sense

Can detect magical auras, spellcasting, and enchantments within 60 ft., even through walls.

#### Celestial Logic

When a creature fails a saving throw against one of the Gaper's spells, it cannot take reactions until the start of its next turn.

#### Hovering Stillness

If the Gaper does not move on its turn, it gains **+2 AC** and is immune to being Grappled or Restrained until the start of its next turn.

---

## Actions

#### Twin Mantra (At-Will)

The Gaper casts two cantrips per round: one [_Omen_](/en/library/spells/omen) (DC 12 Wis save) and one [_Judgement_](/en/library/spells/judgement) (DC 12 Dex save), targeting different creatures within 60 ft.

#### Fold Static (Recharge 5–6)

Unleashes fold energy in a 15 ft. cone. Each creature must make a DC 12 Constitution save or take [% 3d8 bludgeoning %] and fall prone. Half on success.

---

#### Spellcasting

The Gaper is a spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). It requires no components to cast its spells and has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Omen_](/en/library/spells/omen), [_Judgement_](/en/library/spells/judgement),
- **1st-level (3/day)**: [_Sanctuary_](/en/library/spells/sanctuary), [_Shield of Faith_](/en/library/spells/shield-of-faith), [_Command_](/en/library/spells/command), [_Arcane Sense_](/en/library/spells/arcane-sense)
- **2nd-level (2/day)**: [_Hold_](/en/library/spells/hold), [_Shatter_](/en/library/spells/shatter), [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step), [_Silence_](/en/library/spells/silence)
