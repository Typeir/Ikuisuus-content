Where ordinary Hounds of Tindalos devour space, **Gapers rewrite it**. Their large, yawning torsos contain both flesh and prophecy — divine code etched into unknowable space.
They are the whispering agents of the Canvas, and the only prayer they offer is annihilation.

---

## Traits

#### Gaping Maw of Revelation

Creatures that start their turn within 5 ft. must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or become **Charmed** until the end of their next turn. If already  they are instead **Paralyzed** for 1 round.

#### Fold-sense

Can detect magical auras, spellcasting, and enchantments within 60 ft., even through walls.

#### Celestial Logic

When a creature fails a saving throw against one of the Gaper's spells, it cannot take reactions until the start of its next turn.

#### Hovering Stillness

If the Gaper does not move on its turn, it gains **+2 AC** and is immune to being Grappled or Restrained until the start of its next turn.

---

## Actions

#### Twin Mantra (At-Will)

The Gaper casts two cantrips per round: one _Toll the Dead_ (DC 13 Wis save) and one _Sacred Flame_ (DC 13 Dex save), targeting different creatures within 60 ft.

#### Fold Static (Recharge 5–6)

Unleashes fold energy in a 15 ft. cone. Each creature must make a DC 13 Constitution save or take 3d8 thunder damage and fall prone. Half on success.

---

#### Spellcasting

The Gaper is a spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). It requires no components to cast its spells and has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: _Toll the Dead_, _Sacred Flame_, _Thaumaturgy_
- **1st-level (3/day)**: _Sanctuary_, _Shield of Faith_, _Command_, _Detect Magic_
- **2nd-level (2/day)**: _Hold Person_, _Shatter_, _Misty Step_, _Silence_
