## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Elite Platonian Hunter has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### End Of Life Cycle

When reduced to 0 hit points, the hunter erupts in a 300-foot-radius explosion (blocked by solid terrain).  
After a three turn delay, the hunter explodes.
Each creature within range must make a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **180 (40d8)** force damage and becoming **blinded** until the end of its next turn on a failure, or half as much damage on a success.

#### Multi-Purpose Tool

The Elite Platonian Hunter is proficient in all skills and has expertise in **Intimidation**.

#### Multi-Dimensional Movement Maneuvers

The Elite Platonian Hunter disengages automatically at the start of every turn, without using an action.

#### Innate Spellcasting

The Elite Platonian Hunter can cast each of the following spells once per day without expending spell points or components:

- [_Shield_](/en/library/spells/shield) — **2 spell points**
- [_Pass Without Trace_](/en/library/spells/pass-without-trace) — **2 spell points**
- [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step) — **3 spell points**
- [_Invisibility_](/en/library/spells/invisibility) — **3 spell points**
- [_Greater Invisibility_](/en/library/spells/greater-invisibility) — **5 spell points**

#### Spell Point Reserve

The Elite Platonian Hunter has **4 spell points**, which can be spent to cast any of its known spells again.

#### Clockwork Reactions

The hunter does not consume a reaction for opportunity attacks, but can only perform one per target per round

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Elite Platonian Hunter makes one attack with its main weapon and two attacks with its Fated Blade, or two attacks with any weapon, plus an attack with a Disambiguator, if it has one available.

- **Muon Blaster** _(Ranged Weapon, Two-Handed)_  
  _Ranged Weapon Attack_: +13 to hit, range 120/300 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 4d12 + 8 force damage

- **Fated Blade** _(Melee Weapon, Finesse, One-Handed)_  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 2d10 + 7 slashing damage  
  **Effect**: Once per day per creature, the target must succeed on a **DC 20 Charisma saving throw** or become **vulnerable to all damage** from this blade. The Hunter also gains **truesight** on that target for 1 minute. Additionally, the target loses all magic effects currently affecting it, such as _bless_ or _haste_.

- **Particle Renders** _(Melee Weapon, One-Handed)_  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 2d8 + 7 slashing damage  
  **Effect**: After a successful hit, the Hunter may make one additional free attack with this weapon. This extra attack does not stack.

- **Disambiguator** _(Melee Weapon, One-Handed, recharge 4-6)_
  _Melee Weapon Attack_: +15 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 2d6 + 9 slashing damage, 2d12 necrotic damage.
  The plationian hunter choses an effect from the following list after it hits:

- **Effect, the Toll**: The target must roll a DC 20 Constituion saving throw, or fail a death saving throw. Additionally, the target loses all magic effects currently affecting it, such as _bless_ or _haste_.
- **Effect, the Cave**: The target must roll a DC 18 charisma saving throw or be Stunned, Paralyzed, Put to Sleep and Petrified (Petrification takes two full rounds to take effect). The target cannot repeat this saving throw and can only be cured with curse removal.

The hunter heals half of the damage dealt with this attack.

- **Thruster Axe-Kick** (Gap Closer)
  _Special Melee Weapon Attack_: +12 to hit, range 10–30 ft., one target.  
  The Hunter launches forward on hydraulic thrusters, kicking with its bladed limb, it lands on an unoccupied space 5ft away from it's target.  
  **Hit**: 3d10 + 7 slashing damage  
  **Effect**: If the target is Large or smaller, it must make a **DC 18 Strength saving throw** or be knocked prone.  
  If the target takes damage from this attack, it [catches fire](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions).

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Elite Platonian Hunter can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Weapon Attack.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Hunter makes one attack with its Muon Blaster, Fated Blade, or Particle Renders.
- **Thruster Step.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Hunter moves up to 30 ft. without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Drone Deployment.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Hunter deploys two antimagic drones in unoccupied spaces within 30 ft. The drones act on the Hunter's initiative count +10.
