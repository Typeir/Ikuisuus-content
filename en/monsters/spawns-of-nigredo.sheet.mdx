# Spawn of the Black One – Servants of Nigredo

---

## Spawn of Hollow Joints

_Medium Construct (Undead), Chaotic Evil_

| **Armor Class**           | **Hit Points**  | **Speed**            |
| ------------------------- | --------------- | -------------------- |
| 17 (rusted armor plating) | 120 (16d8 + 48) | 30 ft., climb 30 ft. |

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA    |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------ |
| 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 16 (+3) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 6 (–2) |

- **Damage Resistances**: Cold, Necrotic
- **Damage Immunities**: Poison, Psychic
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified, Frightened, Paralyzed, Poisoned
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 15 ft., passive Perception 12
- **Languages**: Understands Hidden and Empyrean but does not speak
- **Challenge**: 9 (5,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Aura of Entropic Fear

Creatures that start their turn within 10 ft. of the Spawn must succeed on a **DC 18 Wisdom saving throw** or be **frightened** until the start of their next turn.

#### Mechanical Ruin

When this creature is reduced to 0 HP, it explodes in a spray of freezing oil and metal shards. Each creature within 10 ft. must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw** or take 14 (4d6) cold damage and 7 (2d6) piercing damage.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Spawn makes two attacks: one with its Crushing Fist and one with its Spinal Latch.

- **Crushing Fist.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 15 (2d10 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
- **Spinal Latch.** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, range 30 ft., one target. _Hit:_ Target is **grappled and restrained** (escape DC 14) by a wormy chain of bone and tendons.

## Spawn of Iron Hunger

_Medium Construct (Undead), Chaotic Evil_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points**   | **Speed** |
| --------------- | ---------------- | --------- |
| 18              | 105 (14d10 + 28) | 25 ft.    |

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT    | WIS     | CHA    |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------ | ------- | ------ |
| 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) | 15 (+2) | 6 (–2) | 12 (+1) | 5 (–3) |

- **Damage Resistances**: Necrotic, Cold, Fire
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified, Frightened, Exhaustion
- **Senses**: Blindsight 30 ft., passive Perception 11
- **Languages**: Understands Hidden and Empyrean but does not speak
- **Challenge**: 9 (5,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Gorge Pulse

When the Spawn kills a creature, it erupts in a necrotic belch. All creatures within 10 ft. must succeed on a **DC 17 Constitution saving throw** or take 22 (4d10) necrotic damage.

#### Corpse Magnetism

This creature has advantage on attacks against **prone or paralyzed** targets.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Spawn makes two Iron Maul attacks.

- **Iron Maul.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 19 (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.
- **Centipede Torrent (Recharge 5–6).** The Spawn vomits a stream of undead centipedes in a **30-foot cone**. Each creature in the area must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw** or take 21 (6d6) necrotic damage and be **paralyzed until the end of their next turn**. On a success, creatures take half damage and are not paralyzed.

## Spawn of Dead Air

_Medium Construct (Undead), Chaotic Evil_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points**  | **Speed**                  |
| --------------- | --------------- | -------------------------- |
| 16              | 100 (13d8 + 39) | 30 ft., fly 40 ft. (hover) |

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) | 20 (+5) | 10 (+0) |

- **Damage Resistances**: Cold, Necrotic, Thunder
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified, Paralyzed
- **Senses**: Darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 15
- **Languages**: Understands Hidden and Empyrean but does not speak
- **Challenge**: 9 (5,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Aura of Paralysis

At the start of its turn, creatures within 10 ft. must succeed on a **DC 17 Constitution saving throw** or be **paralyzed until the end of their next turn**.

#### Stillborn Flight

This creature floats unnaturally, its wings stitched together from wires and bone. It is always considered under the effect of _Feather Fall_.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Spawn makes two Still Touch attacks.

- **Still Touch.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 14 (3d6 + 4) cold damage and the target is **unable to speak or vocalize** until the end of their next turn.
- **Necrostatic Pulse (Recharge 5–6).** Emits a 60-foot cone of null energy. Creatures in the area must make a **DC 17 Wisdom saving throw** or fall **prone and frightened**. Undead instead **heal 17 (3d10) HP**.

| --------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 17              | 95 (11d8 + 44) | 30 ft.    |

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 12 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) |

- **Damage Resistances**: Cold, Necrotic
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15
- **Languages**: Understands Hidden and Empyrean but does not speak
- **Challenge**: 9 (5,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Aura of Despair

Creatures within 15 ft. have disadvantage on saving throws against **fear or paralysis** effects.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Spawn makes two Rusted Lance attacks.

- **Rusted Lance.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage. If the target is frightened or paralyzed, they take an additional 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

---

## Bonus Actions

#### Memento Mori

The Spawn unleashes a psionic scream. One creature of its choice within 60 ft. must make a **DC 16 Wisdom saving throw** or take 18 (4d8) psychic damage and be unable to cast spells until the end of its next turn.
