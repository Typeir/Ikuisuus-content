## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
Nigredo has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of their turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)
When Nigredo fails a saving throw, they can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Endless Form

Nigredo’s lower body is an endlessly long, winding mass extending as a 120-ft stretch of terrain. As a bonus action, they shifts up to 60 ft. of this terrain.
It counts a solid object, grants cover, the top of it is difficult terrain, and it is immune to damage.

#### Claimed Death

Nigredo grows every time they claim a death. Every 3 claimed deaths add 30 ft. to Endless Form and grant them one extra legendary deed per round (maximum 6).

#### Spore Cloud Aura

Creatures starting their turn within 15 ft. must succeed on a DC 22 CON save or take 4d8 necrotic damage and become diseased (disadvantage on saves, healing halved) for 1 minute.

#### Sunlight Vulnerability

In direct sunlight, Nigredo has disadvantage on attacks and saving throws. If reduced to 0 HP in direct sunlight, Nigredo is instantly destroyed, disintegrating into rust and rot.

#### Ventralis Rot

Nigredo saturates the air with **Stachybotrys Ventralis** spores.
If a creature interacts with Nigredo in any way (touching, **starting its turn within 30 ft.** of them, or being struck by them), it must succeed on a **DC 22 Constitution saving throw** or roll in the [_Black Rot_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/black-rot) table
Creatures **immune to disease and curses** ignore this feature and instead take **2d6 necrotic damage**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Nigredo makes three **Impale Drive** attacks, or two plus **Flyburst** or **Exhale the Rot**.

- **Impale Drive**
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 15 ft.
  Hit: 4d10 + 8 piercing plus 4d8 necrotic damage. Target makes a DC 22 STR save or is impaled, grappled, and dragged 15 ft. If already impaled, the target has vulnerability to necrotic damage until Nigredo’s next turn.

- **Flyburst (Recharge 4–6)**
  Nigredo releases swarms of necrotic flies from every segment of their Endless Form.  
  All creatures within 15 ft. of any part of Nigredo's body must succeed on a DC 22 CON save or take **10d8 necrotic damage** and become **blinded** until the start of their next turn. On success, creatures take half damage and avoid blindness.

- **Exhale the Rot (Recharge 5–6)**
  Nigredo exhales spores in a 90-ft. cone. Creatures make a DC 22 CON save or take 12d8 necrotic damage and become rotted (disadvantage on CON saves, healing halved) for 1 minute, half as much damage on a save.

#### Ashfall of Empires (1/day)

Rotting spores fall in a 120-ft radius. Creatures make three DC 22 DEX saves with escalating effects depending on their current Black Rot failed saves:

- 0 failed saves: 8d10 necrotic
- 1 failed save: 10d10 necrotic
- 2 failed saves: 12d10 necrotic, blinded (1 minute)
- 3 failed saves: 14d10 necrotic, stunned until the start of Nigredo's next turn, blinded (1 hour)

## Legendary Deed: Act

Nigredo can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Drill Reposition.** (Costs 1 Deed) Move freely through Endless Form terrain, reappearing anywhere along its length.
- **Crush with Mass.** (Costs 1 Deed) Creature touching Endless Form makes a DC 22 DEX save or takes 6d10 bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone.
- **Rotwarp Pulse.** (Costs 2 Deeds) Teleport one creature 30 ft. along Endless Form, DC 22 CON save or take 6d10 necrotic damage and become diseased.
- **Cast Spell.** (Costs 2 Deeds) Cast a spell of 4th level or lower.
