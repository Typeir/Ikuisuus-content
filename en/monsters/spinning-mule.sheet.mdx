# Spinning Mule

_Medium Humanoid (Acheron), Unaligned_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| ---------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 23 (Reinforced Wheel Harness) | 78 (12d10 + 12) | 60 ft., climb 30 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 6 (-2) | 10 (+0) | 5 (-3) |

- **Saving Throws**: Strength +9, Constitution +6  
- **Damage Resistances**: bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks  
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified  
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10  
- **Languages**: Common  
- **Challenge**: 7 (2,900 XP)  
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Charge-Only Attacker
The Spinning Mule can only make attacks as part of a **Charge**.  
It can make **only one attack per turn**.

#### Charging Accuracy
The Spinning Mule gains a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls** made as part of a Charge for every **10 feet** moved during that Charge.

#### Moving Target
The Spinning Mule gains a **+1 bonus to AC and saving throws** until the start of its next turn for every **10 feet** moved during a Charge.

#### Rolling Frame
After the Spinning Mule has moved at least **20 feet** in a straight line, it does not provoke opportunity attacks **this round**.

#### Turning and Braking
The Spinning Mule uses **vehicle turning and braking rules**, with a maneuverability score of **10**.

#### Bound Survivor
The Spinning Mule has disadvantage on saving throws against **stun** and **paralysis**.

---

## Actions

#### Charge
The Spinning Mule moves in a straight line up to its speed.  
If it moves at least **20 feet** and ends its movement adjacent to a creature, it may make a **Ram** attack.

The Spinning Mule may Dash before or during this movement.

---

#### Ram  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  

_Hit:_ 1d4 + 6 slashing damage, plus **Charge Damage**.

For every **5 feet** the Spinning Mule moved this turn, the Ram deals **+1d4 slashing damage**.

On hit, the target must make a **Constitution saving throw** with a base **DC 14**, increased by **+1 for every 10 feet** the Spinning Mule moved this turn; on a failure, the target gains **1 Bleed stack per 10 feet** moved, and on a success gains none.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Slain
When the Spinning Mule is **slain**, it ruptures outward in a spray of spikes; all creatures within **5 feet** must make a **DC 14 Constitution saving throw**, taking **1d4 piercing damage** and gaining **1d4 Bleed stacks** on a failed save, or half damage and no Bleed stacks on a success.
