## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The War Counselor has **2 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

### Kratteous Core

The War Counselor’s Constitution score is **25**.

If The War Counselor would be reduced to 0 hit points, he instead drops to **1 hit point**, gains **1 level of exhaustion** (12-level exhaustion track), and may immediately take **one action**.  
This effect can occur once per encounter.

### Divine Bulwark

While The War Counselor is within **60 feet of [_Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-goddess-yskeia)**, [_Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-goddess-yskeia) gains a **+2 bonus to all saving throws**, and the first time each round she would fail a saving throw, she instead succeeds.

### Martyr of Command

When The War Counselor is reduced to 0 hit points, [_Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-goddess-yskeia) immediately regains **200 hit points**, and each creature of [_Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-goddess-yskeia)'s choice within 120 feet must succeed on a **DC 30 Wisdom saving throw** or become **terrified** of her for 1 minute.

### War Priest Techniques

Whenever The War Counselor uses his action to cast a spell, he gains advantage on weapon attack rolls until the end of his next turn.

### Multiattack

The War Counselor makes **two** melee attacks.

- **Blessings of Yskeia**
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +21 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 38 (4d12 + 12) slashing damage plus 22 (5d8) radiant damage.

- **Shield Shotguns**
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +21 to hit, reach 10 ft., all creatures in a **15-foot cone** originating from The War Counselor’s shield.  
  _Hit:_ 33 (6d10) piercing damage.
  Each creature hit must succeed on a **DC 25 Strength saving throw** or be **pushed 20 feet away** from The War Counselor and knocked **prone**.
  The War Counselor can choose to travel 20 feet backwards as part of this attack, using the backblast.

## Bonus Actions

### Shoulder-Mounted Grenade Launcher (Recharge 6)

The War Counselor fires a shoulder-mounted grenade in a high arc.

This effect functions as the spell [_Fireball_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:fireball), cast at **6th level** (DC **27** Dexterity saving throw).

- Creatures in a **20-foot-radius sphere** take **11d6 fire damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

### Divine Blunderbuss

As a bonus action, The War Counselor manifests a divine blunderbuss. This functions as _Spiritual Weapon_ with the following changes:

- The blunderbuss makes ranged spell attacks using The War Counselor’s **Wisdom**.
- _Ranged Spell Attack:_ +21 to hit, range 60 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 22 (4d10) force damage.
- The blunderbuss has the **Loading** property and can fire **only every other round**.
- The blunderbuss does not require concentration.

### Channel Divinity (2/Day): Guided Strike

When The War Counselor makes an attack roll, he can add **+10** to the roll after seeing the result.

---

## Checkmate (1/Short Rest)

At the start of his turn (No action required), The War Counselor executes a perfectly drilled killing sequence.

The War Counselor immediately gains an action and gains the following benefits until the end of his turn:

- Each time he takes the **Attack** action, he makes **one additional weapon attack**.
- He can make **one Divine Blunderbuss attack** this turn, ignoring its Loading property.
- Opportunity attacks against The War Counselor are made with disadvantage.

---

## Reactions

### Shield of Counsel

When a creature within 30 feet is hit by an attack The War Counselor can see, The War Counselor can use his reaction to interpose himself.  
The attack instead targets The War Counselor, and The War Counselor gains resistance to all damage from that attack.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

The War Counselor can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Cast a Spell** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  The War Counselor casts a spell of 9th level or lower that he has prepared, using a spell slot as normal.

- **Advance the Line.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  The War Counselor moves up to half his speed and makes one melee attack.

- **Stand Firm (Costs 2 Deeds).**  
  Until the start of his next turn, The War Counselor and all allies within 30 feet cannot be moved against their will or knocked prone.
