| ------------------ | -------------- | --------- |
| 13 (chitinous shell) | 68 (8d8 + 32)  | 40 ft.    |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) | 18 (+4) | 2 (−4)  | 10 (+0) | 4 (−3)  |

- **Skills**: Stealth +5, Athletics +9
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 30 ft., Passive Perception 10
- **Languages**: —
- **Challenge**: 5 (1,800 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +3  

---

## Traits

#### Skittering Menace

The centipede crab can climb on vertical surfaces and ceilings without needing ability checks and without spending extra movement, and has a burrowing speed equal to its normal speed.

#### Ambusher

In the first round of combat, the crab has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn yet.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The centipede crab makes two Claw attacks.

- **Claw.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 14). Until the grapple ends, the crab can't use that claw on another target.
- **Mandible Constriction.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature grappled by the crab. _Hit:_ 13 (3d6 + 3) piercing damage. The grappled creature is also **restrained and silenced** until the grapple ends. A restrained and silenced creature cannot perform somatic or verbal components of spells. The target can attempt to escape this effect by succeeding on a Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check against DC 14, ending the grapple, restraint, and silence.

---

## Reactions

#### Skitter Away

When a creature misses the crab with a melee attack, the crab may move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
