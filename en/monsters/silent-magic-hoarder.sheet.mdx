The Silent Magic Hoarder carries a hoard of unstable magical rings.

At the start of each of the Silent Magic Hoarder’s turns, it activates **More Than One Ring**.

All spells are cast using the Silent Magic Hoarder’s spell save DC and spell attack bonus.  
These effects **do not require concentration**.

The Silent Magic Hoarder casts the spells at a target, point, or area it can see within the spell’s range.

#### Innate Spellcasting (Psionics)

**Spell Save DC**: 16  
The Hoarder can innately cast the following spells, requiring no components:

- **At will**: _Detect Magic_, _Detect Thoughts_, _Mage Hand_, _Minor Illusion_
- **3/day each**: _Blur_, _Mirror Image_, _Misty Step_, _Suggestion_, _Silence_
- **1/day each**: _Confusion_, _Dimension Door_, _Polymorph_, _Synaptic Static_

#### Silent Presence

The Hoarder produces no natural sound, and its voice is heard only as faint murmurs.  
Creatures within **30 feet** have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing, and spellcasters within this range must succeed on a **DC 16 Concentration check** to cast a spell with a verbal component.

#### All-Around Eyes

The Hoarder has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks and cannot be surprised.

#### Reality Saturation

The Hoarder has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.  
Whenever it succeeds on such a saving throw, it regains **10 hit points**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Hoarder makes **three Slam attacks**, or it uses **Hurl Relic** once and makes two Slam attacks.

- **Slam.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach **10 ft.**, one target.  
  _Hit:_ 12 (2d4 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

#### Hurl Relic.

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, range **60/180 ft.**, one target.  
_Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage.  
On a hit, roll once on **More Than One Ring** and apply the effect centered on the target.

#### Disruptive Pulse (Recharge 5–6).

Each creature of The Hoarder’s choice within **30 feet** must make a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw**, taking **27 (6d8) psychic damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
Creatures that fail are also **stunned until the end of their next turn**.

## Reactions

#### Phase Flicker

When The Hoarder is hit by an attack, it can teleport up to **30 feet** to an unoccupied space it can see.

---

## Legendary Deeds

The Hoarder has **2 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (1/Day)

When The Hoarder fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Hoarder can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn.

- **More Than One Ring.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Hoarder activates **More Than One Ring**.
- **Slither Away**. (Costs 2 Deeds) The Hoarder teleports up to **60 feet** to an unoccupied space it can see.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Doomed

The Hoarder summons [Bayle the Great](/en/library/monsters/abominable-avian), a giant abominable duck, to fight by his side.

#### Slain

When The Hoarder is **slain**, it activates **More Than One Ring** three times, resolving each roll in sequence, centered on itself.
