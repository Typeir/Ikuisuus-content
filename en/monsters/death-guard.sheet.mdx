## Traits

### Specialized for Death

A Death guard chooses a specialization when initiative is rolled, decided by the DM. 
The specialization determines certain traits, actions, and spellcasting abilities of the death guard (see [_Specializations_](/en/library/monsters/death-guard#specializations)).

### Execution

When the death guard hits a creature with a weapon attack using a **two-handed melee weapon**, the attack deals **18 additional damage** of the weapon’s damage type.  
This extra damage is doubled on a critical hit.  
If the target is reduced to **18 hit points or fewer** after this damage is applied, the death guard may choose to kill the creature instantly.
This can only be used **once per round**.

---

### Death’s Retinue

As an action, the death guard animates corpses to create one or more **Death Guard Retinues** (see **Death Guard Retinue**).  
A death guard can have only the number of retinues listed in its **Specialization**. If it would create more, the excess retinues collapse immediately.

---

### Greater Abundance and Decay

As an action, a death guard creates **Greater Abundance and Decay**, a **25-foot-radius** area centered on a point it can see within **120 feet**. The area lasts **1 minute**. Only one Greater Abundance and Decay can exist at a time.

All **death guards** treat the area as **their** Abundance and Decay. A death guard can move the area even if a different death guard created it.

- Creatures other than death guards and their retinues inside the area have their **Armor Class reduced by 5**.
- The area is **difficult terrain** for enemies.
- When a creature that is not immune to the effects of the area moves within it, that creature gains a stack of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding) for every 5 feet it travels, or takes **1d4 piercing damage** per 5 feet if it is immune to [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding).
- Once per turn, when a death guard or one of its retinues deals damage to a creature inside the area with a melee attack or a spell, **that death guard** regains **5 hit points**.
- As a **bonus action**, a death guard can move the area up to **20 feet** along the ground.
- The area can only be moved once per round.

---

### Deathless Will

When the death guard is reduced to **0 hit points**, it does not fall unconscious or become destroyed. Instead, it enters a **Deathless State** until the end of its next turn.

While in this state:

- The death guard continues to act normally.
- The death guard ignores effects that would destroy it for having 0 hit points.

At the end of its next turn, the death guard is destroyed immediately.

## Actions

### Greatblade
_Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit; reach or range varies by specialization; one target.  
**Hit:** damage varies by specialization (see below).

- **Death Guard of Blood (Greataxe).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_; reach 5 ft.  
  **Hit:** 17 (1d12 + 11) slashing damage.

- **Death Guard of Rime (Greatsword).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_; reach 10 ft.  
  **Hit:** 15 (2d6 + 8) slashing damage.  
  On a hit, the target is pushed 5 ft. and its speed is reduced by 5 ft. until the end of its next turn.  
  On a miss, the target still takes 6 slashing damage.

- **Death Guard of Graves (Glaive).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_; reach 10 ft.  
  **Hit:** 15 (1d10 + 10) slashing damage.

- **Death Guard of Mists (Greatbow).**  
  _Ranged Weapon Attack_; range 150/600 ft.  
  **Hit:** 15 (2d6 + 8) piercing damage.  
  **Execution** applies to this attack.

## Specializations

### Death Guard of Blood

- **Retinues**: 1

**Blood Ward (Reaction).**  
When the death guard is hit by an attack, it increases its Armor Class against that attack by **1d12**. If this causes the attack to miss, the attack deals no damage. The death guard can use this reaction **18 times per long rest**.

**Heartburn (5/Long Rest).**  
One creature the death guard can see within **30 feet** must succeed on a **DC 18 Constitution saving throw** or suffer one of the following effects, chosen by the death guard:

- **Blood Sublimation**: The creature takes **2d8 + 4 fire damage**, has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks until the end of the death guard’s next turn, and gains **1 stack of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding)**.
- **Blood Deposition**: The creature takes **4 cold damage**, its speed is halved, it can make only one attack on its turn, and if it casts a spell before the end of the death guard’s next turn it must succeed on a concentration check or the spell fails. The creature gains **1 stack of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding)**.

**Bloodspire.**  
When the death guard makes an attack against a creature inside **Greater Abundance and Decay**, it may replace one weapon attack with a melee spell attack (**+11 to hit**, reach 5 ft.). On a hit, the target takes **4d8 magical piercing damage** and must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or be **grappled** (escape DC 18).

**Heartbreak (2 Uses; 1/Short Rest).**  
When the death guard hits a creature grappled by Bloodspire, it detonates the spire. The target must make a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw**. On a failure, the target takes **8d6 magical piercing damage** and gains **5 + 1d4 stacks of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding)**. On a success, the target takes half damage and gains **1 stack of [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/conditions#bleeding)**.

#### Bloodboil Spellcasting

The death guard is an **9th-level spellcaster**. Intelligence is its spellcasting ability (spell save DC **18**, spell attack **+10**). It has the following spell slots: **1st (4), 2nd (3), 3rd (3), 4th (3), 5th (1)**.

It has the following spells prepared:

- **1st level**: [_Death Grips_](/en/library/spells/death-grips), [_Detect Magic_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:detect-magic), [_Fog Cloud_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:fog-cloud), [_False Life_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:false-life)

### Death Guard of Rime

- **Retinues**: 1
- **Damage Resistances**: Cold

**Rimeforged Weapon.**  
The death guard’s melee weapon attacks have **+5 feet reach**. On a hit, the target is pushed **5 feet** and its speed is reduced by **5 feet** until the end of its next turn. If the attack misses, it still deals **6 slashing damage**. The second melee attack the death guard makes on its turn deals **cold damage**.

**Avalanche Blade.**  
The death guard can summon an **Avalanche Blade** as a **bonus action**.
The blade is a gargantuan ice construct that automatically attacks creatures that enter its space once per turn.

> #### Avalanche Blade
>
> | **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Damage Threshold** |
> | --------------- | -------------- | -------------------- |
> | 14              | 56             | 15                   |
>
> - **Size**: Gargantuan object (20 ft. section)
> - **Material**: Ice
> - **Resistances**: **Slashing, Piercing**
> - **Vulnerabilities**: **Force**
> - **Immunities**: **Poison**, **Psychic**, **Necrotic**, **Radiant**, **Cold**
>
> - **Reactive Aura**: The Avalanche can take one reaction per turn.
>
> - **Reactions — Cold Cleave.**  
>   _Melee Weapon Attack:_ 14, reach 5 ft.; one target.  
>   **Hit:** 8d6 + 6. Target's speed is reduced to 0 ft. until the end of their next turn.

The death guard can summon only one Avalanche Blade at a time and can use this feature a number of times equal to its Strength modifier per long rest.

**Flash Freeze.**  
The death guard ignores resistance to cold damage. Once per round, when it deals cold damage to a creature, it deals an additional **18 cold damage** to that creature if it has not already used Execution that round.

**Dance of Mana.**  
Once per turn, when the death guard deals cold damage to a creature, **Avalanche Blade** moves up to **30 feet** toward that creature. Each creature the blade passes through must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or take damage equal to one roll of the blade’s Cold Cleave.

### Death Guard of Graves

- **Retinues**: 3 (all retinues share a single hit point pool)

**Death Coil.**  
Once per round, when the death guard uses Execution or casts a spell, it may have that attack or spell originate from the space of one of its retinues within **30 feet**.

**Explode Corpse.**  
As an action, the death guard detonates a corpse or one of its retinues within **10 feet** of it or another retinue. Each creature within **5 feet** must make a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **18 necrotic damage** on a failure or half damage on a success. The detonated corpse or retinue is destroyed.

**Kalmora’s Grasp (Recharge 6).**  
As an action, one creature within **30 feet** of the death guard or one of its retinues must succeed on a **DC 18 Charisma saving throw**. On a failure, the creature is banished for **1 minute**, is incapacitated, automatically fails Strength and Dexterity saving throws, and takes **5d12 necrotic damage** at the start of each of its turns. The creature repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. On a success, the creature takes half damage and is not banished.

### Death Guard of Mists

- **Retinues**: 1

**Distant Execution.**  
Execution applies to two-handed ranged weapon attacks. The attack deals **2 additional damage** against a target with half cover, or **5 additional damage** against a target with three-quarters cover.

**Ghastly Aim.**  
For the purpose of targeting, total cover is treated as three-quarters cover unless the obstacle is exceptionally thick. The death guard uses its Intelligence modifier for Dexterity (Stealth) checks and does not suffer disadvantage on Stealth checks due to armor. While its retinue is within **5 feet**, the death guard has half cover.

**Twinned Death (Recharge 5-6).**  
The guard of the mists can create a second area called **Länsihenki’s Shroud** as an action. Instead of reducing Armor Class, it is heavily obscured for all creatures except death guards and their retinues.
The death guard can move the area up to **20 feet** as a bonus action on its turn.

- **Vortex Swap**: The death guard swaps all willing creatures between the two areas. Unwilling creatures must succeed on a **DC 19 Charisma saving throw** to resist the swap.
- **Collision Event**: The death guard causes Länsihenki’s Shroud to collide with Greater Abundance and Decay.
  Each creature of their choice within abundance and decay must make a **DC 19 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **12d10** necrotic damage, and being **stunned** until the end of their next turn on a failure.
  Creatures that succeed take half damage and are not stunned.
