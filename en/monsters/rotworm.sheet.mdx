### Traits

##### Rot-Fed Husk

The rotworm does not require air, food, drink, or sleep.

##### Necrotic Instability

When the rotworm is reduced to **0 hit points**, it ruptures violently.  
One creature **of its choice** within **5 feet** of it regains **7 (2d6)** hit points.
