# Mycelial Marauder

_Large Aberration, Neutral Evil_

<BlendedImage
  src='/library/images/monsters/mycelial-marauder.webp'
  alt='Mycelial Marauder'
/>

A shambling column of writhing black mycelial strands, the Mycelial Marauder is a hunter-spore of [Nigredo](/en/library/monsters/nigredo), Lord of Flies and Rot. Born from corpse-piles saturated with _Stachybotrys Ventralis_, these aberrations hunt in bonded pairs — one flushes prey while the other ensnares. They do not kill quickly. They drink physical vigor through their fungal threads, savoring the slow collapse of strength until the victim cannot lift their own arms. Only then do they drag the husk back to fertilize the spore-bed.

These creatures fear open sky. They lair in rot-choked caves, collapsed Empyrean undercrofts, and the corpse-forests of [Kalmora](/en/library/world/the-great-tale-of-everything). Where two are found, a third is seldom far — they bud asexually, and a mated pair produces a sporeling within a fortnight of claiming fresh territory.

## Traits

#### Paired Hunters

While the marauder is within **30 feet** of another conscious Mycelial Marauder:

- It has **advantage on attack rolls** against creatures it is grappling.
- The **escape DC** of its grapples increases by **2**.

#### Siphon Vigor

A creature **grappled** by the marauder at the start of the marauder's turn must succeed on a **DC 14 Constitution saving throw** or gain **1 level of Withering**. Each Withering level imposes a cumulative **−2 penalty to Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution ability checks and saving throws**. If the total penalty reaches **−6**, the creature is **paralyzed** until the grapple ends.

All Withering levels end when the creature finishes a **short or long rest**. _Lesser Restoration_ or similar magic also removes them.

#### Sunlight Sensitivity

While in **direct sunlight**, the marauder has **disadvantage on attack rolls** and **Wisdom (Perception) checks** that rely on sight.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The marauder makes two **Mycelial Lash** attacks, or one **Mycelial Lash** and one **Constrict**.

- **Mycelial Lash.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 13 ([% 2d8 +4 slashing %]) plus 9 ([% 2d8 dark %]).  
  If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is **grappled** (escape DC 15). The marauder can grapple up to **two** creatures at a time — one per lash. A lash grappling a creature cannot be used to attack other targets.

- **Constrict.**  
  One creature **grappled** by the marauder.  
  The marauder tightens its fungal strands. The target takes **22 ([% 4d8 +4 bludgeoning %]) damage**, and the marauder immediately triggers **Siphon Vigor** against that creature (no action required).

## Reactions

#### Death Burst

When the marauder is reduced to **0 hit points**, its body ruptures in a final release of spores. Each creature within **10 feet** of it must succeed on a **DC 14 Constitution saving throw** or immediately roll once on the [_Black Rot_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/curses/black-rot) table. Any creature **grappled** by the marauder when it dies automatically fails this save.
