- **Saving Throws**: Strength +15, Constitution +14, Wisdom +11, Charisma +14
- **Skills**: Athletics +15, Intimidation +14, Religion +12, Perception +11

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
Heart of the Brume has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

#### Entropic Sarcophagus

Heart of the Brume is encased in a sealed tombsteel shell.

- Heart of the Brume has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks
- Heart of the Brume has advantage on saving throws made to resist being pushed, pulled, or knocked prone
- Heart of the Brume cannot benefit from bonuses to movement speed granted by nonmagical effects

#### Grave Chill

Heart of the Brume’s tombsteel armor holds up to 12 charges.  
When a creature hits Heart of the Brume with a melee weapon attack, the attacker immediately takes 15 cold damage as frozen rime shards erupt from the armor. This effect requires no action or reaction.

- Each activation consumes 1 charge
- Charges regenerate at midnight, recovering 6 + 1d6 charges
- This cold damage ignores resistance from nonmagical or mundane sources

#### Ferocious Rider

The Heart of the Brume's mount, a [_Lunar Manticore_](/en/library/monsters/lunar-manticore), can take the attack action even when used as a mount by Heart of the Brume.
While mounted, the Heart of the Brume's mount gains a +7 to attack and damage rolls, and resistance to all damage.

While mounted, Heart of the Brume gains the following benefits:

- Cannot be critically hit
- Automatically succeeds on the first saving throw it makes each round

#### Doctrine of the Brume

Heart of the Brume projects a disciplined doctrinal aura in a 20-foot radius while conscious.

For each other rider of the Brume within range, all riders gain the following benefits (bonuses stack):

- +3 bonus to all saving throws
- +2 bonus to Armor Class

**Formation Thresholds**

- Three active auras:

  - Resistance to all damage
  - Immunity to forced movement
  - Cannot be knocked prone
  - Riders and Mounts automatically Disengage at the start of their turns.

- Two active auras:

  - Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing, fire, cold, lightning, and necrotic damage
  - Advantage on saving throws against being pushed, pulled, or knocked prone

- One or fewer active auras:
  - No resistances or movement immunities from this feature

A rider’s aura is suppressed only while that rider is incapacitated.  
Paralyzed, stunned, or unconscious creatures do not contribute to the doctrine.

#### Central Dogma

At the start of each of Heart of the Brume’s turns, each allied rider of the Brume within Doctrine range regains 30 hit points.

- This effect does not stack with itself
- This is the only passive healing effect available to the trio

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Heart of the Brume makes two attacks with its Grand Empyrean Sword-Spear, replacing any with their other weapons if available.

---

- **Heavenpiercing Sword-Spear**  
  Melee Weapon Attack: +17 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  Hit: 21 (2d10 + 10) slashing damage plus 22 (5d8) radiant damage.  
  This weapon is Heavy, Martial, and forged from tombsteel. It is treated as magical for overcoming resistance.

- **Breathstealer** (recharge 4-6)
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 7 (1d4 + 10) piercing damage. The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or become **Paralyzed until the end of their next turn**.

- **Silencer** (Recharge 5–6)
  The Heart fires their rifle in a **15-foot cone**. Each creature in the cone must make a **DC 25 Dexterity saving throw**:
- **Failure**: 70 (10d10) + 10 piercing damage and knocked prone
- **Success**: Half damage, no knockdown

- **Hand of Judgment** (Recharge 6)
  _Ranged Spell Attack:_ +12 to hit, range 60 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 80 (10d10 + 25) thunder damage.
  The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or be **stunned for 1 minute**. 
  The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
  The Heart must concentrate on the stun.

## Legendary Deed: Act

Heart of the Brume can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Coordinated Strike.** (Costs 1 Deed)
  Heart of the Brume makes one melee weapon attack with its Grand Empyrean Sword-Spear.

- **Relentless Advance.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Heart of the Brume moves up to **20 feet** without provoking opportunity attacks.  
  This movement cannot benefit from bonuses to speed.

- **Structural Reinforcement (Costs 2 Deeds).**  
  Heart of the Brume or one allied rider of the Brume within **20 feet** gains **20 temporary hit points** and advantage on the next saving throw it makes before the end of its next turn.

- **Mount.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Heart of the Brume mounts its creature if it is within 5 feet and willing.

- **Dismount.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Heart of the Brume dismounts its creature and may move up to **10 feet** immediately after.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Empire Breaker

The Heart of the Brume slams its sword-spear into the ground, creating a shockwave that ripples through the battlefield.
All creatures within a 50-foot radius centered on Heart of the Brume must make a DC 22 Strength saving throw.
On a failure, a creature takes 27 (6d8) bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone.
On a success, the creature takes half damage and is not knocked prone.
On a success, the creature takes half damage and is not pushed.
At the start of its next turn, Heart of the Brume pulls the spear out of the ground, creating a secondary shockwave.
All creatures within a 50-foot radius centered on Heart of the Brume must make a DC 22 Strength saving throw.
On a failure, a creature takes 27 (6d8) bludgeoning damage and is swallowed by the earth, becoming incapacitated until freed.
In order to free itself, the creature must use its action to make a DC 35 Athletics check, or another creature must do it for them.
While incapacitated this way, the creatures is [_Suffocating_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#suffocating) and takes 27 (6d8) bludgeoning damage at the start of each of its turns.
On a success, the creature takes half damage and is not swallowed.
Prone creatures automatically fail the saving throw against being swallowed.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP)

While Heart of the Brume is bloodied and not incapacitated:

- Central Dogma restores 50 hit points instead of 30
- Heart of the Brume gains advantage on Constitution saving throws

If Heart of the Brume becomes incapacitated, these benefits end immediately.
