# Steel Guard of the Bloodmoon

_Large Monstrosity, Lawful Evil_

| **Armor Class**           | **Hit Points** | **Speed**          |
| ------------------------- | -------------- | ------------------ |
| **35 (back)**, 17 (front) | 150 (12d10+72) | 25 ft. (swim/walk) |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 22 (+6) | 12 (+1) | 22 (+6) | 8 (-1)  | 12 (+1) | 9 (-1)  |

- **Saving Throws**: STR +10, CON +10
- **Damage Resistances**: bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
- **Condition Immunities**: frightened
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
- **Languages**: Empyrean
- **Challenge**: 10 (5,900 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Iron Carapace

When attacked from behind (DM’s discretion, flanking, or any attack that would target its back), the Steel Guard’s AC becomes **35**.
#### Superiority Dice

The Steel Guard has **5 superiority dice (d10s)**. It regains all expended dice when it rolls initiative.

#### Martial Maneuvers

The Steel Guard knows the following maneuvers:

- **Grappling Strike.** After it hits a creature with a melee attack, it can expend one superiority die and attempt to grapple as a bonus action, adding the die to the Athletics check.
- **Lunging Attack.** It can expend one superiority die to extend its reach by 5 ft. On hit, add the die to the damage roll.
- **Trip Attack.** When it hits with a melee attack, it can expend one superiority die to force a DC 18 Strength saving throw. On failure, the target is knocked prone and takes the superiority die’s damage.
- **Brace.** When a creature enters its reach, it can spend a superiority die to make one weapon attack against that creature as a reaction. On a hit, add the superiority die to damage.

#### Crushing Frame

The Steel Guard counts as one size larger for grappling and shoving.

#### Siege Weaponry

Its weapons deal double damage to objects and structures.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Steel Guard makes two attacks with its weapon.

- **Giant Stake.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 20 (4d6 + 6) piercing damage. Creatures hit by the stake must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be **pinned** to a surface and knocked prone (the Steel Guard can only have one pinned creature at a time). A pinned creature can use its action to make a DC 16 Strength check, freeing itself on a success. The Steel Guard can use its bonus action to free the pinned creature, reclaiming its stake in the process.
- **Pummel.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 14 (3d4 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

#### Giant Hammer

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 32 (4d12 + 6) bludgeoning damage. When using the hammer, the Steel Guard cannot Multiattack. The hammer's attacks are cleaving arcs, dealing half damage to all creatures within 5 ft. of the target. All creatures that take damage from the attack are pushed 10 ft. away from the Steel Guard.

---

## Reactions

#### Shielded Stance

When hit by an attack from the front, the Steel Guard may roll a d10. On an 8–10, it reduces the damage by the number rolled + 6.

---

## Legendary Deed (Shared with Ludwig)

If Ludwig is present, he may spend **1 legendary deed** at the end of another creature's turn to command one Steel Guard to make a single weapon attack or move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
