# Monsters

This bestiary contains information about the creatures from the _Vile and Beautiful lands of Damocles_. 
All manners of beasts, from the lowliest vermin to the mightiest monstrosities, can be found within.

A monster's combat rating determines the expected level of a party of four adventurers to be able to take it down without casualties.
This value assumes the characters are archetypal, unoptimized and carry no magical items.
As such, well equipped or specialized parties will often punch well above their weight, even up to double their expected combat rating.
