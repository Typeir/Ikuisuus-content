# Scaffoldsman Ribboner

_Medium Humanoid (Acheron), Lawful Neutral_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| ---------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 18 (Chain Harness) | 78 (12d8 + 24) | 35 ft., climb 35 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 8 (-1) |

- **Saving Throws**: Dexterity +8, Strength +7  
- **Skills**: Acrobatics +8, Athletics +7  
- **Damage Resistances**: bludgeoning damage from nonmagical attacks  
- **Condition Immunities**: Frightened  
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12  
- **Languages**: Common  
- **Challenge**: 7 (2,900 XP)  
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Chain Movement
The Ribboner ignores difficult terrain while climbing, balancing, or moving along chains, cables, scaffolds, ledges, or corpse-chains.  
Vertical movement does **not** cost extra movement.

#### Mutual Arrest (Reaction)
When a creature within **20 feet** would fall, the Ribboner fires a grappling hook, negating the fall and pulling the creature up to **20 feet** in any direction.  
This can target allies or enemies.

#### Worksite Control
While the Ribboner has a creature **grappled**, that creature has disadvantage on saving throws made to resist being **moved**, **pulled**, or **knocked prone**.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack
The Ribboner makes **two attacks**, choosing from **Throwing Dagger**, **Stake**, **Hammer**, or **Hook Shot**.

---

#### Throwing Dagger  
_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 9 (1d10 + 4) piercing damage.

If the target is within **10 feet of an edge or drop**, the Ribboner may push the target **5 feet**.

---

#### Stake  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage.

If the target is adjacent to a wall, chain, corpse-chain, or vertical surface, it must succeed on a **DC 16 Strength saving throw** or be **grappled** (**escape DC 16**).

- This grapple **lasts until the end of the target’s next turn**, until the target escapes, or until the Ribboner releases the stake (no action required).
- While grappled this way, the target’s **speed is 0** and it cannot move away from the surface it is staked to.

---

#### Hammer  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 11 (2d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage.

If the target is **grappled**, it must succeed on a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** or be **stunned until the end of its next turn**.

---

#### Hook Shot  
_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, range 40/80 ft., one target or surface  
_Hit:_ 9 (1d10 + 4) piercing damage.

On hit, the Ribboner chooses one:

- Pull itself to the target  
- Pull the target up to **20 feet**  
- Move the target to any adjacent **5ft** space.

---

#### Tie
One creature **grappled** by the Ribboner must succeed on a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw** or be **restrained until the end of its next turn**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Slain
When the Ribboner is **slain**, it immediately performs the following before collapsing:

- Uses **Hook Shot** on a creature or surface within **40 feet**
- Pulls itself and **one grappled creature** up to **20 feet**
- Immediately releases the hook
