# Delphytion Insolam

_Huge Monstrosity, Unaligned_

<BlendedImage
  src='/library/images/monsters/delphytion.webp'
  alt='Delphytion Insolam'
/>

A **Delphytion Insolam** is a chitinous solar monstrosity born from the festering corpse-light of the slain sun god **Päivätär**.
Its body—an anchor-headed insult to life itself, a mixture of fishlike, serpentine and horribly insectoid features, crowned by a great translucent orb filled with pale, sickening amber amniotic fluid.
Within this sun-womb writhe hundreds of larval **Sunspawn**: serpentine, shrimp-like offspring awaiting release.
The Delphytion’s needle-limbs float a full foot from the orb, held in place by an unseen repulsive field, and its long dolphin-tail sports spout-like vents that hiss superheated vapor.
When threatened, the orb’s glow intensifies to blinding brilliance as the creature lowers its core and lets its limbs whirl in a screaming cyclone of chitin and light.

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

The Delphytion Insolam has **1 legendary deed per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Solar Vessel

While not prone and not attacked from above 20 feet, the Delphytion Insolam has **resistance to all damage** and cannot be knocked prone. It emits **bright light in a 60-foot radius** and dim light for an additional 60 feet.

#### Twilit Weakness

The Delphytion's core sits within the top 10 feet of its 30-foot-tall frame. As an **excessively tall creature**, it always holds the [high ground](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/contextual-conflict#high-ground) against attackers of size Large or smaller — only a creature positioned above its upper boundary can overcome this (see **High Ground**).

It has the following vulnerabilities:

- While [_prone_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#prone), it loses resistance to all damage and becomes **vulnerable to all damage**.
- Attacks made against it from a position **20 feet or more above ground level** bypass its resistance to all damage and deal damage as if it were [_prone_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#prone). This represents striking down into its exposed core — the one angle its towering frame cannot defend.

Whenever the Delphytion takes damage to which it is vulnerable, it reflexively births **1d4 Sunspawn** (see **Sunspawn**) into unoccupied spaces within 10 feet of it. Sunspawn created this way act immediately after the Delphytion in initiative.

#### Spout Vents

The Delphytion has no conventional anatomy. Vents near its tail and anchor-head expel superheated vapor. At the start of each of its turns, each creature within 10 feet of it must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or take **7 (2d6) fire damage** and be [_blinded_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#blinded) until the start of its next turn.

## Minor Actions

#### Dusk Star

The Delphytion falls **prone** and enters its **dusk form**. While in dusk form:

- Its walking speed is **doubled** (90 ft.).
- Its limbs rotate in a screaming vortex. Each creature within **15 feet** of it at the start of the Delphytion's turn must make a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **22 (4d10) piercing damage** on a failure, or half as much on a success. A creature that fails this save is also knocked [_prone_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#prone).
- Its core is lowered to ground level and the **Solar Vessel** trait is suppressed (no damage resistances, no light emission).

The Delphytion can end dusk form as a Minor Action (see **Dawning Glory**).

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Delphytion Insolam makes three attacks: two with its **Needle Limbs** and one with either its **Barbed Proboscis** or **Tail Sweep**.

- **Needle Limb.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +12 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 18 (2d10 + 7) piercing damage. If the target is Large or smaller, it must succeed on a **DC 20 Strength saving throw** or be [_grappled_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#grappled). The Delphytion can grapple up to two creatures this way.

- **Barbed Proboscis (Recharge 5–6).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature **grappled** by the Delphytion.  
  **Hit**: 27 (4d10 + 7) piercing damage.
  The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or be implanted with a **Sunspawn embryo**.
  At the start of each of the implanted creature’s turns, it takes **11 (2d10)** poison damage as the embryo feeds.
  The embryo dies if the host receives **20 or more points of holy damage** or if any effect cures disease.
  After **3 rounds**, the embryo erupts: the host takes **33 (6d10)** piercing damage and a fully formed **Sunspawn** appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of it.

- **Tail Sweep.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., cone (all creatures in a 15-foot cone).  
  **Hit**: 16 (2d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage. Each Large or smaller creature hit must succeed on a **DC 20 Strength saving throw** or be pushed **15 feet** away and knocked [_prone_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#prone).

## Reactions

**Vent Discharge.**  
_When the Delphytion takes damage from a melee attack,_  
The Delphytion releases a burst of superheated vapor from its spout vents. The attacker must succeed on a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw** or take **14 (4d6) fire damage** and be [_blinded_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#blinded) until the end of its next turn.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Delphytion Insolam can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn.

#### Engender Sunspawn (Costs 1 Deed)

The Delphytion regurgitates a larval Sunspawn and hurls it as a projectile at a creature within **60 feet** that it can sense.

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, one target.  
**Hit**: 11 (2d6 + 4) chemical damage, and a **Sunspawn** appears in an unoccupied space adjacent to the target. The Sunspawn acts immediately after the Delphytion in initiative.

#### Dawning Glory (Costs 1 Deed)

The Delphytion ends its **dusk form**, stands up (removing the [_prone_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#prone) condition), and its **Solar Vessel** trait reactivates. When it rises, amniotic fluid splashes from its orb in a 15-foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **18 (4d8) holy damage** on a failure, or half as much on a success.
The fluid lingers as a **radiant pool** until the end of the Delphytion’s next turn; a creature that enters the pool for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there takes **9 (2d8) holy damage**.
