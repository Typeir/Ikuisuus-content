_Large Aberration, Chaotic Evil_

Discarded flesh born from great Silent Ones often develops an animalistic, brutal nature. This leads to them being routinely mistaken by
great wyrms or dragons of legend, and subsequently hunted down by the locals of their areas.

> With a Hail of Harpoons... With every last drop of my being! — Last words of Sire Ahav of Irémia.

## Traits

#### Amphibious

The abominable avian can breathe air and water.

#### Gold Glimmer

The abominable avian emits a faint, mesmerizing glow. Creatures that start their turn within 10 feet of it or enter this area for the first time on a turn must succeed on a **DC 16 Wisdom saving throw** or be **charmed** until the end of their next turn.

#### Storm Bladder

The abominable avian’s body is swollen with volatile electrical organs.  
It has advantage on Constitution saving throws against being stunned or paralyzed.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The abominable avian makes **two attacks**: one with its bite and one with its talons.

- **Bite.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage.

- **Talons.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

#### Quacke (Recharge 5–6)

The abominable avian releases a catastrophic blast of compressed air in a **60-foot cone**.  
Each creature in the area must make a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw**, taking **50 (10d8) thunder damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Creatures that fail the save are also **deafened and knocked prone** until the end of their next turn.  
Objects and structures in the area take double damage.

#### Lightning Dive

The abominable avian flies up to **60 feet** in a straight line. Each creature whose space it passes through must succeed on a **DC 15 Dexterity saving throw** or take **22 (4d10) lightning damage** and be knocked prone.
The abominable avian does not provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach.

---

## Reactions

#### Abominable Reflex

When a creature the abominable avian can see targets it with a melee attack, the abominable avian can use its reaction to have its lightning organs lash out, forcing the attacker to make a **DC 15 Dexterity saving throw** or take 9 (2d8) lightning damage become **paralyzed** until the end of its next turn.
