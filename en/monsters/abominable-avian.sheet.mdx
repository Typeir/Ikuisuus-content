## Abominable Avian

_Large Aberration, Chaotic Evil_

| **Armor Class**    | **Hit Points**   | **Speed**                       |
| ------------------ | ---------------- | ------------------------------- |
| 16 (natural armor) | 170 (20d10 + 60) | 30 ft., fly 60 ft., swim 30 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 4 (-3)  | 12 (+1) | 18 (+4) |

- **Saving Throws**: Dex +7, Con +8, Cha +8
- **Skills**: Perception +5, Stealth +7, Arcana +6
- **Damage Resistances**: lightning, thunder
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15
- **Languages**: Understands Abyssal but cannot speak.
- **Challenge**: 10 (5,900 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Amphibious

The abominable avian can breathe air and water.

#### Gold Glimmer

The abominable avian emits a faint, mesmerizing glow. Creatures within 10 feet of it must succeed on a **DC 16 Wisdom saving throw** or be **charmed** until the end of their next turn.

#### Winged Horror

The abominable avian has a permanent **flying speed of 60 feet** and does not provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of a creature’s reach.

#### Storm Bladder

The abominable avian’s body is swollen with volatile electrical organs.  
It has advantage on Constitution saving throws against being stunned or paralyzed.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The abominable avian makes **two attacks**: one with its bite and one with its talons.

- **Bite.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage.

- **Talons.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

#### Quacke (Recharge 5–6)

The abominable avian releases a catastrophic blast of compressed air in a **60-foot cone**.  
Each creature in the area must make a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw**, taking **50 (10d8) thunder damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Creatures that fail the save are also **deafened and knocked prone** until the end of their next turn.  
Objects and structures in the area take double damage.

#### Lightning Dive

The abominable avian flies up to **60 feet** in a straight line. Each creature whose space it passes through must succeed on a **DC 15 Dexterity saving throw** or take **22 (4d10) lightning damage** and be knocked prone.

---

## Reactions

#### Abominable Reflex

When the abominable avian takes damage from a melee attack, the attacker takes **7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage**, as a mass of tentacles lashes out against them.
