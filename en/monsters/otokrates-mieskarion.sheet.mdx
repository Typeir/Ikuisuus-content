## Traits

#### Ant Rider

Otokrates rides a [giant fire ant called Wildfire](/en/library/monsters/giant-fire-ant) as a mount.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Otokrates makes two melee weapon attacks with his **Perpetual Flameblade**.

- **Perpetual Flameblade**
  _Melee Spell Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 11 (3d6) fire damage. This weapon is always active and is treated as magical.

---

#### Spellcasting

Otokrates is a **9th-level cleric**. His spellcasting ability is **Wisdom** (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Sacred Flame_](/en/library/spells/sacred-flame), [_Thaumaturgy_](/en/library/spells/thaumaturgy), [_Light_](/en/library/spells/light)
- **1st-level (4 slots)**: [_Burning Hands_](/en/library/spells/burning-hands), [_Shield of Faith_](/en/library/spells/shield-of-faith)
- **2nd-level (3 slots)**: [_Heat Metal_](/en/library/spells/heat-metal), [_Hold Person_](/en/library/spells/hold-person)
- **3rd-level (3 slots)**: [_Fireball_](/en/library/spells/fireball), [_Daylight_](/en/library/spells/daylight)
- **4th-level (3 slots)**: [_Wall of Fire_](/en/library/spells/wall-of-fire)
- **5th-level (1 slot)**: [_Flame Strike_](/en/library/spells/flame-strike)

---

## Reactions

#### Bloodlash Rebuke

When Otokrates is hit by a melee weapon attack, he may cast [_Bloodlash Rebuke_](/en/library/spells/bloodlash-rebuke) as a reaction. The attacker takes 11 (2d10) fire damage and must succeed on a **DC 16 Strength saving throw** or be **pushed 10 feet away**.
