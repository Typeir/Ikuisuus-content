_Large Construct, Unaligned_

| --------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------- |
| 17              | 148 (16d10 + 64) | 0 ft., see **Pipe Network** |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 22 (+6) | 8 (–1)  | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) |

- **Saving Throws**: Constitution +10, Wisdom +6
- **Skills**: Perception +6, Stealth +4 (when submerged in filth)
- **Damage Resistances**: Bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
- **Damage Immunities**: Acid, Poison
- **Condition Immunities**: Grappled, Poisoned, Prone, Restrained, Terrified, Frightened, Charmed
- **Senses**: Blindsight 120 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 16
- **Languages**: Understands Empyrean and Common but cannot speak
- **Challenge**: 12 (8,400 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Avenger has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

**Pipe Network**.

Instead of walking, the Avenger inhabits and manipulates its pipe system.
**Once per round**, it may instantly **shift** to any pipe outlet or _burrow_ it has placed within **300 feet**.
This does not provoke opportunity attacks.

**Burrow** (Pipe Placement).

_As an action_, the Avenger extrudes a new pipe segment up to **60 feet** long through stone, earth, or metal, creating a new outlet for **Pipe Network**.
Outside a sewer/lair, the pipe remains as a slime-filled tunnel large enough for a Medium creature to crawl through (difficult terrain).

#### Reforming body

The avenger regenerates 40 hit points at the start of it's round.

#### Corrosive Bulk

Creatures that hit the Avenger with a melee attack while within 15 feet take **9 (2d8) acid damage**.
Nonmagical weapons that strike it break after **three** hits, gaining a **cummulative -1 modifier to attack and damage rolls** each hit until breakage.  
If struck by nonmagical equipment, the attacker gains **one stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/erosion)**.

**Toxic Domain** (10-foot radius around pipes).

The ground in a 10-foot radius around any active pipe outlet is slick and is difficult terrain.
Creatures that start their turn there must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or **fall prone**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Avenger makes **two** attacks with its **Spine Sickle** or **Acid Tendrils**.

- **Spine Sickle.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach **15 ft.**, one target.  
  _Hit:_ 26 (4d10 + 4) slashing damage and **10 (3d6)** acid damage. The target is **knocked prone** and is affected by the effects of the [_Slow_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:slow) spell until the end of their next turn.  
  If this attack **reduces a creature to 0 hit points**, the Spine Sickle draws and consumes that creature's spine, killing them instantly.  
  The creature **dies instantly**.  
  After consuming a spine this way, the Spine Sickle's reach **permanently increases by 5 ft.** This effect has no upper limit.

- **Acid Tendrils.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach **30 ft.**, one target.  
  _Hit:_ 21 (3d10 + 4) acid damage.  
  The target must succeed on a **DC 18 Strength saving throw** or be grappled, forcing its movement within the Avenger's range.  
  If a creature is already grappled, the Avenger restrains them instead. Only one creature at a time may be restrained this way.

#### Deathcall (Recharge 6).

The Avenger raises its own severed head as it lets out a pressurized scream of acid vapor and psychic noise.  
Each creature within **60 feet** that can hear it must make a **DC 18 Wisdom saving throw**.  
On a failed save, the creature takes **27 (6d8)** psychic damage and is **terrified** until the end of its next turn.  
If the creature was already terrified, it additionally takes **48** acid damage.  
On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and is not terrified.  
If **Toxic Surge** is available, it immediately activates.

## Toxic Surge (Recharge 3–6).##

A pressure blast erupts through all connected outlets.  
Each creature within **15 feet** of any active pipe must make a **DC 18 Constitution saving throw**, taking **31 (7d8) acid damage** and becoming **blinded** until the start of the Avenger's next turn on a failure, or half as much on a success.

## Reactions

- **Pipe Escape.**  
  When hit by an attack or targeted by a spell it can perceive, the Avenger uses **Pipe Network** to shift to another outlet within **300 feet**.
  If used after the attack roll is made, the triggering attack is made at **disadvantage**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, the Avenger can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. It cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

**Rising Pressure**

A random outlet bursts, creating a **10-feet** radius geyser of corrosive sludge.
Each creature in the area must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or take **31 (7d8) thunder damage** and be **pushed 10 feet**.

**Backflow**

As part of this action, the Avenger reverses sewage currents and immediately **relocates to a pipe within 120 feet**, even if it has already shifted using **Pipe Network** this round.
