# Ancient Silent One

_Huge Aberration, Chaotic Evil_

| **Armor Class**    | **Hit Points**    | **Speed**            |
| ------------------ | ----------------- | -------------------- |
| 16 (natural armor) | 276 (24d12 + 120) | 30 ft., climb 30 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 22 (+6) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 8 (–1)  | 16 (+3) | 14 (+2) |

- **Saving Throws**: Strength +10, Constitution +9, Wisdom +7
- **Skills**: Athletics +10, Perception +7
- **Damage Resistances**: Bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
- **Condition Immunities**: Prone
- **Senses**: Blindsight 60 ft., Tremorsense 60 ft., Darkvision 120 ft., Passive Perception 17
- **Languages**: Understands Deep Speech; speaks only in faint murmurs
- **Challenge**: 13 (10,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +5

---

## Traits

#### Amorphous Mass

The Silent One can move through a space as narrow as **5 feet** wide without squeezing. It cannot be knocked prone.

#### Many-Armed Horror

The Silent One has twelve arms and makes six **Slam** attacks on its turn.  
If it begins its turn **grappling no creatures**, each Slam instead deals **double damage**.

#### Digestive Maw (Concentration)

A creature grappled by **three or more arms** must succeed on a **DC 17 Strength saving throw** or be **swallowed**.  
While swallowed, the creature is **blinded** and **restrained**, has **total cover** against effects from outside, and takes **28 (8d6) acid damage** at the start of each of the Silent One’s turns.

The Silent One must maintain this swallow as if **concentrating on a spell**. Each time it takes damage, it must succeed on a **Constitution saving throw** (DC 10 or half the damage taken, whichever is higher).  
On a failure, it immediately **regurgitates all swallowed creatures**, which fall **prone** in spaces within 10 feet of it.

#### Innate Spellcasting (Psionics)

**Spell Save DC**: 15  
The Silent One can innately cast the following spells, requiring no components:

- **At will**: _Detect Magic_, _Detect Thoughts_, _Eldritch Blast_ (5 beams, +9 to hit, 1d10 force each; **pulls the target 10 feet toward the Silent One on a hit**), _Misty Step_
- **3/day each**: _Command_, _Suggestion_, _Zone of Truth_, _Knock_, _Arcane Lock_, _Dimension Door_
- **1/day each**: _Divination_, _Magic Missile_ (7th level), _Gate_

#### Silent Presence

The Silent One’s body produces no natural sound, and its voice is heard only as faint murmurs.  
While this aura is active, creatures within **30 feet** have **disadvantage** on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing, and spellcasters within this range must succeed on a **DC 15 Concentration check** to cast a spell with a **verbal component**.  
The Silent One can suppress or reactivate this aura at the start of its turn (no action required).

#### All-Around Eyes

The Silent One has **advantage** on Wisdom (Perception) checks and **cannot be surprised**.

#### Whirl of Limbs

Each creature of the Silent One’s choice that starts its turn within **10 feet** must make a **DC 18 Strength saving throw**, taking **35 (10d6) bludgeoning damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
**Grappled creatures automatically fail**, and take an additional **10 acid damage**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Silent One makes **six Slam attacks**, or it uses **Hurl Debris** up to three times.

- **Slam.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach **10 ft.**, one target.  
  _Hit:_ 12 (2d4 + 6) bludgeoning damage (**or 24 (4d4 + 12)** if grappling no creatures).  
  A Large or smaller target is **grappled** (escape DC 17). Until the grapple ends, the target is **restrained**.

#### Hurl Debris.

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, range **60/180 ft.**, one target.  
_Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.  
The Silent One can target **up to three creatures** if it has **three or more free arms**.

#### Disintegrate (Recharge 6).

The Silent One casts [_Disintegrate_](/en/library/spells/disintegrate), it's spell save DC is 15.
