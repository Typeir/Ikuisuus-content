# Iron Gammer

_Large Construct, Unaligned_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| ---------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 21 (Iron Plating) | 210 (20d10 + 100) | 30 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 24 (+7) | 10 (+0) | 20 (+5) | 6 (-2) | 12 (+1) | 8 (-1) |

- **Saving Throws**: Strength +12, Constitution +10  
- **Skills**: Athletics +12  
- **Damage Resistances**: bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks  
- **Damage Immunities**: poison, psychic  
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified, Frightened, Charmed, Exhaustion, Poisoned  
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11  
- **Languages**: Common  
- **Challenge**: 13 (10,000 XP)  
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +5

---

## Traits

#### Heavy Frame
The Iron Gammer has advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws made to grapple or restrain creatures.

#### Built for the Drop
The Iron Gammer does not take damage from falling and cannot be knocked prone.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack
The Iron Gammer makes **two Slam attacks**, or it uses **Throw**.

---

#### Slam  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target  
_Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage.

---

#### Throw  
**Action**

The Iron Gammer throws **one creature or object** it is holding, grappling, or that is within reach and unsecured, of **Large size or smaller**, up to **60 feet** in a straight line to an unoccupied space it can see.

If a **creature** is thrown, it takes **1d10 bludgeoning damage** per 10 feet traveled on landing. 
The thrown movement counts as the creature’s movement for the current round. 
Immediately after the Iron Gammer’s turn ends the creature takes its turn, even if it has already acted this round. 
A creature can only benefit from **one gifted turn per round**.

If an **object** is thrown, each creature in the object’s landing space must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or take **3d10 bludgeoning damage**, or half as much on a success. 
The object then comes to rest in the landing space.

If a creature is unwilling, it must succeed on a **DC 20 Strength saving throw** or be thrown. On a success, the creature takes half damage and is not moved.

---

## Reactions

#### Catch
When a creature within **10 feet** of the Iron Gammer would fall, the Iron Gammer grabs the creature, preventing the fall.  
The creature is grappled and placed in an adjacent unoccupied space of the Iron Gammer’s choice.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Slain
When the Iron Gammer is **slain**, it collapses forward violently.  
All creatures within **10 feet** must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or take **4d10 bludgeoning damage** and be knocked prone.
