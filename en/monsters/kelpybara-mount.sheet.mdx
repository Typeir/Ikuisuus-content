## Traits

#### Trampling Charge

If the **Kelpybara** moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a **Jaws** attack, the creature becomes grappled unless it succeeds on a DC 13 Strength saving throw. Additionally, as a Minor Action, the **Kelpybara** can attempt to restrain the creature and itself with a grapple (contested check).

---

## Major Actions

#### Jaws

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 ([% 2d6 +4 bludgeoning %]).

---

#### Spellcasting

The **Kelpybara** is a 4th-level Villein spellcaster. It has **two 2nd-level spell slots** and can cast the following spells:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Eldritch Blast_](/en/library/spells/eldritch-blast) (+1 to hit)
- **2nd-level (2 slots)**: [_Darkness_](/en/library/spells/darkness), [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step)

---

#### Umbral Spellcasting

While obscured or under the night sky, the **Kelpybara** may ritually cast the following spells. Only one effect may be active at a time, and it may target only itself:

- [_Jump_](/en/library/spells/jump)
- [_Water Walk_](/en/library/spells/water-walk)
- [_Expeditious Retreat_](/en/library/spells/expeditious-retreat)
