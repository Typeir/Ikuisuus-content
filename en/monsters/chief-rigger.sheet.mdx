## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Chief Rigger has **1 legendary deed per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (1/Day)
When the Chief Rigger fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **1 time per day**.

#### Master of Equipment

The Chief Rigger carries a functionally infinite bag of trinkets.

- The Chief Rigger can only use trinkets of a rarity **lower than legendary**.
- The Chief Rigger cannot use the **same trinket** again until it has used **every other available trinket** at least once.

<TrinketTable />

#### Resourceful Trickster

The Chief Rigger uses a **DC 22** for all trinket and potion saving throws, and a **+12 bonus** to hit with trinket and potion attacks.

#### Heavy Frame

The Chief Rigger has advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws made to grapple or restrain creatures.

#### Spell Points

At the start of each of the Chief Rigger’s turns, it gains **5 spell points**.  
These spell points can only be spent on **Administer Potion** and **Throw Potion**, and any unspent points are lost at the end of the turn.

The minimum cost of any potion is **1**, and each potion costs **spell level + 1** spell points.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Chief Rigger uses **Use Trinket** twice, or **Use Trinket** once and **Administer Potion** once, or **Administer Potion** twice.

---

#### Use Trinket

**Action**

The Chief Rigger uses one trinket from its infinite bag, following that item’s normal rules for range, damage, conditions, and terrain effects.

If the trinket normally requires placement, the Chief Rigger may throw it within the trinket’s listed range instead.

---

#### Administer Potion

**Action**

The Chief Rigger spends spell points to administer one potion to itself or a creature within **5 feet**, replicating the listed spell’s effect.  
These potion effects **do not require concentration**.

---

#### Throw Potion

**Action**

The Chief Rigger spends spell points to throw one potion to a point it can see within **30 feet**, shattering it on impact and replicating the listed spell’s effect.

If the spell targets a creature, the Chief Rigger selects up to **three creatures** it can see within **10 feet** of the impact point as the targets.  
If the spell creates an area (such as _Silence_), the area is centered on the impact point.

---

## Reactions

#### Catch

When a creature within **10 feet** of the Chief Rigger would fall, the Chief Rigger grabs the creature, preventing the fall.  
The creature is grappled and placed in an adjacent unoccupied space of the Chief Rigger’s choice.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Chief Rigger can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Use Trinket.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Chief Rigger uses **Use Trinket**.
- **Potion.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Chief Rigger uses **Administer Potion** or **Throw Potion**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Slain

When the Chief Rigger is **slain**, it fires [Gore-Hooks](/en/library/items/trinkets/gore-hook) in all directions.

The Chief Rigger makes a [Gore-Hook](/en/library/items/trinkets/gore-hook) attack against **every creature and object** within **100 feet**, using a **+5 bonus** to the attack roll.  
Each hit deals the normal damage of a [Gore-Hook](/en/library/items/trinkets/gore-hook) and applies its normal effects.
