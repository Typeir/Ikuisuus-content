## Traits

#### Inevitable Failure

The Dreadshade **automatically fails all saving throws**, regardless of source.

#### Regenerative Horror

At the **start of each of its turns**, the Dreadshade regains **50 hit points**.  
This regeneration functions even while incapacitated or restrained.

If the Dreadshade **fails a saving throw**, it will stop regenerating until the end of it's next turn.

#### Mark of Pursuit

When the Dreadshade **hits a creature** with a melee attack, that creature becomes **Marked**.

- As a bonus action, the Dreadshade can **teleport** to any Marked creature it can perceive.
- Teleporting does **not** provoke opportunity attacks.
- The Mark persists until the target or the Dreadshade dies.

#### Escalating Precision

Against each individual target, track hits separately.

- Each **subsequent hit** the Dreadshade lands on the same creature **reduces its critical hit range by 1**  
  (20 → 19–20 → 18–20, etc.).
- This reduction has **no minimum**.

#### Legacy of Dread

The Dreadshade’s violence compounds across the battlefield.

- Base damage starts at **15**.
- Each time **any creature within 30 feet scores a critical hit** (including the Dreadshade):
  - The Dreadshade **adds +1d12** to its damage pool
  - All **previous damage dice are maximized**

This escalation is **global**, cumulative, and persists until the dreadshade has not landed a critical hit in the last hour.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Dreadshade makes **four Legacy of Dread** attacks.

---

#### Legacy of Dread

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.

_Hit:_ **15 damage**, plus escalating damage from **Legacy of Dread**.

On hit, the target becomes **Marked** if it was not already.
