---

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
Plato has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

#### Bound Reflex

Every time Plato is **targeted by an attack**, before the attack roll is resolved, Plato gains **50 temporary hit points**.

- This triggers on a hit or a miss.
- These temporary hit points **stack with themselves**.
- The effect triggers even if the attack is later redirected, negated, or absorbed.

---

#### Bound Will

Every time Plato is **subjected to a saving throw**, before rolling, Plato gains **50 temporary hit points**.

- This applies even if the effect deals no damage.
- This applies even if the saving throw is automatically successful or negated.

---

#### Homuncular Retinue

Plato is accompanied by **six nameless wax-bound homunculi**.  
Additional homunculi may be introduced by Battlefield or Lair effects.

- Each homunculus can use **one reaction per round** to protect Plato.
- If Plato would take damage, a homunculus may take that damage instead.

---

#### Reprise of the First (1/Short Rest)

When Plato casts a spell of **1st level or higher**, he may mark it for reprise.

At any point before the end of the encounter, Plato may invoke **Reprise of the First** (no action required):

- The marked spell is immediately **re-cast by Plato the First**, an unseen primordial echo.
- If the spell requires a saving throw, the **save DC becomes 50**.
- If the spell requires an attack roll, the attack **automatically critically hits**.
- This re-cast does **not** consume spell slots, actions, reactions, or concentration.

Once used, this trait cannot be used again.

## Actions

#### Fracture Command

Plato targets one creature he can see within 120 feet.

The target must succeed on a **DC 25 Wisdom saving throw** or be **restrained** until the end of its next turn.


#### Ambition's end

Plato can only use this if within **1 mile** of [_War Goddess Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-goddess-yskeia) 

Plato strikes an object with a lance made of pure Arkhé, if the object is not magical, it is destroyed instantly.
If the object is magical, it takes 100 force damage. If the object has any modifiers to hit or AC, they lose 1d4 + 1 of those modifiers until repaired.
Plato does not roll to hit with this attack, for it is fated.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

Plato can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Direct the Nameless.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  One homunculus immediately moves up to 30 feet and makes one attack or uses Interposition.

- **Containment Pulse.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Every Creature of Plato's choice within 20 feet must succeed on a **DC 25 Strength saving throw** or have its speed reduced to **0** until the end of its next turn.

- **Cast a Spell.** (Costs 2 Deeds)  
  Plato casts a spell of **5th level or lower**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Tethered Destiny

Plato marks up to **three creatures** he can see within 120 feet.

At the start of Plato’s next turn, if Plato is free to use verbal components, each marked creature must succeed on a **DC 35 Constitution saving throw** or be **unable to teleport, plane shift, or benefit from movement other than walking** until the end of its next turn.
Additionally, creatures immediately fail saving throws that cause forced movement, and their movement speed is halved.

---

## Reactions

#### Interposition

When Plato would take damage or be subjected to an effect, a homunculus within 30 feet may move up to 20 feet and become the new target.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP)

Plato immediately gains **200 temporary hit points**.

From this point onward:

- All of Plato's damage becomes force damage.

---

#### Doomed (25% HP)

Reality destabilizes around Plato.

- Plato may immediately regains an use of **Reprise of the First**.
- Plato gains advantage against saving throws of magical effects and spells.
- Plato gains resistance to all damage.
- All of Plato's damage rolls are true damage.

---

#### Slain

When Plato is reduced to 0 hit points, he immediately casts [_Power Word: Kill_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:power-word-kill) on every creature within one mile.
