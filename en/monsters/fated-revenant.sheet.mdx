## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

The Fated Revenant has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Repose)

When the Fated Revenant fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Repose**.

#### Lash of Fate

At the start of each of the Fated Revenant's turns, it may choose one creature it can see within **150 feet**. That creature is violently pulled **50 feet** directly toward the Fated Revenant by a lash of gravitational force.

If the creature ends this movement adjacent to the Fated Revenant, it must succeed on a **DC 27 Strength saving throw** or be knocked **prone** and **restrained** until the end of its next turn.

This forced movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.

#### Colossal Cleave

Whenever the Fated Revenant deals damage with their blades, all other creatures within **15 feet** of that target take **half damage** from the same source (rounded down). This applies to both melee attacks and damage-dealing abilities.

#### Revenant Fortitude

If damage reduces the Fated Revenant to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of **5 + the damage taken**, unless the damage is from a critical hit. On a success, it drops to 1 hit point instead.

#### Graviturge's Mark

The Fated Revenant's **Gravitic Brand** (see Legendary Deed: Act) imposes a **Gravitic Mark** on hit. A marked creature has **disadvantage on Strength checks and saving throws** until the end of its next turn. Multiple marks can be applied to the same creature.

#### Inertial Dampening

The Fated Revenant cannot be forcibly moved against its will by any effect of **5th level or lower**.

#### Warbeast Momentum

When the Fated Revenant moves through a creature's space, that creature must make a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw**. On a failure, the creature is pushed **10 feet** to the side (the Fated Revenant's choice) and knocked **prone**. On a success, the creature is pushed 5 feet and is not knocked prone.

#### Gravitic Charge

When the Fated Revenant moves at least **10 feet in a straight line** immediately before making a **Main Hand Massive Stone Sabre** attack, the attack deals an additional **+5 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet traveled**. There is no maximum bonus.

#### Fiendish Familiar

The Fated Revenant is accompanied by a **Fiendish Familiar** that shares its initiative. The familiar obeys the Fated Revenant's commands and acts on its own turn immediately after the Fated Revenant.

If the familiar is destroyed, the Fated Revenant can summon a replacement as a **Major Action**, which appears in an empty space within 30 feet at the start of the Fated Revenant's next turn. The replacement familiar arrives with full hit points.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Fated Revenant makes **two Main Hand Massive Stone Sabre** attacks. It can also make one **Off-Hand Massive Stone Sabre** attack as a **Minor Action**.

#### Main Hand Massive Stone Sabre

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 27 ([% 4d8 + 9 bludgeoning %]).

If the Fated Revenant moved at least 10 feet in a straight line before this attack, add **+5 damage per 10 feet traveled** (see **Gravitic Charge**).

#### Minor Action — Off-Hand Massive Stone Sabre

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 27 ([% 4d8 + 9 bludgeoning %]).

The target must succeed on a **DC 26 Strength saving throw** or be pushed **15 feet** directly away from the Fated Revenant and knocked **prone**. If the target fails by 5 or more, it is also **stunned** until the end of its next turn.

#### Major Action — Terrain Shift

The Fated Revenant reaches out with gravitational force and moves a piece of terrain (Colossal size or smaller) up to **30 feet** in any direction. This can be a boulder, a section of wall, a collapsed structure, or similar feature.

Any creature in the terrain's path or at its destination must make a **DC 27 Dexterity saving throw**. On a failure, a creature takes **[% 10d8 bludgeoning %]** and is knocked **prone** and pushed **15 feet** in the direction of the terrain's movement.

On a success, a creature takes half damage and is not knocked prone or pushed.

The terrain remains in its new position.

#### Greatbow

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, range 60/180 ft., 30-foot cone.  
_Hit:_ 26 ([% 4d8 +8 piercing %]).

The Fated Revenant's greatbow fires a scatter of dense, stone projectiles. Make a single attack roll against each creature in a **30-foot cone**. The cone originates from the Fated Revenant.

#### Gravitic Stomp

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 22 ([% 3d8 + 9 bludgeoning %]).

The target must succeed on a **DC 24 Dexterity saving throw** or be knocked **prone**. The ground in a **15-foot radius** around the target becomes **difficult terrain** until the end of the Fated Revenant's next turn.

#### Gravity Well

_Ranged Spell Attack:_ +15 to hit, range 120 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 36 ([% 8d8 bludgeoning %]).

The target must succeed on a **DC 27 Strength saving throw** or be pulled **25 feet** toward a point of the Fated Revenant's choice within range.

#### Crushing Wave (Recharge 5–6)

The Fated Revenant swings its sabres in a wide, crushing arc, unleashing a shockwave of condensed gravitational force. Creatures in a **40-foot cone** must make a **DC 27 Strength saving throw**.  
On a failure, a creature takes **45 ([% 10d8 bludgeoning %])** and is pushed **30 feet** directly away from the Fated Revenant, ending **prone**.  
On a success, a creature takes half damage and is pushed **10 feet** (not knocked prone).

#### Tether of Ambition (Recharge 6)

The Fated Revenant designates one creature it can see within **120 feet**. That creature is **Gravitically Tethered** to the Fated Revenant. While tethered, the creature cannot willingly move more than **30 feet** from the Fated Revenant. At the start of each of the Fated Revenant's turns, the tethered creature must make a **DC 27 Strength saving throw** or be pulled **25 feet** toward the Fated Revenant and take **36 ([% 8d8 bludgeoning %])**.

If pulled adjacent, the creature is also knocked **prone** and **restrained** until the end of its next turn. The tether ends when the creature succeeds on the saving throw.

#### Massive Force (Recharge 5–6)

The Fated Revenant hurls a concentrated gravitational vortex at a point it can see within **300 feet**. Creatures in a **30-foot-radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **DC 27 Strength saving throw**.  
On a failure, a creature takes **72 ([% 16d8 bludgeoning %])** and is pulled **30 feet** toward the center.  
On a success, a creature takes half damage and is not pulled.

#### Spellcasting

The Fated Revenant is a **17th-level spellcaster**. Its spellcasting ability is **Intelligence** (spell save DC **27**, +**15** to hit with spell attacks). It does not require material components. It has the following spells prepared:

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Fated Revenant can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Gravitic Brand.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Fated Revenant makes one **Main Hand Massive Stone Sabre** attack. On a hit, the target is wracked by gravitational ripples and gains a **Gravitic Mark**. If the target willingly moves before the start of the Fated Revenant's next turn, it takes an additional **18 ([% 4d8 bludgeoning %])** damage.

- **Off-Hand Swing.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Fated Revenant makes one **Off-Hand Massive Stone Sabre** attack.

- **Gravitic Pull.** (Costs 1 Deed) One creature within 60 feet must succeed on a **DC 27 Strength saving throw** or be pulled **25 feet** toward the Fated Revenant and take **[% 6d8 bludgeoning %]**.

- **Cast a Spell.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Fated Revenant casts one prepared spell of **6th level or lower**.

- **Gravitic Dive.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Fated Revenant launches upward and crashes down at a point within 60 feet. Creatures in a **20-foot radius** must make a **DC 24 Dexterity saving throw** or take **[% 8d8 bludgeoning %]** and be knocked **prone**. On a success, half damage.

- **Rain of Arrows.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Fated Revenant draws its greatbow skyward and looses a volley that rains down upon every creature bearing a **Gravitic Mark** within **300 feet**. Each creature within 30 feet of a marked creature must make a **DC 27 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **[% 8d8 piercing %]** on a failure, or half on a success. This attack does not consume the marks.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Dawn and Dusk (Recharge 5–6)

_Declared at the end of the Fated Revenant's turn; resolves at the start of its next turn._

**Declare**: The Fated Revenant raises both sabres above its head, crossing them in the shape of the **Sigil of Dawn** — a radiant, holy cross of pure light. The blades glow with incandescent brilliance as it coils them against its right side, lowering its stance and preparing to charge.

**Resolve**: The Fated Revenant dashes in a straight line of its choice, covering **any distance between 70 and 140 feet**. It moves through all spaces in that line, ignoring difficult terrain and creatures for the purposes of movement (it does not provoke opportunity attacks). Every creature in the **line of its dash** must make a **DC 27 Dexterity saving throw**.

On a failure, a creature takes **[% 16d8 holy %]** damage and is pushed **20 feet** to the side of the Fated Revenant's choice, knocked **prone**.

On a success, a creature takes half damage and is not pushed or knocked prone.

The Fated Revenant then **immediately gains the benefits of the Dash action** — this movement does not count as its movement for the turn and does not provoke opportunity attacks.

---

#### Gravitic Annihilation (Recharge 5–6)

_Declared at the end of the Fated Revenant's turn; resolves at the start of its next turn._

**Declare**: The Fated Revenant raises both sabres, and all creatures with a **Gravitic Mark** within its line of sight feel an immense pull toward it. The marks flare with violent gravitational energy.

**Resolve**: All marked creatures within the Fated Revenant's line of sight are pulled **240 feet** toward the Fated Revenant (up to a maximum of 300 feet if they are further away). If they end this movement adjacent to the Fated Revenant, they are knocked **prone** and **restrained** until the end of their next turn.

The Fated Revenant then slams both sabres into the ground, detonating all marks simultaneously in a **60-foot-radius sphere** centered on itself.

Each creature in the area must make a **DC 27 Dexterity saving throw**.  
On a failure, a creature takes **[% 20d8 fire %]** and **[% 20d8 bludgeoning %]** (combined total: **[% 40d8 fire + bludgeoning %]** ), is pushed **40 feet** directly away from the Fated Revenant, and is knocked **prone**.

On a success, a creature takes half damage and is not pushed or knocked prone.

All **Gravitic Marks** are consumed by the detonation.

The ground in the area becomes **difficult terrain** and scorched for 1 minute.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP) — Gravitic Surge

When the Fated Revenant is first reduced to 50% of its maximum hit points, the gravitational field around it intensifies violently.

- The Fated Revenant gains **2 additional legendary deeds per round** (total 5).
- Its **Lash of Fate** range increases to **200 feet**, and the pull strength increases to **70 feet**.
- The Fated Revenant's armor class increases to **23** (gravitic shielding).

On a success, half damage and no pull.

#### Doomed (25%) — Meteoric Descent

The Fated Revenant gains the following Stratagem:

##### Meteoric Descent

_Declared at the end of the Fated Revenant's turn; resolves at the start of its next turn._

**Declare**: The Fated Revenant launches itself into the sky, disappearing from the battlefield entirely as it soars high above, then plummets toward the battlefield as a living meteor. The Fated revenant rises 50 miles into the air, effectively escaping the scene for the duration.

**Resolve**: The Fated Revenant crashes down at a point of its choice within **300 feet**. Each creature within a **1 mile radius sphere** centered on that point must make a **DC 30 Dexterity saving throw**.

On a failure, a creature takes **[% 25d4 bludgeoning %]** and **[% 25d4 holy %]** (combined total: **[% 75d4 bludgeoning + holy %]** ), is pushed **60 feet** directly away from the impact point, and is knocked **prone** and **stunned** until the end of its next turn.

On a success, a creature takes half damage and is pushed **30 feet** (not knocked prone or stunned).

All creatures within 30 feet of the impact point are additionally **deafened** for 1 minute (no save).

The Fated Revenant takes 50 points of true damage after the fall.
