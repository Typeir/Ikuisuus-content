# Brine Sea Worm
_Large Monstrosity, Unaligned_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| ---------------- | -------------- | ---------- |
| 15 (thick scales) | 112 (15d10 + 30) | 30 ft., burrow 20 ft., swim 40 ft. |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 20 (+5) | 12 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 2 (−4) | 10 (+0) | 5 (−3) |

- **Saving Throws**: Str +8, Con +6  
- **Skills**: Athletics +8, Perception +3, Stealth +4  
- **Damage Resistances**: Acid, Cold  
- **Condition Immunities**: Grappled, Prone

- **Challenge**: 7 (2,900 XP)  
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +3  

---

## Traits

#### Brine Dweller
The worm can breathe both air and water.  
While fully submerged in saltwater or industrial brine, it regains **10 hit points** at the start of its turn.

#### Suffocating Coils
A creature **grappled** by the Brine Sea Worm begins **suffocating** at the start of its next turn as its lungs fill with pressurized brine.  
While suffocating, the creature can’t speak or cast spells with verbal components.  
This effect ends if the grapple ends or the worm releases the target.  
A suffocating creature must make a **Constitution saving throw** at the start of each of its turns, suffering **one failed death saving throw** on a failure.

#### Burrower’s Ambush
While underground or submerged, the worm has advantage on **Stealth** checks and can emerge up to 30 ft. as part of a **Bite** attack.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack
The Brine Sea Worm makes two attacks: one with its **Bite** and one with its **Coiling Grasp**.

- **Bite.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 21 (3d10 + 5) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) acid damage.  
If the target is a creature, it is **grappled** (escape DC 16). The worm can grapple only one target at a time.

- **Coiling Grasp.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 16 (3d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage.  
If the target is already grappled, the damage is **maximized (27)** and the target takes an additional **10 acid damage**. The creature is **restrained** until the grapple ends.

- **Brine Jet (Recharge 5–6).**  
The worm sprays a high-pressure jet of saltwater in a **30-foot line**, 5 ft. wide.  
Each creature in the line must make a **DC 16 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **27 (6d8)** bludgeoning and acid damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
A creature that fails the save gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

---

### Reactions

##### Burrow Escape
When the worm takes more than 20 damage from a single attack, it can use its reaction to burrow up to 15 ft. without provoking opportunity attacks, leaving behind a collapsing trail of brine.
