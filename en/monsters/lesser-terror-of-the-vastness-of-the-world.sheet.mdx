## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Lesser Terror has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (2/Day)
When the Lesser Terror fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **2 times per day**.

#### Spellcasting

The Lesser Terror Of The Vastness Of The World is a 7th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following wizard spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Chill Touch_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:chill-touch), [_Minor Illusion_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:minor-illusion), [_Ray of Frost_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:ray-of-frost)
- **1st level (4 slots)**: [_Burning Hands_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:burning-hands), [_Magic Missile_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:magic-missile), [_Shield_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:shield)
- **2nd level (3 slots)**: [_Mirror Image_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:mirror-image), [_Misty Step_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:misty-step)
- **3rd level (3 slots)**: [_Counterspell_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:counterspell), [_Dispel Magic_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:dispel-magic), [_Fear_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:fear)
- **4th level (1 slot)**: [_Phantasmal Killer_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:phantasmal-killer)

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Lesser Terror makes one Boot attack and one Maw attack, or one Blade of Chaos: Creation attack and one Maw attack.

- **The Boot.** _(Melee Weapon, Special)_  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 16 (3d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage, and the target is pushed back 5 feet.
- **Maw.** _(Melee Weapon, Natural)_  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 17 (2d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage.
- **Blade of Chaos: Creation.** _(Melee Weapon, One-Handed)_  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 14 (2d6 + 7) slashing damage plus 6 (1d12) force damage.
  The target must succeed on a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw or be terrified until the end of their next turn. While terrified in this way, the target cannot willingly move closer to the Lesser Terror.

---

## Bonus Actions

#### Maddening Presence (2/Day)

As a bonus action, the **Lesser Terror** can force a creature it can see within 60 feet to make a **DC 16 Charisma saving throw**. On a failed save, the creature is overwhelmed with visions of the vastness of the cosmos and is shunted into a different world-space until the end of the **Lesser Terror's** next turn. The creature also learns a grim truth about the world, of minor importance.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Lesser Terror can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Head of the Sun.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Lesser Terror shoots an igniting ray at a point it can see within 40 ft. Each creature in a 10-foot radius of that point must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or be set aflame. Until someone takes an action to douse the fire, the creature takes 6 (1d10) fire damage at the start of each of its turns.
- **Head of the Moon.** (Costs 1 Deed) Ranged spell attack: +8 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 (1d10) cold damage, and the target's movement speed is reduced to 0 until the beginning of the next round.
- **Head of the Cycle.** (Costs 1 Deed) Ranged spell attack: +8 to hit, range 60 ft., one target that is either on fire or frozen by Head of the Moon. _Hit:_ The target is paralyzed for 1 round.
- **Chaos Swipe.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Lesser Terror makes one Blade of Chaos: Creation attack.
- **Tail Swipe.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Lesser Terror does a tail swipe in a 20-foot cone. Creatures in the area must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, they take 14 (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage and are knocked prone. On a success, they take half damage.

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, the Lesser Terror can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. It cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### Twisting Reality

The Lesser Terror warps the space around it. All terrain within a 60-foot radius becomes difficult terrain for 1 minute.

#### Cosmic Echoes

The Lesser Terror taps into the horrors of the universe. Each creature within 30 feet of the Lesser Terror must make a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw or be compelled to spend their next turn **trying to build a roof** to shield them from the vastness of space, wasting their action, bonus action and reaction on doing so.

#### The Open Fields

The Lesser Terror turns all space within 60 ft. of it into a flat field of wheat for 1 minute. Creatures within the field must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success. Additionally, for each 5 feet any creature moves through the field, they take 2 (1d4) psychic damage.

---

## Regional Effects

The region containing the Lesser Terror's lair is warped by its presence, which creates one or more of the following effects:

#### Distorted Reality

The landscape within 1 mile of the lair is subtly warped, making paths seem longer or shorter, and the stars in the sky appear to shift and swirl.

#### Whispers in the Dark

Faint, unintelligible whispers can be heard within 1 mile of the lair. These whispers are disorienting and cause creatures to have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks made to detect anything other than the Lesser Terror.

#### Nightmares

Creatures that sleep within 1 mile of the lair experience vivid, terrifying dreams of vast, endless space. Upon waking, they must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or gain one level of exhaustion.
