# Apex Cerithol Lunam

_Large Monstrosity, Unaligned_

<BlendedImage
  src='/library/images/monsters/apex-cerithol.webp'
  alt='Apex Cerithol'
/>

**Apex Cerithol Lunami** are enlarged, ancient [_Cerithols_](/en/library/monsters/cerithol). Creatures that have vastly outpaced and outlived their biological design, their innards now barely contained within their fleshy core, their tentacular network so vast any somatic gesture is but a mere reflex to them. They are called "Apex" and not "Ancient" for a reason; few casters are as fierce as them.

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

The Apex Cerithol Lunam has **1 legendary deed per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Lunar Vessel

While closed, the Apex Cerithol Lunam cannot cast spells but gains a +5 bonus to AC and is immune to being charmed or stunned.

#### Open Form (Minor Action)

The Apex Cerithol opens fully, revealing its vasculomuscular system. It loses the AC bonus from **Lunar Vessel**, but gains access to its _Pseudopod Surge_ attack and spellcasting above 3rd level.

#### Mind Mastery

Apex Cerithols are permanently under the effects of [mind blank].

**_Distributed nervous system_.**
Apex Cerithols have several lesser ganglia to operate their multiple limbs and complex anatomical structures, some of which are able to concentrate on spells independently.

- Apex Cerithols have advantage on concentration checks.

- Apex Cerithols can concentrate on three spells at a time.

- When subjected to a concentration saving throw, only one of these three concentrations is affected, chosen by them.

#### Spell Point Casting

- The Apex Cerithol has **150 spell points**

- The Apex Cerithol Lunam can spend **10 spell points per round**, choosing as many spells as it wants for that cost if open (barring cantrips).
  Else it can only cast one spell, using the points to cast the following spells. Spell levels correspond to point costs. The apex cerithol is a 18th level spellcaster.

#### Heightened Reactions

The Apex Cerithol Lunam can take up to **3 reactions per round**, but no more than one per turn.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Apex Cerithol Lunam makes **three _Tentacle Whip_ attacks** or **one _Pseudopod Surge_** if open.

- **Tentacle Whip.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 32 ([% 5d10 +9 slashing %]).

- **Pseudopod Surge (Open Only).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +19 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 66 ([% 8d12 +10 bludgeoning %]).  
  The target must succeed on a **DC 27 Strength saving throw** or be hurled up to **120 feet** in a direction of the Apex Cerithol’s choosing and knocked prone.  
  If the target collides with a solid surface, it takes an additional **14 ([% 4d6 bludgeoning %])**.

## Reactions

**Bloodlash Rebuke (1st-level Unique Spell)**  
 _When a creature within 60 ft. succeeds on a saving throw or hits with an attack roll_  
 The Apex Cerithol Lunam sprays burning acidic blood from a pulsing orifice.  
 The creature must succeed on a **DC 25 Dexterity saving throw** or take **[% 4d10 %] damage**: **[% 2d10 chemical %]** and **[% 2d10 fire %]**. If it fails, it must **reroll the triggering d20**, using the new result. The Apex Cerithol gains **advantage** on the next roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Apex Cerithol Lunam can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

#### Cantrip Volley (Costs 1 Deed)

The Apex Cerithol Lunam targets up to **10 creatures** within 120 feet that it can sense. It casts **Envenom**, **Chill**, or **Omen** on each of them as part of the same action, as a level 11 spellcaster.
