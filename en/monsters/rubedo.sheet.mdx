# Rubedo – The Red Rebis

_Huge Aberration, Lawful Good_

| **Armor Class**    | **Hit Points**      | **Speed**          |
| ------------------ | ------------------- | ------------------ |
| 28 (natural armor) | 1,020 (60d12 + 600) | 40 ft., fly 60 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX**  | **CON**  | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | -------- | -------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 24 (+7) | 35 (+12) | 30 (+10) | 22 (+6) | 26 (+8) | 20 (+5) |

- **Saving Throws**: Dexterity +22, Constitution +19, Wisdom +17
- **Skills**: Perception +17, Intimidation +14
- **Damage Resistances**: Fire, Radiant
- **Damage Immunities**: Poison; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified, Poisoned, Exhaustion
- **Senses**: Truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 27
- **Languages**: Common, Empyrean, Hidden
- **Challenge**: 31 (175,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +10

---

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Rubedo has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)

When Rubedo fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Armor Constraint

While Rubedo’s Armor Class is **above 25**, damage dealt by **Flame Belch** is **halved** (round down).

#### Ruinous Aura

Rubedo’s supercritical presence irradiates flesh, blood, and thought alike.  
Once per turn, if a creature is **hit by Rubedo**, **starts its turn within 15 feet of Rubedo**, or **takes damage from one of Rubedo’s spells or lair effects**, it must succeed on a **DC 24 Constitution saving throw** or be affected by [_Red Ruin_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/red-ruin).  
This saving throw DC increases to **25 while Rubedo is Wounded**, **26 while Bloodied**, and **27 while Doomed**.  
Creatures immune to disease or curses do not ignore this effect; instead, they roll with advantage.

#### Spellcasting

Rubedo is a **17th-level spellcaster**. It has the following spell slots: 1st (4), 2nd (3), 3rd (3), 4th (3), 5th (3), 6th (2), 7th (2), 8th (1), 9th (1).  
Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 25, +17 to hit with spell attacks). It does not require material components. It has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips**: [_Sacred Flame_](/en/library/spells/sacred-flame), [_Create Bonfire_](/en/library/spells/create-bonfire), [_Word of Radiance_](/en/library/spells/word-of-radiance), [_Thaumaturgy_](/en/library/spells/thaumaturgy)

- **1st level (4 slots)**: [_Death Grips_](/en/library/spells/death-grips), [_Waltzing Blades_](/en/library/spells/waltzing-blades), [_Guiding Bolt_](/en/library/spells/guiding-bolt), [_Burning Hands_](/en/library/spells/burning-hands)

- **2nd level (3 slots)**: [_Aganazzar’s Scorcher_](/en/library/spells/aganazzars-scorcher), [_Flaming Sphere_](/en/library/spells/flaming-sphere), [_Shatter_](/en/library/spells/shatter)

- **3rd level (3 slots)**: [_Fireball_](/en/library/spells/fireball), [_Erupting Earth_](/en/library/spells/erupting-earth), [_Blinding Smite_](/en/library/spells/blinding-smite)

- **4th level (3 slots)**: [_Wall of Fire_](/en/library/spells/wall-of-fire), [_Vitriolic Sphere_](/en/library/spells/vitriolic-sphere)

- **5th level (3 slots)**: [_Flame Strike_](/en/library/spells/flame-strike), [_Destructive Wave_](/en/library/spells/destructive-wave), [_Immolation_](/en/library/spells/immolation)

- **6th level (2 slots)**: [_Blade Barrier_](/en/library/spells/blade-barrier), [_Harm_](/en/library/spells/harm)

- **7th level (2 slots)**: [_Fire Storm_](/en/library/spells/fire-storm), [_Delayed Blast Fireball_](/en/library/spells/delayed-blast-fireball)

- **8th level (1 slot)**: [_Incendiary Cloud_](/en/library/spells/incendiary-cloud)

- **9th level (1 slot)**: [_Storm of Vengeance_](/en/library/spells/storm-of-vengeance)

#### Overlapping Annihilation

Whenever Rubedo casts a spell of 1st level or higher, it may immediately cast [_Sacred Flame_](/en/library/spells/sacred-flame) targeting a different creature it can see within range.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Rubedo makes **four attacks**, choosing from **Sorcery Ring** or **Dreamcatcher Relics**.  
Rubedo can replace **one** of these attacks with **No Further Questions**, if available.

---

- **Sorcery Ring**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +22 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 45 (6d10 + 12) force damage plus 27 (6d8) fire damage.

---

- **Dreamcatcher Relics**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +22 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 36 (8d8) force damage.

The target must succeed on a **DC 25 Charisma saving throw** or become **[_Laid Bare_](/en/library/items/heirlooms/dreamcatcher#fate-arisen)** for **1 minute**.  
A creature that succeeds is immune to this effect until the next dawn.

On a hit, a **_Cloud of Daggers_** spell is created in the target’s space:

- The spell is cast at **1st level**
- It **does not require concentration**
- The duration is **infinite**
- The spell ends only if dispelled or if Rubedo is destroyed

---

- **No Further Questions** (Recharge 6)  
  Rubedo fires its shotgun in a **20-foot cone**.  
  Each creature in the cone must make a **DC 26 Dexterity saving throw**:

- **Fail**: 90 (20d8) piercing damage and is **pushed 20 feet** directly away from Rubedo
- **Success**: Half damage, no push

---

- **Flame Belch** (Recharge 4–6)  
  Rubedo expels output from its stomach-mouth in a **60-foot-long, 10-foot-wide line**.  
  Each creature in the line must make a **DC 26 Dexterity saving throw**:

- **Fail**: 88 (16d10) force damage plus 22 (4d10) fire damage
- **Success**: Half damage

---

## Reactions

#### Inertial Shift

When Rubedo takes **60 or more damage** from a single source, it may move up to half its speed **without provoking opportunity attacks**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

Rubedo can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Ring Cut.** (Costs 1 Deed)
  Rubedo makes one **Sorcery Ring** attack.

- **Relic Strike.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Rubedo makes one **Dreamcatcher Relics** attack.

- **Snap Step.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Rubedo moves up to 30 feet **without provoking opportunity attacks**.

- **Brace.** (Costs 2 Deeds)  
  Rubedo gains resistance to one damage type until the start of its next turn.

- **Vent Burst.** (Costs 3 Deeds)  
  Rubedo uses **Flame Belch**, if available.

- **Cast a Spell.** (Costs 2 Deeds)  
  Rubedo casts a spell of 5th level or lower.  
  Casting a spell this way still triggers **Overlapping Annihilation**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Rubedo can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. It cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

---

#### Supercritical Core

Rubedo becomes Supercritical for a round.  
Until the start of the next round, Rubedo produces an aura of 15 feet.  
Creatures that enter this area for the first time on a turn or start their turn there take **18 (4d8) force damage** and must make a **DC 25 Constitution saving throw** or roll on [_Red Ruin_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/red-ruin).  
Every foot of movement in this area costs four extra feet of movement.

---

#### Relic Suppression

Rubedo targets up to **three creatures** it can see within **120 feet**.  
Each target must succeed on a **DC 25 Charisma saving throw** or have all heirlooms of rarity **very rare** or higher unattuned until the start of the next round.

---

#### Blades of Wars Past

Terrain in the entire lair erupts with blades, blackened with ruin.

Rubedo’s attack range increases to cover the entire lair.  
Every 5 feet of willing movement deals **1d4 damage** to creatures other than Rubedo.

---

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Fallen Blade Manifestation

Rubedo designates up to **two unoccupied spaces** it can see within **60 feet**.  
At resolution, a **Fallen Blade** manifests in each designated space.

> **Fallen Blade**
>
> | **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Damage Threshold** |
> | --------------- | -------------- | -------------------- |
> | 20              | 220            | 30                   |
>
> - **Size**: Huge object (15-foot space)
> - **Resistances**: Slashing, Piercing
> - **Vulnerabilities**: Force
> - **Immunities**: Poison, Psychic, Necrotic, Radiant
> - **Fixed**: The Fallen Blade cannot move.
> - **Reactive Aura**: The Fallen Blade can take **one reaction per turn**.
> - **Reaction — Entropic Cleave**  
>   _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +17 to hit, reach 10 ft.; one target.  
>   **Hit:** 54 (12d8) slashing damage. The target’s speed becomes **0** until the end of its next turn.
> - **Automatic Swing**  
>   Once per turn, when a creature **enters** the Fallen Blade’s space or **starts its turn** in it, the Fallen Blade can immediately use **Entropic Cleave** (using its reaction).

Fallen Blades last until destroyed or until Rubedo is destroyed.  
Rubedo may have up to **six** Fallen Blades active at once.

If a Large or larger object obstructs the space designated by Rubedo for a Fallen Blade, the Fallen Blade cannot manifest there, and Rubedo takes **30 (6d10) psychic damage**.

#### Storm of Blades

Rubedo starts pulling out the bound blades from its crown.  
Unless restrained or disarmed at any point until the Stratagem resolves, Rubedo gains **Multiattack (12 attacks)** until the end of its next turn.  
All attacks this round must be **Dreamcatcher Relics**.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Wounded (75% HP)

- Rubedo’s **Armor Class is permanently reduced by 1**.
- Rubedo may **declare one Stratagem immediately**, even if it has already declared a Stratagem this turn.
- Creatures in the lair take **1d12 force damage** when they start their turn there.

---

#### Bloodied (50% HP)

- Rubedo’s **Armor Class is permanently reduced by an additional 2** (total –3).
- Rubedo may **declare one Stratagem immediately**, even if it has already declared a Stratagem this turn.
- **Sorcery Ring** deals an additional **18 (4d8) fire damage**.
- Creatures in the lair take an additional **2d12 force damage** when they start their turn there.
- All of Rubedo’s attacks score a critical hit on a roll of **19 or 20**.

---

#### Doomed (25% HP)

- Rubedo’s **Armor Class is permanently reduced by an additional 3** (total –6).
- Rubedo may **declare one Stratagem immediately**, even if it has already declared a Stratagem this turn.
- At initiative count **20** (losing initiative ties), each creature within **40 feet** of Rubedo must make a **DC 24 Dexterity saving throw**.
- Creatures in the lair take an additional **3d12 force damage** when they start their turn there, for a total of **6d12**.
- Rubedo becomes perpetually affected by the spells [_Freedom of Movement_](/en/library/spells/freedom-of-movement), [_Haste_](/en/library/spells/haste), and [_Starpath_](/en/library/spells/starpath).
- All of Rubedo’s attacks cause [_Red Ruin_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/red-ruin) on a hit.

---

#### Slain

When Rubedo is reduced to 0 hit points, it erupts in a cataclysmic explosion.  
Each creature within **150 feet** must make a **DC 30 Dexterity saving throw**:

- **Fail**: 150 (20d12 + 40) force damage and is **knocked prone**
- **Success**: Half damage, not knocked prone

Creatures brought to 0 hit points or less this way are disintegrated along with everything they are carrying.
