## Sun Catcher Cub

_Large Monstrosity, Unaligned_

| **Armor Class** | **Hit Points** | **Speed** |
| --------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| 11              | 30             | 45 ft.    |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 7 (-2)  | 14 (+2) | 6 (-2)  |

- **Senses**: Passive Perception 16, darkvision 60ft
- **Languages**: --
- **Challenge**: 2 (400 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +2

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Sun Catcher Cub makes one Claws attack and one Jaws attack.

- **Claws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.
- **Jaws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 4) piercing damage.

---

## Bonus Actions

#### Hide in Mane

Once per day, the creature can close its flower-like mane and set its Armor Class to 17, at the cost of going blind and deaf until it emerges (bonus action).

- **Senses**: Passive Perception 18, darkvision 60ft
- **Languages**: --
- **Challenge**: 5 (1,800 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +3

---

## Traits

#### Tool User

This creature may use its posterior appendages and tail to grasp weapons and shields, but it will move at 30 ft. when standing on its hind legs.

#### Ironwood Staff

Ironwood Staff: +9 to hit, 21 (4d6 + 7) bludgeoning magical damage. Target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be silenced until the end of their next turn.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The creature makes one weapon attack (Claws or Ironwood Staff) and one Jaws attack.

- **Claws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 17 (3d6 + 6) slashing damage.
- **Ironwood Staff.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 21 (4d6 + 7) bludgeoning damage. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be silenced until the end of their next turn.
- **Jaws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 24 (4d8 + 6) piercing damage.

#### Shrieking Roar

The creature uses its petals to emit a shrieking noise while it roars; all creatures that are not Sun Catchers and can hear it within 100 feet must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of it until the end of their next turn. If the creature has direct line of sight, they are paralyzed instead.

#### Spellcasting

Kipinöitä is a 4th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following wizard spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: [_Mage Hand_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:mage-hand), [_Minor Illusion_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:minor-illusion), [_Booming Blade_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:booming-blade)
- **1st level (4 slots)**: [_Magic Missile_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:magic-missile), [_Silvery Barbs_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:silvery-barbs), [_Thunderwave_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:thunderwave), [_Disguise Self_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:disguise-self)
- **2nd level (2 slots)**: [_Misty Step_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:misty-step), [_Pass Without Trace_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:pass-without-trace)
