## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Terror has **2 legendary deeds per round** (3 while Doomed), and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)
When the Terror fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Terror of Otherness (Aura, 120 ft.)

Creatures of Huge size or smaller suffer dislocation of perception.

- Creatures have **disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws**.
- Creatures cannot gain advantage on saving throws from allied effects.
- The aura is always active while the Terror is conscious.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Terror of Otherness makes **three attacks**, choosing from **Bite**, **Claw**, **Tail**, or **Wing Slam**.  

- **Bite**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 32 (4d10 + 10) piercing damage plus 13 (3d8) force damage.

- **Claw**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 25 (3d8 + 10) slashing damage.

- **Tail**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 30 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 28 (4d8 + 10) bludgeoning damage.  
  The target must succeed on a **DC 22 Strength saving throw** or be pushed 30 feet and knocked prone.

- **Wing Slam**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 30 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 26 (4d6 + 10) bludgeoning damage.  
  Creatures of Huge size or smaller must succeed on a **DC 22 Strength saving throw** or be knocked prone.

#### Breath of Unbecoming (Recharge 5–6)

The Terror exhales fractured reality in a **90-foot cone**.

Each creature in the area must make a **DC 22 Wisdom saving throw**.

- **Fail**: 54 (12d8) force damage and is **Terrified** until the end of its next turn.
- **Success**: Half damage, no terrified condition.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Terror of Otherness can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Detect.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Terror makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.
- **Tail Attack.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Terror makes one Tail attack.
- **Wing Shift.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Terror moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Manifest Shade (Costs 2 Deeds).** The Terror manifests a **Shade of Otherness**.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, the Terror can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. It cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### Shades of Otherness

The terror selects an individual and a target.

The selected creature makes a **DC 22 Wisdom saving throw**, which determines how potently the target's shade will manifest:

- **Fail by 5 or more**: Shade is [_Heroically Awakened_](/en/library/rules/creatures-critters-and-characters/heroic-awakening).
- **Fail**: Shade manifests normally.
- **Success**: Shade manifests bloodied, and with 3 exhaustion levels.
- **Success by 5+**: Shade manifests at 1HP and with 5 exhaustion levels.

A Shade:

- Acts immediately after the Terror
- Has an exact copy of the targets statistics and equipment.
- All of its damage becomes psychic damage.

When a Shade is destroyed, its anchor takes **22 psychic damage** (no save).
> If the target is unavailable, unknown, or cannot be replicated, use a Shade Statlet instead.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Wounded (50% HP) — Angry Rattling Beast

The Terror’s internal scaffolding ruptures.

- Terrain within 60 feet of the Terror becomes difficult terrain.
- **Wing Shift** may be used for **1 legendary deed**.
- The terror inflicts [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions) and [_Burning_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions) with its attacks.
- The terror takes 25 true damage at the start of its turn.

#### Doomed (25% HP) — Lucid Martyr

The containment chamber collapses and the battlefield opens vertically (minimum 120 ft).

- The Terror's gains 80 flying speed.
- Legendary Deeds increase to **3 per round**.
- **Flyby Attacks**: The Terror may move up to its flying speed and make **one attack** during that movement without provoking opportunity attacks.
- The terror gains access to [_True Light_](/en/library/spells/true-light), and can cast it once.
