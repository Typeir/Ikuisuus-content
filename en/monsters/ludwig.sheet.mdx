## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Ludwig has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Long Rest)

When Ludwig fails a saving throw, he can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per long rest**.

#### Bloodboil Aura

Constant. Creatures that start their turn within 15 ft. take **5 true damage**, unavoidable.

#### Regeneration

Ludwig regains **20 HP** at the start of each of his turns. While in Bloodrage, he instead regains **30 HP**.

---

## Bloodrage (1/Short Rest)

As a **bonus action**, Ludwig enters a divine fury for **5 rounds** (30 seconds). While active:

- Gains **50 temporary HP** immediately.
- Gains **advantage on the first attack** he makes each round.
- Can make **one extra attack** as part of his Multiattack.
- All his attacks deal an extra **2d6 fire damage**.
- Gains **15 temporary HP** at the start of each turn.

**If Ludwig neither deals no damage during his turn, Bloodrage ends early.**

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Ludwig makes **three attacks**, which can be any combination of _Mooncleaver_, _Gore Hurl_, _Bloodspire Growth_, _Impale_, or a cantrip. While in Bloodrage, he makes **four**.

- **Impale.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Ludwig impales a grappled creature on a blood spire.  
  _Hit:_ The target takes **4d10 piercing damage** and is **Restrained** until the spire is destroyed (AC 15, 30 HP).  
  A creature can use its action to make a DC 15 Athletics check, freeing itself on a success.

- **Mooncleaver.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 2d10 + 10 slashing + 2d6 cold damage, plus **2d6 fire** if Bloodrage is active.

- **Gore Hurl.**  
  Ludwig throws a **grappled creature** up to 60 ft. The creature lands in a **20-ft. radius** area. All creatures there must make a **DC 19 Dexterity saving throw** or take **6d6 bludgeoning damage** and fall prone. The thrown creature automatically takes the damage and is no longer grappled.

- **Bloodspire Growth (Recharge 5–6).**
  Ludwig charges up to 30 ft. in a straight line. A massive spire of divine blood erupts beneath a target, who must make a **DC 19 Strength saving throw** or take **8d10 piercing damage**, be **Restrained**, and launched atop a **60-ft. tall spire**.
  Creatures in a **20-ft. wide** trail must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take **4d6 fire damage** and be **pushed 5 feet away**.
  The path remains aflame until the start of Ludwig’s next turn, causing **4d6 fire damage** to creatures that enter the path or end their turn there.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Grand Mooncleave (Recharge 6)

**Declare (Turn 1)**: Ludwig channels apocalyptic power into Mooncleaver.  
**Resolve (Turn 2)**: Creatures in a **600-ft.-long**, **40-ft.-wide**, **80-ft.-tall** line must make a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw**.

- **Fail**: 12d10 + 50 force damage and are knocked prone.
- **Success**: Half damage.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Ludwig can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. He cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### Blood Sublimation / Blood Deposition

All creatures of Ludwig's choice in the lair must roll a d20. The creature with the highest result has its blood Sublimate, taking 50 fire damage at the end of their turns, and is affected by the [_Bane_](/en/library/spells/bane) and [_Heat Metal_](/en/library/spells/heat-metal) spells.  
The creature with the lowest result has its blood Deposition, taking 50 cold damage at the end of their turns, and is affected by the [_Slow_](/en/library/spells/slow) spell.

In order to avoid this effect the two creatures must stay within 5ft of each other until the next initiative count 20 (losing ties).

#### Lunar Deluge

A swarming deluge of lunar meteor swarms the lair in 5ft bands stretching horizontally or vertically (Ludwig's choice) across the arena. Each band is separated by 5ft from one another.
Each creature in the affected area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
The affected becomes difficult terrain until the next initiative count 20 (losing ties). Ludwig's next use of Lunar deluge must not follow the same direction of the previous one.

#### Spire Burst

Ludwig commands the blood spires in his lair to erupt violently. Each creature pinned by a spire takes 100 piercing damage and is launched 100ft into the air.
The explosion covers a 30ft radius, forcing all creatures other than the pinned creature to make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or take 6d6 fire damage and 6d6 piercing damage, half as much on a success.

## Legendary Deed: Act

Ludwig can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Attack.** (Costs 1 Deed) Ludwig makes one _Mooncleaver_ attack.
- **Dragstep.** (Costs 1 Deed) Ludwig moves up to 20 ft. and attempts to **grapple each creature** he moves through. Grappled creatures are **Restrained by blood chains**, but his hands remain free.
- **Lesser Spire of Blood (Costs 2 Deeds).** A blood spire erupts beneath a creature within 30 ft. The target must make a **DC 19 Dexterity saving throw** or take **8d10 piercing damage** and be launched 20 ft. into the air (landing prone unless it succeeds a DC 15 Acrobatics check).
- **Cast a Spell (Costs 2 Deeds).** Ludwig casts one spell of 5th level or lower.
- **Severing Blood (Recharge 6) (Costs 2 Deeds).** One creature within 60 ft. must succeed on a **DC 22 Constitution saving throw** or become **Paralyzed** as blood congeals around their veins. While paralyzed this way, the creature also takes **1d10 fire damage** at the start of each of its turns. This burning cannot be extinguished by nonmagical means.  
  At the end of each of its turns, the creature may repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP): Moonfall

Ludwig leaps into the air, floating 300 ft. above the ground, and using gravity magic he declares a grand Moonfall.

- **Declare (Turn 1)**: Ludwig roars, blood and lunar fire erupting from his body as he leaps into the air to call the heavens down. Ludwig gains immunity to stunned, restrained, prone, and paralyzed during this.
- **Charge (Turn 2)**: The sky above the battlefield fractures. A colossal bone-white meteor swarm descends, its shadow covering the whole arena.
- **Resolve (Turn 3)**: Ludwig unleashes [_Meteor Swarm_](/en/library/spells/meteor-swarm), but with its **blast radius covers the whole lair**.

Each creature must succeed on a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw** or take **20d6 fire damage + 20d6 bludgeoning damage**. On a successful save, a creature takes half damage.

#### Doomed (25% HP): Long Night

At the brink of death, Ludwig manifests the **Serpentine Dreams**, a curved moonlight greatsword shimmering with the cerulean light of kuutar, and fights with it alongside Mooncleaver. His stance becomes eerily calm, each movement carrying both crushing weight and blinding speed.

- **Resurgence**: Upon entering this phase, Ludwig gains advantage on all saving throws until the end of his next turn.
- **Overclocked Will**: Whenever ludwig takes the attack action and misses, he can choose to succeed instead. Ludwig can use this feature once per round.
- **Bonus Attack**: Ludwig can make one additional Serpentine Dreams attack as a **bonus action** on his turn.

#### Serpentine Dreams

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ 2d12 + 10 force + 4d8 cold damage.

#### Mooncleave (Recharge 5–6)

Ludwig sweeps Serpentine Dreams in a great lunar arc.
All creatures in a **30-foot cone** must make a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw**.

- **Failure**: 4d8 cold + 4d8 force damage.
- **Success**: Half damage.

Additionally, Ludwig gains access to the following features:

#### Surgical Lunge

Ludwig may replace one attack with a 20 ft.-long, 5 ft.-wide line. This includes attacks from his legendary deeds.

- Each creature in the line must succeed on a **DC 20 Dexterity saving throw** or take damage equal to Serpentine Dreams’ base weapon dice (2d12 + 2d8).
- Ludwig moves along the line without provoking opportunity attacks.

#### Iron Reserve

Once per round, Ludwig may replace a Mooncleaver attack with a Serpentine Dreams Strike. He gains **temporary hit points equal to the damage rolled** (dice only, no modifiers).

#### Saint’s Reversal (Reaction)

When Ludwig is targeted by a melee attack, he may contest the attack roll with one of his own.

- If he wins, the attack misses and he makes one Serpentine Dreams Strike against the attacker.
- If he loses, resolve the attack normally.

#### Saint’s Footwork

After Ludwig makes a melee attack (hit or miss), he may move **5 feet**. This movement can provoke opportunity attacks.

#### Martial Domination

When Ludwig hits a creature with a melee attack, that creature must succeed on a **DC 20 Charisma saving throw** or suffer the effects of [_Bane_](/en/library/spells/bane) for 1 minute.

- The target may repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.
- Once a creature saves against this effect, it is immune to Ludwig’s Martial Domination for 24 hours.

#### Hesitation is Defeat (Legendary Deed, Costs 2)

Ludwig makes one Serpentine Dreams Strike. If the attack hits, he regains his reaction.
