## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Abandoned Old War Machine has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Null Core
When reduced to 0 hit points, the War Machine erupts in a 300-foot-radius nullwave (blocked by solid terrain).  
All ongoing spells or magical effects in the area end instantly.  
After a three turn delay, the war machine explodes.
Each creature within range must make a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **180 (40d8)** force damage and becoming **blinded** until the end of its next turn on a failure, or half as much damage on a success.

#### Acid Overflow
When the War Machine takes damage, acid leaks from its seams.  
All creatures within 5 ft. of the machine take **8 (2d8)** acid damage and each has to succeed on a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw** or gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

#### Rust Breaker
The War Machine scores a critical hit on a roll of 20; this threshold is lowered by 1 for each stack of **[_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** on the target.

#### Construct Nature
The War Machine does not require air, food, drink, or sleep.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The War Machine makes three attacks with any combination of its weapons.

- **Pseudopod Slam.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 18 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning and acid damage.  
The target gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)** and must succeed on a **DC 19 Strength saving throw** or be **knocked prone**.

- **Acid Shotgun.**  
The War Machine fires a cone of acidic shrapnel in a **30-foot cone**.  
Each creature in the area must make a **DC 19 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **37 (8d8)** acid damage on a failure or half as much on a success.  
Creatures that fail are **blinded** until the end of their next turn and gain **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

- **Nulled Blade (Recharge 4–6).**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 22 (4d10 + 9) force damage.  
The target must make a **DC 20 Charisma saving throw** or lose all magical effects currently affecting it, taking **10 force damage** for each effect removed and for each stack of **Erosion** on it.  
The creature becomes **immune to those effects** until it finishes a short or long rest and is **vulnerable to damage from the Nulled Blade** until the end of its next turn.

- **Barely Functional Disambiguator (Recharge 4–6).**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 2d6 + 9 slashing plus 2d12 necrotic damage.  
After hitting, the War Machine chooses one effect:  

- **Effect — The Toll**: The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or fail one death saving throw. It also loses all magical effects currently affecting it.  
- **Effect — The Cave**: The target must succeed on a **DC 19 Charisma saving throw** or be **stunned, paralyzed, put to sleep, and petrified** (petrification completes after two rounds). This effect cannot be repeated and can only be removed by a curse removal.  

The War Machine heals **half of the damage dealt** with this attack.  
After each attack with the Disambiguator, it must succeed on a **DC 25 Constitution saving throw** or immediately cast **[_Fireball_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:fireball)** as an 8th-level spell centered on itself and automatically fail the save.

**Thruster Axe-Kick.**  
_Special Melee Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, range 10–30 ft., one target.  
The War Machine launches forward on hydraulic thrusters, landing 5 ft. from its target.  
_Hit:_ 3d10 + 6 slashing damage.  
If the target is Large or smaller, it must make a **DC 19 Strength saving throw** or be **knocked prone**.  
A target that takes damage also gains **1 stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Abandoned Old War Machine can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Pseudopod Lash.** (Costs 1 Deed) The War Machine makes one **Pseudopod Slam** attack.  
- **Null Spark.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The War Machine emits a brief nullwave; all ongoing magical effects on creatures within 15 ft. are suppressed until the start of its next turn.
