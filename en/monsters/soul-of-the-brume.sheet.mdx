# Soul of the Brume

_Medium Humanoid (Empyrean), Lawful Evil_

| **Armor Class**      | **Hit Points**   | **Speed** |
| -------------------- | ---------------- | --------- |
| 24 (Tombsteel armor) | 270 (30d8 + 135) | 30 ft.    |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 22 (+6) | 18 (+4) | 20 (+5) |

- **Saving Throws**: Constitution +13, Intelligence +14, Wisdom +12, Charisma +13
- **Skills**: Arcana +14, Religion +14, Insight +12, Perception +12
- **Damage Resistances**: Fire, Lightning, Acid, Radiant.
- **Condition Immunities**: Terrified
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 22
- **Languages**: Common, Empyrean
- **Challenge**: 23 (50,000 XP)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +7

---

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Soul of the Brume has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Entropic Sarcophagus

Soul of the Brume is encased in a sealed tombsteel shell designed to endure annihilation rather than evade it.

- Soul of the Brume has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks
- Soul of the Brume has advantage on saving throws made to resist being pushed, pulled, or knocked prone
- Soul of the Brume cannot benefit from bonuses to movement speed granted by nonmagical effects

---

#### Grave Chill

Soul of the Brume’s tombsteel armor holds up to 12 charges.  
When a creature hits Soul of the Brume with a melee weapon attack, the attacker immediately takes 15 cold damage as frozen rime shards erupt from the armor. This effect requires no action or reaction.

- Each activation consumes 1 charge
- Charges regenerate at midnight, recovering 6 + 1d6 charges
- This cold damage ignores resistance from nonmagical or mundane sources

---

#### Ferocious Rider

The Soul of the Brume's mount, a Lunar Chimera, can take the Attack action even when used as a mount by Soul of the Brume.  
While mounted, the Soul of the Brume's mount gains a +7 bonus to attack and damage rolls, and resistance to all damage.

---

#### Doctrine of the Brume

Soul of the Brume projects a disciplined doctrinal aura in a 20-foot radius while conscious.

For each other rider of the Brume within range, all riders gain the following benefits (bonuses stack):

- +3 bonus to all saving throws
- +2 bonus to Armor Class

**Formation Thresholds**

- Three active auras:
  - Resistance to all damage
  - Immunity to forced movement
  - Cannot be knocked prone
  - Riders and Mounts automatically Disengage at the start of their turns

- Two active auras:
  - Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing, fire, cold, lightning, and necrotic damage
  - Advantage on saving throws against being pushed, pulled, or knocked prone

- One or fewer active auras:
  - No resistances or movement immunities from this feature

A rider’s aura is suppressed only while that rider is incapacitated.  
Paralyzed, stunned, or unconscious creatures do not contribute to the doctrine.

---

#### Calculated Ascent

While mounted, Soul of the Brume ignores disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Scala ad Infernum

**Declare**: Soul of the Brume designates a 30-foot-radius, 80-foot-tall vertical column within 120 feet.
The ground in that 30 foot radius becomes searingly hot.
Every feet of movement within the column costs an additional 4ft of movement. creatures take 10 fire damage per 5 feet moved within the column's ground area.

**Resolve (start of Soul of the Brume’s next turn)**:  
The staircase erupts in a blast of brimstone, rising to the sky in a pillar of fire.
Creatures in the area take 45 (10d8) fire damage and is restrained until the end of their next turn.

The staircase stays as an obscuring, light emitting pillar of fire until the end of the Soul's next turn.
It sheds bright light in a 60-foot radius and dim light for an additional 60 feet.
When the pillar ends, all creatures on top of it fall to the ground below.
Damage from this fall is fire damage instead of bludgeoning.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

Soul of the Brume can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Arcane Lash.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Soul of the Brume makes one melee weapon attack.

- **Measured Reposition.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Soul of the Brume moves up to 20 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.

- **Forced Elevation.** (Costs 2 Deeds)  
  One creature within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 22 Strength saving throw or be lifted 20 feet into the air.

---

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP)

While Soul of the Brume is bloodied and not incapacitated:

- Spell save DC increases to 23
- Fire damage dealt by Soul of the Brume ignores resistance
- Every time the soul of the brume inflicts damage, they also inflict the [_Burning_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#burning) condition.

If Soul of the Brume becomes incapacitated, these benefits end immediately.
