# Golden Pride

_Large Elemental, Neutral_

| **Armor Class**    | **Hit Points**       | **Speed**          |
| ------------------ | -------------------- | ------------------ |
| 16 (natural armor) | 170 ([% 16d8 +98 %]) | 40 ft., fly 40 ft. |

| **STR** | **DEX** | **CON** | **INT** | **WIS** | **CHA** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- |
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 8 (−1)  | 12 (+1) | 20 (+5) |

- **Saving Throws**: Dex +6, Con +9, Cha +9
- **Skills**: Insight +5, Intimidation +9
- **Damage Resistances**: Radiant
- **Damage Immunities**: —
- **Condition Immunities**: Frightened
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
- **Languages**: Understands the languages of its summoner but cannot speak.
- **Challenge**: 11 (7,200 XP)
- **Tier Bonus**: +4

---

## Traits

#### Aura of Splendor

The Golden Pride emanates an aura of radiant authority in a 10-foot radius. Creatures of the pride's choice within the aura gain a **+1 bonus to saving throws**. This bonus stacks with the auras of other Golden Prides (maximum +5).

#### Unshakable

The first time the Golden Pride fails a saving throw, it succeeds instead. Once per Recovery.

---

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The pride makes two **Claw** attacks and one **Bite** attack, or casts two cantrips.

#### Claw

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 9 ([% 1d8 +4 slashing %]) plus 7 ([% 2d6 holy %]).

#### Bite

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 ([% 2d6 +4 piercing %]) plus 7 ([% 2d6 holy %]).

#### Roar of Majesty (1/recovery)

The pride looses a thunderous roar. Each creature of its choice within 20 feet must succeed on a **DC 17 Charisma saving throw** or take 10 ([% 3d6 holy %]) and be **frightened** until the end of its next turn.

#### Crown of Dominion (1/recovery)

_Range: 120 feet, Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute._ One humanoid of the pride's choice that the pride can see within range must succeed on a **DC 17 Charisma saving throw** or become **charmed** by the pride for the duration. While the target is charmed in this way, a burning halo of golden light appears above its head. The charmed target must use a Major Action before moving on each of its turns to make a melee attack against a creature other than itself that the pride mentally chose. If there are no creatures, the target takes 8 ([% 2d6 holy %]) and can act normally. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

#### Overwhelming Presence (1/recovery)

The pride fixes its gaze upon a creature within 30 feet. That creature must make a **DC 17 Charisma saving throw**. On a failed save, the creature is **stunned** and **prone** until the end of its next turn.

---

#### Spellcasting

The Golden Pride is a **7th-level spellcaster**. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will)**: _Word of Radiance_, _Thaumaturgy_
- **1st level (4 slots)**: _Command_, _Guiding Bolt_, _Shield of Faith_
- **2nd level (3 slots)**: _Hold Person_, _Zone of Truth_
- **3rd level (3 slots)**: _Fear_, _Spirit Guardians_
- **4th level (1 slot)**: _Banishment_
