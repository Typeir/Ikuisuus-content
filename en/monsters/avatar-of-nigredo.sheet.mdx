## Traits

#### Avatar Form
This is a splinter of Nigredo’s true body — a localized knot of flesh and clockwork. It does not have Endless Form, but leaves behind **rot-slick terrain** (10-ft. radius around it is difficult terrain).

#### Spore Cloud Aura
Creatures starting their turn within 10 ft. must succeed on a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** or immediately roll on the [Ventralis Rot Table](/en/library/monsters/nigredo).

#### Sunlight Vulnerability
While in direct sunlight, the Avatar has disadvantage on attack rolls.

## Actions

#### Multiattack
The Avatar makes two **Impaling Claw** attacks or one **Impaling Claw** plus **Flyburst**.

- **Impaling Claw.**  
_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 2d10+5 piercing + 2d8 necrotic. Target must succeed on a **DC 16 Strength save** or be grappled (escape DC 16). Grappled creatures take 2d8 necrotic damage at the start of their turn.

- **Flyburst.**  
A cloud of corpse-flies erupts in a 15-ft. radius. Creatures in the area must make a **DC 16 Constitution save** or take **4d8 necrotic damage** and be **blinded** until the start of the Avatar’s next turn. On success, they take half damage and avoid blindness.

#### Exhale the Rot (Recharge 6).  
The Avatar exhales spores in a 30-ft. cone. Each creature in that area must make a **DC 16 Constitution save**, taking **5d8 necrotic damage** and triggering **Ventralis Rot** even on a success.

---

## Bonus Actions

#### Black Mist Step (Recharge 4–6)
The Avatar moves up to **60 feet in a straight line**, ignoring difficult terrain and **not provoking opportunity attacks**.  
Each creature in the line must succeed on a **DC 16 Constitution saving throw** or take **4d8 necrotic damage** half as much on a success.

#### Crushing Weight
If the Avatar is grappling a creature, it can use a bonus action to crush them, dealing **2d8 bludgeoning damage** automatically.

---

## Reactions

#### Rotting Grasp
When a creature within reach misses the Avatar with a melee attack, it may make one Impaling Claw attack against that creature.

## Spellcasting (Limited)

The Avatar is an innate spellcaster (save DC 16, +6 to hit). It can cast the following spells, requiring no components:

- **At will**: [_Toll the Dead_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:toll-the-dead), [_Chill Touch_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:chill-touch)
- **1/day each**: [_Blight_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:blight), [_Hold Person_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:hold-person)
