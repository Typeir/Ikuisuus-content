# Lunar Chimera

The Lunar Chimera is a massive, three-headed monstrosity that roams the lunar surface.
Each of its heads represents a different aspect of the moon's power: the lion embodies strength and ferocity, the serpent symbolizes cunning and agility, and the goat represents wisdom and magic.
Together, they form a terrifying creature that commands both physical prowess and arcane might.

## Traits

## Shared Body

The chimera is comprised of three entities that share a single body. Each entity has its own stat block, actions, and traits.

## Area Effects (Shared)

When the Lunar Chimera is subjected to an **area-of-effect**, the effect applies to **only one** of the three stat blocks.  
Resolve the saving throw using the **least successful save** among them.

## Legendary Deeds (Shared)

The Lunar Chimera has **3 legendary deeds per round**, shared across its three stat blocks, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Lunar Chimera can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Savage Swipe.** (Costs 1 Deed) The lion makes one **claw** attack.
- **Constriction Flick.** (Costs 1 Deed) The serpent makes one **Tail Strike**.
- **Baleful Chant (Costs 2 Deeds).** The goat casts a spell.
- **Drag and Rend (Costs 2 Deeds).** One creature grappled by the lion takes 22 (3d8 + 9) slashing damage, and suffers 8 [_Bleeding_](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions#bleeding) stacks.

### Traits

#### One with the Moon

This member is immune to magical effects created by the other chimeric members, and vice versa.
When the Chimera recieves healing or gains temporary hit points from any source, they can choose which member benefits from it, provided that member is still alive.

#### Antimagic Fur

The lion is resistant to damage from spells and has advantage on saving throws against spells.

#### Night Stalker

The lion can see through magical darkness as if it were normal darkness.

#### Undying Resolve

When the lion is reduced to 0 hit points, but members of the Lunar Chimera are still active, it can still move, but cannot take actions or reactions of any kind.

#### Metabolic engine

When the Goat or Serpent dies, the lion enters a raging state. While raging, it is resistant to all damage, and immune to psychic damage.
Additionally, it's attacks deal an extra 8 damage of their type per dead member.
If any member is revived, the rage persists, and, if the same member were to fall again, the damage bonuses stack.
The damage bonus from this trait applies to legendary deeds as well, and cannot exceed +32 damage.

### Actions

#### Multiattack

The lion makes three attacks: two with its claws and one with its bite, or it can use its Crushing Pounce and make one bite attack.

- **Claw**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +17 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 22 (3d8 + 9) slashing damage.

- **Bite**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +17 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 26 (3d10 + 9) piercing damage.

- #### Crushing Pounce
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +17 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
  _Hit:_ 18 (2d8 + 9) bludgeoning damage, and the target is **grappled** (escape DC 24).  
  Until the grapple ends, the target is **restrained**.

### Actions

#### Tail Strike

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 19 (2d10 + 8) bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) poison damage.  
The target must succeed on a **DC 22 Constitution saving throw** or become **Paralyzed** until the end of its next turn.
Paralyzed creatures take maximized damage from this attack. This doubles on critical hits.

#### Bile spray (Recharge 5–6)

As a Bonus Action, the serpent can exhale a spray of corrosive bile in a 15-foot cone.
Each creature in that area must make a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw**, taking 27 (6d8) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
Creatures that fail the save are also **blinded** until the end of their next turn.

#### Vitriolic blood

When the lion or goat dies, the serpent can, on each of it's subsequent turns, use it's reaction to emit a Bile Spray from the dead member's body.
The spray originates from the dead member's position, and uses the serpent's saving throw DC.

### Spell Point Casting

#### Chimeric Spellcasting

The Lunar Chimera (goat) is a 15th-level spellcaster. It has **80 spell points**, and can spend **up to 8 spell points per round**.
Spell level corresponds to spell point cost, and lower-level spells may be **upcast** by spending additional points, following the spell’s normal upcasting rules.

Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (**spell save DC 22**, **+14** to hit with spell attacks). It has the following spells prepared:

The Lunar Chimera’s spellcasting is performed by the **goat**, but the effects of its magic may be exploited by the **entire chimeric congregation**.

When the goat casts a spell that allows **subsequent actions, bonus actions, reactions, or ongoing effects** (such as _Vampiric Touch_, _Bigby’s Hand_, _Storm Sphere_, _Crown of Stars_, or similar spells), **any chimeric member** may use those options on its turn, provided the spell’s normal rules are followed.

- The spell is still considered **cast by the goat**.
- **Concentration**, if required, is maintained solely by the goat.
- The spell ends immediately if the goat loses concentration or is incapacitated.

All spells cast in this way are considered **magical effects created by the goat** for the purposes of _One with the Moon_.

### Actions

#### Lunar Reconstitution

At the **start of the goat’s turn**, if any other chimeric member (lion or serpent) is at **0 hit points**, the goat may reconstitute them.

When it does so, the goat expends its **action**, **bonus action**, and **reaction** for the round.

The reconstituted member immediately returns to life with **half hit points** and acts normally on its next turn.

This effect does not require concentration, does not consume spell points, and cannot be counterspelled.
