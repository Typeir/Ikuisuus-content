# Cerithol Lunam

_Large Monstrosity, Unaligned_

<BlendedImage src='/library/images/monsters/cerithol.webp' alt='Cerithol' />

A **Cerithol Lunam** is a grotesque, fleshy orb that dwells deep within the corpse-womb of the half-slain moon goddess. Half-Blind and bulbous, it moves on four bloated intestine-like ungulate hooves and is covered in translucent, sticky mucus. When threatened, it splits open to reveal a writhing core of veins, flesh and razor-sharp bones that lashes out across impossible distance. It casts spells from holes that open briefly to hiss, bleed, or scream, uses malformed apendages to communicate in windings, and its acidic blood burns like starfire.

## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

The Cerithol Lunam has **1 legendary deed per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Lunar Vessel

While closed, the Cerithol Lunam cannot cast spells of level 3 or higher but gains a +4 bonus to AC and is immune to being charmed or stunned.

#### Open Form (Minor Action)

The Cerithol opens fully, revealing its vasculomuscular system. It loses the AC bonus from **Lunar Vessel**, but gains access to its _Pseudopod Surge_ attack and spellcasting above 3rd level.

#### Spell Point Casting

- The Cerithol has **50 spell points**

- The Cerithol Lunam can spend **5 spell points per round**, choosing as many spells as it wants for that cost if open (barring cantrips).
  Else it can only cast one spell, using the points to cast the following spells. Spell levels correspond to point costs. The apex cerithol is a 18th level spellcaster.

#### Heightened Reactions

The Cerithol Lunam can take up to **3 reactions per round**, but no more than one per turn.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Cerithol Lunam makes two _Tentacle Whip_ attacks or one _Pseudopod Surge_ if open.

- **Tentacle Whip.**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_: +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 20 ([% 3d10 +7 slashing %]).

- **Pseudopod Surge (Open Only).**  
  _Melee Weapon Attack_: +12 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target.  
  **Hit**: 40 ([% 5d12 +7 bludgeoning %]). The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Strength saving throw** or be hurled up to **60 feet** in a direction of the Cerithol’s choosing and knocked prone. Colliding with terrain deals [% 3d6 additional %].

## Reactions

**Bloodlash Rebuke (1st-level Unique Spell)**  
 _When a creature within 60 ft. succeeds on a saving throw or hits with an attack roll_  
 The Cerithol Lunam sprays burning acidic blood from a pulsing orifice.  
 The creature must succeed on a **DC 18 Dexterity saving throw** or take **[% 4d10 %] damage**: **[% 2d10 chemical %]** and **[% 2d10 fire %]**. If it fails, it must **reroll the triggering d20**, using the new result. The Cerithol gains **advantage** on the next roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Cerithol Lunam can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

#### Cantrip Volley (Costs 1 Deed)

The Cerithol Lunam targets up to **10 creatures** within 120 feet that it can sense. It casts **Envenom**, **Chill**, or **Toll the Dead** on each of them as part of the same action, as a level 5 spellcaster.
