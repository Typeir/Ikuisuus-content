### Traits

##### Erosive Body

Any creature that hits the Sludgeling with a melee attack or touches it takes **3 (1d6)** acid damage and gains **1 stack of Erosion**.

##### Construct Nature

The Sludgeling does not require air, food, drink, or sleep.

---

### Actions

##### Rusting bite

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 8 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage.  
The target’s Dexterity score is reduced by **2** (minimum 1) as its joints harden and corrode.  
Each hit also applies **one stack of [_Erosion_](/en/library/rules/illnesses/erosion)**.  
A creature reduced to **Dexterity 0** by this effect becomes **petrified**, its body turning into a crusted mineral shell.  
The Dexterity reduction lasts until the creature finishes a **long rest** or receives **greater restoration**.

**Petrifying bile**
The forlorn sludgeling eviscerates out in a 15ft cone of guts, acid and muck. Each creature must make a DC 13 dexterity saving throw or become **petrified**.
The saving throw difficulty grows by 1 per erosion stack on the target.
