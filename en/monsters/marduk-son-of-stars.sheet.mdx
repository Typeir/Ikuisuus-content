# Marduk, Son of Stars

## Traits

##### Legendary Deeds
Marduk has **3 deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

##### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)
When Marduk fails a saving throw, he can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

##### Segmented Recharge

Marduk rolls **one recharge die** at the start of each of his turns.

- Abilities belonging to the **other aspect** may be recharged on a roll of 4-6.
- If Marduk recharges abilities belonging to the **other aspect**, he may recharge two abilities this turn,
  Otherwise he may only recharge one ability belonging to his **current aspect**.

##### Refusal

Marduk cannot benefit from healing effects other than his own traits.

### Traits

##### Mortal Persistence

At the start of each of Marduk’s turns, he gains **25 temporary hit points**.  
If Marduk would be reduced to 0 hit points in this form, he instead drops to **1 hit point** and immediately shifts into **Dragon Aspect**. This effect can occur once per encounter.

##### Unwavering Blows

Marduk's weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20, and ignore resistance to damage.
If the target is **terrified of Marduk**, Marduk scores a critical hit on a roll of 17-20.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Test of Will

**Declare:**
Marduk draws a single, unwavering line of judgment across the battlefield.

A **15-foot-wide, 120-foot-long line** extends from Marduk in a direction of his choosing and remains until the start of Marduk’s next turn.
While this line persists, it is considered **difficult terrain**.

When the line appears, creatures within 300ft of Marduk **that are not within the line** are immediately pushed 20 feet away from the line.
A creature that **enters the line for the first time on a turn** or **starts or ends its turn within the line** may immediately **remove 1 stack of Dwarfed Will**.
A creature that **leaves the line** for the first time on a turn must succeed on a DC 25 **Charisma saving throw** or become **Frightened of Marduk** until the end of its next turn, and gains 1 stack of **Dwarfed Will**.

**Resolve (Start of Marduk’s next turn):**
All creatures within the line must make a DC 25 Charisma saving throw. Roll **55d10** force damage. Divide the total damage evenly among all creatures in the line (round down).
Each creature takes its share on a failed save, or half its share on a successful save.
Creatures that take damage from this effect lose one stack of **Dwarfed Will** (to a minimum of zero), two if they succeeded on the saving throw.
Creatures that have no stacks of **Dwarfed Will** automatically succeed on this saving throw, and are resistant to this damage.
Creatures outside the line when the effect resolves take 20d6 psychic damage.

#### Snuff the Unworthy

**Declare:**
Marduk targets one creature he can see within 60 feet.
The target must succeed on a DC 25 Constitution saving throw or be **blinded** until the end of Marduk’s next turn.

**Resolve (start of Marduk’s next turn):**
The target takes 62 (14d8) psychic damage per stack of **Dwarfed Will** it has. If the target has more than 3 stacks of **Dwarfed Will**, the damage is maximized.
The target loses all stacks of **Dwarfed Will** if it survives.

Marduk cannot use Snuff the Unworthy if he has not used **Test of Will** in the last minute.
