## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Xanthous has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of his turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)

When Xanthous fails a saving throw, he can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Poison of the Mind

Xanthous's very presence causes the mind to unravel and disassemble itself. If a creature interacts with Xanthous in any way, such as speaking, touching, or being struck by him in any way, they must succeed on a **DC 22 Wisdom saving throw** or be affected by [_Poison of the Mind_](/en/library/rules/life-blood-and-ruin/illnesses/poison-of-the-mind). Characters immune to sickness and curses ignore this feature, and take 6d6 psychic damage instead.

#### Twin Reactions

Xanthous can take up to **2 reactions per round**, but no more than one per turn.

#### Angels

Xanthous can summon and control up to four angels, each taking their turn directly after his own.
Each angel shares Xanthous's hit point pool, and when an angel takes damage, Xanthous takes equally as much "indirect" damage.
When Xanthous is reduced to 0 hit points, all angels immediately perish.
All angels are tethered to Xanthous by a 60 ft. long, sulphurous umbilical cord.

#### Dual Spellcasting

Xanthous is a 20th-level spellcaster. He has the following spells slots 1st (4), 2nd (3), 3rd (3), 4th (3), 5th (3), 6th (2), 7th (2), 8th (1), 9th (1).
Xanthous always casts spells as though he were under the effect of the [_Twin Spell_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/sorcerer) metamagic option, targeting two creatures instead of one. if the spell imposes an area, Xanthous instead uses [_Heighten Spell_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/sorcerer) on all affected creatures instead of one.
His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 26, +18 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips**: [_Poison Spray_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:poison-spray), [_Eldritch Blast_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:eldritch-blast), [_Minor Illusion_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:minor-illusion)

- **1st level (4 slots)**: [_Silvery Barbs_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:silvery-barbs), [_Shield_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:shield), [_Grease_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:grease)
- **2nd level (3 slots)**: [_Acid Arrow_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:acid-arrow), [_Hold Person_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:hold-person), [_Misty Step_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:misty-step)
- **3rd level (3 slots)**: [_Fear_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:fear), [_Stinking Cloud_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:stinking-cloud), [_Sulphurous Sphere_](/en/library/spells/sulfurous-sphere)
- **4th level (3 slots)**: [_Phantasmal Killer_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:phantasmal-killer), [_Greater Invisibility_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:greater-invisibility), [_Phantasmal Force_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:phantasmal-force)
- **5th level (3 slots)**: [_Cloudkill_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:cloudkill), [_Dominate Person_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:dominate-person), [_Enervation_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:enervation), [_Forbidden Sun_](/en/library/spells/forbidden-sun)
- **6th level (2 slots)**: [_Psychic Crush_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:psychic-crush), [_Sunbeam_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:sunbeam), [_Eyebite_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:eyebite), [_Disintegrate_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:disintegrate)
- **7th level (2 slots)**: [_Psychic Scream_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:psychic-scream), [_Power Word Pain_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:power-word-pain), [_Divine Word_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:divine-word)
- **8th level (1 slot)**: [_Mind Blank_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:mind-blank), [_Incendiary Cloud_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:incendiary-cloud)
- **9th level (1 slot)**: [_Foresight_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:foresight), [_Meteor Swarm_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:meteor-swarm)

- **Angelical Link**: Xanthous can summon angels with which he shares a health pool. Anytime an angel takes damage, Xanthous takes half as much "indirect" damage.

- **Unbreakable Mind**: If Xanthous isn't dealt at least 100 direct damage in a round, he regenerates all the damage he took, up to half his maximum hit points, including angelical link damage.

- **Weakened Form.** Xanthous is currently diminished.
  - He is vulnerable to all damage he is not resistant or immune to.
  - He cannot cast spells of **6th level or higher**.
  - His **Multiattack** is reduced to **three strikes** instead of five.
  - He suffers a **–7 penalty** to all saving throws, attack rolls, and skill checks.
  - His **AC is reduced by 7**.
  - His attacks deal damage only and do not impose additional effects (no saving throws for exhaustion, restraint, fear, etc.).
  - Xanthous begins combat with **no angels under his command**.
  - This condition lasts until the next day.

- **Scourge of the Mind**: Xanthous immediately knows when one is withholding information, as it understands when something has not reached the cusp of its being, even words, phrases, or truths, even if he does not know the truth itself. Additionally, Xanthous always knows each creature's vulnerabilities or least developed areas.

- **Aura of Devotion**: Any creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of Xanthous must succeed on a DC 24 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the [_Charm Person_](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:charm-person) spell until the end of its next turn. A creature can only be affected by this once every minute.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Xanthous strikes five times, once with a claw, twice with his tendrils, and twice with his Eye Beams.

- **Tombsteel Claws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 25 (2d12 + 10) piercing damage plus 8 (4d4) cold damage. The target must succeed on a **DC 24 Constitution saving throw** or gain 1 level of exhaustion as tombsteel fragments bite into their marrow.

- **Tendrils.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +18 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 31 (4d10 + 10) piercing damage. The target is grappled (escape DC 24). While grappled, the target is also restrained and silenced (cannot provide verbal or somatic components).

- **Eye Beams.** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +17 to hit, range 60/120 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 28 (3d12 + 10) radiant damage. The target must succeed on a **DC 24 Wisdom saving throw** or suffer psychic backlash, becoming terrified until the end of its next turn. If the creature was already terrified, it instead takes an additional 31 (5d12) psychic damage.

#### Spellcasting

Xanthous casts a spell.

---

## Bonus Actions

**Mind Wraps**: As a bonus action, Xanthous can grab (Athletics contest) a creature and grapple them. Xanthous can grab up to 4 creatures this way. Xanthous must succeed on Concentration checks to keep the grapples.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Xanthous can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. He cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### First Cross / Second Cross

Xanthous projects a 45-degree cross that's 15 feet wide and 200 feet long. The cross is made of egg-shaped **acid** or **cold** bombs. These count as difficult terrain, and explode when a creature enters their space for the first time in a turn, forcing them to make a **DC 24 Dexterity saving throw** or take 21 (6d6) damage of the corresponding type and fall prone.
Each cross counts as a separate deed.

---

## Legendary Deed: Act

Xanthous can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Quick Reflexes.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  Xanthous regains two reactions.

- **Awaken Angel.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  One of Xanthous's four angel-appendages drops from their shell, tethered to it by a 60 ft. long cord. Angels count as invocations and take turns after Xanthous.

- **Enact Angelical Will (Costs 1 Deed).**  
  As a legendary deed, an awakened angel takes a turn. During this turn, the angel has a +10 bonus to every attack roll, damage roll, saving throw, and skill check.

- **Loving Embrace of the Miracle.** (Costs 1 Deed)  
  If a creature is directly below Xanthous, it must succeed on a **DC 19 Wisdom saving throw**, regardless if Xanthous is currently weakened or not. If the creature fails, Xanthous assumes total control of it until the end of their next turn.

**Pure Radiance** (Recharge 5-6) (Costs 2 Deeds):

_Xanthous emits a pulse of psychic energy in a 60-foot cone._

Each creature in that area must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 26).
On a failed save, a creature takes 4d10 psychic damage, and 4d10 radiant damage and is charmed by Xanthous for 1 minute.
While charmed in this way, the creature regards Xanthous as a trusted friend to be heeded and protected.
This effect does not end early if Xanthous or his allies harm the creature or their allies, as the creature believes they must have a reason for it.
The charmed target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and is not charmed.
