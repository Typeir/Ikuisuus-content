## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds
The Platonian Hunter Commander has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### End Of Life Cycle

When reduced to 0 hit points, the hunter erupts in a 300-foot-radius explosion (blocked by solid terrain).  
After a three turn delay, the hunter explodes.
Each creature within range must make a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **180 (40d8)** force damage and becoming **blinded** until the end of its next turn on a failure, or half as much damage on a success.

#### Multi-Purpose Tool

The Commander is proficient in all skills and has expertise in **Intimidation**.

#### Multi-Dimensional Maneuvers

The Commander automatically disengages at the start of each of its turns.

#### Innate Spellcasting

The Commander can cast each of the following spells once per day without components:

- [_Shield_](/en/library/spells/shield) — **1 spell point**
- [_Misty Step_](/en/library/spells/misty-step) — **2 spell points**
- [_Greater Invisibility_](/en/library/spells/greater-invisibility) — **4 spell points**

#### Spell Point Reserve

The Commander has **5 spell points**, which can be spent to cast its innate spells again.

#### Clockwork Reactions

The Commander does not expend its reaction when making opportunity attacks, but it can only make **one per target per round**.

#### Maneuvers

The Commander has **4 superiority dice (d10s)**. Maneuver save DC = **20** (8 + Proficiency 5 + Strength 7). It knows the following maneuvers:

- **Commander's Strike.** Order an ally to attack as a reaction, adding the superiority die to damage.
- **Maneuvering Attack.** On hit, add the die to damage and allow an ally to move up to half speed without provoking opportunity attacks from the target.
- **Disarming Attack.** On hit, add the die to damage; target must succeed on a Strength save or drop one held item.
- **Parry.** Use reaction to reduce melee damage taken by the die + Dexterity modifier.
- **Riposte.** When a creature misses the Commander with a melee attack, it may attack back as a reaction, adding the die to damage.

#### Unyielding Resolve

The commander regains one superiority die at the start of his turn.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

The Commander makes two attacks with **Bound Athena** and one with the **Disambiguator**, or any three weapon attacks of its choice.

- **Bound Athena.** _(Melee Weapon, Two-Handed)_
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 27 (3d10 + 10) radiant damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage.

###### Athena's Judgment (1/Day per creature)

On a hit, the target must succeed on a **DC 21 Wisdom saving throw** or suffer the following:

- **Vulnerability** to all damage from Bound Athena for 1 minute.
- The Commander gains **truesight** on that target for the same duration.
- All magical effects currently affecting the target (beneficial or harmful) are **immediately siphoned**. These effects end on the target and are transferred to the Commander, persisting for their remaining duration as though it were the original target.

- **Disambiguator (Recharge 4–6).** _(Melee Weapon, One-Handed)_
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 21 (3d6 + 10) slashing damage plus 20 (3d12) necrotic damage.
  On hit, the Commander chooses one effect:

  - **The Toll.** Target must succeed on a **DC 21 Constitution saving throw** or suffer 1 failed death save and lose all active magical effects (such as _bless_ or _haste_).
  - **The Cave.** Target must succeed on a **DC 21 Charisma saving throw** or be stunned, paralyzed, put to sleep, and petrified (petrification takes two full rounds to take effect). The target cannot repeat this saving throw and can only be cured with curse removal.

  The Commander heals for half the total damage dealt by this attack.

- **Particle Render.** _(Melee Weapon, One-Handed)_
  _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.
  _Hit:_ 17 (2d8 + 8) slashing damage.
  After a successful hit, the Commander may make one additional free attack with this weapon (this does not stack).

- **Thruster Axe-Kick** (Gap Closer)
  _Special Melee Weapon Attack:_ +14 to hit, range 10–30 ft., one target.  
  The Commander launches forward with thrusters, landing in an unoccupied space 5 ft. from the target.  
  _Hit:_ 25 (3d10 + 8) slashing damage. If the target is Large or smaller, it must succeed on a **DC 19 Strength saving throw** or be knocked prone.
  If the target takes damage from this attack, it [catches fire](/en/library/rules/steel-and-strife/conditions).

## Legendary Deed: Act

The Platonian Hunter Commander can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Weapon Attack.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Commander makes one attack with Bound Athena or Particle Renders.
- **Thruster Step.** (Costs 1 Deed) The Commander moves up to 30 ft. without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Maneuver.** (Costs 2 Deeds) The Commander uses one of its maneuvers.
- **Disambiguate.** (Costs 3 Deeds) The Commander makes one Disambiguator attack.
