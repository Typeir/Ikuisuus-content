## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Gigas has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of its turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short Rest)

When Gigas fails a saving throw, it can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Pre-Edict Entity

A Primordial Null predating the Canvas's first Edicts, Gigas cannot be targeted by Divination spells of 5th level or lower, and has **advantage on saving throws** against Divination spells of 6th level or higher. Effects that would forcibly transport Gigas to another plane automatically fail.

#### Graft-Born Arsenal

Gigas bears six additional limbs taken from the corpse of the Red One and fused over thirteen thousand years of deliberate grafting. Gigas cannot be disarmed of the Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista. Gigas ignores the **Loading** property of the Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista for the purpose of its Multiattack action.

#### Hollow Title

Whenever Gigas reduces a creature to 0 hit points, it regains **30 hit points** and recovers **1 expended use of Null and Void** (to a maximum of 3).

#### Preferred Distance

While no hostile creature is within 60 feet of Gigas, all ranged weapon attacks made by Gigas have **advantage**.

## Actions

#### Multiattack

Gigas makes **three attacks**: two with the **Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista** and one with a **Grafted Limb**. If no hostile creature is within 15 feet, Gigas may replace the Grafted Limb attack with a third Ballista attack.

---

#### Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista _(Loading — see Graft-Born Arsenal)_

_Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, range 300/1200 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ 8 + 5d12 piercing + 4d12 cold damage.
This weapon deals **double damage** to objects, constructs, and structures.

Whether this attack hits or misses, the bolt continues in a **10-foot-wide line** from the target's space to the weapon's maximum range. Each creature in this line other than the primary target must succeed on a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw** or take **3d12 piercing + 2d12 cold damage**. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage.

---

#### Grafted Limb

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.
_Hit:_ 3d10 + 8 force damage plus 2d8 fire damage.
The target must succeed on a **DC 20 Constitution saving throw** or take an additional **2d8 necrotic damage** as residual radiance bleeds from the dead graft. Creatures immune to disease and curses are not subject to this secondary effect.

---

#### Expulsion Strike _(Bonus Action)_

Gigas drives a grafted limb into a creature within 15 feet and hurls it away. The target must succeed on a **DC 23 Strength saving throw** or take **4d8 + 8 bludgeoning damage**, be pushed **30 feet** directly away from Gigas, and fall **prone**. On a successful save, the target takes half damage and is neither pushed nor knocked prone.

---

## Reactions

#### Null and Void _(3/Long Rest)_

**Trigger**: A creature Gigas can see within 120 feet ends its turn.

Gigas unmakes the causal chain of that turn. The triggering creature must succeed on a **DC 25 Charisma saving throw**. On a failed save, all of the following from that turn are retroactively reversed for all creatures within 50 miles of Gigas:

- All damage dealt or received during that turn
- All healing and temporary hit points gained or lost during that turn
- All conditions applied or removed during that turn
- All positions changed by willing movement or forced movement during that turn
- All active spell and ability effects that resolved during that turn

Spell slots, ability charges, and other resources expended on the nullified turn are **not** recovered.

A creature that fails this save has no clear memory of the nullified turn; it is aware only that a moment has passed without leaving a mark.
A creature that succeeds on this saving throw is immune to **Null and Void** for 24 hours.

## Legendary Deed: Act

Gigas can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature's turn.

- **Volley Step.** _(Costs 1 Deed)_ Gigas makes one Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista attack and immediately moves up to 20 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Grafted Rage.** _(Costs 1 Deed)_ Gigas makes two Grafted Limb attacks.
- **Forbidden Sun.** _(Recharge 6, Costs 2 Deeds)_ Gigas casts [_Forbidden Sun_](/en/library/spells/forbidden-sun) without expending a spell slot.

---

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Gigas can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. It cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### Fateful Seal

Gigas seals a region of causal space around a point it can see within 120 feet. Until the start of the next round, creatures within a **30-foot-radius sphere** centered on that point cannot teleport or be teleported. Abilities already active that would move a creature in this way end immediately.

#### Null Rift

Gigas declares a 300ft long, 5ft thick, 50ft tall line extending from itself in a direction it can see. The line is drained of all will and purpose, and anything that enters it is instantly stunned until the end of it's next turn, as it's existence is momentarily cessated.
When gigas declares this line, all creatures in it's path must succeed on a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw**. If they succeed, they are pushed 10 feet away from the line in a direction of their choosing, otherwise they are stunned as normal.

#### Tomb hail

Gigas fires a hail of tombsteel harpoons into the air. At the start of each creature's turn, one harpoon falls to the ground at a point Gigas can see within 120 feet. Each creature within 10 feet of that point must succeed on a **DC 22 Dexterity saving throw** or take **2d8 piercing damage** and **2d8 cold damage** and be **restrained** until the end of their next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes no damage.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### Dead Star Calculus

_Declared at the end of Gigas's turn; resolves at the start of its next turn._

**Declare**: Gigas designates a target it can see within 1,200 feet and begins calculating the precise ballistic trajectory. Until this stratagem resolves, the designated target **cannot benefit from cover** of any kind against Gigas's Gigantic Tombsteel Ballista attacks.

**Resolve**: Gigas fires a single decisive shot. The designated target must succeed on a **DC 23 Dexterity saving throw** or take **12d12 piercing + 8d12 cold damage** and be **stunned until the end of its next turn**. On a successful save, the target takes half damage and is not stunned.

Each creature within 10 feet of the target at the moment of impact must also make the saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes **5d12 piercing + 4d12 cold damage** and is pushed **20 feet** away from the point of impact. On a success, half damage, no push.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### Bloodied (50% HP) — Borrowed Radiance

When Gigas is first reduced to 50% of its maximum hit points, the grafted limbs surge with residual force drawn from the body they once belonged to — the red ruin of a slain Hiisi, burning away even pain.

- Gigas gains **1 additional legendary deed per round** (total 4).
- All **Grafted Limb** attacks deal an additional **2d8 radiant damage**.
- At this moment, all creatures within 120 feet must succeed on a **DC 22 Constitution saving throw** or take **6d8 radiant damage** and be **blinded until the end of their next turn**. On a successful save, a creature takes half damage and is not blinded.

---

#### Doomed (25% HP) — Hecatoncheire By Will

Gigas announces, to no one in particular, that it holds the Hecatoncheire title.

- Gigas's creature type becomes **Monstrosity**. It retains all other traits except as noted here.
- For the remainder of the fight, Gigas can replace one attack each round with a spell of level 6 or lower.
- Whenever Gigas deals fire damage to a creature, that creature must succeed on a **DC 22 Intelligence saving throw** or have a memory of its choice be permanently, irrevocably erased.

---

#### Slain — Null Collapse

When Gigas is reduced to 0 hit points, the void at its core implodes.
Each creature within 60 feet must succeed on a **DC 22 Charisma saving throw** or take **8d12 psychic damage** as the collapsing null pulls at the edges of structured thought. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage.

The grafted limbs of the Red One detach and crumble to ash.
