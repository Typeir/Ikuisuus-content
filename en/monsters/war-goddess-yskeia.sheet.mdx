## Traits

#### Legendary Deeds

Yskeia has **3 legendary deeds per round**, and regains all expended deeds at the start of her turn.

#### Legendary Deed: Resist (3/Short rest)

When Yskeia fails a saving throw, she can expend a legendary deed to succeed instead. This feature can be used **3 times per Short Rest**.

#### Primeval Plating

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/primeval-plating'
  customHandler='destructible_component'
/>

Yskeia's armor is made of an ancient alloy that hardens and softens at will.

Yskeia has 4 Primeval Platings that can be destroyed, each facing a different direction (front, back, left, right).
Each plating has AC 30, 100 HP, and resistance to all damage types barring force.
When a plating is destroyed, Yskeia loses 3 AC (to a minimum of 23) and takes 100 true damage as the plating shatters internal mechanisms.

#### Magic Resistance

Yskeia has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

#### Magical Weapons

All of yskeias attacks are considered magical for the purposes of overcomming resistances.

#### Siege Monster

Yskeia deals quadruple damage to objects and structures.

#### Marked for Decommission

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'

  customHandler='mark_target'

When Yskeia strikes a creature with any of its attacks, the target is **Marked for Decommission** until the end of Yskeia's next turn. Only one creature can be marked at a time.

#### Protected Air space

When a creature Yskeia can see within her lair attempts flight, Yskeia can use her reaction to force the creature to make a **DC 35 Dexterity saving throw**.  
On a failed save, the creature takes **243 force damage** and is **stunned until the start of its next turn**.  
On a success, it is forced 10 feet toward the ground, and takes no damage.

## Actions

#### **Multiattack**

Yskeia makes up to **three attacks per turn**, choosing an attack with her main cannon, and any combination of weapons.
She can replace any attack with a **drone deployment**, **Missile Batteries**, or **mortar volley**.

---

#### **The Frontline (Shield)**

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +22 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 51 (7d10 + 10) bludgeoning damage.  
A creature hit by The Frontline must succeed on a **DC 25 Strength saving throw** or be **pushed 20 feet away**.

---

#### **Spear of Retribution**

_Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +22 to hit, reach 20 ft. or range 120/360 ft., one target. This attack critically strikes on a roll of 18, 19 or 20.  
_Hit:_ 67 (12d8 + 13) piercing damage plus 31 (7d8) radiant damage.  
On a critical hit, the spear permanently reduces the creature’s proficiency bonus by **1**, until removed by a spell such as [_Greater Restoration_](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:greater-restoration) or similar, cast at a minimum slot level of 9.  
On hit, Yskeia may choose to **reel the target 30 feet toward her**. The target must succeed on a **DC 25 Strength saving throw** to resist being pulled.

Each mortar can target a point within **5 miles**.

#### **Flamethrower Legs**

Creatures reduced to 0 HP by this damage are **disintegrated into molten slag**.  
Creatures that fail the save are **pushed 15 feet away** from Yskeia.  
Yskeia can also make a **kick or stomp** attack (melee +22 to hit, 10 ft. reach, 32 (4d10 + 10) bludgeoning + 17 (3d10) fire damage) as a bonus action after using this ability.

## Legendary Deed: Act

Yskeia can expend legendary deeds to use the options below. Only one legendary deed can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn.

---

#### **Reposition** (Costs 1 Deed)

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/reposition'
  customHandler='geometry_teleport'
/>

Yskeia instantly relocates to any point that maintains the exact same distance from the creature currently **Marked for Decommission**.  
This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks, ignores terrain, and renders her immune to all damage and effects until the repositioning ends.

---

#### **Mutually Assured Destruction** (Costs 2 Deeds)

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/mutually-assured-destruction'
  customHandler='damage_reflection'
/>

Yskeia designates one creature she can see within 300 feet.  
The target must succeed on a **DC 32 Charisma saving throw** or become linked to her for 1 minute.  
While linked, all damage the target deals to Yskeia is reflected back to it as **true damage**, and any healing the creature receives is instead granted to Yskeia.  
The effect ends early if the creature succeeds on another **DC 32 Charisma saving throw** at the end of its turns.  
A creature that succeeds on either saving throw is immune to this effect for 24 hours.

---

#### **Attack** (Costs 1 Deed)

Yskeia makes one weapon attack of her choice.

---

#### **Faterender Railgun (Recharge 6)** (Costs 1 Deed)

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/faterender-railgun-recharge-6'
  customHandler='text_pipe'
/>

Yskeia fires a blinding rail of divine kinetic energy in a **10-foot-wide, 3000-foot-long line**.  
All creatures in the area must make a **DC 35 Dexterity saving throw**, **taking force damage equal to the sum of their ability scores** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
If a creature does not have at least one score equal to or greater than 20, it dies instantly on a failed save, or has its maximum and current hit points reduced to 1 on a success.
This damage ignores resistances, immunities, and cover.  
The shot targets creatures in sequence, beginning with the closest along the line.  
If a creature is reduced to 0 hit points by this attack, the creature is disintegrated and the shot continues to the next creature in line.

Objects and structures instead take 200 damage.

If the target is **Marked for Decommission**, it takes double damage and is also Paralyzed until the end of their next turn.

Once fired, the Faterender Railgun cannot be used again until it recharges.

## Legendary Deed: Lair

At the start of the creature with the highest initiative's turn, Yskeia can expend a legendary deed to use one of the following lair deeds. She cannot use the same lair deed two rounds in a row.

#### **Arms Race**

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/arms-race'
  customHandler='text_pipe'
/>

Yskeia designates two targets within 1 mile and fires a pair of **ordinance maelstroms**.  
Each maelstrom strikes its mark, embedding into the ground or air around the target as a **5-foot-thick, 5-foot-radius ring of superheated alloy and pressure**.

At the start of every creature’s turn in the initiative ladder, both maelstroms **expand by 5 feet** in radius, up to a **maximum of 60 feet**.  
The maelstroms remain perfectly circular and centered on their original deployment points.

- The maelstroms are **hollow**; creatures can remain within their center until the expanding edge reaches them.
- A creature whose space is touched by a maelstrom’s circumference must make a **DC 25 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **30 (6d10)** fire and **30 (6d10)** piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
- This damage also affects Yskeia, though she resists both fire and piercing.
- When a maelstrom reaches its maximum radius, it destabilizes and collapses at the end of that round.

If the two maelstroms’ circumferences **collide or overlap**, they trigger a catastrophic resonance:  
each creature within **300 feet** of the collision point must make a **DC 25 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **100 force damage** on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
After detonation, both maelstroms are destroyed and cannot be redeployed until the start of the next round.

> #### Ordinance Maelstrom Data
>
> | **Radius Range**                  | **Thickness** | **Maximum Radius** |
> | --------------------------------- | ------------- | ------------------ |
> | 5 ft → 60 ft (expands +5 ft/turn) | 5 ft          | 60 ft              |
>
> - **Damage**: 30 (6d10) fire + 30 (6d10) piercing (DC 25 Dex save ½)
> - **Collision Damage**: 100 force (DC 25 Dex save ½)
> - **Targets**: All creatures whose space the maelstrom’s edge passes through
> - **Notes**: Treated as high-velocity debris; cannot be dispelled or destroyed by magical means, save for disintegration effects.

#### **Sky Aflame**

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/sky-aflame'
  customHandler='environmental_zone'
/>

Yskeia fires a volley of atmosphere-piercing warheads into the upper sky.  
The munitions detonate above the battlefield, burning the air itself into plasma.

For the next **3 rounds**, the sky over a **1-mile radius** becomes a field of superheated debris and choking smoke.  
All creatures in the area immediately gain the conditions **[Burning](/en/library/rules/conditions)** and **[Suffocating](/en/library/rules/conditions)** until they exit the zone or the effect ends.

- Any effect or item that would remove the _Burning_ condition fails while within this zone.
- Nonmagical flames and breathable air cease to exist in the affected area.
- The sky glows white-orange, and visibility is reduced to **30 feet** due to radiant overexposure.
- Spells or effects that create or manipulate weather automatically fail.

When the effect ends, the atmosphere reforms over 1d4 minutes, leaving behind vitrified glass and scorched terrain.

#### **Tides of Ruin**

<Meta
  target='generator'
  type='feature'
  featureId='war-goddess-yskeia/tides-of-ruin'
  customHandler='text_pipe'
/>

From one side of the battlefield, a surge of discarded weaponry and war machines floods forward in a solid wall of motion.

The **Tides of Ruin** form a **10-foot-thick front** that spans the entire battlefield and advances **5 feet at the end of each turn**.  
Up to **four tides** may be active simultaneously, each moving independently from a direction of Yskeia’s choosing.

Any creature caught within or entering a tide’s space must make a **DC 30 Strength saving throw**.  
On a failed save, it takes **300 (60d10)** bludgeoning damage and is **restrained**, pinned within the mass until it escapes (DC 28 Strength check at the end of its turns).
When the mass moves, restrained creatures are moved along with it.  
On a success, it takes half as much damage and is pushed 30 feet ahead of the advancing front.

- The Tides destroy all nonmagical terrain, structures, and barriers they pass through.
- Unattended objects of Huge size or smaller are automatically crushed or swept away.
- When a tide reaches the far edge of the arena, it collapses, creating a shrapnel storm.  
  All creatures within **40 feet** of the collapse must make a **DC 25 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **200 (40d10)** piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

## Legendary Deed: Stratagem

#### **Lattice of Fire**

Yskeia activates a battlefield-wide firing grid.  
Thousands of precision kinetic rounds are released in perfect intervals, forming a **lattice of intersecting lines** that spans the entire arena.

Lines spaced 5 feet apart covering the entire lair become paths of concentrated fire.  
Creatures occupying any of these lines must make a **DC 30 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **180 (40d8)** piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.  
A creature that fails the save is also **knocked prone**.

- The **5-foot gaps** between lines are the only safe zones.
- All nonmagical structures and terrain features caught in the grid are obliterated.
- Yskeia can chose whether the lines are horizontal or vertical, but never in any other orientation.

## Legendary Deed: Phase

#### **Wounded (75%) — Call to Counsel**

When Yskeia is first reduced to **75% of her maximum hit points**, she lowers her weapons and speaks a single word that echoes through the air like an order remembered from an ancient war.

From the distance, **[_War Counselor Of Yskeia_](/en/library/monsters/war-counselor-of-yskeia)**, descends onto the field — a dwarven cleric, armored in sun-marked bronze and carrying her blessed armaments.  
He joins the initiative immediately after Yskeia.

> “Her voice did not falter. Nor did my shield.” — War Counselor Of Yskeia

---

#### **Doomed (25%) — Athena Protocol**

When Yskeia is reduced to **25% of her maximum hit points**, she turns to War Counselor Of Yskeia and holds out the **Spear of Retribution**.  
Without hesitation, he takes it.  
Unburdened, Yskeia reaches for the blade that was made to end Fate itself — **Athena**, broken long ago by the White Homunculus.

**Athena** is a +4 divine weapon of impossible radiance.  
From this point onward, Yskeia replaces all attacks made with the Spear of Retribution with Athena.

#### **Athena, Blade of Resolution**

_Melee Weapon Attack:_ +27 to hit (Wisdom-based), reach 25 ft., one target. Athena critically strikes on a roll of 18, 19, or 20.  
_Hit:_ **122 (20d10 + 15)** slashing damage plus **66 (12d10)** radiant damage.
On a hit, Athena strips away magical protections: the target loses all spell effects, resistances, immunities, and temporary hit points until the end of Yskeia's next turn.
On a critical hit, the target's **AC is permanently reduced by 2** until removed by a spell such as [_Greater Restoration_](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:greater-restoration) or similar, cast at a minimum slot level of 9.

While wielding Athena:

- War Counselor Of Yskeia wields the **Spear of Retribution**, dealing **58 (10d8 + 13)** piercing plus **27 (6d8)** radiant damage.
- Whenever Athena strikes a creature **Marked for Decommission**, that creature’s **AC is permanently reduced by 1**.
  This effect stacks and can only be undone by a spell such as [_Greater Restoration_](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:greater-restoration) or similar, cast at a minimum slot level of 9.

> _“She laid down command and took up judgment.” — The Dreamcatcher, on their confrontation with Yskeia_
